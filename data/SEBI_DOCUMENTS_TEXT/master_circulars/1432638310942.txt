Securities  and  Exchange  Board  of  India  (SEBI)  has  been  issuing  various 
circulars/directions  from  time  to  time.  In  order  to  enable  the  users  to  have  an

Circular in respect of Stock Exchange & Clearing Corporation has been prepared. 
 
This Master Circular is a compilation of the circulars/communications issued by
