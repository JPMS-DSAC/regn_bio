Sub: Master Circular for Stock Exchange - Cash Market 

  
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has been issuing various circulars 
from  time  to  time.  In  order  to  enable  the  users  to  have  an  access  to  all  the 
applicable circulars at one place, Master Circular for Stock Exchange/ Cash Market 
has been prepared. 
 
This  Master  Circular  is  a  compilation  of  the  circulars  issued  by  SEBI  up  to 
December 31, 2010 and shall come into force from the date of its issue.
