Dear Sir/Madam 

 

Sub: Enhanced Supervision of Stock Brokers / Depository Participants 

 
Please  refer  to  SEBI  Circular  No.  SEBI/HO/MIRSD/MIRSD2/CIR/2016/95  dated 

September  26,  2016  regarding  the  captioned  subject.  Following  representations 

from  Exchanges  as  well  as  market  participants,  it  has  been  decided  that  the 

aforesaid Circular would be made effective from April 1, 2017. Various  timelines 

mentioned  in  the  circular  for  the  different  activities  will  consequently  get 

postponed.  Accordingly, the timeline indicated in the relevant paragraphs would 

stand revised as per Annexure. 

 

Yours faithfully, 

 

Debashis Bandyopadhyay  

General Manager  

Market Intermediaries Regulation and Supervision Department
