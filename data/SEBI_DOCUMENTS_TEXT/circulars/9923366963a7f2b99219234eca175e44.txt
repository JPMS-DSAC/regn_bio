Circulars 


DIVISION CHIEF SECONDARY MARKET DEPARTMENT - I
SMD(N)/JS/170/96 January 15, 1996
To,
The Presidents / Executive Directors of all Stock Exchanges
Dear Sir,
Notices of the Board/Council meetings to SEBI nominated directors on the Exchange.
We have received a communication from the DCA, New Delhi stating that their nominee directors on your Stock Exchange do not get adequate notice of the Board / Council meetings.
The Stock Exchange is requested to ensure that hence- forth the notices of such meeting reach the concerned SEBI nominee Directors on the Exchanges well in time.
Yours faithfully, sd/-
R.V. NABAR
