SMD/POLICY/CIR-6//97
May 07, 1997.
To Executive Directors/Presidents/ Managing Directors of all Stock Exchanges.
Dear Sir,
Based on the suggestions/representations received from various Stock Exchanges, SEBI has examined the applicability of Rule 8(1)(f) and 8(3)(f) of the Securities Contract (Regulation) Rules, 1957, relating to Fund Based Activities of Brokers. It has been opined that borrowing and lending of funds, by a trading member, in connection with or incidental to or consequential upon the securities business, would not be disqualified under Rule 8(1)(f) and 8(3)(f).
Yours sincerely,    
M.D. PATEL EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR SECONDARY MARKET DEPARTMENT
