August 14, 1998
The Executive Directors/Managing Directors, of all Stock Exchanges & The Administrator, Magadh Stock Exchange   
Dear Sir,
Sub : Modifications in the format of the Monthly Development Report.
Please refer to the circular no. SMD/POLICY/CIR-8/97 dated April 28, 1997 on the revised format for the Monthly Development Report (MDR). Enclosed please find the modified format for reporting details of completed settlements during the month, forming point no.1 of Section-I of the MDR.
You are requested to incorporate the modifications in point No. 1 of Section-I, as per the enclosed format. Please note that there are no changes in the format of the MDR from Point No. 2 onwards.
You are therefore, requested to submit the MDR in the modified format from the month of August 1998 onwards.
Yours faithfully,
P. K. BINDLISH
