Securities and Exchange Board of India 

 

 

CIRCULAR 

 
 

 

 

 

 

            

CIR/CFD/DIL/6/2011 
 
To 
All Registered Merchant Bankers 
 
Dear Sir/Madam, 
 
Sub:  Filing  Offer  Documents  with  SEBI  under  SEBI  (Issue  of  Capital  and  Disclosure 

   September 28, 2011 

Requirements) Regulations, 2009 

 

1.  Please 

refer 

to  SEBI  Circular  Nos.  SEBI/CFD/ICDRR/2/2009/29/09 

and 
CIR/CFD/DIL/9/2010 dated September 29, 2009 and October 13, 2010 respectively on 
the captioned subject.  
 

2.  As  a  green  initiative,  it  has  been  decided  to  reduce  the  number  of  copies  of  offer 
documents being submitted to SEBI. Also, considering the availability of the soft copies 
of the offer documents in the websites of SEBI and the concerned merchant bankers, it 
is hereby prescribed as under:- 

a)  Three  copies  of  the  draft  offer  document  shall  be filed  with  the  relevant  office  of 

the Board. 
 

b)  One copy of the offer document shall be filed with the relevant office of the Board.   

 

3.  All the other provisions of the above mentioned circulars remain unchanged.  

 

4.  Applicability 

The  above  provisions  shall  be  applicable  for  all  draft/final  offer  documents  filed  with 
SEBI on or after the date of this circular. 
 

5.  The  above  listing  conditions  are  specified  in  exercise  of  the  powers  conferred  under 
Section 11 read with Section 11A of the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 
1992.  
 

6.  This  circular  is  available  on  SEBI  website  at  www.sebi.gov.in  under  the  categories 

“Legal Framework” and “Issues and Listing”. 

Yours faithfully,
