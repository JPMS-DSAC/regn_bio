Subject: Composition of arbitration committee 

 
1.  SEBI,  vide  its  various  communications  has  mandated  all  stock  exchanges 
that  not  be  more  than  twenty  percent  of  the  members  of  the  arbitration 
committee shall be trading members.  

 
2.  It  has  now  been  decided  to  do  away  with  the  representation  of  trading 
members  on  arbitration  committee/panel  of  all  stock  exchanges.  It  is, 
henceforth,  stipulated  that  the  arbitration  committee/panel  shall  not 
comprise of any trading members.  

3.  The stock exchanges are advised to:- 

a.  make necessary amendments to the relevant bye-laws, rules and 
the  above  decision

website; and 

c.  communicate  to  SEBI,  the  status  of  implementation  of  the 
provisions  of  this  circular  in  the  Monthly  Development  Reports  to 
SEBI.
