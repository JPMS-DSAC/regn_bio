Dear Sir / Madam, 

Subject: Permitting Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPI) to invest in Municipal Bonds  

 

1.  RBI  vide  A.P.  (DIR  Series)  Circular  No.  33  dated  April  25,  2019  has  permitted  FPIs  to 

 

invest in municipal bonds. 
 

2.  It  is  decided  that  in  accordance  with  the  provisions  of  Regulation  21(1)(p)  of  SEBI 
(Foreign  Portfolio  Investors)  Regulations,  2014,  FPIs  are,  henceforth,  permitted  to 
invest in municipal bonds.  
 

3.  This  circular  is  issued  in  exercise  of  powers  conferred  under  Section  11  (1)  of  the 

Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992.  
 

4.  A  copy  of  this  circular  is  available  at  the  web  page  “Circulars”  on  our  website 
www.sebi.gov.in. Custodians are requested to bring the contents of this circular to the 
notice of their FPI clients. 

Yours faithfully,
