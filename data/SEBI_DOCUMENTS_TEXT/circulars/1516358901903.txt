5.  The simultaneous filing of documents as mentioned above i.e. physical and online 
shall start from February 1, 2018 and continue till March 31, 2018. Thereafter, from 
April 1, 2018 physical filing of the aforesaid documents shall be discontinued and 
only online filing will be accepted.  
 

6.  SEBI registered Merchant Bankers and recognized stock exchanges have already 

been advised to activate their online accounts.  
 

7.  This circular is issued in exercise of the powers conferred under Section 11(1) of 
the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992, to protect the interests of 
investors  in  securities  and  to  promote  the  development  of  and  to  regulate  the 
securities market.
