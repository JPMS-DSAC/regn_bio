Securities  and  Exchange  Board  of  India  Act,  1992  and  Regulation  17  of  the  SEBI 
(KYC  (Know  Your  Client)  Registration  Agency)  Regulations,  2011  to  protect  the 
interests of investors in securities and to promote the development of, and to regulate 
the securities markets.
