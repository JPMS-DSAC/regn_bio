Sub.: Applications Supported by Blocked Amount (ASBA) facility in public issues 
 
1. 

This  has  reference  to  circular  No.  SEBI/CFD/DIL/ASBA/1/2009/30/12  dated 
December 30, 2009 in the captioned matter, wherein as para 2(c)(i), ASBA facility 
was  available  to  all  the  investors  except  Qualified  Institutional  Buyers  (QIBs).  In 
this regard SEBI has decided to extend the ASBA facility to QIBs in public issues 
opening on or after May 1, 2010.
