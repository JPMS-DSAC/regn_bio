3.   Further, it has now been clarified by the Government  of India and  Reserve Bank 
of  India  that  FII  investments  shall  be  restricted  to  only  listed  debt  securities of 
companies.  It  is  also  clarified  that  the  FIIs  subscription  to  Commercial  Papers 
would be within the ceiling of US $1.5 billion (earlier US $500 million) fixed for 
debt instruments (other than G-Sec and T-Bills).  

 
4.    The  revised  individual  limits  for  investment in  Government  securities  and  limits 
for  Corporate  Debt  will  be  advised  to  the  100%  debt  FIIs/  Sub  Accounts 
separately.
