Dear Sir/Madam, 
 
Sub:  Procedure  to  deal  with  cases  prior  to  April  01,  2014  involving  offer  / 
allotment  of  securities  to  more  than  49  and  up  to  200  investors  in  a 
financial year. 
 
1.  SEBI,  vide  circular  No.  CIR/CFD/DIL3/18/2015  dated  December  31,  2015, 
prescribed  the  procedure  to  deal  with  cases  involving  offer  /  allotment  of 
securities to more than 49 and up to 200 persons.  

 

2.  Para  7  of  the  Circular  provides  for  submission  of  a  certificate  from  an 
independent  peer  reviewed  practicing  Chartered  Accountant  certifying 
compliance as prescribed in the circular. 

3.  It  has  now  been  decided  that  the  certification  as  provided  in  Para  7  of  the 
Circular dated December 31, 2015 may also be provided by an independent 
peer reviewed practicing Company Secretary.  

 

The  Stock  Exchanges  are  advised  to  bring  the  provisions  of  this  circular  to  the 
notice of listed entities and also to disseminate the same on their websites.
