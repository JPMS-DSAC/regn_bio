To All Registered Merchant Bankers 
 
Dear Sirs, 
 
Sub.: Reduction in filing fees for offer documents. 
 
1.0 

In  exercise  of  the  powers  conferred  by  section  30  read  with  clause  (k)  of  sub-
section (2) of section 11 of the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992 
(15 of 1992), SEBI has amended paragraph (1) of Schedule IV of the Securities 
and Exchange Board of India (Buy Back of Securities) Regulations, 1998, clause 
(1)  of  Schedule  IV  of  the  Securities  and  Exchange  Board  of  India  (Merchant 
Bankers)  Regulations,  1992  and  sub-regulation  (3)  of  regulation  (4)  and  sub-
regulation  (3)  of  regulation  (18)  of  the  Securities  and  Exchange  Board  of  India 
(Substantial  Acquisition  of  Shares  and  Takeovers)  Regulations,  1997,  thereby 
revising the fees payable on offer documents filed with SEBI. 

The amendments have come into force on April 01, 2008. 

 
2.0 
 
3.0  A  copy  of  the  Securities  and  Exchange  Board  of  India  (Payment  of  Fees) 
(Amendment) Regulations, 2008 issued in this regard, vide notification no. F. No. 
11/LC/GN/2008/21669  dated  March  31,  2008  and  published  in  the  Official 
Gazette, is enclosed at Annexure I. 

 
4.0  All merchant bankers are advised to take note of the aforementioned amendments 

and ensure compliance.  

 
Yours faithfully, 
 
 
Neelam Bhardwaj
