Sub: Revisions in submission of reports on two way fungibility of ADRs/GDRs 

Ref:- SEBI circular no. IMD/CUST/11/2003 dated December 11, 2003  

2. 

1.  The report prescribed in the above mentioned circular shall now be submitted by the 
custodians  as  part  F  of  the  monthly  report  specified  in  SEBI  circular  dated  July  21, 
2008. For ease of reference the format is appended herewith. 
In  view  of  the  above  custodians  need  not  submit  the  said  format  in  hard  copy  or  in 
floppies separately. 
It has also been decided to do away with the submission of copies of contract notes to 
SEBI.  As  and  when  required,  SEBI  shall  requisition  the  custodians  to  provide  the 
copies of the contract notes.  

3. 

4.  The  custodians  of  securities  are  requested  to  take  note  of  the  above  mentioned 

revisions. 

 
A copy of this circular is available at the web page “F.I.I.” on our website www.sebi.gov.in.
