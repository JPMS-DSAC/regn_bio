2.  As specified in the table above, the period beginning from March 23, 2020 till May 17, 2020 
shall be excluded for computing the existing timelines specified in Regulation 74 (4) and 
74 (5) of SEBI (Depositories & Participants) Regulations,  1996 and the aforementioned 
circular dated December 23, 2011. Further,  a 15-day time period after  May 17, 2020  is 
allowed to the SEBI registered intermediary, to clear the back log. 
 

3.  Stock Exchanges and Depositories are directed to bring the provisions of this circular to 
the notice of their members / participants and also disseminate the same on their website.
