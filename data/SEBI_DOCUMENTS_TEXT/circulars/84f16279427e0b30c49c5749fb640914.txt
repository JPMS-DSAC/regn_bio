Dear Sir,
Sub : Allotment of Code to The Capital Stock Exchange of Kerala Ltd.
Please refer to our Circular No.SMD-I/22532 dated October 19, 1993, whereby codes were alloted to different Stock Exchanges. As you are aware that SEBI has recently recognised The Capital Stock Exchange of Kerala Ltd. Accordingly, The Capital Stock Exchange of Kerala Ltd. has been allotted Code No.25 and the members will be issued registration number beginning with INB25.
For the purposes of trading and settlement operations, you are advised to inform the members of your Exchange accordingly.
