 Order against Shri Shailesh Jhaveri and Smt. Harsha M. Shah
in the matter of M/s. Ojas Technochem Products Ltd.
 
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated November 13, 2007 against Shri Shailesh Jhaveri and Smt. Harsha M. Shah in the matter of M/s. Ojas Technochem Products Ltd. restraining the two entities from accessing the securities market and also prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities either directly or indirectly for a period of two years for violating the provisions of Regulations 4 (b) and (d) of Securities and Exchange Board of India (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices Relating to Securities Market) Regulations, 1995
 
The full text of the order is available on the website : www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
November 13, 2007
