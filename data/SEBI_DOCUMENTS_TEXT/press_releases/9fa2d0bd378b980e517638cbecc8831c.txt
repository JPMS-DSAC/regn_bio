Visit to SEBI as a part of financial education initiatives of SEBI
As part of its financial education initiatives to impart basic understanding of securities market, Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) invites students (of classes IXth Standard and above) from schools, colleges and professional institutes in batches of 60. A slide show regarding securities market, SEBI and its role as a regulator would be part of the visit. This will be followed by a brief question answer session. The head of the institution may send email in this regard to visitsebi@sebi.gov.in or may please write to:
The Division Chief,Investor Awareness Division,Office of Investor Awareness and Education,Securities and Exchange Board of India,Plot C4 A, G Block, Bandra Kurla Complex,Bandra (E), Mumbai- 400051Ph: 022 2644 9188Fax: 022 26449027
Further details are available at the website: http://investor.sebi.gov.in or https://www.sebi.gov.in
