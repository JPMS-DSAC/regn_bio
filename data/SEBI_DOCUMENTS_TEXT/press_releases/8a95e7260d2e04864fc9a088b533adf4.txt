PRESS RELEASE
PR No223/2007

Order against M/s. Bhavik Rajesh Khandhar Share and 
Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd., Member, BSE
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  July 25, 2007 to the effect that no penalty has been imposed upon M/s. Bhavik Rajesh Khandhar Share and Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd., Member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) and the enquiry proceedings against the said entity has been disposed of in the matter of M/s. Sword and Shield Pharma Ltd.
The full text of the above order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
July 26, 2007
