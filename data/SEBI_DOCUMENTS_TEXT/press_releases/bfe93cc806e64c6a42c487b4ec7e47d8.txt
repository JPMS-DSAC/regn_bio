PR No.13/2007


 
Order against M/s. Dalmia Securities Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of 
M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. 
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order dated January 11, 2007 against M/s. Dalmia Securities Pvt. Ltd., broker, National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. (NSE), Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) and Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE), in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. disposing of the show cause notice issued against the broker without imposing any penalty.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
