Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  May 29, 2009 imposing a penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of M/s. Ballabh Dass Daga, Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Association Ltd., for a period of two months in respect of its dealing in the shares of M/s. Sharadraj Trade Finance Ltd.
The order shall come into force after expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
