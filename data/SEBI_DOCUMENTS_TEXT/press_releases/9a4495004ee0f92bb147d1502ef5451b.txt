PR No.225/2010 
Consent Order on the application submitted by M/s. Bharti Consumer Marketing Pvt. Ltd. and others
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. K.M. Abraham and   Shri Prashant Saran, has passed a consent order dated October 06, 2010 on the consent application submitted by M/s. Bharti Consumer Marketing Pvt. Ltd. on behalf of self and on behalf of M/s. Agallochun Investment Pvt. Ltd., M/s. Aught Investment Pvt. Ltd., M/s. Anil Hospitality Ventures Ltd., M/s. Rahil Marketing Pvt. Ltd., M/s. Agranil Marketing Ltd. and M/s. Ascent Knowledge Systems Ltd.  (the “applicants”)   for delay in making the public announcement in terms of Regulation 14(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers), Regulations, 1997 for acquisition of 19, 53,234 equity shares of the    M/s. Anil Product Ltd.
The applicant without admitting or denying guilt has remitted a sum of ` 1,00,000/- (Rupees one lakh only) towards settlement charges in the matter.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
