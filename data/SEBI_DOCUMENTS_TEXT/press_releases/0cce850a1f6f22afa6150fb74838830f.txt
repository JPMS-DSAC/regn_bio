 PRESS RELEASE




January 27, 2004 PR No.22/2004
DIVISION OF CORPORATE RESTRUCTURING 
In the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of JCT Ltd 
 
An application was made by Shri M M Thapar, Chairman and Managing Director of the M.M.Thapar Group of companies, to the Securities and Exchange Board of India (hereinafter referred to as "SEBI") on behalf of Provestment Securities Pvt. Ltd. (PSL) (hereinafter referred to as "the acquirer") seeking exemption from complying with the provisions of regulations 10 and 12 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 (hereinafter referred to as "the said Regulations") in respect of proposed acquisition of 4,23,72,972 (12.33%) equity shares and voting rights of the JCT Ltd (hereinafter referred to as "the target company").
 
This application was referred to the Takeover Panel. The Takeover Panel recommended grant of exemption as sought.
 
SEBI in terms of Regulation 4(6) of the Regulations has passed an order dated January 22, 2004, granted exemption, to the Acquirer from complying with the provisions Chapter III of the Regulations with regard to the proposed acquisition of 4,23,72,972 (12.33%) equity shares and voting rights of the target company.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the SEBI website at www.sebi.gov.in.
