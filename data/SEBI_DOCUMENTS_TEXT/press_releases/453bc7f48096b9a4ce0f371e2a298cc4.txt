PR No.177/2010
Order against M/s. Deepak Jhunjhunwala & Co., member, CSE
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated    July 26, 2010, warning the broker M/s. Deepak Jhunjhunwala & Co., member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd. for its dealings in the shares of Radiant Financial Services Ltd. and further advised it to exercise adequate caution while functioning as a stock broker.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
