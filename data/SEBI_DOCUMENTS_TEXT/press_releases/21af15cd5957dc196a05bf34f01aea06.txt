Order in the case of Shri Ajay Goda and Shri Nimish Goda in the matter of Database Finance Limited
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated November 17, 2009 disposing of the show cause notice dated November 22, 2006 issued under Section 11, 11B and 11(4) of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Regulation 11 of SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices Relating to Securities Markets) Regulations 2003 to Shri Ajay Goda and Shri Nimish Goda in the matter of Database Finance Limited without passing any direction against them.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
