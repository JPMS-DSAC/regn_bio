PRESS RELEASE
PR No.125/2006

Order against Shri Chandravadan J. Dalal, Member, BSE Ltd.
Enquiry proceedings initiated by SEBI against Shri Chandravadan J Dalal, Member, the Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd.  (BSE Ltd.) for trading in the scrips of Global Telesystems Limited, Adani Exports Limited and Himachal Futuristic Communications Limited have been abated on account of the death of Shri Chandravadan J. Dalal, the sole proprietor of the firm.
The order has been passed by Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI on April 17, 2006.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
April 27, 2006
