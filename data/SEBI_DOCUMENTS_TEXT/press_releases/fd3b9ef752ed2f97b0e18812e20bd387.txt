PRESS RELEASE
PR No.250/2006

Order in the matter of M/s. Abhipra Capital Ltd., Member, NSE
 
 Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  November 02, 2006 warning M/s. Abhipra Capital Ltd., Member, National Stock Exchange (NSE)  to be more cautious in its dealings with securities and adhere to the provisions of SEBI Act and the Regulations.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
