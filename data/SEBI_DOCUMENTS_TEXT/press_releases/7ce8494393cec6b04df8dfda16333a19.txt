PRESS RELEASE
PR No.319/2007


 
A Note on ‘New Products in F&O Segment’ is placed for public comments
In continuation with SEBI Board decision to introduce new products in derivatives segment – based on the interim recommendations of the Derivatives Market Review Committee headed by Professor M. Rammohan Rao – a Note on the same is put on SEBI website to seek comments/ suggestions, on or before December 21, 2007.
