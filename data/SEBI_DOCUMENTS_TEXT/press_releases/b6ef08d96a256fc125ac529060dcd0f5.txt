PRESS RELEASE
PR No.246/2004
  
Registration of M/s. Prabhat Investments,
Member, DSE, suspended
Shri A.K.Batra, Whole Time Member, SEBI vide order dated August 31, 2004, has suspended the certificate of registration of M/s. Prabhat Investments, member, Delhi Stock Exchange, for a period of two months. This order shall come into force on expiry of three weeks from the date of this order. 
The penalty is imposed for various irregularities by the member while dealing in the scrip of M/s. Vatsa World Ltd.
 
The full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in

 

Mumbai
September 23, 2004
