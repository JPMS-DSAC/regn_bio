PRESS RELEASE
August 18, 2004 PR No.187/2004

 
Registration of M/s.Option Securities Pvt. Ltd.,
Member of Delhi Stock Exchange, suspended
Whole Time Member, SEBI vide order dated August 16, 2004, has suspended the certificate of registration of M/s. Option Securities Pvt. Ltd., member Delhi Stock Exchange, for a period of One month. This order shall come into force on expiry of three weeks from the date of this order. The penalty is imposed for various irregularities by the member while dealing in the scrip of M/s.Vatsa World Ltd.
The full text of the order is available on our website www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
August 18, 2004
