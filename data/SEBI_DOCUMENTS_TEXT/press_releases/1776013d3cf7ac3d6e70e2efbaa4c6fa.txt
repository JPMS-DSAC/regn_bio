PR No.56/2007

 
Order against M/s. Titan Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd., Member, CSE 
in the matter of M/s. DSQ Industries Ltd.
 
 
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order on February 12, 2007 suspending certificate of registration of M/s. Titan Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd., member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Association Ltd., (SEBI Regn. No.INB031051437) for a period of six months in the matter of M/s. DSQ Industries Ltd.
 
The certificate of registration of M/s. Titan Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd., member of CSE  will remain suspended from March 05, 2007 till September 04, 2007.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
