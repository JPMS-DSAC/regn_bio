PR No.58/2010
ORDER IN THE MATTER OF OASIS MEDIA MATRIX LTD 
IN RESPECT OF MR. VRAJESHKUMAR R PATEL
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 04, 2010 restraining Mr. Vrajeshkumar R Patel, from buying, selling or dealing in securities market whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of six months from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
