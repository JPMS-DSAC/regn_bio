PRESS RELEASE
PR No.10/2008


Order in the matter of M/s. Vijay Textiles Limited 
 


Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 11, 2008 against the Trading Member, Galaxy Broking Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Vijay Textiles Limited, imposing a minor penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of M/s. Galaxy Broking Ltd., for a period of fifteen days.
 
The order will come into effect immediately after the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
