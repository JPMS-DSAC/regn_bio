April 06, 2004 PR No.77/2004
SUBJECT: OFFER DOCUMENTS RECEIVED AND OBSERVATIONS ISSUED BY SEBI DURING THE PERIOD 29/03/2004 TO 04/04/2004
 
ISSUED BY: CORPORATION FINANCE DEPARTMENT – DIVISION 
OF ISSUES AND LISTING
During the period 29/03/2004 to 04/04/2004, four offer documents were received by SEBI. During the same period, SEBI had issued two observations on offer documents filed with it.
List I gives details of offer documents received and List II gives details of offer documents on which observations were issued.
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in ) under "Primary Market".
