PR No.138/2010
Order against M/s. Basant Periwal & Co., member, CSE
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated June 15, 2010 imposing a penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of M/s. Basant Periwal & Co., Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd. (CSE), for a period of one month in respect of its dealings in the shares of Bakra Pratisthan Ltd.
 
The order shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
Mumbai
June 17, 2010
