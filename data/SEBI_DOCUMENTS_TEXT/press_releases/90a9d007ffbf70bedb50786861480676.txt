PRESS RELEASE
PR No.104/2005

                                                                                                            
Registration of SUN F&C Mutual Fund, cancelled
On a request made by SUN F&C Mutual Fund, Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) vide its order dated August 05, 2005 has cancelled the certificate of registration of SUN F&C Mutual Fund and has also withdrawn the approval granted to SUN F&C Asset Management (I) Pvt. Ltd. to act as the asset management company.  This order has been passed by Shri Madhukar,   Whole Time Member, SEBI.
Consequently, with immediate effect, the SUN F&C Mutual Fund, the Board of Trustees of SUN F&C Mutual Fund and the SUN F&C Asset Management (I) Pvt. Ltd. cannot carry out any activity as a mutual fund, trustees or asset management company as the case may be, in accordance with the regulations. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
August 19, 2005
