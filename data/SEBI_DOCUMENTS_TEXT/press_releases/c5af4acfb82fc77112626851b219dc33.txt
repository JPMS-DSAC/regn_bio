PR No.78/2010
ORDER IN THE MATTER OF CALS REFINERIES LIMITED AND OTHERS
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 25, 2010, confirming the ad interim ex-parte order dated June 04, 2009 against Chimming Trading Company Limited in the matter of alleged manipulative trading by certain connected entities in the scrips of Cals Refineries Limited and others.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
