PR No.218/2010
Consent Order on the application submitted by M/s. Venkatesh Shares Investment in the case of M/s. Doctors Biotech Ltd.
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. K.M.Abraham and Shri Prashant Saran has passed a consent order dated September 24, 2010 on the application submitted by M/s. Venkatesh Shares Investment, in the matter of M/s. Doctors Biotech Ltd., in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted a sum of ` 2,00,000/- (Rupees two lakh only) and shall undergo a voluntary debarment of one year  from accessing the securities market towards the settlement terms. 
The order has come into force with immediate effect. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
