PR No.220/2007

 
Order against M/s. T.J. Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Sun Infoways Ltd.
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated July 17, 2007, against  M/s. T.J. Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd., broker, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) in the matter of M/s. Sun Infoways Ltd., directing the broker to be careful and cautious in future while dealing in securities.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
