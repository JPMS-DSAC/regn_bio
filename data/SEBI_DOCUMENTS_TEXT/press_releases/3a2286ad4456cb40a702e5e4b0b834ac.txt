PRESS RELEASE
PR No.19/2005

 
Registration of M/s. CFL Securities Ltd., member of NSE, suspended
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated January 19, 2005 suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. CFL Securities Ltd., member of National Stock Exchange, for a period of six months.
 
M/s. CFL Securities Ltd., violated the provisions of SEBI circulars, SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices) Regulations and SEBI Stock Broker Regulations.
 
The full text of the order is available on our website www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
January 25, 2005
