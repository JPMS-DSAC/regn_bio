PR No.288/2007

Order against M/s. Jyotish Bhogilal Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. 
in the matter of M/s. MOH Ltd.
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated October 24, 2007, has disposed off the show cause notice issued to M/s. Jyotish Bhogilal Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd., (SEBI Registration no. INS010736432) Sub-Broker of  M/s. Prabhudas Lilladhar Pvt. Ltd, Member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) in the matter of M/s. MOH Ltd. without prejudice to any other action, in accordance with law.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
