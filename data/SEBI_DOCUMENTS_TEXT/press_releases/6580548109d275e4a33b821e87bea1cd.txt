
 
Jan 13, 2015

PR No.:  
8/2015


Shri S. Raman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 07, 2015, in the matter of Summary Proceedings in respect of Mr. Shreenath Kapoor, a registered stock broker of The Calcutta Stock  Exchange Ltd.,  suspending  the certificate of registration of stock broker Mr. Shreenath Kapoor till the time the outstanding  registration fee (as on the date of payment) are fully paid. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
