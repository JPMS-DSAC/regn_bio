PRESS RELEASE
PR No.85/2007

Order against M/s. Dimensional Securities Private Limited in the matter of M/s. Vatsa Music Ltd.
M/s. Dimensional Securities Private Limited, Member, National Stock Exchange have been censured for non-exercising due skill and care as required under the Regulations and also have been advised to be careful and not to repeat the violation/lapses in future to avoid major penalty. The order has been passed by Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI on March 05, 2007.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
