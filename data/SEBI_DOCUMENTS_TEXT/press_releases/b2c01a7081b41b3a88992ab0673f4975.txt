PR No.169/2010
Order in the matter of Parsoli Corporation Ltd.
 
Shri M. S. Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  July 22, 2010, in the matter of Parsoli Corporation Ltd., restraining Parsoli Corporation Ltd. from buying, selling or dealing in securities market in any manner whatsoever or accessing the securities market directly or indirectly for a period of six months from the date of the order.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
