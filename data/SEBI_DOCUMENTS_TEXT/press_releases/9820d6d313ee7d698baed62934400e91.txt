PRESS RELEASE
PR No.319/2004
 
 Offer documents received / withdrawn and observations issued by SEBI from November 22, 2004 to November 28, 2004
During the period November 22, 2004 to November 28, 2004, SEBI had not received any offer documents and had not issued any observations on offer documents filed with it. During the same period, no offer document was withdrawn.
