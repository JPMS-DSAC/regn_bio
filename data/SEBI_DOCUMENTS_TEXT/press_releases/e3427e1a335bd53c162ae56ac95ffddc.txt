AMENDMENTS TO SEBI (DISCLOSURE AND INVESTOR PROTECTION) GUIDELINES, 2000.
 
SEBI, vide its circular SEBI/CFD/DIL/DIP/12/2004/8/4  dated April 8, 2004 issued to all the registered Merchant Bankers has amended SEBI (DIP) guidelines 2000 with immediate effect. 
The amendments have been carried out inter alia to introduce the facility of shelf prospectus, strengthening the requirements for preferential issues and simplifying some operational procedures.     All these policy initiatives taken together will help in enhancing  transparency and efficacy in the market.
Details of the aforesaid circular is available in SEBI website at www.sebi.gov.in. Full text of SEBI (DIP) guidelines 2000 including the amendments issued vide this circular is also available in SEBI’s web site under Primary Market Section.
