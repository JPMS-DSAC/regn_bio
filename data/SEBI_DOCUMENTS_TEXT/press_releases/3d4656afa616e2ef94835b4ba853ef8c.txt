PR No.65/2010
Order in the matter of Oasis Media Matrix Ltd. in respect of Mr. Jatin Manubhai Shah, Mrs. Varshaben Jatin Shah and Mr. Manubhai Bikhalal Shah
 
Shri M. S. Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 08, 2010, in the matter of Oasis Media Matrix Ltd., restraining Mr. Jatin Manubhai Shah and Mr. Manubhai Bikhalal Shah, from buying, selling or dealing in the securities market in any manner whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of three years and one year respectively from the date of Order. Further the order disposed off the Show Cause Notice issued to   Mrs. Varshaben Jatin Shah without any direction.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
