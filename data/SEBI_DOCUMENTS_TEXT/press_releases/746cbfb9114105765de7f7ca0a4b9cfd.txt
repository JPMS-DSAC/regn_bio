First meeting of SMILE Task Force held
 
The first meeting of the Securities Market Infrastructure Leveraging Expert (SMILE) Task Force under the Chairmanship of Dr P J Nayak, Chairman and Managing Director, UTI Bank, was held on May 12, 2004. The terms of reference of the Task Force are given in the press release dated April 23, 2004, which is available on the SEBI website www.sebi.gov.in. The SMILE Task Force, which has been constituted to carry out a thorough health check up of the Indian securities’ market deliberated on various issues including assessment of the infrastructure requirements for market participants across various segments.
While the Task force has decided to meet select intermediaries and investor representatives in the securities market, it also decided that it would be desirable to facilitate other institutions and the public at large in making suggestions for the consideration of the Task Force. Accordingly, persons desirous of sending suggestions, may send the same by e-mail to smile@sebi.gov.in or through post to Shri Pratip Kar, Executive Director, SEBI, 29th floor, Centre-1, World Trade Centre, Cuffe parade, Mumbai – 400 005, to reach by the end of June 2004.
