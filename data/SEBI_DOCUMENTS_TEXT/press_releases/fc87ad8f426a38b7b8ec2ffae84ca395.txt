PR No.30/2008

Order against M/s. Fincap Portfolio Ltd., Member, NSE

Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  January 30, 2008 with respect to M/s. Fincap Portfolio Ltd. in the matter of M/s. DCM Shriram Consolidated Ltd. imposing a minor penalty of ‘Censure’ on M/s. Fincap Portfolio Ltd., Member of the National Stock Exchange of India Ltd.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
