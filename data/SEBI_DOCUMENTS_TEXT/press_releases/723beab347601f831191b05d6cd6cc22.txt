Orders against M/s. Mukesh Dokania & Co. and M/s. Dinesh Kumar Modi & Co., Members, Calcutta Stock Exchange in the matter of M/s. Akshay Fiscal Services Ltd. and Ashika Credit Capital Ltd.

The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
