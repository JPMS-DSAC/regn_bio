PR No.307/2009
Grant of exemption to M/s. Agrawal Trademart Private Limited for the proposed acquisition  through preferential allotment of equity shares of 
M/s. Maral Overseas Limited
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 30, 2009, granting exemption to M/s. Agrawal Trademart Private Limited (acquirer) from the applicability of Regulation 10 and Regulation 11 (1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of the proposed acquisition of 1,97,50,000  equity shares of Rs.10/- each of M/s. Maral Overseas Limited  @ Rs. 10.00/-per share, through preferential allotment.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Takeover Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirer from making an open offer.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
