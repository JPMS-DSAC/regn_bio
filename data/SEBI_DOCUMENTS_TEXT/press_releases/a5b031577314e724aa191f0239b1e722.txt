PRESS RELEASE
PR No.221/2007

Certificate of registration of M/s. Royal Investments,
suspended for fifteen days
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  July 23, 2007, against  M/s. Royal Investments, sub-broker of M/s. B.D. Shah Securities Pvt. Ltd., (Broker,  Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd.) in the matter of   M/s. MOH Ltd. imposing a minor penalty of suspension of certificate of registration (SEBI Registration no. INS010275712)  for a period of fifteen days.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.      
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
