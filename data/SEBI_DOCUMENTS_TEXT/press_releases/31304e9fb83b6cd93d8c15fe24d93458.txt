 PRESS RELEASE



PR No.335/2004

Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of Hifco Marwel Ltd. 
An Order dated December 17, 2004 was passed by SEBI granting exemption to Mr. Tejaswy Nandury and Mrs. Sobha Rani Nandury (acquirers) from making an open offer under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of proposed acquisition of 9,22,000 equity shares of Hifco Marwel Limited (HML) subject to certain conditions.
The acquirers are relatives of the main promoter Mr. N J Yasaswy being son and wife, respectively. The promoter group is holding 35.83% shares in HML and are already in control of the target company.  The acquirers propose to acquire 9,22,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each in cash by way of preferential allotment to them to revive the operations of HML. Pursuant to the proposed acquisition, acquirers along with other promoters would hold 74.95%. 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer subject to certain conditions.
The full text of the order is available on our website:  www.sebi.gov.in
