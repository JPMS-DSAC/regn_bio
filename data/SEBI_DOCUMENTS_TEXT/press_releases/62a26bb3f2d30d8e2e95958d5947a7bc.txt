PRESS RELEASE
December 08, 2003 PR No.297/2003 
SUBJECT: OFFER DOCUMENTS RECEIVED & OBSERVATIONS ISSUED BY SEBI DURING THE PERIOD 01 /12/2003 TO 05/12/2003 
ISSUED BY: CORPORATION FINANCE DEPARTMENT –
DIVISION OF ISSUES AND LISTING 
 
During the period 01/12/2003 to 05/12/2003, one draft offer document was received by SEBI. During the same period, SEBI had issued observations on three offer documents filed with it. 
List I gives details of offer document received and List II gives details of offer document on which observations were issued.
 
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in ) under "Primary Market".
