AMENDMENTS TO SEBI (DISCLOSURE AND INVESTOR PROTECTION) GUIDELINES, 2000.
SEBI has amended SEBI (DIP) Guidelines 2000 vide its circular dated January 20, 2006.  The amendments inter-alia provide for use of various other mode of making refunds to the applicants viz ECS (Electronic Clearing Service), direct credit, RTGS (Real Time Gross Settlement)  etc.
SEBI has also issued necessary instructions to all registered Merchant Bankers , Registrars and Bankers to issue vide relevant circulars dated January 20, 2006 inter-alia stating that the applicants residing in 15 centers namely Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Bhubnehwar, Kolkata, Chandigarh, Chennai, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Kanpur, Mumbai, Nagpur, New Delhi, Patna and Thiruvananthapuram, where clearing houses for ECS are managed by Reserve Bank of India, will get refunds through ECS.  In other centers, refunds will be despatched through registered/ ordinary post, as is the current practice. In addition to this, applicants who are disclosed to be eligible for direct credit or for RTGS will get refunds through the said routes.
The aforesaid circulars are available on SEBI website at www.sebi.gov.in. Full text of SEBI (DIP) guidelines 2000 including the amendments is also available on SEBI’s web site under the section “Issues and listing”.
