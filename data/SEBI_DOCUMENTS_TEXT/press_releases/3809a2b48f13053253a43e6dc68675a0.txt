PRESS RELEASE
PR No.16/2008

M/s. APS Capital Services Pvt. Ltd., Member, PSE, censured

Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated January 16, 2008, imposing a minor penalty of ‘censure’ on the stock broker viz.  M/s. APS Capital Services Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INB111015931), Member, Pune Stock Exchange Ltd.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
