
 
Mar 29, 2012

PR No.:  
39/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  March 27, 2012 in the matter of Insider Trading by Shri J. E. Talaulicar in the shares of Tata Finance Ltd. in respect of JIP Investment Share & Stock Sub-Broker. Vide the said order the certificate of registration of the JIP Investment Share & Stock Sub-Broker has been suspended for a period of four months.
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
