Offer documents received / withdrawn and observations issued by SEBI from August 23, 2004 to August 29, 2004
During the period August 23, 2004 to August 29, 2004, one offer document was received by SEBI. SEBI had issued observations on one offer document filed with it. List I gives details of offer document received and List II gives details of observations issued during the period.
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in) under "Public Issues etc. "
