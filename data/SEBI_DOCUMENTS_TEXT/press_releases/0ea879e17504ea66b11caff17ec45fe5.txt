 PR No.247/2008 
SEBI passes eight consent orders in the matter of IPO irregularities 
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. T.C. Nair and Shri M.S.Sahoo has passed two consent orders on November 04, 2008 and six consent orders on November 05, 2008 in the matter of “irregularities in the IPOs“ on applications submitted by the following persons :  

Shri Rajan Vasudevbhai Dapki 
Ms. Shilpa Rajan Dapki 
Ms. Ritaben R. Thakkar
Ms. Veenaben Y. Thakkar
Late Vasudevbhai Gordhandas Dapki
Shri Monal Y. Thakkar
Deep Stockbroking Pvt. Ltd 
Shri Deepak Madhukant Patel

 
In the matter of “irregularities in IPOs”, SEBI passed ad interim ex-parte order on April 27, 2006, prohibiting the above mentioned entities from buying, selling and dealings in securities either directly or indirectly till further orders. These entities were alleged to have acted as financiers to key operators for cornering of shares  of various IPOs. These entities have undergone prohibition, since passing of ex-parte order.
