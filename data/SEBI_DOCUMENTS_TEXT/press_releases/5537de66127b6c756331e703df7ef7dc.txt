Order in the matter of M/s. Nissan Copper Ltd.
 
Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 24, 2007 against Shri Mukesh Agarwal, Shri Tanmay Agarwal and Smt. Sharvari M.Agarwal in the matter of their dealings in the shares of M/s. Nissan Copper Ltd., confirming the interim order dated January 17, 2007.
 
The full text of the order is available on website: www.sebi.gov.in
