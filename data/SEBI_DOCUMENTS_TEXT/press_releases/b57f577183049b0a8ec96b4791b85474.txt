Suggestions received – A regulated institutional placement
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has been receiving suggestions for introducing the facility of regulated institutional placements in India as an efficient domestic alternative to the GDR / FCCB route.  In order to invite comments / suggestions / views of the public and others concerned in this regard, the said suggestions have been put on SEBI’s website (www.sebi.gov.in) under the heading “Suggestions received – A Regulated Institutional Placement” in the sub-section “Reports for Public Comments” in section “Reports / Documents”.  Comments on the same may be sent to Ms. Srishti Chavan, Manager or Ms. Neelam Bhardwaj, Deputy General Manager, Division of Issues and Listing, SEBI over email at srishtijc@sebi.gov.in or neelamb@sebi.gov.in or faxed to 91-22-22045633 on or before February 18, 2006.
