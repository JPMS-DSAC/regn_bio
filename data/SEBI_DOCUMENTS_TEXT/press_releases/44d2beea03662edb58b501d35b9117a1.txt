IPO Investigations –– Confirmation of Interim directions against Opee Stock Link Ltd., Deepakkumar Shantilal Jain and Pratik Mafatlal Shah

Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has issued orders dated February 8, 2008 and February 15, 2008 in the matter of IPO Investigations against  Opee Stock Link Ltd., Deepakkumar Shantilal Jain and Pratik Mafatlal Shah.
Pursuant to the ex-parte ad interim order passed by Whole Time Member, SEBI on April 27, 2006 in the matter of IPO investigations and after considering the submissions made by the said entities, the following directions were issued:
“I hereby confirm the ad interim order of Securities and Exchange Board of India dated April 27, 2006 against Opee Stock – Link Ltd. (Permanent Account Number AAACO4774G) and Shri Deepak Shantilal Jain (Permanent Account Number AFYPJ4589E).
I hereby confirm the ad interim order of Securities and Exchange Board of India dated April 27, 2006 against Shri Pratik Mafatlal Shah (Permanent Account Number ADBPS7374M).”
 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
