PR No.51/2007


Order against M/s. Ballabh Das Daga, Member, CSE 
in the matter of M/s. DSQ Industries Ltd.
 
 
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order on February 08, 2007 suspending certificate of registration of M/s. Ballabh Das Daga, member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Association Ltd., SEBI Registration   No. INB030492218 for a period of four months in the matter of M/s. DSQ Industries Ltd.
 
The certificate of registration of M/s. Ballabh Das Daga, member of CSE  will remain suspended from March 01, 2007 till June 30, 2007.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
