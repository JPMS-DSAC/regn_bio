PRESS RELEASE
PR No.116/2007

Order against M/s. Agbros Securities Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of 
M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  March 29, 2007, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Agbros Securities Pvt. Ltd., member of Calcutta Stock Exchange, in the matter of    M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd., for a period of seven days.
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
