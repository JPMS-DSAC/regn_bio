
 
Sep 25, 2012

PR No.:  
85/2012


Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 24, 2012, granting exemption to the Government of India (GoI) from the applicability of regulations 3(1), (2) and 4 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 2011, in respect of the proposed acquisition pursuant to allotment of equity shares on account of conversion of debentures held by GoI, which would result in increase in shareholding of GoI from 0.0000011% to 55.57%.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel has also recommended granting of exemption.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
