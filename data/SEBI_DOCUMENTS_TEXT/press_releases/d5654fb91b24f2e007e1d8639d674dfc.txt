PRESS RELEASE
PR No.46/2008


                                                                                      
Order in the matter of follow on public issue of shares of 
Syndicate Bank Limited


Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated   February 12, 2008 in the matter of M/s. SSKI Corporate Finance Pvt. Ltd., M/s. Enam Financial Consultants Pvt. Ltd., M/s. SBI Capital Markets Ltd. & M/s. JM Morgan Stanley Pvt. Ltd. relating to the follow on public issue of shares of Syndicate Bank Ltd. directing to close the proceedings pending before him pursuant to the show cause notices issued to them.
 
The order further states that the pending Adjudication Proceedings shall continue and be concluded independently without getting influenced by this order, in accordance with SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry and Imposing Penalties by Adjudicating Officer) Rules, 1995.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
