PR No.53/2008

 
Order against Continental Controls Ltd. and its Chairman and Managing Director Shri Navin Thakkar in the matter of Continental Controls Ltd. 

Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 14, 2008 restraining Continental Controls Ltd. from accessing the securities market for a period of two years and restraining Shri Navin Thakkar, Chairman and Managing Director of Continental Controls Ltd. from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities for a period of two years.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
