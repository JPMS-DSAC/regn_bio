PR No.360/2009
Order against M/s. P. K. Agarwal & Co., Member, CSE
Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 20, 2009, imposing a penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of M/s. P. K. Agarwal & Co., Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd., for a period of five years in respect of its dealings in the shares of M/s. Nageshwar Investment Ltd. The period of prohibition already undergone by the broker vide order dated November 30, 2005 and May 31, 2006 shall be set off against the aforesaid penalty.
The order shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
