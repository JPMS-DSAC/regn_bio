 PRESS RELEASE
January 12, 2004 PR No.05/2004
 
Sub: M/s. Parasrampuria Plantations Ltd., Mumbai -Order under section 11B of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with the SEBI (Collective Investment Schemes) Regulations, 1999 passed by SEBI
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) vide its Order dated December 29, 2003 has debarred M/s. Parasrampuria Plantations Ltd. (PPL) and its concerned officials from dealing in securities market and from accessing the securities market for a period of five years from the date of the Order.
 
PPL has failed to comply with the earlier Order of SEBI dated October 8, 2003 directing it to refund the money collected under the scheme(s) with returns due to the investors as per the terms of the offer within a period of one month from the date of the said Order.
 
The text of the order is available on the SEBI website www.sebi.gov.in
