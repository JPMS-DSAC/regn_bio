PRESS RELEASE
PR No. 36/2005
M/s. Swadha Securities Ltd., member of 
Calcutta Stock Exchange, Censured
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India has censured M/s. Swadha Securities Ltd, member of Calcutta Stock Exchange vide order dated January 27, 2005 to be more cautious in future in its dealings in the securities market and strictly adhere to the provisions of the SEBI Act, 1992 and the Rules and Regulations made thereunder.  M/s. Swadha Securities Ltd. has been censured in connection with its dealings in M/s. Blue Print Securities Limited.
 
The full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
February 22, 2005
