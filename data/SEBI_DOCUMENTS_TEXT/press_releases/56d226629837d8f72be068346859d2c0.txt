PR No.262/2007


Grant of exemption to Balaji Trading Enterprises Pvt. Ltd. (the acquirer) for proposed acquisition of shares of Jumbo Bags Ltd. (target company)
 
Shri G Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated September 14, 2007 granting exemption to acquirer from complying with regulation 11(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of the proposed acquisition of 20 lacs shares of the target company. The acquirer is part of the promoter group of the target company.
 
The acquirer proposes to acquire 20 lacs equity shares of the target company on conversion of the warrants to be acquired through preferential allotment. Pursuant to the aforesaid acquisition, the shareholding of the acquirer would increase from 18.67% to 32.68% and of the total promoter group would increase from 38.30% to 46.04% of the total voting capital of the target company (considering the expanded capital taking into account the conversion of the warrants).
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
