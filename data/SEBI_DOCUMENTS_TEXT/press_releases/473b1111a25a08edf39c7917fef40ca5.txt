SEBI Issues Consultation Paper on“Review of SEBI (Issue of Capital & Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2009"SEBI has placed a Consultation Paper on “Review of SEBI (Issue of Capital & Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2009" on the website for public comments. The full text of the Consultation Paper is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in. Comments are invited on the Consultation Paper latest by May 25,2018.
