PR No.80/2011




Consent Order in the matter of M/s. Tech Trek India Limited
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. K. M. Abraham and  Shri Prashant Saran, has passed consent order dated May 31, 2011 on the consent application submitted by M/s. Tech Trek Limited for non-compliance with the provisions of Regulation 6(2) and 6(4) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 for the year 1997 and Regulation 8(3) for the years 1998 to 2010, relating to disclosures to be made to the Stock Exchanges.
The applicant has remitted a sum of  ` 7,75,000/- (Rupees Seven Lakh and Seventy Five Thousand only) towards the settlement charges in the matter.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
June 02, 2011
