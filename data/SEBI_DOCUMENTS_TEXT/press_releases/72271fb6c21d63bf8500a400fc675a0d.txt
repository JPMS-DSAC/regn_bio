PR No.83/2009
Order in the matter of MCI Securities Private Limited and Maneklal Chunilal Investments Private Limited
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  February 03, 2009 canceling the certificates of registration of the sub-broker MCI Securities Private Limited. The enquiry proceedings against Maneklal Chunilal Investments Private Limited have been disposed of without any further penalty.
The order comes into force immediately on the expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order in the case of MCI Securities Private Limited.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
