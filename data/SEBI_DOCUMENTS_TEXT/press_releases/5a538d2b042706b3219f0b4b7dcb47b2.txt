PR No.:09/2009
Re-allocation of debt investment limit 

The enhanced debt limits in corporate debts category were allocated to entities on October 22, 2008. The unutilized limits in Government debt and corporate debt category were re-allocated to the entities on December 12, 2008. 
After assessment of the utilization, the unutilized debt limits are being re-allocated to entities lower down in the list.
The list of entities and the allocated limits is placed in Annexure.
In terms of SEBI circular dated November 06, 2008, in case the allocated limits are not utilized by the entities in 11 working days, i.e. by January 21, 2009, the same shall be withdrawn and allocated to the entities lower down in the list.
