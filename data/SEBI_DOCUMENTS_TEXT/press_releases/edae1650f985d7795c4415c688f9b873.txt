PR No.:  
52/2012


SEBI issued Circular No. Cir/ISD/1/2011 dated March 23, 2011 & an addendum to it on  March 24, 2011 (available on www.sebi.gov.in) addressing the way unauthenticated market related news or rumours circulated by SEBI Registered Market Intermediaries through various modes of communication has to be dealt with.
 
SEBI has now issued Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs')  on responsible handling of unauthenticated market related news or rumours by SEBI Registered Intermediaries which would assist them in complying with the various provisions contained in the said Circulars.
 
The full text of the FAQ’s is available on the website : www.sebi.gov.in
