PRESS RELEASE
PR No.11/2005 
 Registration of M/s. Niraj Kumar Balasaria Pvt. Ltd.,
member of Calcutta Stock Exchange, suspended
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) vide order dated January 07, 2005, in the matter of M/s. DSQ Industries Ltd., has suspended the certificate of registration of M/s. Niraj Kumar Balasaria Pvt. Ltd., SEBI Reg. No. INB 030037115, member of Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE) for a period of three months. The penalty is imposed for creation of artificial market in the shares of M/s. DSQ Industries Ltd. by M/s. Niraj Kumar Balasaria.
 
The certificate of registration of M/s. Niraj Kumar Balasaria Pvt. Ltd. will be suspended from January 28, 2005 till April 27, 2005.
 
The full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
January 11, 2005
