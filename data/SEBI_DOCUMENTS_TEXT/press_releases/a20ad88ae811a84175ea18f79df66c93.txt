25th August, 2004
 
PUBLIC COMMENTS INVITED FOR THE FIRST REPORT OF THE SMILE TASK FORCE
The Securities Markets Infrastructure Leveraging Expert (SMILE) Task Force constituted by SEBI, under the Chairmanship of Dr. P J Nayak, Chairman and Managing Director, UTI Bank Ltd. has submitted its first report on ‘Infrastructure and Process Flows for the Primary Market’. 
This report is placed on the SEBI website (www.sebi.gov.in) for public comments.  
Comments on the aforesaid report may be sent to Shri S C Das, Executive Director at  das@sebi.gov.in and copy to Shri Pratip Kar, Executive Director at pkar@sebi.gov.in on or before September 15, 2004.
