PRESS RELEASE
PR No.93/2006

Order against Mr. Suresh Motwani and others
Mr. Suresh Motwani, Jaydeep Investments Pvt. Ltd., New Bharat Builders Pvt. Ltd., Sunearth Ceramics Ltd., Habiscus Investments Pvt. Ltd., Onlooker Investments Pvt. Ltd., Ms. Meenal Chhabaria, Mr. Suresh Hemwani, Mayfair Realtor Pvt. Ltd., Mr. Madan Relwani, M’Belle International Pvt. Ltd., Alkan Projects Pvt. Ltd., Mr. Sushil Thakkar, Mr. Gopu Belani, Mr. Kishore Motwani and Mr. Vinod Motwani have been debarred from buying, selling or dealing or accessing, in any manner directly or indirectly, the securities market for a period of three years. The above entities have been found guilty of violation of SEBI Act, 1992, SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices relating to the securities market) Regulations, 1995 read with relevant provisions of SEBI (PFUTP) Regulations, 2003 while dealing in the shares of Sunearth Ceramics Ltd. The order has been passed on February 02, 2006 and shall come into force with immediate effect. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
Mumbai
February 15, 2006
