PR No.145/2009
Order against Shri Kalpesh R Sheth in the matter of Genus Commutrade Ltd.
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated April 21, 2009, in the matter of Genus Commutrade Ltd. restraining Shri Kalpesh R Sheth from accessing the securities market and also prohibiting him from buying, selling or otherwise dealing or associating with the securities market in any manner and in any capacity for a period of two years.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
