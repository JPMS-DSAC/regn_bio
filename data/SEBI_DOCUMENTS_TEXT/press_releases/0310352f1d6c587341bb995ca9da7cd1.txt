PR-330/2004
Shri Madhukar assumes charge as Whole Time Member, SEBI
Shri Madhukar has assumed charge as Whole Time Member of the Securities and Exchange Board of India on December 14, 2004. Shri Madhukar will be in charge of the departments of Corporation Finance, Legal Affairs, Enquiry & Adjudication, Office of Investor Assistance & Education, Research & Training and General Services Department. 

Prior to this assignment, Shri Madhukar was Chairman and Managing Director, United Bank of India (UBI) and Industrial Investment Bank of India (IIBI). Under his stewardship as CMD, from July 2001 to February 2004, United Bank of India showed unprecedented turnaround in all spheres of its performance. During his tenure as Chairman and Managing Director, IIBI from April 2003 to February 2004, which he held as an additional responsibility, IIBI also showed equally impressive progress in its operations. Shri Madhukar also held positions abroad including that of the Managing Director of State Bank International Limited, Mauritius – an offshore joint venture of SBI in Mauritius, which he established and held charge from 1990 to 1996. The entity also remained as a vanguard among all such units in that country right since its inception.
Starting his career in banking as a Probationary Officer in State Bank of India, Shri Madhukar rose to become the Deputy Managing Director in SBI. He has held various senior positions in India and abroad. He also held the post of Managing Director, State Bank of Bikaner and Jaipur (SBBJ) from February 2000 to July 2001.
Mumbai
December 17, 2004
