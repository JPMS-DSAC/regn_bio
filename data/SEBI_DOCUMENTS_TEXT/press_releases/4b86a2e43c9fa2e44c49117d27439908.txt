Shah (9) M/s Comet Investments Pvt. Ltd. (10) Addvalue Financial Services Pvt. Ltd. (11) Rohit Gandhi (12) Meeta A. Gala (13) M/s Caplon Securities & Investments Pvt. Ltd.(14) Mitesh V. Shah, (15)Dharmen J. Sheth from accessing the securities market and prohibiting them from buying, selling or dealing in securities in any manner whatsoever for a period of six months.
 
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
