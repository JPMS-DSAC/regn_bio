PRESS RELEASE
PR No.216/2006

Registration of M/s. Malay Investment & Financial Services Pvt. Ltd.,
Member of UPSE, suspended
 
Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 19, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Malay Investment & Financial Services Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INB100830439), member of the Uttar Pradesh Stock Exchange (UPSE) for a period of fifteen days with effect from October 10, 2006. 
 
M/s. Malay Investment & Financial Services Pvt. Ltd.,  will not be entitled to carry on the business as a broker with effect from October 10, 2006 to October 24, 2006.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
