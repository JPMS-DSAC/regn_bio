PRESS RELEASE
PR No.186/2007

Order against Shri Mahesh Kumar Damani in the matter of 
M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
 
 
Shri V.K Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated June 13, 2007, has disposed of the enquiry proceedings against Shri Mahesh Kumar Damani, broker of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) and National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. (NSE) directing the broker to be careful in the future while dealing in securities market.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
