A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri M. S. Sahoo and Dr. K.M. Abraham, has passed a consent order dated June 15, 2009, on an application submitted by Nirmal Bang Securities Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of Sun Infoways Ltd. in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.4,00,000/- (Rupees four lakh only) towards the terms of consent in the matter.
