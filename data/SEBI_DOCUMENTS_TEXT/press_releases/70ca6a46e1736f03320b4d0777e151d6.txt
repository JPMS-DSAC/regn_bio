
 
Apr 11, 2012

PR No.:  
41/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has vide orders dated  April 09, 2012 revoked the interim directions issued on June 04, 2009 and also the confirmatory orders issued on December 31, 2009 against Alosha Vanijya P. Ltd. and on January 19, 2010 against Amar Packagings P. Ltd., Stupendors Traders P. Ltd. and Samta Jain.  The orders dated April  9, 2012 also disposed of the show cause notices against the aforesaid entities without any further directions.
 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
