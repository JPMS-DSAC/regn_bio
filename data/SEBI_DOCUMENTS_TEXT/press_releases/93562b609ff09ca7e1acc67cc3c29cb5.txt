
 
Sep 09, 2016

PR No.:  
138/2016


Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member (WTM), SEBI has passed an order dated September 7, 2016 under sections 11, 11A and 11B of the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992 in the matter of Neesa Technologies Limited having its registered office at 9th Floor, Cambay Grand, Behind PERD Centre, Near Sola Over Bridge, Thaltej, Ahmedabad–380054 (Gujarat). The order was passed pursuant to the directions of Hon’ble Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT) order dated July 14, 2016 remanding back SEBI order dated June 2, 2016 qua Mr. Nimain Charan Biswal for fresh decision.
 
Based on the facts and circumstances of the case and the submissions of Mr. Biswal, WTM did not find the need to issue any directions against him in the matter.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
