PR No.258/2009
Order against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd. Member, CSE
Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  August 13, 2009, disposing of the enquiry proceedings against Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd., Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Association Ltd., in the matter of Subh Laxmi Projects Ltd.  As the said broker had already been prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities from September 29, 2005 to August 11, 2008, in accordance with interim order passed by SEBI, no further penalty has been imposed.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
