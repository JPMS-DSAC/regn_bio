PR No.84/2010
Order in respect of Om Vijay Nivesh Pvt. Ltd. and its director Shri Amit Jain 
in the matter of Oracle Credit Ltd.
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 26, 2010 disposing of investigation proceedings against Om Vijay Nivesh Pvt. Ltd. and its director Shri Amit Jain directing them to be careful and cautious while dealing in the securities market and also directed them to strictly comply with the securities laws which govern their activities in the securities markets.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
