PRESS RELEASE
PR No.46/2007

 
Order against Ms. Ashadevi Beriwala, in the case of 
M/s. Roofit Industries Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI vide order dated  February 07, 2007 in the case of M/s. Roofit Industries Ltd.,  has restrained      Ms. Ashadevi Beriwala  from buying, selling and dealing or accessing the securities market in any manner  for a period of three  months from the date of the order.
The full text of the above Order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
