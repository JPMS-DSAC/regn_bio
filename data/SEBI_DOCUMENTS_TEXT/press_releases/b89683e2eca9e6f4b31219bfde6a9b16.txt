PRESS RELEASE
PR No.19/2008 

Order in the matter of M/s. Dhanlaxmi Lease Finance Limited

Shri V.K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated January 23, 2008 in the matter of failure to exercise due diligence in the public issue of M/s. Dhanlaxmi Lease Finance Limited, disposing off the Show Cause Notices issued to M/s. Monarch Projects and Finmarkets Ltd. and its Directors Shri Rajeshkumar V Patel, Shri Kamlesh M Shah, Shri Ashwin P Patel, Shri Yogesh J Shah, Shri Mahendra P Vora, Shri Narendra Kapoor, Shri Pravinkant V Desai and Shri S M Gupta.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
