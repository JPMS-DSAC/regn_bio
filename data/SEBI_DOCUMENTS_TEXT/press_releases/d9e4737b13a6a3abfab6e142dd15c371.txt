PRESS RELEASE
PR No.15/2008


Order against ARJ Shares & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd.


Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 16, 2008 against ARJ Shares & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (erstwhile Jhunjhunwala Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd.) Member of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), in the matter of Sun Infoways Ltd., imposing a minor penalty of ‘Censure’ on the broker.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
