Shri V.K. Chopra assumes charge as Whole Time Member, SEBI
Shri V.K.Chopra has assumed the office of Whole Time Member, Securities and Exchange Board of India on 6th June, 2006.  Prior to this assignment he held the position of the Chairman and Managing Director of Corporation Bank during December 2004 to March 2006.
Shri Chopra, 60 years, is a Fellow Chartered Accountant and is a Certified Associate of Indian Institute of Bankers.
He started his career as an Officer in Central Bank of India in 1969. He served the Bank for 31 years in various capacities including overseas stint. 
Shri Chopra was appointed as Executive Director of Oriental Bank of Commerce in September 2000.  He was elevated as Chairman & Managing Director of Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) in July 2003. During his tenure as Chairman & Managing Director of SIDBI, he was also for sometime holding additional charge as Chairman & Managing Director of Punjab & Sind Bank.
