PR No.282/2007
 


Grant of exemption to Ankur Services and Growth Fund Ltd. pursuant to preferential allotment of equity shares of 
Jain Studios Ltd. (Target Company)
 
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed order dated October 17, 2007, granting exemption to Ankur Services and Growth Fund Ltd. from making an open offer in terms of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, with regard to increase in their voting rights from 54.79% to 74.97% pursuant to preferential allotment of equity shares of Jain Studios Ltd.
 
Pursuant to the said increase in voting rights, there would not be any change in control of the target company.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebLgov.in
