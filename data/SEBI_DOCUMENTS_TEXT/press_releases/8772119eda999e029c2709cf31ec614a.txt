PR No.15/2007

 
Order against M/s. BLB Shares and Financial Services Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order dated  January 11, 2007 against M/s. BLB Shares and Financial Services Ltd., broker of National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. (NSE), Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) and Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE), in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd., disposing of the show cause notice issued against the broker without imposing any penalty.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
