PRESS RELEASE
PR No.67/2007

 
Order against M/s. Shailee Securities in the matter of 
M/s. Spanco Telesystems and Solutions Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  February 22, 2007 directing that the Certificate of Registration granted to  M/s. Shailee Securities (SEBI Regn. No. INS010316714/09960), a sub-broker affiliated to M/s. Pramod Kumar Jain Pvt. Ltd., be suspended for a period of fifteen days.  The period of suspension shall be operative after 21 days of passing of the order.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
