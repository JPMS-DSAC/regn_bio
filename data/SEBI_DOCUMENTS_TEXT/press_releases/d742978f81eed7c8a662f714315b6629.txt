PR No.277/2007

M/s. SMK Shares & Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd., Member of BSE, censured
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 09, 2007, against M/s. SMK Shares & Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd., Member of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) in the matter of M/s. Sun Infoways Ltd., imposing a minor penalty of ‘Censure’ on the broker.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
 
 
 Mumbai
 October 10, 2007
