PRESS RELEASE
PR No.104/2006

Order against M/s. Abhijat Trading Co.  Pvt.  Ltd.
M/s. Abhijat Trading Co. Pvt. Ltd. has been restrained from accessing the securities market and prohibited from buying, selling and dealing in securities for a period of one year for violation of the provisions of SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade practices Relating to Securities Market), Regulations, 1995 read with relevant provisions of SEBI (PFUTP) Regulations, 2003 for their trading in the scrip of M/s. Shripur Gold Refinary Ltd. 
The order has been passed on February 02, 2006 and shall come into force with immediate effect. 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
  
Mumbai
February 16, 2006
