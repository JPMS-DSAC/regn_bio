Grant of exemption to Mr. Narendra Surana and others (acquirers) for the proposed acquisition of voting rights in M/s. Bhagyanagar India Limited (target company) 
Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 30, 2009, granting exemption to Mr. Narendra Surana and others (acquirers) from the applicability of Regulation 11(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of their proposed increase in voting rights from 59.75% to 69.01% pursuant to the target companyâ€™s proposed buy-back of equity shares.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
