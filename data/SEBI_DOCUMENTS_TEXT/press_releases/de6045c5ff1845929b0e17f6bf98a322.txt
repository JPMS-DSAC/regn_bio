PRESS RELEASE
PR No.80/2007

Order against Shri Ashwin S.Mehta in the matter of
M/s. Western Projects Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI vide Order dated February 23, 2007 has directed that the proceedings against Shri Ashwin S.Mehta in the matter of his dealings in the scrip of M/s. Western Projects Ltd. be dropped.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
