PR No.290/2007


Order against M/s. Abhinav Investment for its dealings
in the shares of M/s. Adani Exports Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 24, 2007 against M/s. Abhinav Investment, restraining it from accessing the securities market either directly or indirectly and also prohibiting it from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities, in any manner whatsoever, for a period of six months for its dealings in the shares of M/s. Adani Exports Ltd.(now known as M/s. Adani Enterprises Ltd.).
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
