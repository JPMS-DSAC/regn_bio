PR No.199/2009
Order against Karuna Cables Ltd., Mr. Mahendra C. Shah, Ceetee Trading and Leasing Private Ltd., Mr. Sanjay B. Shah in the matter of Karuna Cables Ltd.
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated   June 15, 2009, in the matter of Karuna Cables Ltd. restraining Karuna Cables Ltd. and its Managing Director Mr. Mahendra C. Shah, Ceetee Trading and Leasing Private Ltd. and its director Mr. Sanjay B. Shah from accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of six months.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
