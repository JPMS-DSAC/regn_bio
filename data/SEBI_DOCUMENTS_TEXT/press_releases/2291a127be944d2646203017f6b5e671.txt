PR No.279/2010
Order in respect of Ms. Ushma P. Sheth in  the matter of 
Prudential Pharmaceuticals Limited
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an Order dated December 15, 2010 in the matter of Prudential Pharmaceuticals Limited warning Ms. Ushma P. Sheth to be cautious in her future dealings and exercise due care and diligence in the conduct of her affairs as a securities market participant. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
Mumbai
December 20, 2010
