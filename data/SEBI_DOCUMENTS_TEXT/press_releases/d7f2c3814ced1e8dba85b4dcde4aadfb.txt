PR No.:  
108/2004 


Market movements
 
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) continues to be on high alert. SEBI has held a special surveillance meeting last Friday to take stock, of the market movements and, of the sharp fall on Friday in particular. SEBI has reinforced its emphasis on the stock exchanges also to remain extraordinarily watchful of any unusual movements in the market and report to SEBI immediately, in addition to taking action themselves. SEBI has called for data from the exchanges on Friday for analysis and examination and would take stern action against any individual / entity if found indulging in market misconduct and breaching the ground rules.
SEBI remains fully committed to protecting the interests of the investors and integrity of the markets. 
 
Mumbai
May 16, 2004
