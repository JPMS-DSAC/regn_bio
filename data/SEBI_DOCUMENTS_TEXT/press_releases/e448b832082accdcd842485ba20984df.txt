PRESS RELEASE
PR No.92/2007
Order in the matter of M/s. HFCL Infotel Ltd.
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  March 07, 2007 directing that communication of observations on the draft offer for sale document filed by M/s. HFCL Infotel Limited be withheld till the proceedings under Section 11 B of the SEBI Act are disposed of.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
