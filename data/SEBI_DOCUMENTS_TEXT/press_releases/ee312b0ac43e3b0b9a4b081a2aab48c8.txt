PRESS RELEASE
PR No.275/2008


Grant of exemption to M/s. Jindal Photo Investments Ltd. and 
others (acquirers) for the proposed acquisition of voting rights in 
M/s. Jindal Poly Films Ltd. (target company) 


Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 24, 2008, granting exemption to M/s. Jindal Photo Investments Ltd. and others (acquirers) from  the applicability of Regulation 11(2)  of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of their proposed increase in voting rights from 55.21% to 73.60% pursuant to the target company’s  proposed  buy-back of equity shares.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
