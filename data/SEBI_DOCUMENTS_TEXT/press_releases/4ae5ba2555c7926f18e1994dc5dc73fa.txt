
 
Aug 07, 2013

PR No.:  
71/2013


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), has passed an order dated August 02, 2013, in the matter of inspection of SMC Global Securities Limited, prohibiting SMC Global Securities Limited from taking up any new assignment or contract or launch a new scheme for a period of three months.
The Order shall come into effect immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the Order. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
