
 
Dec 04, 2012

PR No.:  
102/2012


 
Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated Nov 27, 2012 restraining Mascon Information Technologies Ltd from accessing the securities market and further prohibit it from buying , selling or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, or being associated with the securities market in any manner for a period of two years from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in.
