
 
Feb 28, 2012

PR No.:  
24/2012


A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri Prashant Saran and  Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal has passed a consent order dated February 14, 2012 on the application submitted by M/s. RNR Trading Pvt Ltd. and M/s. Ruia Industries Pvt. Ltd. for delayed compliance of Regulations 7(1) and 7(1A) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 and failure to make open offer under Regulation 11(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of Vinaditya Trading Company Ltd. The applicants without admitting or denying guilt have remitted a sum of ` 5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakh only) towards settlement charges, in the matter.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
