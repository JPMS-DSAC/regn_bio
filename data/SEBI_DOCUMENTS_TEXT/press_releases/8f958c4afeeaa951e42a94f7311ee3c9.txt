PRESS RELEASE
PR No.57/2005

 
Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
Southern Petrochemicals Industries Corporation Ltd. 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)  has passed an order dated March 10, 2005, granting exemption to the acquirer viz. FICON Holdings Ltd. and ACM Educational Foundation (acquirers) from making an open offer under Regulation 11(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of Southern Petrochemicals Industries Corporation Ltd. (SPIC).
 
ACM Educational Foundation is holding 1,20,000 equity shares i.e. 0.13% of the paid up equity share capital of the target company.  FICON Holdings Ltd. is not holding any shares in the target company as on the date of the application.  The shareholding of the promoter group after the proposed acquisition would increase from 30.05% to 43% of the paid up capital of the target company.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 
The proposed acquisition is for the purpose of improving the financial condition of the target company under the Corporate Debt Restructuring Mechanism instituted by the Reserve Bank of India.
 
The full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
