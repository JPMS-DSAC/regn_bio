PR No.102/2009
Order against Pious Overseas Private Ltd., Mr. N.K.Ajmera and  Mr. Giriraj Kothari in the matter of The Dhar Textile Mills Ltd.
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 05, 2009, in the matter of The Dhar Textile Mills Ltd. prohibiting Pious Overseas Private Ltd. (POPL), Mr. N.K.Ajmera (Director of POPL) and Mr Giriraj Kothari from buying, selling or dealing in securities in any manner directly or indirectly for a period of fifteen days.
The order shall come into effect immediately.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
