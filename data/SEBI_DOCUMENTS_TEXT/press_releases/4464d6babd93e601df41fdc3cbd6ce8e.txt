1.  SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014 (“RA Regulations”) were notified on September 01, 2014 and came into effect from December 01, 2014. The RA Regulations are available on the SEBI website www.sebi.gov.in.
2.  In order to address the queries of various market participants on the applicability and interpretation of the RA Regulations, FAQs have been issued. The said FAQs are available on the website at www.sebi.gov.in under the section "FAQs".
