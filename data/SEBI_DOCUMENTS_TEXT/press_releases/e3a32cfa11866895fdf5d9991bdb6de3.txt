PR No.132/2007

 
Order against M/s. PDC Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member of CSE
 
 
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order against M/s. PDC Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member of Calcutta Stock Exchange, on  April 05, 2007, in respect of the irregular transactions in the shares of M/s. Globe Stocks and Securities Ltd. imposing a major penalty of suspension of  certificate of registration for a period of six months.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
