PR No.119/2010
Order in the matter of Consortex Karl Doelitzsch (I) Ltd.
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated   May 10, 2010 in the matter of Consortex Karl Doelitzsch (I) Ltd. restraining Mr. Nitin C. Vora, Ms. Jyoti Vora, Mr. Bharat Kanakia, Ms. Sonal B. Kanakia, Ms. Meena A. Kanakia, Mr. Yogesh Parekh, Ms. Trupti Parekh, Ms. Chetana R. Valia and   Ms. Shilpa C. Timbadia from buying, selling or dealing in the securities market in any manner whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of one year from the date of the Order.
 
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
