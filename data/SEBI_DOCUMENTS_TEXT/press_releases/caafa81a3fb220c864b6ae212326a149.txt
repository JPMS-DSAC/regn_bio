PR No.41/2008


Consent Order against M/s. Grishma Securities Pvt. Ltd.
 


A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri G. Anantharaman and Dr. T. C. Nair has passed consent order dated January 30, 2008, on an application submitted by M/s. Grishma Securities Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of Shree Yaax Pharma & Cosmetics Ltd. in accordance with SEBI Guidelines dated  April 20, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.2,50,000/- (Rupees Two Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) towards the terms of consent in the matter.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
