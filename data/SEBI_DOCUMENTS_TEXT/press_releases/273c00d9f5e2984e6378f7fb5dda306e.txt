PR No.:  
18/2018


Registration
of JP Morgan Mutual Fund CancelledPursuant to transfer of schemes of JP Morgan Mutual Fund to Edelweiss
Mutual Fund and at the request of JP Morgan Mutual Fund, Securities and
Exchange Board of India (SEBI), vide its letter dated June 13, 2018 has
cancelled the certificate of registration of JP Morgan Mutual Fund and has
withdrawn the approval granted to JP Morgan Asset Management India Private
Limited, to act as the Asset Management Company to the Mutual Fund.Consequently, with immediate effect, the JP Morgan Mutual Fund, JP Morgan
Mutual Fund India Private Limited (Trustee Company) and JP Morgan Asset
Management India Private Limited cannot carry out any activity as a Mutual
Fund, Trustee Company and Asset Management Company respectively.
