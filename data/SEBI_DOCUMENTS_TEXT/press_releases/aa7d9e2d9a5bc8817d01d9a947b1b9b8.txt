PR No.12/2010
Grant of exemption for the proposed acquisition of equity shares of 
M/s. Shree Ganesh Forgings Limited
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated    January 15, 2010, granting exemption to Mr. Deepak Balkrishan Sekhri and others (acquirers) from the applicability of regulation 11 (2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, with regard to their proposed acquisition of 12, 25,490 equity shares of M/s. Shree Ganesh Forgings Limited through preferential allotment, in terms of the CDR Scheme. The proposed preferential allotment would increase the shareholding of the acquirers from 55.22 % to 59.22 % of the paid up capital of the company.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
