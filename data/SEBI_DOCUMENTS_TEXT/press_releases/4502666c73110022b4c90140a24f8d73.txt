PR No.49/2011
Order in the matter of M/s. Murli Industries Limited
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 23, 2011 in the matter of M/s. Murli Industries Limited confirming the directions issued against M/s. Murli Industries Limited, Mr. Bajranglal Bankatlal Maloo, Bajranglal Maloo (HUF), Lalchand B Maloo (HUF), Mr. Lalchand Bankatlal Maloo, Mr. Mahesh Maloo, Mr. Murli S Maloo, Nandlal B Maloo (HUF), Mr. Sunilkumar S Maloo, Krishnum Investments Private Limited, Lakhi Packaging Private Limited, Simple Mining and Power Private Limited and Taitan Management Services Private Limited vide the ad-interim ex-parte Order dated December 2, 2010.
Further, the directions issued vide the ad-interim ex-parte Order dated December 02, 2010 against Kanhaiya Mining and Minerals Private Limited, Ambaji Papers Private Limited, Inco Infrastructures Private Limited, Ramji Agri Business Private Limited, Runicha Alloys & Steel Private Limited and Ramkrishna Fabrications & Machineries Private Limited have been revoked with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
