Order in respect of RRB Securities Limited, Harsai Investments Limited, Ivory Securities Limited, Alaknanda Capital Services Private Limited, Chinar Financial Services Limited, Narmada Capital Services Limited and Gagan Financial Resources Private Limited in the matter of Alps Industries Limited
Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 28, 2010, disposing of show cause notice dated April 16, 2004 issued to RRB Securities Limited, HarsaiInvestments Limited, Ivory Securities Limited, Alaknanda Capital Services Private Limited, ChinarFinancial Services Limited, Narmada Capital Services Limited and Gagan Financial Resources Private Limited in the matter of Alps Industries Limited, without issuing any directions against them.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
