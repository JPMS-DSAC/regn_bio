PRESS RELEASE
December 17, 2003 PR No.308/2003
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has vide its order dated August 20, 2003 restrained Shri Sanjay Bansal, a member of Guwahati Stock Exchange (SEBI Regn. No. INB 131037112) and an NSE sub-broker (SEBI Regn. No. INS231297511) affiliated to M/s ISE Securities and Services Limited, member NSE, from buying, selling or dealing in securities in any manner, directly or indirectly, till further orders.
SEBI has vide order dated December 09, 2003 passed confirmatory order confirming the above prohibition under section 11(4)(b) read with Section 11B of the SEBI Act, 1992. A full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
