PR No.233/2010
Consent Order on the application submitted by Shri Paresh Thakker in the matter of M/s. Hitech Drilling Services India Ltd.
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. K. M. Abraham and  Shri Prashant Saran, has passed a consent order dated October 22, 2010 on the application submitted by Shri Paresh Thakker, in the matter of M/s. Hitech Drilling Services India Ltd. in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders.  The applicant has remitted a sum of  ` 12,00,000/- (Rupees twelve lakh only) towards the settlement charges.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
