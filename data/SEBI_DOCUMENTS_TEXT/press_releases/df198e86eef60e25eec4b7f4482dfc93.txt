AMENDMENTS TO SEBI (DISCLOSURE AND INVESTOR PROTECTION) GUIDELINES, 2000
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), vide its Circular  No. SEBI/CFD/DIL/DIP/14/2005/25/1 dated January 25, 2005 issued to all the registered Merchant Bankers, has amended SEBI (Disclosure and Investor Protection) Guidelines, 2000.
The amendments have been carried out, inter-alia, to bring uniformity in presentation of disclosures in prospectus, to enhance user friendliness of abridged prospectus, to mandate release of pre-issue advertisement in all public issues and to specify some minimum information in the issue advertisements as per the formats included in the guidelines, etc.
Details of the aforesaid circular are available on our website at www.sebi.gov.in.
The full text of SEBI (DIP) Guidelines, 2000, including the amendments issued vide this circular, is also available on our website at www.sebi.gov.in under Primary Market Section.
