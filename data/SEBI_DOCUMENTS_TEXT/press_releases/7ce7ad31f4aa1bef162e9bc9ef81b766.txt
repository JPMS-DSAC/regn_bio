PR No.75/2007

 
Order against M/s. D.B. & Co., Member, CSE in the matter of 
M/s. Patriot Trading Company Limited
 
 
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 26, 2007 against M/s. D.B. & Co., member of Calcutta Stock Exchange Association Ltd. (CSE) in respect of its irregular transactions in the shares of   M/s. Patriot Trading Company Limited, imposing a major penalty of suspension of certificate of registration for a period of six months. As M/s. D.B. & Co. is already undergoing suspension in terms of Securities and Exchange Board of India order dated January 17, 2007, this order shall come into force with immediate effect and run concurrently.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
