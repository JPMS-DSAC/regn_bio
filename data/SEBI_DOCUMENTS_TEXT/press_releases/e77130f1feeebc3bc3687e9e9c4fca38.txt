PRESS RELEASE
PR No.283/2006

M/s. Gabin Capital Markets Ltd., censured

Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  December 06, 2006, imposing a penalty of censure upon M/s. Gabin Capital Markets Ltd. bearing SEBI Reg. No. INS010809734 (BSE) and INS231016333 (NSE) affiliated to M/s. Cochin Stock Brokers Ltd.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in.
 
Mumbai
December 12, 2006
