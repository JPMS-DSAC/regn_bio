PR No.45/2010
Order against M/s Arun Kumar Bhuwalka, member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Grob Tea Ltd.
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 15, 2010 against M/s Arun Kumar Bhuwalka, member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd. censuring the broker for its dealings in the shares of M/s. Grob Tea Ltd. 
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
