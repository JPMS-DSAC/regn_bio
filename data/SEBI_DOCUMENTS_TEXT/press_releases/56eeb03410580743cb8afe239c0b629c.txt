PRESS RELEASE
June 30, 2004 PR No.139/2004
Certificate of Registration of two stock brokers of 
Coimbatore Stock Exchange Ltd., cancelled
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed orders dated June 24, 2004 cancelling the certificate of registration of the following two stock brokers of Coimbatore Stock Exchange Ltd., with immediate effect:




Sr. No.


Name of the Stock Broker


SEBI Registration No.




  


Modern Stock and Shares Pvt. Ltd.


INB210791337




  


A S Shriram (Trade Name: Alasri Investments)


INB210541015




 The aforesaid Stock-Brokers were declared defaulters by the Stock Exchange and ceased to be members of the Exchange and thereby failed to fulfill the pre-requisite condition of registration as Stock-Broker with SEBI.
 The registration has been cancelled in terms of Sub-Regulation (5) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002. 
A full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
