The aforesaid Stock Brokers have failed to pay the required fees and have been declared defaulters by the Cochin Stock Exchange and consequently the brokers have ceased to be members of the said Exchange.  These registrations have been cancelled in terms of the erstwhile Rule 4(a) of SEBI (Stock brokers) Rules, 1992 and the currently applicable Regulation 6A(1) of SEBI (Stock brokers and Sub-brokers) Regulations, 1992 and Sub-Regulations (5) and (6) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002.
 
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
