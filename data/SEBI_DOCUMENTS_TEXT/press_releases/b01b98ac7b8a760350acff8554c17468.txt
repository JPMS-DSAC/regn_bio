Order in the matter of MCI Securities Private Limited, Maneklal Chunilal Investments Private Limited and their directors
Dr. K.M. Mr. Vipin M Shah,   Ms. Daksha V Shah and Ms. Hasumathi V. Shah (Ms. H V Shah) have been revoked.
