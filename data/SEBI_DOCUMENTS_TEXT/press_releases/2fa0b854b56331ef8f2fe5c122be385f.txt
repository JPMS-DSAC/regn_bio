
 
Nov 26, 2012

PR No.:  
100/2012


Shri Rajeev Kumar Aggarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has vide order dated November 15, 2012 issued the following directions:-
(a)  restrained Flex Trade P. Ltd. and Ms. Rashmi Jain from accessing the securities market and further prohibited them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, or being associated with the securities market in any manner for a period of one year from the date of the order.
(b)  the directions passed by SEBI vide orders dated June 04, 2009 and January 19, 2010 against Beejay Investment & Financial Consultants P. Ltd. and             Mr. Sudhir Jain shall be discontinued.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
