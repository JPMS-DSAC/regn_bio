Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of
M/s. Arvind Products Ltd. (target company)
 
 
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated June 28, 2007 granting exemption from complying with regulations 10 and 12 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of the proposed indirect acquisition of shares of M/s. Arvind Products Ltd., (target company) by M/s. Arvind Brands Ltd. (acquirer). The acquirer is part of the promoter group of the target company.
 
The acquirer proposes to acquire 39,200 equity shares of Asman Investment Ltd. from Arvind Mills Ltd., the parent company. 39,200 equity shares account for 49% of the  Asman Investments Ltd.  Asman Investments Ltd. is holding 50.13% equity shares of the target company.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
