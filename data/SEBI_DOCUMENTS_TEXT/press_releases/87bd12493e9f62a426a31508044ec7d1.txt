PR No.197/2010
Order against Mr. Shiv Kumar Gupta, Late Mr. Vivek Gupta and M/s Genomics Biotech Ltd in the matter of Genomics Biotech Limited
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated August 13, 2010, in the matter of Genomics Biotech Ltd, restraining  Mr. Shiv Kumar Gupta and M/s Genomics Biotech Ltd from buying, selling or dealing in securities market in any manner whatsoever or accessing the securities market directly or indirectly for a period of two years from the date of the order and abated the proceedings against Mr. Vivek Gupta without going into merit since he is no more alive to face the penalty.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
