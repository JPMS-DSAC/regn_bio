PR No.114/2009
Consent order on the application submitted by Hira Industries Limited 
in the matter of Hira Ferro Alloys Limited
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri M.S. Sahoo and   Dr. K.M. Abraham, has passed a consent order dated March 24, 2009 on the consent application submitted by Hira Industries Limited  in the matter of Hira Ferro Alloys Limited for  delay in filing a report under Regulation 3(4) read with 3(5) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers), Regulations, 1997.  The applicant without admitting or denying guilt has remitted a sum of Rs.50,000/- (Rupees fifty thousand only) towards settlement charges in the matter.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
