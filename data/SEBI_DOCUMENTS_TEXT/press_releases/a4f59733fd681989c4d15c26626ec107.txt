PR No.308/2008


Order against M/s. Mayekar Investment Private Limited, Sub Broker affiliated to M/s. Ramanlal D. Shah  in the matter of Sawaca Communications Limited 


Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  December 12, 2008, in the matter of Sawaca Communications Limited against M/s. Mayekar Investment Private Limited, Sub Broker affiliated to M/s. Ramanlal D. Shah (SEBI Regn. No. INS010608532)
 
The Whole Tome Member found that though the violations committed by  M/s. Mayekar Investment Private Limited are serious in nature and warrant cancellation of certificate of registration of Mayekar Investment Private Limited but as the certificate of registration of Mayekar Investment Private Limited has already been cancelled vide order dated November 17, 2008, no further action is required. Therefore, the Whole Time Member disposed of the enquiry proceedings against Mayekar Investment Private Limited.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
