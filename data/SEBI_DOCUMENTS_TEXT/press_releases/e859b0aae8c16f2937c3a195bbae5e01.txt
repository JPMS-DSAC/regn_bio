
 
Oct 21, 2011

PR No.:  
140/2011


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed two orders dated October 19, 2011 in the matter of Sun Infoways Limited viz:
 

restraining Hindustan Holdings Ltd. and Vintel Securities Ltd. from accessing the securities market and prohibiting them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, for a period of three months. 

 

restraining Opulent Broking Pvt. Ltd. from accessing the securities market and prohibiting it from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, for a period of three months. 

 
These orders have come into effect immediately.
 
 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
