July 20, 2004 PR No.162/2004
 
Offer documents received / withdrawn and observations 
Issued by SEBI from July 12, 2004 to July 18, 2004
During the period July 12, 2004 to July 18, 2004, no fresh offer documents were received by SEBI. SEBI had issued observations on two offer documents filed with it and one offer document was closed during the same period. List I gives details of observations issued on offer documents received by SEBI. List II gives details of offer documents closed / withdrawn during the period.
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs.100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in) under "Primary Market".
