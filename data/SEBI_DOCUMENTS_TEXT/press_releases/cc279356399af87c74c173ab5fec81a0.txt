PR No.202/2009
Re-allocation of limits to FIIs / Sub-accounts for investment in Government Debt 

As per SEBI PR No.161/2009 dated May 15, 2009 the remaining limit for investment in Government debt were to be allocated to the FIIs/ sub-accounts on a ‘first come first served’ basis in terms of our Circular dated January 31, 2008, subject to a ceiling of Rs.249 cr. per entity. 
The list of entities and the allocated limits are placed in Annexure.
In terms of SEBI circular dated November 06, 2008, time period for utilization of these allocated limits shall be 11 working days i.e. by July 10, 2009.
