PR No.271/2010
Grant of exemption to Mr. Deepak Balkrishnan Sekhri and others for the proposed acquisition of 48,70,130 equity shares of M/s. Shree Ganesh Forgings Limited through preferential allotment 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 9, 2010, granting exemption to Mr. Deepak Balkrishan Sekhri, Ms. Anita Deepak Sekhri along with persons acting in concert namely, Mr. Akshatt Deepak Sekhri, Ms. Namha Deepak Sekhri, Deepak Sekhri (HUF),  M/s. Namha Profiles Inc.,  M/s. Chandramouli Silk Mills., Mr. P. K. Chakravarty, Mr. Anantha Narayan Nair and Ms. Nalini Vasantkumar Kotian from complying with the provisions of Regulation 11 (2) of the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997, with regard to their proposed acquisition of 48,70,130 shares of Shree Ganesh Forgings Limited at a price of  Rs.12.32 per share, through preferential allotment, in terms of a Corporate Debt Restructuring Scheme. 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
Mumbai
December 13, 2010
