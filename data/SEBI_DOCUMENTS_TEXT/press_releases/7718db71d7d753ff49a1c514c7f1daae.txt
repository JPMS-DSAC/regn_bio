
 
May 17, 2012

PR No.:  
61/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  May 10, 2012 in the matter of Denim Enterprises Ltd. (now known as Divine Entertainment Ltd.), suspending the certificate of registration of M.P.Vora Shares and Securities Private Limited for a period of one month.
 
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the SEBI website: www.sebi.gov.in
