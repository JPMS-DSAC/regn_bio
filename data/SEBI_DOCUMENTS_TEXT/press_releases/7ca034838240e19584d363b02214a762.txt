PR No.282/2006

 
Registration of M/s. Praveen Kumar & Co., suspended

Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated December 07, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Praveen Kumar & Co. (SEBI Reg. No. INS010726516), a sub-broker affiliated to UPSE Securities Ltd., member of The Stock Exchange, Mumbai for a period of fifteen days with effect from December 28, 2006. 
 
M/s. Praveen Kumar & Co. will not be entitled to carry on the business as a sub-broker with effect from December 28, 2006 to January 11 , 2007.
 
 The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
