SEBI offices shift to new office building in Bandra Kurla Complex

 
Securities and Exchange Board of India has shifted its offices in Mumbai to their new head office building "SEBI Bhavan".
 
The address of SEBI Bhavan is Plot No. C-4A, G Block, Bandra Kurla Complex, Bandra (East), Mumbai– 400 051.
 
The telephone number of the new office is 26449000. The telephone numbers of the Office of Investor Assistance and Education, dealing with investor grievances and guidance, are 26449188/ 26449199.
 
SEBI Bhavan was inaugurated by Honourable Prime Minister of India, Dr. Manmohan Singh on October 6, 2006.
