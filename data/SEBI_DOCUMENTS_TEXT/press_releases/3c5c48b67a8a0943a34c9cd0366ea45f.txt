PRESS RELEASE
PR No.30/2007

M/s. Adroit Financial Services Pvt. Ltd., Member of NSE, censured
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  January 22, 2007, imposing a penalty of censure upon M/s. Adroit Financial Services Pvt. Ltd., (SEBI Reg. No.230853830) Member of the National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. (NSE).
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
