April 30, 2004 PR No.97/2004
 
Order u/s 11 B against Shri Bishwanath Murlidhar pursuant to investigations in the matter of Snowcem India Ltd.
 
 
SEBI vide order dated April 27, 2004 restrained Shri Bishwanath Murlidhar, from buying, selling or dealing in securities in his individual capacity for a period of two years for their role in the price manipulation of the scrip Snowcem India Ltd.
The full contents of the order can be viewed at SEBI Website: www.sebi.gov.in
