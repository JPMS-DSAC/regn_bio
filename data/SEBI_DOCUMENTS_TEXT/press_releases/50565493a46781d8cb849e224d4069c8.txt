PR No.71/2010
Orders in the matter of Cals Refineries Limited and others
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed orders dated  March 09, 2010 and March 10, 2010, confirming the ad interim ex-parte order dated June 04, 2009 against Rakhi Trading Private Ltd. & Peanance Commercial Private Ltd. and Shri Ashok Kumar Jain respectively in the matter of alleged manipulative trading by certain connected entities in the scrips of Cals Refineries Limited and others.
 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
