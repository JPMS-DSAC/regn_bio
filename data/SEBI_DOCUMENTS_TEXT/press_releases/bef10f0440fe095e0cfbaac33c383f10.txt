PR No.192/2008


Consent Order in the matter of MSK Projects (India) Limited 


A Panel consisting of Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI and  Shri M. S. Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed a consent order dated September 22, 2008 on the application submitted by MSK Projects (India) Limited  for non compliance of Regulation 8(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997, for the year 2005, in accordance with SEBI Circular on consent orders dated April 20, 2007. The applicants have remitted a sum of Rs.50,000/- (Rupees fifty thousand only) towards settlement charges, in the matter.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
