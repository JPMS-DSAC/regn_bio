Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  January 12, 2009, in the matter of IPO Investigations against Masat Texurising and Twisting Private Ltd.
Masat Texurising and Twisting Private Ltd. was identified as financier to Roopal Panchal, a key operator in the IPO of FCS Software. Based on the findings, SEBI passed ad interim ex-parte order on April 27, 2006 and Masat Texurising and Twisting Private Ltd. was directed not to buy, sell or deal in the securities market including in IPOs, directly or indirectly, till further directions. Also a detailed show cause notice was issued against it on January 7, 2009.     
Pursuant to that ex-parte ad interim order following directions were issued:
“I confirm the ad interim order and it shall continue till the disposal of show cause
notice dated January 7, 2009.”
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
