PRESS RELEASE
PR No.103/2006

 
Order against M/s. Hotline Securities Pvt. Ltd.
 
M/s. Hotline Securities Pvt. Ltd. has been prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities in any manner, for a period of one year for violation of the provisions of SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade practices Relating to Securities Market), Regulations, 1995 read with relevant provisions of SEBI (PFUTP) Regulations, 2003 for their trading in the scrip of M/s. Tactful Investment Ltd. 
The order has been passed on February 02, 2006 and shall come into force with immediate effect. 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
