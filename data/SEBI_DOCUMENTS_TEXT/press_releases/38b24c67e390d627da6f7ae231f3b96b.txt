PR No.61/2008
 
Order in the matter of Vital Communications Limited
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 20, 2008 against Vital Communications Ltd., Shri J P Madaan, Shri R K Garg, Smt. Subha Jhindal, Shri Vinay Talwar and Shri Vijay Jhindal in the matter of Vital Communications Limited.
Vital Communications Ltd. and its Directors Shri J P Madaan, Shri R K Garg, and Shri Vijay Jhindal have been restrained from accessing the securities market and prohibited from buying, selling and dealing in securities in any manner for a period of two years. Smt. Subha Jhindal has been restrained from accessing the securities market and prohibited from buying, selling and dealing in securities in any manner for a period of six months. The charges against Shri Vinay Talwar have been dropped.   
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on website www.sebi.gov.in
