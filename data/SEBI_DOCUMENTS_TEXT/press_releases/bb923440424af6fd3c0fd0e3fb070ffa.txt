PRESS RELEASE
PR No.277/2008


Orders in the matter of Bombay Dyeing & Manufacturing Co. Ltd.


Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed orders dated   November 28, 2008 in the matter of Bombay Dyeing & Manufacturing Co. Ltd. to the effect that the Show cause notices issued to Mega Stocks Ltd., Mega Resources Ltd., The Hoogly Mills Company Ltd., BPS Securities Pvt. Ltd. and Shri D.K.Singhania are disposed of without any direction. The proceedings against Late Shri Arun Kumar Bajoria are abated. Further, for the reasons stated in the order, no further direction in the matter is passed against M/s. Bluechip Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. sub broker. Shri Naresh Chand Chandak and Shri Niraj Kumar Balasaria are warned to be more cautious in the conduct of their business.
 
The full text of the above orders is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
