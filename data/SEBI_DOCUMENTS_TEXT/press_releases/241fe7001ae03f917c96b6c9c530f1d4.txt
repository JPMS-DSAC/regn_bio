PRESS RELEASE 

                                                                                         PR-166/2005
 
Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of M/s. Nishkalp Investment & Trading  Company Limited resulting in indirect acquisition of shares of M/s India Emerging Companies Investment Ltd.
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), vide order dated  December 22, 2005 has granted exemption to the acquirer – M/s. Tata Industries Limited, from making an open offer under Regulation 10 and 12 of SEBI (Substantial acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of proposed acquisition of 50% equity shares of M/s. Nishkalp Investment & Trading Company Limited pursuant to which the acquirer would indirectly acquire 70.50% in the target company (M/s. India Emerging Companies Investment Ltd.). The order was passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI.
 
The acquirer proposes to acquire 4,00,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each of Nishkalp aggregating to 50% of the share capital of Nishkalp which in turn holds 70.50% of equity shareholding in the target company.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirer from making an open offer.
 
    The full text of the Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
