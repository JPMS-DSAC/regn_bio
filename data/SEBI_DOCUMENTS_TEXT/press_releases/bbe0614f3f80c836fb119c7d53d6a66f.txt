PR No.175/2007

 
Order against promoters of M/s. Adani Exports Ltd.
Dr. T.C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  May 25, 2007 restraining the promoters of M/s. Adani Exports Ltd. (presently known as Adani Enterprises Ltd.) viz. Adani Agro Pvt. Ltd., Adani Impex Pvt. Ltd., Crown International, Shahi Property Developers Pvt. Ltd., Adani Properties Pvt. Ltd., Advance Exports and Intercontinental India from accessing the securities market either directly or indirectly and also prohibiting them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities, in any manner whatsoever, for a period of two years.
The above order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
