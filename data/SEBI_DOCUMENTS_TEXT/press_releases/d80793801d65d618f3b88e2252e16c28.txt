 PRESS RELEASE



PR No.293/2004

Certificates of registration of two brokers suspended
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has suspended the certificates of registration granted to M/s DGP Securities Ltd. and M/s CFL Securities Ltd.  Members, National Stock Exchange (NSE), for a period of two months. The orders passed on October 18, 2004 shall come into force from the expiry of three weeks from the date of these orders.
The full texts of above orders are available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
October 28, 2004
