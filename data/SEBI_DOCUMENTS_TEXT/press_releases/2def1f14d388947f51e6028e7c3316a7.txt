Registration of M/s. N.K.Tiwary, Member of CSE, suspended
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated May 22, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. N K Tiwary (SEBI Reg. No. INB030879612), member of the Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE) for a period of one month with effect from June 12, 2006.  The penalty is imposed for contract notes were not signed, off the floor transactions, dealing with unregistered sub- brokers etc.
 
M/s. N K Tiwary, will not be entitled to carry on the business as a broker with effect from June 12, 2006 to July 11, 2006.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
