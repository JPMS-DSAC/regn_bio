Consent order on the application submitted by M/s. Apollo Sindhoori Capital Investment Limited

A panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri G. Anantharaman and Dr. T. C. Nair has passed a consent order dated January 07, 2008 on the application submitted by M/s. Apollo Sindhoori Capital Investment Limited, in the matter of inspection of its books of accounts and records, in accordance with SEBI Guidelines dated April 20, 2007 for consent order.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
