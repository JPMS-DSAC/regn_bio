PR No.37/2010
Consent order on the application submitted by Mr. Chander Singh 
in the matter of T. Spiritual World Limited
 
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBl, Dr. K. M. Abraham and  Shri Prashant Saran has passed a consent order dated February 03, 2010 on the application submitted by Mr. Chander Singh in the matter of T. Spiritual World Limited  in accordance with SEBl Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.25,000/- (Rupees twenty five thousand only) towards terms of consent in the matter.  Further, the applicant has undertaken voluntary debarment from dealing in securities market for a period of one year.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
