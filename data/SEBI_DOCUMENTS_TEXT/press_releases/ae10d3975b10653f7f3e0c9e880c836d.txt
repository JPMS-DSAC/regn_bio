PR No.103/2011
Order in respect of Somplast Leather Industries Limited and its directors
 
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated June 27, 2011, disposing of the show cause notice dated September 23, 2005 issued to Somplast Leather Industries Limited, Mr. N.R. Vekaria, Mr. R.N. Vekaria, Mr. S.N. Vekaria, Mr. R. D. Patel, Mr. M.P. Shah (alias Mr. Bhagwati Shah),  Mr. Ramanlal Hathi, Mr. Girish Shah Ms. Yogini Vyas, and Mr. Sanjay Patel in the matter of Somplast Leather Industries Limited, without any directions.
 
The Order shall be without prejudice to any other action/proceedings that may be initiated by the Securities and Exchange Board of India against the aforesaid company or its directors, pursuant to the further investigation conducted in the matter.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
July 01,  2011
