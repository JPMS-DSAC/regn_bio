PR No.324/2009
Consent order on the application submitted by Amit Business Ltd. in the matter of dealings in Futures and Options contracts
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri M. S. Sahoo and Dr. K. M. Abraham has passed consent order dated October 23, 2009, on an application submitted by Amit Business Ltd. in the matter of dealings in Futures and Options contracts in accordance with SEBI circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted Rs. 7,50,000/- (Rupees seven lakh fifty thousand only) towards terms of consent in the matter.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
