PR No.10/2011
Order in respect of Shri Gautam N Jhaveri in the matter of 
M/s. Top Telemedia Limited 
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 14, 2011 in the matter of M/s. Top Telemedia Limited restraining  Shri Gautam N Jhaveri from buying, selling or dealing in securities market whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of one year. 
 
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
January 18, 2011
