PR No.357/2009
Order against  M/s. Kedar Nath Agrawal, Member, NSE
 
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated November 18, 2009, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Kedar Nath Agrawal, Member, National Stock Exchange of India Limited, for a period of twenty days.
 
The order will come into effect on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
November 20, 2009
