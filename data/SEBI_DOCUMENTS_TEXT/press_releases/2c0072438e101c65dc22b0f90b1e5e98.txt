PRESS RELEASE
PR No. 21/2005

 
Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
HCL Technologies Ltd. 
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has been passed an order dated January 19, 2005, granting exemption to the acquirer viz. Ms. Roshni Nadar from making an open offer under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of HCL Technologies Limited (HCL).
 
HCL Holding Pvt. Ltd. is a wholly owned subsidiary of M/s. Vama Sundari Investments Pvt. Ltd., Dr. S. N. Balakrishnan holds the entire share capital of the said M/s. Vama Sundari Investments Pvt. Ltd.  HCL Holding Pvt. Ltd. holds 20.62% of shares of HCL.  Dr. S. N. Balakrishnan proposes to transfer the entire shareholding of M/s. Vama Sundari Investments Pvt. Ltd. to the acquirer by way of gift which would result in the indirect acquisition of 20.62% of shares of HCL. After the proposed acquisition, the total shareholding of the promoter group in HCL would continue to be 75.77% and there would be no change in control.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the order is available on the SEBI website at www.sebi.gov.in
