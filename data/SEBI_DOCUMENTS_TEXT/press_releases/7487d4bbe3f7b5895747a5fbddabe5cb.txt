PR No.86/2010
Order in the matter of Eider Infotech Ltd.
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 30, 2010 in the matter of Eider Infotech Ltd restraining Mr. Sanjay Sinha, Eider E-Commerce Ltd., Eider Financial Services Ltd., Centre for Excellence in Fashion Technology, Mr. N K. Jain (proprietor of Parshad & Co. and Saran Investments Ltd.) from buying, selling or dealing in securities and from associating in securities market in any manner whatsoever for a period of one year from the date of the order. Further, vide the said order, the show cause notices issued against Eider Infotech Ltd., Smt. Rama Sinha, Wg. Cdr. Manmohan Dhasmana and Mr. B.L. Khurana are disposed of without any directions.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in.
