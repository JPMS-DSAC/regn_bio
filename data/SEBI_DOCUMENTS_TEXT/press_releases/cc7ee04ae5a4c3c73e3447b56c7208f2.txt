PR No.98/2011
Order against M/s. C.R. Jain & Co. 
 
Shri M.S.Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated June 23, 2011 suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. C.R. Jain & Co. of Delhi Stock Exchange Ltd. till the time outstanding registration fees is paid in full.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order. 
 
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
June 28,  2011
