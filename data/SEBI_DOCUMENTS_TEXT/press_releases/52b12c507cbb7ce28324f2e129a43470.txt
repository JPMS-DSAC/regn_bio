PR No.253/2008


Consent Order in the matter of M/s. Pennar Industries Limited


A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. T.C.Nair and   Shri M.S.Sahoo, has passed consent order dated November 05, 2008, on the application of consent for non compliance of Regulation 6(2) and 6(4)) for the year 1997 and regulation 8(3) for the years 1998 to 2004  of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations 1997 submitted by M/s. Pennar Industries Limited in the matter of M/s. Pennar Industries Limited, in accordance with SEBI Circular on consent orders dated April 20, 2007. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.4,25,000/- (Rupees four lakh twenty five thousand only) towards the terms of consent in the matter.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
