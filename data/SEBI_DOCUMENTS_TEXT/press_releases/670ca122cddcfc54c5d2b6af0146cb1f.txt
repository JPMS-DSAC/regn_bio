PR No.193/2007

 
Order against M/s. Park Light Investments Pvt. Ltd., Member of BSE 
in the matter of M/s. Continental Controls Ltd.
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  June 13, 2007, against M/s. Park Light Investments Pvt. Ltd., Member of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) in the matter of M/s. Continental Controls Ltd., imposing a penalty of Censure on the broker.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
