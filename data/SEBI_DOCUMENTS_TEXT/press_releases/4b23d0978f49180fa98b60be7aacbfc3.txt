PR No.41/2009
Order against M/s. Cardioid Securities Pvt. Ltd., Shri Parimal Bhagvatilal Bhalja and Shri Abhayraj Shukla in the matter of Sarita Software & Industries Ltd.
Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 14, 2009, in the matter of Sarita Software & Industries Ltd. restraining  1) M/s. Cardioid Securities Pvt. Ltd., 2) Shri Parimal Bhagvatilal Bhalja and   3) Shri Abhayraj Shukla from accessing the securities market either directly or indirectly and also prohibiting them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing or otherwise dealing in securities, in any manner whatsoever, for a period of six months.
The order comes into force with immediate effect.                                                                      
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
