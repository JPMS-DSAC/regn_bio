PR No.171/2007

Order against M/s. Active Finstock Pvt. Ltd., Member of BSE, in the matter ofM/s. Mazda Fabrics and Processors Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated May 24, 2007, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Activ Finstock Pvt. Ltd., member of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), in the matter of M/s. Mazda Fabrics and Processors Ltd., for a period of one month.
 
This order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
