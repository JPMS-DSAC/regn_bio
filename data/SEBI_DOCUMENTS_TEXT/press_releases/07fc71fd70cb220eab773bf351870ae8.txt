PR No.234/2009
Order against M/s. Ballabh Dass Daga, Member, CSE
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated    July 28, 2009 imposing a penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of M/s. Ballabh Dass Daga, Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE), for a period of three months in respect of dealings in the shares of Bacchhat Investment & Finance Ltd.
The order shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
