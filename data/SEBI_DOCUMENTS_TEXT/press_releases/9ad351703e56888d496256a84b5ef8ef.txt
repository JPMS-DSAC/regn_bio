PR No.203/2007


 
Order against Shri Mukesh Mansukhlal Kothari in the matter of 
M/s. Solid Carbide Tools Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated June 15, 2007, has prohibited Shri Mukesh Mansukhlal Kothari from buying, selling or dealing in securities in any manner for a period of two years in the matter of trading in the scrip of M/s. Solid Carbide Tools Ltd.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
