PRESS RELEASE
PR No.281/2004
  
Exemption Granted to Lotus Chocolate Company Ltd. for Proposed Acquisition of Shares
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an Order dated October 12, 2004 granting exemption to the acquirer viz. Shri Devabhaktuni Durga Prasad acting in concert with Shri Alapati Ramakrishna (together being Indian Promoters) from making an open offer under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of Lotus Chocolate Company Limited (LCCL).
The acquirer proposes to acquire the total residual holding of 12,84,020 equity shares (approximately 9.99% of the voting rights) of the target company, remaining with the foreign promoter, Network Foods International Limited. After the proposed acquisition, the voting rights of the Indian promoters would increase from 37.63% to 47.62%.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the Order is available on our website at www.sebi.gov.in
