PR No.03/2010
Order against Mr. Pawan Kumar Goyal and others  in the matter of Peacock Industries  Limited
Dr.  K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 31, 2009  in the matter of M/s. Peacock Industries Ltd. restraining  Mr. Pawan Kumar Goyal, M/s. Goyal Investments, M/s. Goyal Brothers and  M/s. Goyal Securities  from accessing the securities market and prohibited them from buying, selling or dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, for a period of one year.
The order comes into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
