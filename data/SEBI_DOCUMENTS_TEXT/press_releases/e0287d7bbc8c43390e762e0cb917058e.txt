PR No.303/2009
Order in the matter of Ind Tra Deco Ltd.
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 18, 2009, in the matter of Ind Tra Deco Ltd. revoking the directions passed against 16 entities / persons vide earlier orders dated October 5, 2005 and June 20, 2006.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
