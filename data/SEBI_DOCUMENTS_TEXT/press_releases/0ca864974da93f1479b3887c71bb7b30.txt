PR No.244/2010
Order in respect of Advik Laboratories Ltd. and others
in the matter of Advik Laboratories Limited.

Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 27, 2010, in the matter of Advik Laboratories Limited restraining Advik Laboratories Ltd., Mr. V.K. Jain, Mr. Aditya Jain, Ms. Divya Jain, Ms. Kumud Jain, Mr. Rajkumar Jain, Mr. Vivek Jain, Ms. Shivani Jain and Advik Finance and Properties Pvt. Ltd. from buying, selling and dealing or accessing the securities market directly or indirectly in any manner whatsoever for a period of one year from the date of the order. Further, the proceedings against Ms. Shakuntala Jain has been abated since she is no more alive to face the penalty.
 The order has come into force with immediate effect.
 The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
 Mumbai
