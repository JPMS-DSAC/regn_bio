PRESS RELEASE
PR No.222/2006

Registration of M/s. Agarwal Shares & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd.,
Member of UPSE, suspended
 
Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 26, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Agarwal Shares & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INB101040138), member of the Uttar Pradesh Stock Exchange for a period of one month with effect from October 17, 2006. 
 
M/s. Agarwal Shares & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. will not be entitled to carry on the business as a broker with effect from October 17, 2006 to November 16, 2006.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
September 29, 2006
