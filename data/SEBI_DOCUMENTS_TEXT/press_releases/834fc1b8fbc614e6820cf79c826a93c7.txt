Order against  Shri Navzer N.Tara and Shri Sharukh N.Tara in the matter of M/s. Kreb Biochemicals Ltd. 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated December 21, 2006 directing that no action need to be taken against Shri Sharukh N.Tara and Shri Navzer N.Tara, erstwhile partners of M/s. Sharukh N.Tara in the matter of M/s. Kreb Biochemicals Ltd.
The full text of the above Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
