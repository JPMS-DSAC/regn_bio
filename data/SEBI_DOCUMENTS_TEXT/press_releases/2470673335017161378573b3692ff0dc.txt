PR No.192/2007

 
Order against the sub broker M/s. Jyotish Bhogilal Stock Brokers Private Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Continental Controls Ltd.
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  June 15, 2007, against M/s. Jyotish Bhogilal Stock Brokers Private Ltd., sub-broker of M/s. Prabhudas Liladhar Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of Continental Controls Ltd., imposing a penalty of Censure on the sub-broker.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
June 19, 2007
