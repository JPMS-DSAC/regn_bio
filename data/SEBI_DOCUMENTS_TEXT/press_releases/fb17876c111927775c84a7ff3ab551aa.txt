PRESS RELEASE
PR No.273/2004

NOTIFICATION OF SPECIFIED INVESTORS UNDER THE SEBI (CENTRAL DATABASE OF MARKET PARTICIPANTS) REGULATIONS, 2003
Pursuant to the notification of the SEBI (Central Database of Market Participants) Regulations, 2003, SEBI had specified, from time to time,  vide various notifications and circulars, certain categories of intermediaries, their related persons and investors being bodies corporate, etc., who are required to obtain a Unique Identification Number (UIN) under the Central Database of Market Participants (MAPIN database).
In order to further expand the coverage of the MAPIN database, the following new categories of investors in the primary market, the secondary market, and any mutual fund or collective investment scheme (i.e. investors who enter into any securities market transaction) would be required to obtain a UIN under the MAPIN database by March 31, 2005:

All domestic investors not being bodies corporate who carry out a single transaction of value Rs. 1.00 lakh or more.
Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) and their sub-accounts,  and
Foreign Venture Capital Investors (FVCI).   

Necessary notifications to this effect have been issued and are available on our website:  www.sebi.gov.in
