PRESS RELEASE
PR-180/2005
 
Warning issued to M/s. Nirpan Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member of NSE
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated December 27, 2005, warning M/s. Nirpan Securities Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No.INB230648336), Member of National Stock Exchange.
 
The order has been passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI, against M/s. Nirpan Securities Pvt. Ltd. for irregularities in maintaining the contract notes, failed to collect upfront margins, non-maintenance of unique client code etc.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
December 30, 2005
