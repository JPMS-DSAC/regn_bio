PR No.247/2010
Order in respect of M/s. Amrut Securities Ltd. 
in the matter of M/s. Axel Polymers Ltd.
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 09, 2010 in the matter of Axel Polymers Ltd. suspending the certificate of registration of the sub-broker Amrut Securities Ltd. for a period of five days from the date of the order. 
 The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
