PR No.251/2010

ORDER IN RESPECT OF Ms. ADITI A. DALAL IN THE MATTER OF
M/s. PRUDENTIAL PHARMACEUTICALS LTD.
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 11, 2010 in the matter of Prudential Pharmaceuticals Ltd. warning Ms. Aditi A. Dalal (proprietor of M/s. Aditi A. Dalal) to be cautious in her future dealings and exercise due care and diligence in the conduct of her affairs as a securities market participant. 
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
