PR No.72/2010
Order against C. Mackertich Ltd. in the matter of DSQ Biotech Ltd.
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  March 05, 2010 in the matter of DSQ Biotech Ltd. suspending the certificate of registration of the stock broker C. Mackertich Ltd., registered with SEBI as stock broker at BSE and NSE for a period of one month.
The order shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
