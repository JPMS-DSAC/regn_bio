PRESS RELEASE
PR No.60/2008

Order in the matter of Universal Media Network Limited

Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 18, 2008 in the matter of Universal Media Network Limited restraining Shri Kishore Vithalbhai Thakkar, Shri Ashit H Vora, Shri Maulik Patwa,  Mrs. Swati Aslesh Shah, Shri Sanjiv P. Shah, Shri Dixit Babubhai Patel and  Shri Mukesh Bachubhai Vadecha from accessing the securities market and also prohibiting them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing or associating with the securities market in any manner whatsoever for a period of two years.
 
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
