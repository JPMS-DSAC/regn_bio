  PRESS RELEASE
PR No.170/2007

Orders against M/s. Agroy Finance & Investment Ltd., M/s. Delhi Securities Ltd. and M/s. A. Nitin Capital Services Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Shonkh Technologies International Ltd.
Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed three orders dated May 24, 2007, suspending the certificates of registration of M/s. Agroy Finance & Investment Ltd, (Regn. no. INB 050691937), M/s. Delhi Securities Ltd., (Regn no. INB 050626335) and M/s. A. Nitin Capital Services Ltd., (Regn no. INB 050720634), members of The Delhi Stock Exchange Association Ltd. for a period of one month, two months and two months respectively in the matter of M/s. Shonkh Technologies International Ltd.
These orders shall come into force immediately on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the orders.
 
The full texts of the above orders are available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
May 25, 2007
