SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA
M/s. Victor Gaskets India Limited - Exemption from requirements of Rule 19 (2)(b) of the Securities Contracts (Regulation) Rules, 1957 kept in abeyance

M/s. Victor Gaskets India Limited(VGIL) was granted an exemption from requirements of Rule 19 (2) (b) of the Securities Contracts (Regulation) Rules, 1957 for listing of their shares on The Stock Exchange, Mumbai pursuant to a scheme of arrangement between Prefect Circle Victor Limited and VGIL which scheme was approved by the High Court of Bombay. The exemption was granted subject to compliance with the provisions of Clause No.8.3.5 of SEBI (Disclosure and Investor Protection) Guidelines, 2000 and compliance with minimum capital requirement for listing. It was noted that even before the exemption was granted VGIL violated one of the conditions subject to which the exemption was granted.
After giving a hearing, Chairman, SEBI has directed VGIL that the approval dated 8.3.2002 granted for exemption from the requirements of Rule 19 (2)(b) of the Securities Contracts (Regulation) Rules, 1957 shall be held in abeyance until M/s. Victor Gaskets India Ltd. ensures that at least 25% of the paid-up share capital post scheme of Victor Gaskets India Ltd. comprises of public shareholding.
A full text of the order is available on our website www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
