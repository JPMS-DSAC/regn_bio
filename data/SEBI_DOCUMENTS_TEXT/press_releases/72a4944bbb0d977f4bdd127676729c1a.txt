Press Releases 










PR No.252/2010
Order in respect of M/s Stellar Fiscal Services Pvt. Ltd. 
in the matter of M/s ETC Networks Ltd.
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 18, 2010, in the matter of M/s ETC Networks Ltd. restraining M/s Stellar Fiscal Services Pvt. Ltd. from buying, selling or dealing in securities market whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of six months from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
November 19, 2010
