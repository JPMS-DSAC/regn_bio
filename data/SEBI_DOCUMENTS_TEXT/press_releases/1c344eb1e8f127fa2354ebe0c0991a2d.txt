PRESS RELEASE
July 27, 2004 PR No.175/2004
Shri Mukesh Choksi debarred from accessing capital market
for three months
Shri Mukesh Choksi is prohibited, from associating with any corporate body in accessing the capital market and also, from buying, selling or dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, for a period of three months. 
The order was passed by Shri A K Batra, Whole Time Member on July 21, 2004.
This order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is also available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in

Mumbai
