PR No.253/2010
Order in respect of M/s. G-Tech Info Training Limited and Mr. Munir Gazi in the matter of M/s. G-Tech Info Training Limited
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 18, 2010 in the matter of M/s. G-Tech Info Training Ltd., restraining  M/s. G-Tech Info Training Limited and Mr. Munir Gazi from buying, selling and dealing or accessing the securities market directly or indirectly in any manner whatsoever for a period of six months from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
November 22, 2010
