PR No.98/2008 

Consent Order in the matter of M/s. Avinash Information Technology Ltd.

A panel consisting of Chairman, Shri C.B. Bhave and Whole Time Member, Dr. T.C. Nair has passed consent order dated 08/04/2008, on an application submitted by Shri Nilesh J. Patel, in the matter of M/s. Avinash Information Technology Ltd., in accordance with SEBI Guidelines dated 20th April, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.50,000/- towards the terms of consent in the matter.
 
The full text of the order is available on www.sebi.gov.in
