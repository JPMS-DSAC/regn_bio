PRESS RELEASE
PR No.73/2008


Order in the matter of dealing in the shares by Mr. Lalit Dua and his 
associate entities


Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  February 28, 2008 in the matter of dealing in the shares by Mr. Lalit Dua and his associate entities, vacating the interim direction issued against Mr. Lalit Dua and LRS Portfolio and Advisory Services Pvt. Ltd. vide SEBI ad interim, ex-party, order dated March 21, 2006. However, the present order would not have any bearing on the ongoing investigation proceeding in the same matter.
 
The full text of the order is available on the SEBI website: www.sebi.gov.in
