PR No.349/2009
Order against Sterling Holiday Resorts (India) Limited
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated November 10, 2009 against Sterling Holiday Resorts (India) Limited for failing to dematerialize 2,99,800 shares of Gujarat Industrial Investment Corporation Limited. Sterling Holiday Resorts (India) Limited has been directed to dematerialize the 2,99,800 shares of the company standing in the name of Gujarat Industrial Investment Corporation Limited, immediately, but not later than fifteen days from the date of receipt of this Order. The company has been further directed to file a compliance report within a period of fifteen days thereafter, failing which SEBI may initiate appropriate action.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
