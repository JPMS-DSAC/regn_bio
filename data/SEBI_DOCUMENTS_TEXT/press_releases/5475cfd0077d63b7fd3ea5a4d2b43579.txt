PR No.43/2008


Order against NCJ Share & Stock Brokers Ltd. (erstwhile proprietary concern of N C Jain)
 


Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 08, 2008 against NCJ Share & Stock Brokers Ltd. (erstwhile proprietary concern of N C Jain) Member of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), in the matter of Sun Infoways Ltd, imposing a minor penalty of ‘Censure’ on the broker.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
