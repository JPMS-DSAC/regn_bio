PRESS RELEASE
PR No.309/2007


Order in the matter of M/s. Hitechi Jewellery Industries Ltd.
 
Shri V.K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 27, 2007 restraining Shri Manish Radhanpura, Smt. Kavita Radhanpura, M/s. Monalisa Collections, M/s. Earnest CapFund and M/s. Ujwal Investrade from buying, selling and dealing or accessing the securities market in any manner for a period of two years in the matter of M/s. Hitechi Jewellery Industries Ltd.
 
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
