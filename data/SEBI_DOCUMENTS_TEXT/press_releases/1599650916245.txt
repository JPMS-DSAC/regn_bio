 

 

 

 

 PR No.45/2020 

 

Re-lodgement of Transfer Requests of Shares 

 
 

In terms of Regulation 40 (1) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) 
Regulations,  2015,  transfer  of  securities  held  in  physical  mode  has  been  discontinued 
w.e.f.  April  01,  2019.  Subsequently,  vide  Press  Release  No.  12/2019  dated  March  27, 
2019,  it  was  clarified  that  transfer  deeds  lodged  prior  to  deadline  of  April  01,  2019  and 
rejected  /  returned  due  to  deficiency  in  the  documents  may  be  re-lodged  with  requisite 
documents.  

 

  

It has been decided to fix March 31, 2021 as the cut-off date for re-lodgement of transfer 
deeds.  Further, the shares that are re-lodged for transfer (including those request that are 
pending  with  the  listed  company  /  RTA,  as  on  date)  shall  henceforth  be  issued  only  in 
demat mode.
