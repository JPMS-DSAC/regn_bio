M/s. Chheda Shares & Securities, sub broker, censured


Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated February 20, 2008, imposing a minor penalty of ‘censure’ on M/s. Chheda Shares & Securities (SEBI Registration No. INS230534328), sub broker affiliated through M/s. Maniput Investments Pvt. Ltd., Member, National Stock Exchange of India Ltd.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
