OFFER DOCUMENTS RECEIVED / WITHDRAWN AND OBSERVATIONS
ISSUED BY SEBI FROM 3RD JANUARY, 2005 TO 9TH JANUARY, 2005 
During the period 3/01/2005 to 9/01/2005, one offer document was received by SEBI. SEBI had issued observations on one offer document filed with it. No offer document was withdrawn during the period. List I gives details of documents received during the period. List II gives details of observations issued during the period.
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs.100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in ) under “Public Issues etc. “
