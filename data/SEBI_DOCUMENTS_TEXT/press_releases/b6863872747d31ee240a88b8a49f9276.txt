PRESS RELEASE
PR No.51/2006

Orders in the matter of Kwality Dairy (India) Limited
 
The proceedings under SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer And Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002 against Bonanza Stock Brokers Limited and Ramaben Samani Finance Pvt. Limited, both members of Bombay Stock Exchange Limited, in the case of Kwality Dairy (India) Limited have been dropped.
 
The orders to this effect have been passed by Shri Madhukar,  Whole Time Member, SEBI on January 23, 2006.
 
The full texts of the orders are available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
January 27, 2006
