PR No.347/2009
Order in the matter of public issue of Pyramid Saimira Theatre Ltd.
Shri M.S. Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated November 10, 2009 in the matter of public issue of Pyramid Saimira Theatre Ltd. (PSTL) against PSTL, restraining PSTL from dealing in securities in any manner whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of seven years.  PSTL has also been directed to include the order in the Agenda of their next Annual General Body Meeting.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
