PRESS RELEASE
PR No.94/2006

Order against Shri B Jayaprakash
Shri B Jayaprakash has been debarred from buying, selling and dealing or accessing the securities market for a period of one year. Shri B Jayaprakash has been found guilty of violation of SEBI Act, 1992, SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices relating to the securities market) Regulations, 1995 read with relevant provisions of SEBI (PFUTP) Regulations, 2003 while dealing in the shares of Moschip Semiconductors Ltd. The order has been passed on February 01, 2006 and shall come into force with immediate effect.Â
