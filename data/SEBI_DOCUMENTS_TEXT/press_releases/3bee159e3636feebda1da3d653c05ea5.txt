PR No.113/2010
Consent Order on the application submitted by
Shri S.C. Khaneja and Smt. Rajni Khaneja
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. K. M. Abraham and Shri Prashant Saran, has passed a consent order dated April 26, 2010 on an application submitted by Shri S.C. Khaneja and Smt. Rajni Khaneja in the matter of Shivalik Loha Mills Ltd. in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders. 
The applicant, without admitting or denying the charges, has remitted a sum of Rs.4,00,000/- (Rupees Four Lakh only) towards the settlement charges in the matter.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
