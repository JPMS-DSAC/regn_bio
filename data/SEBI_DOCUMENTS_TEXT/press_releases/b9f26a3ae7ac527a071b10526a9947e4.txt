PRESS RELEASE
PR No.314/2007

        
Order against IKM Investment Services Ltd.
 
Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order dated December 6, 2007 against IKM Investment Services Ltd. member of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), National Stock Exchange Ltd. (NSE), Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE) and Delhi Stock Exchange (DSE) in the matter of Sun Infoways Ltd. imposing a minor penalty of ‘Censure’ on the broker.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
December 11, 2007
