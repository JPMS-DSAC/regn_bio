PRESS RELEASE
PR No.261/2004
 
Offer documents received and observations issued by SEBI from September 27, 2004 to October 03,2004
During the period September 27, 2004 to October 03, 2004, SEBI had received two offer documents and issued observations on one offer document filed with it. List I gives details of offer documents received and List II gives details of observations issued during the period.
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in) under "Public Issues etc."
