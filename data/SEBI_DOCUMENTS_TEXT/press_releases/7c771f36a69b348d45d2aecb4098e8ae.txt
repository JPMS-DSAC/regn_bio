Order issued against Kailash Narayan Bhangdia, Member, NSE
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated February 02, 2006 against Kailash Narayan Bhangdia, Member of National Stock Exchange (NSE), suspending the certificate of registration for a period of one month from February 23, 2006. 
The order has been passed against Kailash Narayan Bhangdia for violation of certain provisions of SEBI Act, 1992, SEBI Stock Brokers and Sub-brokers Rules and Regulations made thereunder, circulars issued from time to time. 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
