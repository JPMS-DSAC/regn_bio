Order against M/s. Swadha Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange in the matter of  Akshay Fiscal Services Ltd., Ashika Credit Capital Ltd. and Sharadraj Trade Finance Ltd.
Dr.  T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  January 09, 2009 censuring the broker M/s. Swadha Securities Pvt. Ltd. and advising the broker to be careful in future while dealing with its clients. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
