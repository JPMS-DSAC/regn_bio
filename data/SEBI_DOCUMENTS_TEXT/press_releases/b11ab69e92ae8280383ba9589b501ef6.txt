PR No.230/2010
Grant of exemption to Mr. T. Anil Jain and others (acquirers) for the proposed acquisition of equity shares and voting rights in M/s. Refex Refrigerants Limited (Target Company)
Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  October 15, 2010, granting exemption to Mr. T. Anil Jain, Mr. Jagdish Jain, Mr. A. Tarachand Jain, Mrs. Seema Jain, Sherisha Technologies Private Limited and Refex Energy Private Limited (hereinafter referred to as the acquirers) from the applicability of Regulation 11(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of their proposed increase in percentage of shareholding and voting rights from 57.12% to 58.19% pursuant to the proposed preferential allotment of 4,00,000 equity shares. After this proposed allotment, the shareholding of promoter group will increase from 57.65% to 58.71% in the target company. 
The application seeking exemption from the provisions of the Takeover Regulations was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Takeover Panel had rejected the exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
