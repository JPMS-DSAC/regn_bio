Order against Shri Vidyut Devendrakumar, Member, BSE 
 
Shri Vidyut Devendra Kumar, Member – BSE (Regn. No. INB 01300018) has been awarded a penalty of censure. The broker has been found guilty of non compliance of SEBI circulars while dealing in the shares of V.B. Desai Financial Services Ltd.
 
The order has been passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI on December 30, 2005. 
  
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
