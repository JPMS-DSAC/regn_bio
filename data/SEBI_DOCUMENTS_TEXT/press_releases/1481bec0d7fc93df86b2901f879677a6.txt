Registrations of eight stock brokers, cancelled

NSE    - National Stock Exchange
OTCEI – Over The Counter Exchange of India
ICSEI – Inter Connected Stock Exchange of India
 
The aforesaid eight stock brokers were expelled by the respective Stock Exchanges and ceased to be members of the respective Exchanges. They have also failed in paying the fees to the board. They have, thus, failed to fulfill the pre-requisite conditions of registration as stock broker with SEBI. Their registrations have been cancelled in terms of Sub-Regulation (5) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002.
