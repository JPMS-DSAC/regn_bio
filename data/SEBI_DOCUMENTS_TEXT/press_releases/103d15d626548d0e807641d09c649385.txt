PR No.163/2010
Order against Mr. Rajesh Waghwala in the matter of Prraneta Industries Ltd. 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated July 15, 2010 restraining Mr. Rajesh Waghwala from buying, selling or dealing in securities market whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of two years from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
