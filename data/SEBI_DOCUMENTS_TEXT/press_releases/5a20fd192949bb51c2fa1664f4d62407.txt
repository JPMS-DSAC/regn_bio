PR No.295/2004

CERTIFICATE OF REGISTRATION OF THREE STOCK BROKERS OF COCHIN STOCK EXCHANGE CANCELLED
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has cancelled the certificate of registration of the following three Stock-Brokers with immediate effect:




Sr. No.


Name of the broker


SEBI Regn. No.




1


Biju Ambady


INB090271314




2


K.M.Paulachen


INB090602019




3


M. George John 


INB090689815




 
The aforesaid Stock-Brokers were declared defaulter by Cochin Stock Exchange and hence they ceased to be members of the Exchange. They have also failed in paying the fees to the board. They have, thus, failed to fulfil the pre-requisite conditions of registration as Stock-Broker with SEBI. Their registrations have been cancelled in terms of Sub-Regulation (5) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002.
