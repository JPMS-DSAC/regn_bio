PR No.141/2010
Order against Rajendra Prasad Shah, member, CSE
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated June 16, 2010, imposing a penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of M/s. Rajendra Prasad Shah, Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd., for a period of six months in respect of its dealings in the shares of Bankam Investment Ltd.  In view of the fact that the said stock broker was prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities from September 29, 2005 to August 11, 2008, the penalty is set off against the aforesaid period of prohibition and need not be undergone separately by the stock broker.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
