PR No.276/2010
Order in respect of M/s. Steelco Gujarat Ltd. and its directors 
 
In the matter of non-redressal of investor grievances by M/s. Steelco Gujarat Ltd. and its directors, Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated December 15, 2010 disposed of the show cause notices issued to them without any directions based on proof of redressal of investor grievances submitted by the company.
