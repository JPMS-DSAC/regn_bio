
 
Nov 30, 2015

PR No.:  
276/2015


Shri S Raman, Whole Time Member, Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), has passed an order dated November 20, 2015 against the past director of Sunplant Forgings Limited (SFL), viz. Shri Ameet Singh inter-alia directing that Shri Ameet Singh is prohibited from issuing prospectus or any offer document or issue advertisement for soliciting money from the public for the issue of securities, in any manner whatsoever, either directly or indirectly, till further orders;
The abovementioned past Director of SFL is restrained from accessing the securities market and further prohibited from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in the securities market, either directly or indirectly, till further directions;
Further, the abovementioned past Director of SFL shall provide a full inventory of all his assets and properties.
The company was engaged in fund mobilizing activity through issue of redeemable preference shares (RPS) to more than 49 persons without complying with relevant provisions of the Companies Act, 1956 and the SEBI Act, 1992.
The above mentioned person was earlier a Director of SFL during the offer of RPS by SFL.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
