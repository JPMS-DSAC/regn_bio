PR No.258/2006


Registration of M/s. Akriti Securities Pvt. Ltd., suspended
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 08, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Akriti Securities Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INS010751134), a sub-broker affiliated to UPSE Securities Ltd., Member of  the Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. for a period of (15) fifteen days with effect from November 29, 2006.  
 
M/s. Akriti Securities Pvt. Ltd.  will not be entitled to carry on the business as a   sub-broker with effect from November 29, 2006 to December 13, 2006.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
