PR No.170/2010
Directions for initiating adjudication proceedings against the promoters of M/s. Surana Industries Limited
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  July 22, 2010, directing the initiation of adjudication proceedings against  Mr. G. R. Surana, Mr. Shantilal Surana, Mr. Vijayraj Surana, Mr. Dineshchand Surana, Mrs. Chandanbala Surana, Mrs. Saraladevi Surana, Mrs. Alka Surana and Mrs. Vasantha Surana, for not making the public announcement within the time specified under the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997, in respect of their acquisition of 70,00,000 shares of M/s. Surana Industries Limited.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
July 26, 2010
