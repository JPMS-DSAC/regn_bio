PR No.26/2011
Grant of exemption to M/s. Delta Hospitality and Leisure Private Limited (formerly known as M/s. PLL Delta Hotels Private Limited) for the proposed acquisition of 1,61,27,706 (34.89%) shares and voting rights in M/s. Advani Hotels & Resorts (India) Limited, from M/s. Delta Corp Limited
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 10, 2011, granting exemption to M/s. Delta Hospitality and Leisure Private Limited (formerly known as M/s. PLL Delta Hotels Private Limited), from complying with Regulation 10 of the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997, with regard to its proposed acquisition of 1,61,27,706 (34.89%) shares and voting rights in M/s. Advani Hotels & Resorts (India) Limited, from its holding company, M/s. Delta Corp Limited, at a price of ` 39/- per share.
Â 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel did not recommend an exemption to the acquirer.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
