PR No.390/2009
Order in the matter of M/s. Kwality Dairy (I) Ltd.
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated December 09, 2009 in the matter of M/s. Kwality Dairy (I) Ltd, suspending the certificate of registration of the sub-broker, Shri Sanjay Biyani, sub-broker of the Madhya Pradesh Stock Exchange Securities Ltd.  for a period of fifteen days.
The order shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
December 11, 2009
