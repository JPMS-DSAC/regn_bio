PR No.04/2007

 
Order in the matter of  M/s. Sureshchand S. Jain, broker, NSE
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order dated January 4, 2007 against M/s. Sureshchand S. Jain, broker of National Stock Exchange (NSE), in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. disposing of the show cause notice issued against the broker and imposing no penalty.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
