PR No.36/2009
Order against M/s. Shyamlal Sultania, Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange in the matter of M/s. Subh Laxmi Projects Ltd. and M/s. Amluckie Investment Co. Ltd.
Dr.  T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  January 09, 2009 disposing of the enquiry proceedings against the broker M/s. Shyamlal Sultania. As the said broker has already been prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities from September 29, 2005 to  August 10, 2008, in accordance with interim order passed by SEBI, no further penalty has been imposed.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
