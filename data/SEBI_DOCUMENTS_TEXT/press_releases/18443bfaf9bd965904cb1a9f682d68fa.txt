Order in the case of Roopshri Finvest Private Limited in the matter of 
Motorol Speciality Oils Limited
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated November 17, 2009 disposing of the show cause notice dated August 27, 2008 issued under Section 11, 11B of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Regulation 44 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 to Roopshri Finvest Private Limited in the matter of Motorol Speciality Oils Limited without passing any direction against it.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
