PRESS RELEASE
PR No.177/2007

 
Order against M/s. Media Investments in the matter of
M/s. Warner Multimedia Limited
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated May 30, 2007 against M/s. Media Investments, Sub Broker of M/s. Joindre Capital Services Limited, in the matter of M/s. Warner Multimedia Limited imposing a minor penalty of suspension of certificate of registration (SEBI Regn. No. INS010115710) for a period of one month.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.   
 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
