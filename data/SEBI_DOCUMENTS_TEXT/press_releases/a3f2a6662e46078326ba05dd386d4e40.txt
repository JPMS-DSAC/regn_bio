PR No.40/2009
Order against Shri S K Chadha, Managing Director, M/s. KMF Ltd. in the matter of Ashoka Refineries Ltd.
Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  January 12, 2009, in the matter of Ashoka Refineries Ltd. directing,Shri S K Chadha, Managing Director of M/s. KMF Ltd., shall not operate in the capital market as an intermediary or as a whole time director or partner in an intermediary for a period of six months from the date of passing of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
