Securities and Exchange Board of India on July 16, 2012 had released on its website the draft National Strategy for Financial Education for comments and feedback.
The date for receipt of the comments have been extended till September 08, 2012. Comments can be emailed to iad@sebi.gov.in
The draft National Strategy has been prepared under the aegis of the Sub Committee of the Financial Stability and Development Council (FSDC) and the time line is being simultaneously extended by all financial sector regulators.
