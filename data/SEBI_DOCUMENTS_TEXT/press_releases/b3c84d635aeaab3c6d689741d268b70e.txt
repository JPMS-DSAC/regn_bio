PRESS RELEASE
PR No.214/2006

Registration of M/s. Sakshi Stocks and Shares Pvt. Ltd.,
Member of UPSE, suspended
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated September 15, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Sakshi Stocks and Shares Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INB100858538), member of the Uttar Pradesh Stock Exchange for a period of fifteen days with effect from October 6, 2006. 
 
M/s. Sakshi Stocks and Shares Pvt. Ltd., will not be entitled to carry on the business as a broker with effect from October 6, 2006 to October 20, 2006.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
