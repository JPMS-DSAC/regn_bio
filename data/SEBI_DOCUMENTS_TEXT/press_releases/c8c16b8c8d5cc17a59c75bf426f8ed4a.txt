Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of
Polar Industries Ltd
 
Exemption has been granted to Shri Anil Agarwal, Shri Sunil Agarwal, Smt. Shailja Agarwal , Smt Savitri Devi Agarwal and Smt Vinita Agarwal (acquirers) in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of Polar Industries Ltd. (PIL) from making an open offer under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 subject to the fulfillment of the conditions. The Order dated June 14, 2004 was passed by Shri A. K. Batra, Whole Time Member, SEBI. 
 
The acquirers together with persons acting in concert are holding shares aggregating to 47.28% in PIL. The acquirers propose to acquire 25,04,500 equity shares of Rs.10 each in cash by way of preferential allotment in terms of the first debt restructuring and debt restructuring proposal as approved by Corporate Debt Restructuring Cell for revival of PIL. The acquirers are already in control of PIL and there would not be any change in the composition of the board of directors pursuant to the proposed acquisition.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer. 
The full text of the Order is available on the SEBI website at www.sebi.gov.in
