PR No.206/2010
Order against M/s. Milan Mahindra Securities Pvt. Ltd. 
in the matter of SSI Ltd. 
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 3, 2010, in the matter of SSI Ltd. disposing of the enquiry proceedings against M/s. Milan Mahindra Securities Pvt. Ltd. without imposing any penalty or direction.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in. 
 
Mumbai
September 08, 2010
