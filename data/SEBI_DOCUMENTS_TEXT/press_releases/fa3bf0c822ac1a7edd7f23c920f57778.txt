PRESS RELEASE
PR No.68/2006

Order against Shri Sanjay Mishra and Smt. Anita Drolia 
The proceedings under section 11B of SEBI Act, 1992 against Shri Sanjay Mishra and Smt. Anita Drolia initiated in case of investigation in the scrip of M/s. Today’s Writing Products Ltd. have been dropped.
The order has been passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI on January 25, 2006.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
February 01, 2006
