PRESS RELEASE
PR No.126/2005
 
Exemption granted to promoters of M/s Coromandel Cements Limited

 
Securities and Exchange Board of India, vide order dated September 23, 2005, has granted exemption to the promoter group (acquirers) of M/s Coromandel Cements Ltd., from making an open offer under Regulation 11(1) of SEBI (Substantial acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of M/s Coromandel Cements Limited through preferential allotment subject to the fulfillment of the conditions specified therein. The order was passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI.
 
The exemption is subject to certain conditions including, passing of special resolution under Section 81(1A) of Companies Act in the general meeting of shareholders with facility of postal ballot.  The acquirer proposes to acquire 30,00,000 equity shares by way of preferential allotment in terms of  loan agreement with the bankers for fresh infusion of funds. The shareholding of the promoter group, after the proposed acquisition would increase from 52.38% to 70% of the paid up capital of the target company.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirer from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the SEBI website at www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
