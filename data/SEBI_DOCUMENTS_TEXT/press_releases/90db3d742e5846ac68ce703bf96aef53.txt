PRESS RELEASE
July 26, 2004 PR No.173/2004
 

Orders passed in the matter of public issue of
M/s.Growmore Solvents Ltd.(GSL)

 
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has debarred Shri Maganbhai M Patel, Shri Abdulmonim A. Andani and Shri Shobhag R. Shah, Directors of M/s.Growmore Solvents Ltd.(GSL) from buying, selling or dealing in securities, in any manner whatsoever, for a period of three years. GSL had come out with public issue during 1994. The public issue was undersubscribed and an illusion of subscription was created by these Directors. 
SEBI has also debarred Shri Yashwant Thakkar and Shri Rashmikant Thakkar, Directors of Amrapali Spinning Mills Ltd. from buying, selling or dealing in securities, in any manner whatsoever, for a period of one year for acting in concert with the promoters of Growmore Solvents Ltd. in the irregularities in public issue. 
SEBI has also debarred Registrars to the issue M/s. Nipar Financial Services Ltd. and its two Directors, Shri Nitin Parikh and Shri Akshay Parikh from buying, selling or dealing in securities, in any manner whatsoever, for a period of two years for failing to exercise due diligence in the aforesaid public issue. 
These orders have been passed by Shri A K Batra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, on July 22, 2004 and shall come into force with immediate effect. 
The full texts of the orders are available on our website www.sebi.gov.in
