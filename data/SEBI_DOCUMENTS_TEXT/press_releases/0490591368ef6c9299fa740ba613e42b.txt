Grant of Recognition to National Commodity Clearing Limited as a Clearing CorporationSEBI has granted recognition to M/s. National Commodity Clearing Limited (‘NCCL’) as a Clearing Corporation for a period of one year commencing from September 10, 2018 to September 09, 2019.The grant of recognition to NCCL has been notified in the Gazette of India.
