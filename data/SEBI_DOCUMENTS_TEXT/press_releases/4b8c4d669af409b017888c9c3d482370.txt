PRESS RELEASE
PR No.167/2006

Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of  BPL Ltd. 
 
An Order was passed by Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI on June 14, 2006, granting exemption to the acquirer, Electro Investments Pvt. Ltd., from complying with the provisions of Regulation 11 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997 with regard to the proposed preferential allotment of 170 lac equity shares of BPL Ltd. (target company).
The acquirer is belonging to the promoter group and pursuant to the proposed acquisition its holding would increase from 8.24% to 43.15% and that of the promoter group would increase from 59.09% to 74.66%  of the enhanced equity voting capital of the target company.
The proposed preferential allotment is pursuant to the Scheme approved by Corporate Debt Restructuring mechanism for restructuring the debts of the target company.  There would not be any change in control in the target company pursuant to the proposed allotment.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirer from making an open offer.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
