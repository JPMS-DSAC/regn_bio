PRESS RELEASE
PR No.172/2006

Grant of exemption to acquirers from making an open offer pursuant to Buy Back by M/s. Assam Carbon Products Limited (target company)
 
An Order dated June 29, 2006 was passed by Dr. T.C.Nair,  Whole Time Member, SEBI, granting exemption to The Morgan Crucible Company Plc and Morganite Carbon Ltd, U.K. (acquirers) from applicability of Regulation 11(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997, with regard to increase in voting rights from 51.24% to 59.67% consequent to the proposed  buy back of shares by M/s. Assam Carbon Products Limited.
 
Pursuant to the said increase in voting rights there would not be any change in control of the target company.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers  from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
