PRESS RELEASE
June 4, 2004 PR No.119/2004
 
 
 
Warning issued to M/s. J. M. Morgan Stanley Retail Services Pvt. Ltd., Member of The Stock Exchange, Mumbai
 

The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated June 02, 2004 warning M/s. J. M. Morgan Stanley Retail Services Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INB011054831) member of the Stock Exchange, Mumbai for irregularities in issuance of Contract Notes, non-maintenance of duplicate copy of bills, non-maintenance of client agreement and registration forms etc.A full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
