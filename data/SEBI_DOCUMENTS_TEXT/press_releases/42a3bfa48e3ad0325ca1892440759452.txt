PRESS RELEASE
PR No.274/2007


M/s. J.Bhattacharjee & Co., Member of DSE, censured
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 17, 2007, imposing a penalty of ‘censure’ on M/s. J. Bhattacharjee & Co. (SEBI Reg. No. INB050087511), member of Delhi Stock Exchange Association Ltd.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
 
 
 Mumbai
 October 05, 2007
