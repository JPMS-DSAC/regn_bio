PRESS RELEASE
PR No.116/2005

Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of
Pearl Polymers Ltd.
An Order dated September 2, 2005 was issued by Shri Madhukar,  Whole Time Member, SEBI granting exemption to the promoter group (acquirers) from making an open offer under Regulation 11(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of Pearl Polymers Ltd. through preferential allotment subject to the fulfilment of the conditions specified therein.
The exemption is subject to certain conditions including, ratification of special resolution passed in annual general meeting held on January 28, 2005 through postal ballot. The acquirer proposes to acquire 26,76,181 equity shares by way of preferential allotment in terms of the condition imposed by IDBI for debt restructuring. The shareholding of the promoter group, after the proposed acquisition would increase from 60.80% to 61.9% of the paid up capital of the target company.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirer from making an open offer.
The full text of the Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
