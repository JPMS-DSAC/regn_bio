PRESS RELEASE
PR No.86/2005

Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
Speciality Papers Ltd. 
 
An Order dated June 15, 2005 was issued by Whole Time Member, SEBI granting exemption to the acquirer viz. Shri Mekan J. Gala (acquirer) from making an open offer under Regulation 11(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of Speciality Papers Ltd. (SPL) through preferential allotment subject to the fulfillment of the conditions specified therein.
 
The acquirer proposes to acquire 23,18,388 equity shares by way of preferential allotment in terms of the condition imposed by State Bank of India for sanctioning the credit facilities. The target company was under BIFR for almost 15 years. The shareholding of the promoter group, including that of the acquirer, after the proposed acquisition would increase from 28.08% to 54.44% of the paid up capital of the target company.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirer from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
