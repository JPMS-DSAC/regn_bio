PR No.101/2009
Order against The Dhar Textile Mills Ltd. and its directors Mr. Pankaj Jajoo and Mr. Manish Jajoo
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  March 05, 2009, in the matter of The Dhar Textile Mills Ltd. directing The Dhar Textile Mills Ltd. and its directors Mr. Pankaj Jajoo and Mr. Manish Jajoo to be careful and cautious in future while dealing in securities market.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
