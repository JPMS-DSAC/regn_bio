PRESS RELEASE
July 19, 2004 PR No.158/2004
Certificate of registration of M/s.Home Trade Ltd. cancelled 
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated June 30, 2004, cancelling the certificate of registration of M/s. Home Trade Ltd. in respect of its NSE and BSE memberships (SEBI Registration Nos. INB230624638 and INB010624639) with immediate effect:
The aforesaid stock broker was declared defaulter by the respective Stock Exchanges and ceased to be members of the Exchanges, thereby failing to fulfill the pre-requisite condition of registration as stock broker with SEBI.
The registrations have been cancelled in terms of Sub-Regulation (5) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002.
The full text of the order is available on our website www.sebi.gov.in
