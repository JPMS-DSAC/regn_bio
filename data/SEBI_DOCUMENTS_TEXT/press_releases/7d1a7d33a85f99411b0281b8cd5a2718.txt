PRESS RELEASE
PR No.08/2007


 
Grant of exemption to M/s. Utkal Investments Pvt. Ltd. and   M/s. Renaissance Asset Management Company Pvt. Ltd. (acquirers) for proposed acquisition of shares of M/s. Revathi Equipment Ltd. (target company)
 
An Order dated January 05, 2007 was passed by Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI granting exemption to M/s. Utkal Investments Pvt. Ltd. and M/s. Renaissance Asset Management Company Pvt. Ltd from applicability of Chapter III of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, with regard to increase in their voting rights from 60.87% to 63.71% consequent to the proposed Buyback of the shares by  M/s. Revathi Equipment Ltd.
 
Pursuant to the said increase in voting rights, there would not be any change in control of the target company.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the above Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
