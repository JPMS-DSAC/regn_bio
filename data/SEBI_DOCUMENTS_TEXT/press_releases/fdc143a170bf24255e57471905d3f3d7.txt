PR No.155/2010
Order against M/s. Consortium Securities Private Limited 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  July 05, 2010, advising M/s. Consortium Securities Private Limited, Member, National Stock Exchange of India Ltd., to exercise caution as a stock broker. The said stock broker has further been directed to strictly comply with all statutory requirements that govern its functions in the securities market. 
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
