PRESS RELEASE
PR No.37/2007

Order against M/s. Vidyut Devendrakumar for its dealings in the shares of M/s. Himachal Futuristic Communications Limited
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order dated February 02, 2007 against M/s. Vidyut Devendrakumar, member of the Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) for its dealings in the shares of M/s. Himachal Futuristic Communications Limited, imposing a penalty of suspension of the Certificate of Registration of the broker for a period of one month.  The order shall come into effect on expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
