SEBI (Award for Excellence in Research in Securities Market) Initiative

  
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has instituted the SEBI (Award for Excellence in Research in Securities Market) Initiative to promote research in the area of securities market and allied areas through recognition and honour of outstanding researchers. Under the Initiative, up to 3 (three) awards will be conferred on the 3 (three) best researchers (individuals / institutions) in securities markets every year. The award includes a cash prize of Rs. 1,00,000 (Rupees One Lakh only) and a citation.
 
SEBI invites researcher(s) to submit a brief and focused Synopsis (about 2000 words) of the research paper in accordance with the Initiative. The Synopsis for this year may be sent to researchaward@sebi.gov.inso as to reach SEBI, latest by February 28, 2005. A panel of 2 (two) anonymous referees (called Screening Panel) constituted by the Chairman, SEBI shall assess and identify approximately 15 (fifteen) best Synopses. SEBI shall advise the researcher(s) of the identified Synopses to submit the Research Paper in about 10,000 to 15000 words by a specified date. A panel of 4 (four) anonymous referees (called Selection Panel) constituted by the Chairman, SEBI shall assess and identify up to 3 (three) best research papers. The awards shall be conferred on these researchers in an appropriate function. The assessment by the panels of referees is final and binding on all parties.
 
The details of the Initiative are available at www.sebi.gov.in
