SEBI Chairman inaugurates new office premises of SEBI, New Delhi
Shri M. Damodaran, Chairman, Securities and Exchange Board of India inaugurated the new office premises of the Northern Regional Office (NRO) of SEBI in New Delhi on June 10, 2006. Shri G. Ananthraman, Member, SEBI and Dr. K.P.Krishnan, Joint Secretary (Capital Markets), Govt. of India and Member, SEBI were also present.
 



The new office address of SEBI, NRO is:
Securities and Exchange Board of India Northern Regional Office5th Floor, Bank of Baroda Building16, Sansad MargNew Delhi – 110 001. 
The telephone numbers of the new office are 011- 23724001 – 05 and the fax number is 011 – 23724006.
SEBI, with its head office at Mumbai, has three regional offices at Chennai, Kolkata and New Delhi. The regional offices carry out various developmental and regulatory activities related to securities market in their respective regions.
