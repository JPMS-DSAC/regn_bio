PRESS RELEASE
PR No.134/2007

Orders against M/s. Tropical Securities & Investments Pvt. Ltd. and 
M/s. Pawankumar Parmeshwarlal Choudhary in the matter of 
M/s. Prudential Pharmaceuticals Ltd.
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed two orders dated April 10, 2007, suspending the certificates of Registration of M/s. Tropical Securities & Investments Pvt. Ltd. and M/s. Pawankumar Parmeshwarlal Choudhary, Members of Bombay Stock Exchange, in the matter of   M/s. Prudential Pharmaceuticals Ltd., for a period of one month.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the above orders are available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
Mumbai
April 11, 2007
