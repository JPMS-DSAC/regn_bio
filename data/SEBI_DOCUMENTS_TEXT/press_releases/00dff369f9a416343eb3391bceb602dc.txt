PR No.97/2011
Order in respect of M/s. Kanel Oil & Export Industries Ltd. and its directors
 
In the matter of non-redressal of investor grievances by M/s. Kanel Oil & Export Industries Ltd. and its directors, Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated June 27, 2011 revoked the directions issued to them vide Order dated December 15, 2010 as the company has redressed all the pending investor grievances.
