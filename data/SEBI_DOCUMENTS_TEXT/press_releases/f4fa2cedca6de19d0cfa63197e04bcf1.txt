PR No.371/2009





 
Grant of exemption to Mr. Kulkarni Kiran Prakash, Mr. Pankaj Kumar, Mr. Prashant Mulekar, Ms.Veena Pankaj Kumar, Ms. Vidhi Kumar, Ms. Rajeshwari Kiran Kulkarni, Ms. Jayashree Mulekar, Mr. Sharad Mukund and  Ms. Pramodhini Mulekar for the increase in their voting rights by 7.6% in Geodesic Limited on account of the proposed buy-back
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 30, 2009, granting exemption to Mr. Kulkarni Kiran Prakash, Mr. Pankaj Kumar, Mr. Prashant Mulekar, Ms.Veena Pankaj Kumar, Ms. Vidhi Kumar,  Ms. Rajeshwari Kiran Kulkarni, Ms. Jayashree Mulekar, Mr.Sharad Mukund and      Ms. Pramodhini Mulekar (acquirers) from the applicability of regulations 11 (1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of increase in their voting rights from 22.81% to 30.41% pursuant to the target company's proposed buy-back of equity shares.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
