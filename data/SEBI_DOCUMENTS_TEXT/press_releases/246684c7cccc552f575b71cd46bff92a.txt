Registration of M/s. Super Finvest Services Pvt. Ltd., suspended
 
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated December 27, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Super Finvest Services Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INS230912532), sub-broker affiliated to LSE Securities Ltd., member of National Stock Exchange for a period of fifteen days with effect from January 17, 2007. 
 
M/s. Super Finvest Services Pvt. Ltd. will not be entitled to carry on the business as a sub-broker with effect from January 17, 2007 to January 31, 2007.
 
 The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
