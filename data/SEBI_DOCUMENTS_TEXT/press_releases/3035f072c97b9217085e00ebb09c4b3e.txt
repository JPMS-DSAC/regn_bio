PRESS RELEASE
PR No.316/2007

            
Order in the matter of M/s Zigma Software Limited
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 11, 2007 restraining M/s Net Equity Broking Pvt. Ltd., M/s Enpee Enterprises Pvt. Ltd., Shri Sujal Shah, Shri Atul Gandhi, Ms. Anju Gandhi, Shri Narendra Mistry, Shri Pratik Shah, and Shri Rajkumar Masalia from buying, selling and dealing or accessing the securities market in any manner for a period of one year in the matter of M/s. Zigma Software Limited.
 
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
