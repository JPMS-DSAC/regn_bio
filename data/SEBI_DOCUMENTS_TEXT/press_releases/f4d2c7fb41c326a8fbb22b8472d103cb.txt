PR No.153/2009
Order against M/s. N. M. Lohia & Co., in the matter of  M/s. Toplight Commercial Ltd.
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  April 20, 2009 imposing a penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of M/s. N. M. Lohia & Co., Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd., for a period of four months in respect of its dealings in the shares of M/s. Toplight Commercial Ltd.
The order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
