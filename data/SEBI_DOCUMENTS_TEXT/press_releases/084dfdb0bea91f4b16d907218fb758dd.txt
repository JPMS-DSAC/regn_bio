PR No.238/2010
Order in respect of M/s. Bulls Inc. in the matter of ATN International Ltd.
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 26, 2010 warning M/s. Bulls Inc., Sub Broker, Bombay Stock Exchange Limited to be more careful and cautious in the conduct of its business and to adhere to and comply with all the statutory Provisions while carrying out its activities in the securities market.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
