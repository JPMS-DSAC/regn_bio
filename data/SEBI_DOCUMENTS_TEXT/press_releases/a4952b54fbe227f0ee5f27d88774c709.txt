PR No.288/2010
Consent Order on the application submitted by M/s. DISA Holding II A/S and  M/s. DISA Holding A/S in the matter of M/s. DISA India Limited 
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. K. M. Abraham and  Shri Prashant Saran, has passed a consent order dated December 27, 2010 in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders, on the consent application submitted by M/s. DISA Holding II A/S and  M/s. DISA Holding A/S for non-compliance with the provisions of Regulation 3(3) and 3(4) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in respect of indirect acquisition of 74.27% of the total share capital of M/s. DISA India Limited made on January 5, 2007. 
The applicants without admitting or denying guilt have remitted a sum of  Rs.5,00,000/- (Rupees Five Lakh only) towards the settlement charges in the matter. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
