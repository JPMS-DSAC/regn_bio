PR No.314/2009
Order in the matter of Karuna Cables Limited
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated October 08, 2009, against Shri Kapil Bhuptani, Shri Mahesh Bissa (Perk Investment Pvt. Ltd.), Shri Vasant H. Bissa, Shri Mehul Shah, Shri Tejas Ghelani, Shri Sunil Kuril, Shri Haresh Posnak, Shri Vijay Rathod, Shri Sunil Purohit and Shri Dishank Shah in the matter of Karuna Cables Limited, prohibiting them from buying, selling or dealing in securities market whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of one year from the date of the order.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
