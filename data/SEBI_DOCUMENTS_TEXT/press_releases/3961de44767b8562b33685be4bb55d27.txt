PR No.299/2007

Order in the matter of Prabhu Steel Industries Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 19, 2007 restraining Smt. Sarita Chowdhuri, Shri Rajesh Shah, M/s. Hariyana Metals Ltd. and M/s. Shri Gopal Finance Ltd. from buying, selling and dealing or accessing the securities market in any manner for a period of three months in the matter of Prabhu Steel Industries Ltd. The order further disposed off the show cause notices issued to M/s. Prabhu Steel Industries Ltd. and its directors viz. Shri Ganga Ram Agarwal, Shri Radheshyam L. Agarwal and Shri Krishan Agarwal without any direction.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
