PR No.24/2010
Order against Khandwala Integrated Financial Services Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of Valuenotes.com
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 01, 2010 in the matter of valuenotes.com warning Khandwala Integrated Financial Services Pvt. Ltd. to be careful and cautious in the conduct of its business and to adhere to and comply with all the statutory provisions while carrying out its activities in the securities market.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
