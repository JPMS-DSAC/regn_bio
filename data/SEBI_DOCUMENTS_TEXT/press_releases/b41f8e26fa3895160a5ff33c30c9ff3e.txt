PR No.112/2011
Order in the matter of VCK Share & Stock Broking Services Ltd. 
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order dated July  18, 2011 under Securities and Exchange Board of India (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008 against M/s. VCK Share & Stock Broking Services Ltd. warning them for the irregularities observed during the inspection.  The stock broker has been advised to strictly comply with all the rules and regulations that govern its activities as a stock broker.  Any deviation of the same, in future, would be viewed seriously.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
July 19,  2011
