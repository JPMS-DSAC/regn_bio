PR No.87/2010
ORDERS IN RESPECT OF M/s PNR SECURITIES LIMITED AND 
Mrs. ASHADEVI BERIWALA IN THE MATTER OF SURYA ROSHNI LIMITED
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed two separate orders dated March 30, 2010, in respect of M/s PNR Securities Limited and Mrs. Ashadevi Beriwala in the matter of Surya Roshni Limited, directing the entities to be cautious in their future dealings and exercise due care and diligence in the conduct of their affairs in the securities market.
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in. 
Mumbai
April 1, 2010
