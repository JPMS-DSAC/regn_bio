February 11, 2004 PR No.39/2004


Suspension of certificate of registration.


The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has suspended the following CSE members certificate of registration for a period of six months. The penalty is imposed for their indulgence in off-market transactions and fund based activities.




M/s. Deepak Jhunjhunwala & Co., SEBI Reg. No. INB 031070411
M/s. Pradeep Kayan & Co. SEBI Reg. No. INB 030556517
M/s. MKM Share Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. SEBI Reg. No. INB 030879131
The above members will not be entitled to carry on the business as a Stock Broker with effect from March 1, 2004 till August 31, 2004. The full text of the Order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.\
