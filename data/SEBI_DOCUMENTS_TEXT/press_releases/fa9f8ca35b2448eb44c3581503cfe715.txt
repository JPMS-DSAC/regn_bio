PR No.14/2009
Order against M/s. Active Finstock Pvt. Ltd., Member, BSE
Shri M.S.Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  January 06, 2009, in the matter of GCCL Infrastructure & Projects Ltd. suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Active Finstock Pvt. Ltd., Member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd., having SEBI Registration No. INB 0108493331, for one day.
The order shall come into effect on expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on  the website : www.sebi.gov.in
