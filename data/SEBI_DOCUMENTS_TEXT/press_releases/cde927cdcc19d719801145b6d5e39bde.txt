SEBI orders inquiry into
the alleged leak of ASM listIt has been reported in a section of the press that a list of stocks
which were to be subjected to Additional Surveillance Measures (ASM) was leaked
prior to announcement by Stock Exchanges on May 31, 2018.SEBI has taken note of the same and has ordered an inquiry into the
matter.
