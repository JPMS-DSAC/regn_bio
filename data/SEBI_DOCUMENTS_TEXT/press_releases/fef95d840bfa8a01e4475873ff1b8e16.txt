
 
May 15, 2012

PR No.:

Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order  dated   May 10, 2012, disposing of the show cause notice issued to M/s. Om Gayatri Securities (Proprietor: Mr. Chirag Pujara) in the matter of Somplast Leather Industries Limited without imposing any penalty.
 
The full text of the order is available on the SEBI website: www.sebi.gov.in
