CANCELLATION OF CERTIFICATE OF REGISTRATION OF
3 STOCK–BROKERS OF INTERCONNECTED STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA

 
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has cancelled the certificate of registration of the following 3 Stock-Brokers with immediate effect:

The aforesaid 3 Stock-Brokers were expelled from the Stock Exchange and ceased to be a member of the Exchange, thereby failed to fulfil the pre-requisite condition of registration as Stock-Broker with SEBI.
 
The registration has been cancelled in terms of Sub-Regulation (5) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002. A full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
