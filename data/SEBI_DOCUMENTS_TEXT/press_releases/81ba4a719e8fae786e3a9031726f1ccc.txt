PR No.290/2006

 
 Order against M/s. Tackel Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd. 
in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.

Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated December 20, 2006 against M/s. Tackel Stock Broking Services Pvt. Ltd., broker, Calcutta Stock Exchange, in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. disposing of the show cause notice issued against the broker.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
December 21, 2006
