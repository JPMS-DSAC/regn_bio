PRESS RELEASE
PR No.313/2004
  

M/s. Manoj Kumar Tibrewal, member of CSE, restrained from accessing securities market in the matter of M/s. DSQ Industries Ltd.

 
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) vide order dated November 08, 2004, in the matter of M/s. DSQ Industries Ltd., has restrained M/s. Manoj Kumar Tibrewal, SEBI Reg. No. INB 030809417, member of Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE) for a period of two years from accessing securities market and from dealing in securities. The penalty is imposed for creation of artificial market in the shares of M/s. DSQ Industries Ltd. by M/s. Manoj Kumar Tibrewal.
M/s. Manoj Kumar Tibrewal, will not be entitled to access the securities market and deal in securities with effect from November 08, 2004 till November 07, 2006.
The full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
November 18, 2004
