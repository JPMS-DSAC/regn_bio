May 18, 2004 PR No.109/2004

 Market is safe

 
A surveillance meeting with the exchanges, depositories and senior SEBI officials was convened today at 8 a.m. under the chairmanship of Shri G.N. Bajpai, Chairman, SEBI and was participated by Shri T.M. Nagarajan and Shri A.K. Batra, Whole Time Members, SEBI.
The meeting took a comprehensive and incisive review of the settlement, risk management and the market trends. It is reaffirmed that there are no issues connected with settlement at all and it will go through smoothly and the risk management measures are robust and healthy, as testified by the events of the last couple of days. The investors are reassured that the market is safe and efficacious.
SEBI is keeping continuous watch on the market and the movements and will be taking appropriate measures as warranted by the situation.
Analysis of relevant data, is being done continuously by SEBI and any person/entity, found to be even trying to break the rules or indulge in misconduct/ misdemeanour, will be dealt with very severely and appropriately notwithstanding the size and shade of the entity.
