PRESS RELEASE
PR No.254/2006

Order against Shri Chandravadan J.Dalal, Member, BSE
 
Enquiry proceedings initiated by SEBI against Shri Chandravan J.Dalal, Member of the Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) for trading in the scrip of M/s. Padmalaya Telefilms Ltd. have been abated on account of the death of Shri Chandravadan J.Dalal, the sole proprietor of the firm. 
 
The order has been passed by Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI on November 02, 2006.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
