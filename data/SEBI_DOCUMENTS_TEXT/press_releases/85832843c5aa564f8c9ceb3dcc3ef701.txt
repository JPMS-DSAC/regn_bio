PR No.:  
26/2012


Order in respect of Claridges Investment and Finance Private Limited in the matter of Mascon Global Limited
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated February 27, 2012 suspending the certificate of registration of the stock broker, Claridges Investment and Finance Private Limited for a period of two weeks.
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
