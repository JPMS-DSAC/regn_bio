PR No.311/2009
Orders in the matter of Cals Refineries Limited and others
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed two orders dated September 30, 2009, in the matter of alleged manipulative trading by certain connected entities in the scrips of Cals Refineries Limited and others, confirming the ad interim ex-parte order dated June 04, 2009 against 1) Maruti Securities Limited and 2) Acme Craft Private Limited & Mr. Shyam Sunder Shah.  
 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
