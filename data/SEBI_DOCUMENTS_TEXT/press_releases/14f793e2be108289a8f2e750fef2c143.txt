PR No.299/2009
Order against M/s. Porecha Global Securities Pvt. Ltd. and Shri Arun Porecha, Members, BSE
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 09, 2009, in the matter of M/s. Mobile Telecommunications Ltd. suspending the certificates of registration of M/s. Porecha Global Securities Pvt. Ltd. and Shri Arun Porecha, Members, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) for a period of one month.
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
