SEBI Issues Consultation Paper on “Revisiting the public issue process"SEBI has placed a Consultation Paper on “Revisiting the public issue process" on the website for public comments. The full text of the Consultation Paper is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.  Comments are invited on the Consultation Paper latest by August 15, 2018.
