PR No.201/2010
Grant of exemption for the proposed acquisition of shares in 
M/s. GIVO Ltd. (Target Company) 
Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order datedAugust 30, 2010 granting exemption to Thakral Brothers (Pte) Limited (acquirer) from complying with the provisions of Regulation 11(2) of Securities and Exchange Board of India (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 [Takeover Regulations] with respect to their proposed increase in percentage shareholding and voting rights, pursuant to its proposed acquisition of 2,28,00,000 equity shares of the target company upon conversion of External Commercial Borrowings of Rs.11.97 crore into equity shares  through a preferential allotment. After this proposed allotment, the shareholding of promoter group will increases from 64.17% to 74.31% in the target company.
The application seeking exemption from the provisions of the Takeover Regulations was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Takeover Panel had rejected the application.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
