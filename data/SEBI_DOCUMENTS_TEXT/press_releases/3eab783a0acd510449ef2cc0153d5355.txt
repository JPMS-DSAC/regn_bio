PR No.109/2011
Order in respect of Ms. Surabhi Jain in the matter of 
trading by connected entities
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated July 08, 2011 in the matter of trading by connected entities, revoking interim directions issued vide ad interim ex-parte order dated June 04, 2009 and confirmatory order dated January 19, 2010 against Ms. Surabhi Jain and also disposing of the show cause notice without any further direction.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
July 13,  2011
