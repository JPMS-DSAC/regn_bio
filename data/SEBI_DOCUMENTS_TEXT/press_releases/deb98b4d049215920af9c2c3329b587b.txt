
 
May 15, 2012

PR No.:  
57/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  May 11, 2012 restraining Sarang Chemicals Limited from accessing the securities market and prohibiting it from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, for a period of 2 (two) years.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the SEBI website: www.sebi.gov.in
