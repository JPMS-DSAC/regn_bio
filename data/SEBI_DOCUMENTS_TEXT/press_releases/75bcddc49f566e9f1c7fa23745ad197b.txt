PR No.269/2010
Order against M/s. Sunchan Securities Limited
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), has passed an order dated December 08, 2010 canceling all the registrations granted by SEBI to M/s. Sunchan Securities Ltd., as a stock broker of National Stock Exchange of India Ltd., Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd., OTC Exchange of India and MCX Stock Exchange Ltd. 
The Order shall come into effect immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the Order. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
Mumbai
December 09, 2010
