
 
Apr 15, 2015

PR No.:  
106/2015


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an Ex Parte-Ad-Interim order on March 27,2015 in the matter of M/s. ERIL Mutual Benefit India Limited issuing the following directions.

ERIL Mutual Benefit India Limited and its promoters/directors including Mr. Biplab Kumar Dey Ms. Juthika Ghosh and Mr. Chandan Sinha Roy are restrained from mobilizing funds through the issue of equity shares or through any other form of securities, to the public and/ or invite subscription, in any manner whatsoever, either directly or indirectly till further directions
The aforesaid entities are prohibited from issuing prospectus or any offer document or issue advertisement for soliciting money from the public for the issue of securities, in any manner whatsoever, either directly or indirectly, till further directions,
The aforesaid entities shall not dispose of any of the properties or alienate the assets of the Company or dispose off any of their properties or alienate their assets;
The aforesaid entities shall not divert any funds raised from public at large through the issuance of the impugned equity shares, kept in its bank accounts and/or in the custody of the company without prior permission of SEBI until further orders
The aforesaid entities are restrained from accessing the securities market and are further prohibited from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities in any manner whatsoever, either directly or indirectly, till further directions
The aforesaid entities shall provide a full inventory of all their assets and properties and details of all their bank accounts, demat accounts and holdings of shares/securities, if held in physical form.
