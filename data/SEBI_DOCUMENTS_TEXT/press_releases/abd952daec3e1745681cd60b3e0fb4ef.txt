PRESS RELEASE

 
 
PR-165/2005
 
Registration of M/s. S.N.Surekha & Co., 
member of Calcutta Stock Exchange, suspended
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has suspended the certificate of registration of M/s. S. N. Surekha & Co. (SEBI Registration No. INB030605019) member of the Calcutta Stock Exchange for a period of 1 (One) month with effect from January 11, 2006. The penalty is imposed for non-segregation of client’s funds and own funds, dealing in unlisted securities and issuing contract notes in form ‘A’, executing off the floor transactions etc.
 
M/s. S. N. Surekha & Co., member of the Calcutta Stock Exchange will not be entitled to carry on the business as a Stock Broker with effect from January 11, 2006 till February 10, 2006.  
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
December 26, 2005
