
 
Nov 09, 2011

PR No.:  
144/2011


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 3, 2011, in the matter of Datasoft Application Software (India) Limited disposing of the enquiry proceedings against Ruchiraj Shares & Stock Brokers Private Limited by directing it to be cautious and careful while dealing for its clients in the securities market.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
