PRESS RELEASE
PR No.150/2005

Order against Shri Jimmy Jal Gazdar
 
Shri Madhukar,  Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order on 18.11.2005 against Shri Jimmy Jal Gazdar, Chairman and Managing Director, Cochin Malabar Estates and Industries Ltd.  The order is in compliance of direction issued by Securities and Appellate Tribunal in appeal No.247 of 2004, for deciding the preliminary issue of jurisdiction of SEBI in issuing show cause notice dated 05.02.2004 against Shri Gazdar.  The Whole Time Member has held that SEBI had jurisdiction for issuing the said show cause notice against Shri Gazdar for violation of Takeover Regulations, 1994 which were repealed and Takeover Regulations, 1997 had come into force. It was further felt that, proceedings pursuant to the show cause notice dated 05.02.2004 may be taken up by SEBI, if deemed fit, after expiry of the period of 15 days from the date of receipt of the order by Shri Gazdar. 
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
