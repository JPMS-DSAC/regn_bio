PRESS RELEASE
PR No.273/2007


M/s. Time Capital Ltd., Member of NSE, censured
 
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 17, 2007, imposing a penalty of ‘censure’ on M/s. Time Capital Limited (SEBI Reg. No. INB230771832), member of National Stock Exchange of India Ltd.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
 
 
 Mumbai
October 05, 2007
