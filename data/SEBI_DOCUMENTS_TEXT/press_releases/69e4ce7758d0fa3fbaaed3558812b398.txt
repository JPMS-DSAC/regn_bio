CANCELLATION OF CERTIFICATE OF REGISTRATION OF 
A STOCK–BROKER OF NATIONAL STOCK EXCHANGE OF INDIA
 
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has cancelled the certificate of registration of M/s. Shree Krishna & Co. (SEBI Registration No. INB230662920), erstwhile member of National Stock Exchange with immediate effect.
The aforesaid Stock-Broker was expelled from the Stock Exchange and ceased to be a member of the Exchange, thereby failed to fulfill the pre-requisite condition of registration as Stock-Broker with SEBI.
The registration has been cancelled in terms of Sub-Regulation (5) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002. A full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
