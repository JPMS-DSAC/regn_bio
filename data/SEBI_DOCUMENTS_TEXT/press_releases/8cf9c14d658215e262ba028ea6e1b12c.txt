PRESS RELEASE
PR No.311/2007

Order against M/s. Yatin D. Shah, Member, Pune Stock Exchange Ltd., in respect of its dealings in the shares of Home Trade Ltd.
 
 
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated November 29, 2007   imposed a major penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of M/s. Yatin D. Shah, Member, Pune Stock Exchange Ltd., (SEBI registration no. INB 110245316), for a period of 18 months in respect of its dealings in the shares of Home Trade Ltd.
 
The certificate of registration of M/s. Yatin D. Shah will remain suspended from December 20, 2007 till June 19, 2009.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
November 30, 2007
