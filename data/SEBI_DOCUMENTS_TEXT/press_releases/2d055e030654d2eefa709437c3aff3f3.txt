PR No.04/2010
Order in the matter of Cals Refineries Ltd. and others
Dr.  K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 31, 2009  confirming the ad interim ex-parte order dated June 04,  2009 against M/s. Alosha Vanijya Private Ltd. and its director Mr. Rajendra Kumar Agarwal in the matter of alleged manipulative trading by certain connected entities in the scrips of Cals Refineries Ltd. and others.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
