PRESS RELEASE
PR No.258/2008


Order against Mr. Atin Das, sub-broker


Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 04, 2008, disposing of the enquiry proceedings against Mr. Atin Das, sub broker. As the said sub broker has already been prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities from February 20, 2007 till date, in accordance with interim order passed by SEBI, no further penalty has been imposed.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
