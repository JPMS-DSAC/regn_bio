India to host IOSCO 2007 Annual Conference
 
The annual conference of the International Organisation of Securities Commissions (IOSCO) will be held in India in the year 2007 and hosted by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI). This was decided today at the IOSCO annual conference being held in Amman, Jordan. India was represented in the meeting by Shri G.N. Bajpai, Chairman, SEBI and Shri T.M. Nagarajan, Whole Time Member, SEBI.
IOSCO is an international body of securities markets regulators that was created in 1983, with headquarters in Madrid, Spain and has 181 member agencies. The objectives of the organisation’s members are :

to cooperate together to promote high standards of regulation in order to maintain just efficient and sound markets; 


to exchange information on their respective experiences in order to promote the development of domestic markets; 


to unite their efforts to establish standards and an effective surveillance of international securities transactions; 


to provide mutual assistance to promote the integrity of the markets by a rigorous application of the standards and by effective enforcement against offenses. 

IOSCO members meet every year at the Annual Conference to discuss important issues related to world securities and futures markets. SEBI is an active member of the IOSCO and Shri G.N. Bajpai, Chairman, SEBI is the Chairman of the Task Force on Corporate Governance.
