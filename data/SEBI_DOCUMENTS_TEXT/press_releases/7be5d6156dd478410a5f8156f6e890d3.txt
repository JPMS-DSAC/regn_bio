PR No.58/2008

Consent Order in the matter of Blue Information Technology Ltd.

A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, Shri G. Anantharaman and   Dr. T. C. Nair has passed consent order dated 15/02/2008, on an application submitted by M/s. Harvestdeal Securities Ltd, in the matter of Blue Information Technology Ltd., in accordance with SEBI Guidelines dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.50,000/- (Rupees fifty thousand only) towards the terms of consent in the matter and also agreed for a voluntary bar from activating broking firm for business as a Trading Member of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. for the period of three months from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
