PR No.199/2009
Order in the matter of Karuna Cables Ltd.
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated   June 15, 2009, in the matter of Karuna Cables Ltd. revoking the directions issued by Securities and Exchange Board of India vide orders dated November 29, 2005 and July 25, 2006 of prohibition on buying, selling or dealing in the securities of Karuna Cables Ltd.  against Mrs. Hasu M. Shah, Mr. Mit Shah, Mr. Akshat Shah,   Mr. Kirti C. Shah, Mr. Jaswant C. Shah, Mr. C.V.V. Prasad, Mr. N.S. Ranganath,    Mr. Pavankumar Sanwarmal, N.G. Builders & Developers, Mr. Narendrapal Gupta and Mrs. Manju Gupta. The proceedings against Mr. Vijay Bhagwandas Shah became infructuous owing to his demise.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
