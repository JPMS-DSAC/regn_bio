PRESS RELEASE
PR No.53/2007

Order against  Shri Rajendra Kumar Chokhany in the matter of
M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 12, 2007 against Shri Rajendra Kumar Chokhany, member of Calcutta Stock Exchange in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. disposing of the show cause notice issued to the broker without imposing any penalty.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
February 13, 2007
