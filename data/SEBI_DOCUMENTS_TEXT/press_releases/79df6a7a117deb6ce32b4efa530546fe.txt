PRESS RELEASE
July 06, 2004 PR No.144/2004
 
Suspension of certificate of registration of M/s. Man Mohan Damani
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has suspended the certificate of registration of M/s. Man Mohan Damani., SEBI Reg. No. INB 030766312, member Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE) for a period of six months. The penalty is imposed for their indulgence in off-market transactions and fund based activities.
M/s. Man Mohan Damani, member of CSE will not be entitled to carry on the business as a Stock Broker with effect from July 23, 2004 till January 22, 2005.
The full text of the Order is available on our website www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
July 6, 2004
