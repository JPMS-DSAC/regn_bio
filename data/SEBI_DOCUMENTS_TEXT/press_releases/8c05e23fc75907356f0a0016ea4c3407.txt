PRESS RELEASE
PR No.273/2006

 
Registration of M/s. ARP Shares (Pvt.) Ltd., suspended





Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated December 04, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. ARP Shares (Pvt.) Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INS010727738), sub-broker affiliated to UPSE Securities Ltd., member of The Stock Exchange, Mumbai for a period of seven days with effect from December 25, 2006. 
 
M/s. ARP Shares (Pvt.) Ltd. will not be entitled to carry on the business as a sub-broker with effect from December 25, 2006 to December 31, 2006.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
