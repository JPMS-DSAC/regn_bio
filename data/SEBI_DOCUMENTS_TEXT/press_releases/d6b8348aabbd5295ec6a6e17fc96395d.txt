PR No.366/2009




 
Consent order on the application submitted by 
M/s. Shirpur Gold Refinery Limited
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri M. S. Sahoo and  Dr. K. M. Abraham has passed a consent order dated November 25, 2009, on the application submitted by M/s. Shirpur Gold Refinery Limited in the matter of non-disclosures under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997, in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs. 4,25,000/- (Rupees four lakh  twenty five thousand only) towards the settlement charges in the matter.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
