PR No.83/2011




Order in the matter of M/s. Balaji Industrial Corporation Limited
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated June 02, 2011 in the matter of M/s. Balaji Industrial Corporation Limited disposing of the Show Cause Notice issued to Skokie Traders Private Limited (earlier known as Smruthi Agro Farms Private Limited), Kyvalya Traders Private Limited (earlier known as Kyvalya Agro Farms Private Limited), Ramya Traders Private Limited, Sudheer Real Estates Private Limited and Vyshnavi Real Estate & Property Limited without any directions.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
