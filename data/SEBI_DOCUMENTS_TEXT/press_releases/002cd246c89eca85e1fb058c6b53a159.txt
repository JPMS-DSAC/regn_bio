PRESS RELEASE
PR No.76/2007

Order against Shri Harvinder Kumar Bagai in the matter of
M/s. Media Video Ltd.
 
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  February 23, 2007 against Shri Harvinder Kumar Bagai in the matter of  M/s. Media Video Ltd.,  debarring him from accessing the capital market and dealing in securities directly or indirectly for a period of one year with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
