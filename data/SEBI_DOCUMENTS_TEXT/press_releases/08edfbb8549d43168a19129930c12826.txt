PR No.110/2009
Order against MBD Securities Private Limited in the matter of Prudential Pharmaceuticals Limited
Shri M. S. Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 20, 2009, in the matter of Prudential Pharmaceuticals Ltd., Member, BSE, issuing a warning to MBD Securities Pvt. Ltd. to be cautious in its future dealings and exercise due care and diligence in the conduct of its affairs as a stock broker.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
