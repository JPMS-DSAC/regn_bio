PR No.29/2008


SEBI extends refund facility through ECS to additional 53 centres


Securities and Exchange Board of India, in consultation with the Reserve Bank of India,  today, extended the facility of refunds to investors applying for public/rights issues through Electronic Clearing Service (ECS)  to additional 53 centres. With this, ECS facility will be available in 68 centres. With the increase in the number of centres, a large number of investors are expected to benefit from this facility in obtaining refunds in a time efficient manner.
 
SEBI has also issued necessary instructions to all registered Registrars to the issue, Merchant Bankers, and Bankers to Issue, in this regard.
SEBI had amended the Disclosure and Investor Protection (DIP) Guidelines in January 2006 to provide for various modes of making refunds to applicants viz. ECS / Direct Credit / Real Time Gross Settlement (RTGS) / National Electronic Funds Transfer (NEFT).
 
The above circulars are available on the website at www.sebi.gov.in
