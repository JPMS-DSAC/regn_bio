Shri. Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 12, 2014 under sections 11 and 11B of the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992 and regulation 32 of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 in respect of SAL Care Private Limited and Shah Alloys Limited, disposing off the show cause notice dated April 03, 2014 in the matter of acquisition of shares of S.A.L. Steel Limited by SAL Care Private Limited.

The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
