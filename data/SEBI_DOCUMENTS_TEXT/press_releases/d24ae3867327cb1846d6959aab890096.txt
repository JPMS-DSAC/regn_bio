PR No.06/2011
Order in respect of  D. G. Goenka Equities Pvt. Ltd.  in the matter of 
Design Auto Systems Ltd.
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 04, 2011 in the matter of  Design Auto  Systems Ltd. suspending the certificate of registration of D. G. Goenka Equities Pvt. Ltd., member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), for a period of two months.
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
January 10, 2011
