PR No.213/2006

Order in the matter of Global Trust Bank
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order on September 14, 2006 disposing off the show cause notice issued to   M/s. Phulchand Sons Investments Pvt. Ltd., in the matter of Global Trust Bank.
 
The Show Cause Notice is disposed off on the grounds that there is no corroborative evidence against M/s. Phulchand Sons Investments Pvt. Ltd. to establish that they had created artificial volume and indulged in circular trades while dealing in the scrip of Global Trust Bank.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
