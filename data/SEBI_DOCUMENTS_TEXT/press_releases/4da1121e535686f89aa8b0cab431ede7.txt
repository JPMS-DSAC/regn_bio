PR No.259/2007


                                           
Order against M/s. O J Financial Services Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of Havells India Limted
 
 
Dr. T.C. Nair, Whole-time Member, SEBI,  vide an order dated September 17, 2007 has disposed off the enquiry proceedings against M/s. O J Financial Services Pvt. Ltd., member NSE [SEBI registration no.: INB 230772831], in the matter of its dealings in the scrip of Havells India Limited, without imposing any penalty.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the SEBI website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
 
September 19, 2007.
