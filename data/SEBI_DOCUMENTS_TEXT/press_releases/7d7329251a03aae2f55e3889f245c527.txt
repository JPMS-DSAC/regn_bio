PR No.43/2007

Order against M/s. Bharat Bhushan & Co. and Bharat Bhushan Equity Traders Limited in the matter of M/s. Information Technologies (India) Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order dated  February 05, 2007 against M/s. Bharat Bhushan & Co., member of Delhi Stock Exchange (DSE) and M/s. Bharat Bhushan Equity Traders Ltd., member of National Stock Exchange (NSE) in the matter of Information Technologies (India) Limited, imposing a penalty of Censure against the brokers.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
February 07, 2007
