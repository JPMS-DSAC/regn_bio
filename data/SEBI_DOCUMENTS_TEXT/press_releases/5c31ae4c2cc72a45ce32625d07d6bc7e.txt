PR No.13/2010
Consent Order on the application submitted by M/s. Classic Finance in the matter of Denim Enterprises Limited
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri M. S. Sahoo and Dr. K. M. Abraham, has passed an order dated January 13, 2010, on the consent application submitted by M/s. Classic Finance, in the matter of Denim Enterprises Ltd., in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.1,50,000/- (Rupees one lakh fifty thousand only) towards the terms of consent in the matter.
 
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
