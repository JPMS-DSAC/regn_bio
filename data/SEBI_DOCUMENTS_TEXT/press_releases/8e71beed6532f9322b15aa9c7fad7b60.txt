PRESS RELEASE
PR No.52/2008

Order against M/s. Charmi Investment, Shri Parshwa Finance,
M/s. Rujul Finance Ltd. and Shri Ashish P. Shah

Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 14, 2008 against sub-brokers M/s.Charmi Investment (SEBI Registration No. INS231624813), Shri Parshwa Finance (SEBI Registration No. INS 011245110), M/s. Rujul Finance Ltd. and Shri Ashish P. Shah in the matter of creation of artificial volumes and offloading of unlisted shares of Shalibhadra Infosec Ltd.
M/s. Charmi Investment and Shri Parshwa Finance have been restrained from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities for a period of one year in the proprietary account. M/s. Rujul Finance Ltd. and Shri Ashish P. Shah are also restrained from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities for a period of one year.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
