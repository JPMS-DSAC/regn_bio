
 
Sep 23, 2011

PR No.:  
130/2011


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 21, 2011 granting exemption to Mr. Pranav Kumar Ranade and others (together referred to as “promoters”) in respect of proposed increase in their voting rights /shareholding in M/s. Eon Electric Limited (Target company), from complying with the provisions of Regulations 11(1) of Securities and Exchange Board of India (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 [Takeover Regulations], pursuant to the proposed buyback of equity shares by the Target company.
 The application seeking exemption from the provisions of the Takeover Regulations was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Takeover Panel recommended the grant of exemption to the promoters from making an open offer.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
