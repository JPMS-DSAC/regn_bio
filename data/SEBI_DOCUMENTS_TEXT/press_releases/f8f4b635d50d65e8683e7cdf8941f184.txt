
 
Oct 19, 2011

PR No.:  
138/2011


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated October 13, 2011 in the matter of Orient Trade Link Limited viz.,
i.            restraining Mr. Gajinder Paul Dhody and 21st Century Media Works from accessing the securities market and prohibiting them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, for a period of six months from the date of the order.
ii.            disposing the Show Cause Notice issued to Pramukh Securities Pvt. Limited without any direction.
 
The Order has come into effect immediately.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
