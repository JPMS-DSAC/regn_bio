
 
Apr 22, 2013

PR No.:  
44/2013


Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated April 05, 2013, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s M. Bhiwaniwala & Co. Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd., for a period of three months, in respect of its dealings in the shares of G. R. Industries and Finance Ltd.
 
The order shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
