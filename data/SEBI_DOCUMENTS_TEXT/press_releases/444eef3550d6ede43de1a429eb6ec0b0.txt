PRESS RELEASE
PR No.97/2007

Order against M/s. JVS Securities Private Ltd. in the matter of 
M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 15, 2007, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. JVS Securities Private Ltd., member of National Stock Exchange (SEBI Registration  No. INB230752430) and Calcutta Stock Exchange (SEBI Registration   No. INB030724038), in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd., for a period of seven days.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in 
 
 
Mumbai
March 16, 2007
