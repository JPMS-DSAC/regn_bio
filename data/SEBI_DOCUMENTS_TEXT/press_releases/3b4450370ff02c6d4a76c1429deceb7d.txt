PRESS RELEASE
PR No.195/2007

Order against M/s. Triveni Management Consultancy Services Ltd., 
Member of NSE
 
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated June 19, 2007, has ‘censured’ M/s. Triveni Management Consultancy Services Ltd., Member of National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. (NSE), for their dealing in the scrip of M/s. Snowcem India Ltd.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
June 20, 2007
