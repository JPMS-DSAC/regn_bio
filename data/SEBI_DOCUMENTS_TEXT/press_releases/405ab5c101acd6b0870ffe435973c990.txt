PRESS RELEASE
July 09, 2004 PR No.151/2004

Securities transaction tax proposed in the Finance Bill 2004 
 
 
The representatives of broking community had met SEBI Chairman today to express their concerns and apprehensions about the Securities transaction tax proposed to be levied on all securities transactions. The representatives also stated that they have sought an appointment with the Finance Minister to discuss this issue.
A question was raised on the date of applicability of this provision. It is being clarified that the securities transaction tax proposed in the Finance Bill 2004 would come in to effect only upon the passing of Finance Bill by the Parliament and the Gazette notification and not with immediate effect.
  
Mumbai
July 9, 2004
