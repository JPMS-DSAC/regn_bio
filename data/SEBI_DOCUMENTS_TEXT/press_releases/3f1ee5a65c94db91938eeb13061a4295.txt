  PRESS RELEASE
PR No.28/2008


Order against M/s. Surana Maloo & Co., Auditors of 
M/s. Oasis Media Matrix Limited


Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 30, 2008 disposing off the show cause notice dated May 14, 2007 against M/s Surana Maloo & Co. (SMC), Auditors of M/s. Oasis Media Matrix Limited (OMML).
 
“In the present matter it is undisputed that OMML has not received full consideration for the re-issued shares. SMC, however, has certified that OMML has received the full consideration for the shares re-issued. I have noted that SEBI has already initiated adjudication and prosecution proceedings against SMC. In view of this, I do not find it appropriate to pass directions under Section 11 and 11B of SEBI Act against SMC”   the order said.
 
The order comes into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
