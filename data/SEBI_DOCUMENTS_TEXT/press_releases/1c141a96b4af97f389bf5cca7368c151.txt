PR No.283/2009
Consent Order on the application submitted by Shri Manoj Kumar Bothra
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri M.S. Sahoo and   Dr. K.M. Abraham, has passed a consent order dated September 01, 2009 on the consent application submitted by Shri Manoj Kumar Bothra.  The applicant, without admitting or denying guilt, has remitted a sum of Rs.25,000/- (Rupees twenty five thousand  only) as well as undertaken voluntary debarment from buying, selling or dealing in the securities market in any manner whatsoever, whether directly or indirectly, for a period of two months from September 07, 2009.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
