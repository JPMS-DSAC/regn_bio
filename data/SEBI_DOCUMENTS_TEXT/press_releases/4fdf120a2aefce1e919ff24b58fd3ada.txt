PR No.14/2011
Order in respect of M/s. Harbinger Trading Company Private Limited, 
M/s. Aditi A. Dalal and M/s. Shriya Capital Services Private Limited in the matter of M/s. VXL Instruments Limited
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  January 17, 2011 directing that:
(a)  M/s. Harbinger Trading Company Private Limited and M/s. Shriya Capital Services Private Limited are restrained from accessing the securities market and are further prohibited from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in the securities market, either directly or indirectly, for a period of two months.
(b)   M/s. Aditi A. Dalal is restrained from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in the securities market, in its proprietary accounts, either directly or indirectly, for a period of two months.
(c)   As M/s. Harbinger Trading Company Private Limited is already restrained from accessing the securities market and further prohibited from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in the securities market, either directly or indirectly, for a period of two months, vide an Order dated January 17, 2011 in respect of its dealing in the shares of Ceat Limited during the period from January to March 2000 and March to May 2001, the period of debarment ordered hereinabove against Harbinger Trading Company Private Limited would run concurrently.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
