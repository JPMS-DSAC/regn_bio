PR No.287/2009
Allocation of limits to FIIs/Sub-accounts for investment in Government Debt
1.    As per SEBI Circular No. IMD/FII & C/40/2009 dated September 04, 2009, unutilised investment limits for Government debt was available for allocation to the FIIs/ Sub-accounts in an open bidding platform. The bidding for these limits took place today on the NSE offered platform.
2.    Pursuant to the bidding, 12 successful bidders (list enclosed as annexure) got allocation of investment. These limits shall be utilized by the allocated entities within 45 days of the allocation.
