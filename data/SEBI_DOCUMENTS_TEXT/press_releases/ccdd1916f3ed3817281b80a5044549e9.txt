Press Releases 











PR No.75/2011
Order in respect of failure to redress investor grievances by 
Kaleidoscope Films Limited and its directors 
In the matter of non-redressal of investor grievances by the below mentioned company and its directors, Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed order dated May 26, 2011 restraining them from accessing the securities market and prohibit them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, till all the pending investor grievances against the Company are resolved.




1
 


Kaleidoscope Films Ltd. 
(earlier known as Gujarat Investment Castings Ltd.)


Company
 




2


Prakash B. Dhebar


Director




3


Smita Mahendra Pandya


Director




4


Sarju Jayantilal Parikh


Director




The order has come into effect immediately.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
