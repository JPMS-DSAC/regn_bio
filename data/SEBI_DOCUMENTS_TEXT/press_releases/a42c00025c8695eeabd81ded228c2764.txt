Order in the matter of South East Asia Marine Engineering and

Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated   March 11, 2010, in the matter of South East Asia Marine Engineering and Constructions Limited disposing of the Enquiry proceedings against Nirmal Bang Securities Pvt. Ltd., Bama Securities Ltd. and Bang Securities Pvt. Ltd. without imposing any penalty or direction.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
