
 
Aug 11, 2014

PR No.:  
91/2014


Mr. S. Raman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an interim order on August 08, 2014 directing Viswas Real Estates and Infrastructures India Limited (VREIL) and its Directors  Mr. Vaka Saradhi and Mr. Eshararao Gundala:
 a)- not to collect any fresh money from investors under its existing schemes; 
b)- not to launch any new schemes or plans or float any new companies to raise fresh moneys; 
c)- to immediately submit the full inventory of the assets including land obtained through money raised by VREIL; 
d)- not to dispose of any of the properties or alienate the assets including land obtained directly or indirectly through the money raised by VREIL; 
e)- not to divert any funds raised from public at large, kept in bank account(s) and/or in the custody of VREIL; 
f)- to furnish all the information/details sought by SEBI within 15 days from the date of receipt of this order, including, 
i. Details of amount mobilized and refunded till date, 
ii. Scheme wise list of investors and their contact numbers and addresses, 
iii. PAN of the aforementioned Directors and 
iv. Audited Accounts for the last three years. 
 
 The company had launched Collective Investment Schemes without obtaining certificate of registration from SEBI.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
