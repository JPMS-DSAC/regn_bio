PRESS RELEASE
PR No.234/2007

 
Order against M/s. M. S. Consultancy Services in the matter of 
M/s. MOH Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated       August 09, 2007, against  M/s. M. S. Consultancy Services, sub-broker of  M/s. ACME Shares and Stock Pvt. Ltd. (now Apex Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd.), Broker, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), in the matter of M/s. MOH Ltd. imposing a minor penalty of suspension of certificate of registration (SEBI Registration no. INS010530116)  for a period of one month.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.      
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
