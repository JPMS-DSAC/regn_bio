PR No.193/2010
Consent Order on the application submitted by M/s. Wallfort Share & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of Mazda Fabrics & Processors Limited 
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. K. M. Abraham and  Shri Prashant Saran, has passed a consent order dated August 12, 2010, on an application submitted by M/s. Wallfort Share & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of Mazda Fabrics & Processors Ltd. in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.10,00,000/- (Rupees ten lakh only) towards the terms of consent in the matter and has also agreed to undergo a voluntary moratorium on their proprietary trading for a period of one year.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
