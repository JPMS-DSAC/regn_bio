PR No.186/2010
Order against Ashok Kumar Kayan, Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd. in the matter of M/s Amluckie Investment Company Ltd.
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated August 02, 2010, warning the broker Ashok Kumar Kayan, Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd. for its dealings in the shares of Amluckie Investment Company Ltd. and further advised it to exercise adequate caution while functioning as a stock broker.
The full text of the above order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
