PRESS RELEASE
 
PR-62/2005
Public comments invited for issues relating to obtaining
Unique Identification Number (UIN)
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has constituted a Committee under the Chairmanship of Shri Jagdish Capoor, former Deputy Governor of Reserve Bank of India and currently Chairman, HDFC Bank Ltd. to re-examine issues relating to obtaining Unique Identification Number (UIN) in terms of SEBI (Central Database of Market Participants) Regulations, 2003. While the Committee would be meeting select intermediaries and investor representatives in the securities market, any institution or person desirous of sending suggestions may send them by e-mail to mapin@sebi.gov.in or through post to Shri V S Sundaresan, General Manager, SEBI, 29th Floor, Centre-1, World Trade Centre, Cuffe Parade, Mumbai – 400 005, to reach by April 15, 2005.
 

Mumbai
