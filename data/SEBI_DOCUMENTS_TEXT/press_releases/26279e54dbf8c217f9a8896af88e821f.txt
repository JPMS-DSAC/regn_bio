
 
Jun 01, 2015

PR No.:  
143/2015


Shri S Raman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an interim order cum Show Cause Notice dated May 29, 2015 in the matter of Susk India Ltd. (“the Company”), inter-alia issuing following directions against the company and it directors/ promoters–


not to collect any fresh moneys from investors from its existing scheme;
not to launch any new scheme/plan,
not to float any new companies/firm to raise fresh moneys;
not to dispose of any of the properties or alienate the assets of the existing scheme;
not to divert any funds raised from public at large, kept in bank account(s) and/or in the custody of the  company;
to immediately submit the full inventory of the assets owned by Susk India out of the amounts collected from  the "customers"/investors under its existing schemes;
to furnish all the information sought by SEBI within 21 days of the receipt of the Order.


The company was prima facie found to be engaged in fund mobilizing activity from the public, by floating/sponsoring/ launching 'collective investment scheme' as defined in Section 11AA of the SEBI Act without obtaining a certificate of registration from SEBI as required under Section 12(1B) of the SEBI Act and the CIS Regulations.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in.
