PR No.204/2009
Order in the matter of Pyramid Saimira Theatre Ltd.
Shri M. S. Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated June 25, 2009, restraining Mr. Kishore S. Jain, Mr. Jayantilal R. Jain, Mr. Shripal J. Shah, Mr. Rajeshkumar P. Jain, and Mr. Pravin Kumar Jain from buying, selling or dealing in the securities market in any manner whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of three years.
It is also directed that the unlawful gains made by the above named shall be disgorged and an interest @20% shall be paid on the unlawful gains, from the date of sale of the shares so allotted till the date of disgorgement. The same shall be paid by them within 45 days of receipt of the order. In case the aforesaid amount is not paid within the specified time, the above persons shall be restrained from buying, selling or dealing in securities market in any manner whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a further period of seven years, without prejudice to SEBIâ€™s right to enforce disgorgement.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
