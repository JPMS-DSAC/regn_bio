  PRESS RELEASE
PR No.12/2008


Order in the matter of M/s. Prakash Fotran Softech Ltd.


Shri V.K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 11, 2008 restraining M/s. Prakash Fotran Softech Ltd. and its Directors viz. Shri P.C. Jain, Shri Rajesh Jain and Shri Rakesh Jain from buying, selling and dealing or accessing the securities market in any manner for a period of one year. The order has further disposed of the show cause notices issued to Shri Mago J. B. Singh, Shri Sanjay Shah, Shri Pramod Jain and Shri Ganesh Singh Jhabua without any direction in the matter of M/s. Prakash Fotran Softech Ltd.
 
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
