PRESS RELEASE
PR No.233/2007

Certificate of registration of M/s. Modern Security Traders, suspended
 
 
Dr. T.C.Nair,  Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated August 07, 2007, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Modern Security Traders (SEBI Reg. No. INS231166518), a sub-broker affiliated to LSE Securities Ltd., member of National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. for a period of fifteen (15) days with effect from August 28, 2007. 
 
M/s. Modern Security Traders, will not be entitled to carry on the business as a sub-broker with effect from August 28, 2007 to September 11, 2007.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in.
