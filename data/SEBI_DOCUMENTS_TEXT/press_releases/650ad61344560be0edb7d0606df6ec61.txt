PRESS RELEASE
PR No.81/2007

Order against M/s. Pink Panther Securities Pvt. Ltd. and six others
in the matter of M/s. Media Video Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  February 23, 2007, directing that no directions needs to be issued to 1) M/s. Pink Panther Securities Pvt. Ltd., 2) Triple Star Fincap Pvt. Ltd., 3) RPB Holdings & Finance Pvt. Ltd., 4) Sabhya Fincap Pvt. Ltd., 5) S. Arun Portfolio Leasing Pvt. Ltd., 6) Midway Investments Pvt. Ltd. and 7) Junkool Holdings & Finance Pvt. Ltd. and the show cause notices issued to these entities are being disposed of accordingly.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
