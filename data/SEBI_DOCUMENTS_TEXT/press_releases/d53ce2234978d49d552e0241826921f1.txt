PR No.157/2010
Consent Order on the application submitted by Ruchi Soya Industries Limited
 
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr.K.M.Abraham and Shri Prashant Saran, has passed consent order dated June 24, 2010, in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders, on the application submitted by Ruchi Soya Industries Limited in the matter of Param Industries Limited (since amalgamated with Ruchi Soya Industries Limited). The applicant has remitted Rs.1,00,00,000/- (Rs. One Crore Only) towards the settlement charges, in the matter.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
