PRESS RELEASE
PR No.272/2004

Offer documents received and observations issued by SEBI from October 04, 2004 to October 10, 2004
During the period October 04, 2004 to October 10, 2004, SEBI had issued observations on two offer documents filed with it.  During the same period, one offer document was withdrawn.  List I gives details of observations issued and List II gives details of cases withdrawn during the period.
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in) under “Public Issues etc.”
