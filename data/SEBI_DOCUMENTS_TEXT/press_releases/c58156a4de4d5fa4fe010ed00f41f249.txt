PRESS RELEASE
PR No.143/2005

M/s Vishal J. Shah & Co., member Calcutta Stock Exchange, censured
Securities and Exchange Board of India(SEBI) vide order dated October 14, 2005, has imposed a minor penalty of ‘Censure’ on M/s Vishal J. Shah & Co., SEBI Reg. No. INB 031122618, member Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE). The action has been imposed in the case of DSQ Industries Ltd.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
October 25, 2005
