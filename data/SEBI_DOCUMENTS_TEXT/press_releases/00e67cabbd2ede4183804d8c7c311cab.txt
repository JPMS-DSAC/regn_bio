PR No.79/2011




Order against M/s. Credent Portfolio Management Private Limited and its Directors, Mr. Amaranjay Kumar and Mr. Mrityunjay Kumar
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed order dated  June 01, 2011 confirming the directions issued vide the ad-interim ex-parte Order dated October 11, 2010 against M/s. Credent Portfolio Management Private Limited and its directors, Mr. Amaranjay Kumar  and Mr. Mritunjay Kumar.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
June 02, 2011
