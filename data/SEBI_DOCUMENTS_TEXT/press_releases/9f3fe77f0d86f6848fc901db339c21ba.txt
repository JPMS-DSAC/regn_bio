PRESS RELEASE
June 30, 2004 PR No.138/2004

Offer documents received / withdrawn and observations issued
by SEBI from 14th June, 2004 to 27th June, 2004

 
During the period 14/06/2004 to 27/06/2004, three offer documents were received by SEBI. SEBI gave observations on one offer document. List I gives details of offer documents received and List II give details of observations issued.
During the period from 31.5.2004 to 27.6.2004, three offer documents were closed by SEBI. List III gives details of such offer documents closed.
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in ) under "Primary Market".
