
 
Dec 20, 2013

PR No.:  
123/2013


Shri. Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 19, 2013, granting exemption under Regulation 11 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 2011 (Takeover Regulations) to the Government of India (“GoI” or “Acquirer”) from the applicability of Regulation 3(2) of Takeover Regulations with respect to its proposed increase in shareholding from 55.24% to 66.57%, pursuant to allotment of 11,85,83,771 equity shares of Dena Bank (Applicant) by way of preferential allotment.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
