PRESS RELEASE
PR No. 265/2004


  

 

Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
M/s. Hindustan Copper Ltd.
 

The Securities and Exchange Board of India has granted exemption to the acquirer viz. Government of India from making an open offer under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of M/s. Hindustan Copper Ltd. vide order dated October 01, 2004. 
The acquirer holds 98.95% of the total paid up share capital of M/s. Hindustan Copper Ltd. and after the proposed acquisition the shareholding will increase to 99.478%. Against the budgetary support of Rs. 365.34 crore already received, M/s. Hindustan Copper Ltd. proposes to issue 36,53,40,000 equity shares of Rs. 10/- in the name of the President of India after receipt of shareholders’ approval. 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer. 
The full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
