PR No.37/2011



Orders in respect of M/s. Bhartendu Financial Services Private Limited,  M/s. Kardam Financial Services Private Limited, M/s. Leaprofin Securities Limited, M/s. Suresh Kumar and M/s. Rakesh Mishra in the matter of Hoffland Group 
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed five orders dated March 08, 2011 in the matter of Hoffland Group cancelling the certificate of registration of five stock brokers viz. M/s. Bhartendu Financial Services Private Limited-Member Bombay Stock Exchange Limited, M/s. Kardam Financial Services Private Limited-Member Cochin Stock Exchange Limited, M/s. Leaprofin Securities Limited-Member OTCEI, M/s. Suresh Kumar and M/s. Rakesh Mishra-Members of Uttar Pradesh Stock Exchange Limited. 
The orders shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the orders. 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
Mumbai
March 10, 2011
