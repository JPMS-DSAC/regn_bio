Order in the matter of M/s. GHCL Ltd.

Comosum Investment Pvt. Ltd., (4) M/s. Altar Investment Pvt. Ltd., (5) M/s. ILAC Investment Pvt. Ltd. and (6) M/s. Dalmia Housing Finance Ltd. in the matter of M/s. GHCL Ltd. discontinuing the directions given vide ex-parte interim order dated   April 25, 2007.  
 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
