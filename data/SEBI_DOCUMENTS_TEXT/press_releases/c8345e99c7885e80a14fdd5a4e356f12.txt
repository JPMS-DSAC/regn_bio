PRESS RELEASE
PR No.291/2007


Order against the sub broker M/s. Shreepati Holdings & Finance Pvt. Ltd.
in the matter of M/s. Sun Infoways Ltd.
 
Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 29, 2007 suspending the Registration of M/s. Shreepati Holdings & Finance Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INS010831936), sub broker of M/s. JCL Securities Ltd., member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), for a period of two months in the matter of M/s. Sun Infoways Ltd.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
October 31, 2007
