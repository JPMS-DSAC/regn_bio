PR No.149/2010
Order in the matter of Cals Refineries Limited and others
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  June 30, 2010, confirming the ad interim ex-parte order dated June 04, 2009 against Venture Business Advisors Private Limited in the matter of alleged manipulative trading by certain connected entities in the scrips of Cals Refineries Limited and others.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
