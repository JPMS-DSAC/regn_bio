PR No.89/2008


  
Order against M/s. Yellow Saphire Investment Ltd. in the matter of
M/s. Jaiprakash Industries Limited.


Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated March 10, 2008 against M/s. Yellow Saphire Investment Ltd. now amalgamated with M/s. Merrygold Investment Limited in the matter of M/s. Jaiprakash Industries Limited, disposing of the proceeding under Section 11(4) and 11B of the SEBI Act 1992 initiated against the entity.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
