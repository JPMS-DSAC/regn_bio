  PRESS RELEASE
PR No.18/2008

 
Certificate of Registration of M/s. Blue Chip Capital Market Pvt. Ltd.,
Sub Broker, cancelled
 

Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated January 17, 2008, cancelling the certificate of registration of M/s. Bluechip Capital Market Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INS230408739), sub-broker affiliated to  M/s. BPS Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member, National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
