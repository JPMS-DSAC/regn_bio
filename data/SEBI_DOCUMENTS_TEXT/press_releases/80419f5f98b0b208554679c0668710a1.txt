PR No.76/2008

 
M/s. SKSE Securities Ltd., Member of NSE & BSE, censure

Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated February 26, 2008, imposing a penalty of ‘censure’ upon M/s. SKSE Securities Ltd. (SEBI Reg. Nos. INB231076036 and INB011076032) member of the National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. and Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. respectively.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
