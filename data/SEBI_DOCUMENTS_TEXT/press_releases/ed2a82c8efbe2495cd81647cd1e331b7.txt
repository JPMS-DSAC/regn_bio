PR No.20/2009
Orders against Shri Sanjiv Pravinchandra Shah and Classic Consultancy 
in the matter of Monalisa Infotech Ltd. 
Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed orders dated January 09, 2009, in the matter of Monalisa Infotech Ltd. restraining 1) Shri Sanjiv Pravinchandra Shah and 2) Classic Consultancy from accessing the securities market and prohibit them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities in any manner whatsoever, whether directly or indirectly for a period of six months.
These orders come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the orders are available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
