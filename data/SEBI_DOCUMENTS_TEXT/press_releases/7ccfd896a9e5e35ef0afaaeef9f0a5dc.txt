
 
Feb 13, 2015

PR No.:  
35/2015


Shri S. Raman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has prima-facie found that Suvidha Farming and Allied Limited (the company) having its registered office at House No. 176, Near Habibganj, Behind Railway Track, Narayan Nagar, Hushangabad Road, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh has been illegally raising money from public and accordingly passed an interim order dated February 12, 2015Â against the company and its directors, Shri Vinod Kumar Shankhwar, Shri Rajendra Karn Rajpoot, Shri Pradeshi Ram, Shri Jagdish Bisvas and Shri Rajneesh Dutta under sections 11(1), 11 (4) and 11B of SEBI Act, 1992 read with Regulation 65 of SEBI (Collective Investment Scheme) Regulations, 1999.
The order directs the company and its directors not to collect any fresh moneys from investors from its existing scheme or not to launch any new scheme/plan or float any new companies to raise fresh moneys; not to dispose of any of the properties or alienate the assets including land obtained directly or indirectly through the money raised by the company; not to divert any funds raised from public at large, kept in bank accounts and or in the custody of the company; to furnish all the information sought by SEBI, within 15 days of the receipt of the Order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
