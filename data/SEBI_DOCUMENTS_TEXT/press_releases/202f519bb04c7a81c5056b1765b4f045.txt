
 
Feb 18, 2016

PR No.:  
26/2016


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order on February 04, 2016 in the matter of M/s GCA Marketing P Ltd., its promoters and Directors.
The Registered Office of the Company is situated at F-77, Civil Lines, Bhatinda, Punjab. The order directed the company GCA Marketing Private Limited and its Directors Sh Amardeep Singh Cheema  & Sh Gurdeep Singh to abstain from collecting any money from investors or launch or carry out Collective Investment Schemes; to wind up existing Collective Investment Schemes and refund the investors through ‘Bank Demand Draft’ or ‘Pay Order’ within a period of 3 months from the date of the Order and thereafter within a period of fifteen days, submit a winding up and repayment report to SEBI; not to dispose of or alienate or sell off any of the properties/assets obtained directly or indirectly through money raised by GCA except for purpose of making refunds to investors; to provide full inventory of their assets and properties and details of bank accounts, demat accounts and holdings of shares/ securities. The orders further restrains GCA Marketing Private Limited and its Directors Sh Amardeep Singh Cheema  & Sh Gurdeep Singh from accessing securities markets and prohibits them from buying, selling and dealing in securities for a period of 4 years
The Company was engaged in fund mobilizing activity from public by floating/sponsoring/ launching Collective Investment Scheme (CIS) as defined in section 11AA of the SEBI Act, 1992.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
