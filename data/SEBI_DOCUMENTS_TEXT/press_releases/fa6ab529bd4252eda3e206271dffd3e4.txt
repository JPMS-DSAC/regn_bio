PRESS RELEASE
PR No.274/2006

M/s. Kattikaran & Co., censured

Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  December 04, 2006, imposing a penalty of censure upon M/s. Kattikaran & Co. (SEBI Reg. No.INS231013512), a sub-broker affiliated to M/s. Cochin Stock Brokers Ltd., member of National Stock Exchange. 
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
December 07, 2006
