PR No.45/2011
Order in respect of Triumph International Finance India Limited 
in the matter of SSI Limited
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  March 17, 2011 in the matter of SSI Limited disposing of the enquiry proceedings against Triumph International Finance India Limited without passing any further directions since their certificate of Registration has already been cancelled vide order dated May 16, 2002.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
March 22, 2011
