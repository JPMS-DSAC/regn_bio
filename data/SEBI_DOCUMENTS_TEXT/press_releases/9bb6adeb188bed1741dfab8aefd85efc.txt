
 
May 15, 2012

PR No.:  
58/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed two orders dated  May 10, 2012 in the matter of Stocknet International Ltd., suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Arihant Stocks & Shares and M/s. Shree Hari Hira Stock Broking (P) Ltd. for a period of two weeks.
 
These orders shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the orders.
 
The full text of the orders is available on the SEBI website: www.sebi.gov.in
