PRESS RELEASE
PR No.146/2006

  
Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
Salguti Plastics Ltd. 
 
An Order dated May 19, 2006 was passed by Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI granting exemption from applicability of Regulation 11(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, subject to certain conditions as mentioned therein, in respect of the proposed acquisition of 25,00,000 equity shares of the face value of Rs. 10 each of Salguti Plastics Ltd. @ Rs.16 per share.
The proposed acquisition is through preferential allotment to the promoter group and pursuant to the said acquisition, there would not be any change in control of the target company.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
