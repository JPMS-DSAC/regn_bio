Order in the matter of M/s. Emerging Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member, NSE

Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated December 06, 2006, directing that no penalty be levied upon M/s. Emerging Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member, National Stock Exchange in the matter of M/s. Hovell’s India Ltd. 
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
