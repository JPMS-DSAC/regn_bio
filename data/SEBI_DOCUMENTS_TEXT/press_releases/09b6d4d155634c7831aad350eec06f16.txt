 PRESS RELEASE



PR No.332/2004

Registration of  Ballabh Das Daga,
Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange, suspended
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has suspended the certificate of registration of Ballabh Das Daga, Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange for a period of one month.  The order was passed on December 13, 2004. This order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one (21) days from the date of this order.
The penalty is imposed for irregularities by the member while dealing in the scrip of M/s. M P Investment & Consultancy Services Ltd.
The full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
December 20, 2004
