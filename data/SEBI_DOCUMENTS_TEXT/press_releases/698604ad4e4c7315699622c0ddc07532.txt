
 
Jul 21, 2011

PR No.:  
116/2011


Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated   July 19, 2011 regarding violation of regulation 11(2) of Takeover Regulations  by the acquirers – Mr. S.R.B. Ramesh Chandra and others wherein following directions were issued to the acquirers u/s 11, 11(4) and 11B of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with Regulations 44 & 45 of Takeover Regulations –
To divest 41,96,790 shares constituting 4.9% of equity share capital of Bheema Cements Ltd within a period of two months from the date of the Order and transfer the profits, if any, arising out of such disinvestment, to the Investor Protection and Education Fund established under Regulation 3 of the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Investor Protection and Education Fund) Regulations, 2009.
 
Appointment of merchant banker by the acquirers for the purpose of carrying out such disinvestment and the merchant banker would file a report to the SEBI detailing the compliance of the above direction within two weeks from the date of such compliance.
 
To initiate Adjudication proceedings against the aforesaid acquirers in respect of their contravention of Regulation 11(2) of the Takeover Regulations  by acquiring 41,96,790 shares without making public announcement.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
