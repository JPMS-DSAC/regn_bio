Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
Pasupati Acrylon Limited
 
An Order dated December 30, 2005 was passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI granting exemption from regulation 11 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, subject to certain conditions as mentioned therein, in respect of the proposed preferential allotment of 1,40,00,000 equity shares of the target company to promoter group entities viz. Prabhat Capital Services Limited (acquirer) and the persons acting in concert (PACs) with it viz. Shubh Exim Ltd., Sind Wave Finance Services, Gurukripa Finvest P Ltd. and Inder Overseas P Ltd.  Pursuant to the proposed preferential allotment, the shareholding of promoter group would increase from 47.43% (which includes 7.84% equity shares held by PICUP which is a State level Financial Institution) to 56.93%.
 
The proposed allotment is in pursuance of Corporate Debt Restructuring (CDR) mechanism and there would not be any change in composition of the Board of Directors or control of the target company pursuant to the aforesaid allotment. 
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirer from making an open offer. 
 
The full text of the Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
