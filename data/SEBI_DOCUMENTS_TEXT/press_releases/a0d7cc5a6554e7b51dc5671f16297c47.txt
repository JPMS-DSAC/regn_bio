 PRESS RELEASE



PR No.259/2004

Registration of three brokers, suspended
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has suspended the certificates of Registration of M/s. CFL Securities Ltd., and M/s. Pawankumar Parmeshwarlal for one month each and M/s. Tropical Securities and Investment Pvt. Ltd., for two months for their involvement in market manipulation in the scrip of M/s. Surya Roshni Ltd. The orders have been passed on September 29, 2004, and shall come into force with effect on expiry of three weeks from the date of the orders. 
The full text of the above orders are available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
October 01, 2004
