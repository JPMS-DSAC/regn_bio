PRESS RELEASE
PR No. 249/2004
  

Order against Shri Ashok K.Sardana, Member of
Bhubaneshwar Stock Exchange


The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) vide its order dated October 29, 2002 had restrained Shri Ashok Kumar Sardana (SEBI Regn. No. INB170265714), member of Bhubaneshwar Stock Exchange from accessing the securities market and prohibited him from buying, selling or dealing in securities, till further orders. Subsequently, enquiry proceedings were initiated against him.
Pursuant to completion of the enquiry proceedings, SEBI has passed an order dated September 22, 2004 by which the proceedings against Shri Ashok K. Sardana under the enquiry regulations stands terminated and that the prohibition imposed upon Shri Sardana vide orders dated October 29, 2002 and January 16, 2004 have been vacated.
 

The full text of the order is available on our website www.sebi.gov.in
