PR No.110/2010
Order in the matter of Alka Securities Limited
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated April 29, 2010, prohibiting Alka Securities Limited (Member, National Stock Exchange of India Limited) from buying, selling, dealing in securities on behalf of its clients, in any manner, whatsoever, either directly or indirectly, until further orders.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
