Order against Shri Vishal J. Shah, Director, M/s. Western Projects Ltd.
in the matter of M/s. Western Projects Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI vide Order dated February 23, 2007 has directed that the proceedings against Shri Vishal J. Shah in the matter of  M/s. Western Projects Ltd. be dropped.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
