PR No.275/2007

 
M/s. Shilpa Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd., Member of BSE, censured
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 05, 2007, against M/s. Shilpa Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd., Member of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Sun Infoways Ltd., imposing a minor penalty of ‘Censure’ on the broker.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
 
 
 Mumbai
 October 08, 2007
