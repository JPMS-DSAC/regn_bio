SUBJECT : OFFER DOCUMENTS RECEIVED AND OBSERVATIONS ISSUED BY SEBI DURING THE PERIOD 16/02/2004 TO 22/02/2004
ISSUED BY : CORPORATION FINANCE DEPARTMENT –
DIVISION OF ISSUES AND LISTING
 
During the period 16/02/2004 to 22/02/2004, two offer documents were received by SEBI. During the same period, SEBI had issued four observations on offer documents filed with it. List I gives details of offer documents received and List II gives details of offer documents on which observations were issued.
 
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in ) under "Primary Market".
