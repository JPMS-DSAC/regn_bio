PRESS RELEASE
PR No.318/2007

Order in the matter of M/s. Vijay Textiles Limited 
 
Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 11, 2007 restraining Shri Nimesh Chitalia, Ms. Ajodhyabai Kabra, Shri Sanjay Soni, Ms. Anita Soni, Shri Jayesh C. Shah and Durga Drug Agency (Proprietor: Ms. Krupaben Soni) from buying, selling and dealing in securities for a period of six months in the matter of M/s. Vijay Textiles Limited.
 
The order comes into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
December 17, 2007
