 PRESS RELEASE

CORPORATION FINANCE DEPARTMENT
DIVISION OF ISSUES & LISTING 
Review of SEBI (Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOP) and 
Employee Stock Purchase Scheme (ESPS )) Guidelines 1999
SEBI (Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOP) & Employees Share Purchase Scheme (ESPS). Guidelines 1999 (herein after referred to as the Guidelines) came into effect from June 19, 1999. Amendments to ESOP Guidelines were carried out vide Circular no. SEBI/PMD/MBD/ESOP/2/2003/30/6 dated 30th June 2003. It is proposed to amend the SEBI (ESOP & ESPS) Guidelines 1999 in order to elaborate on certain specific aspects as well as to streamline existing requirements and to correct a few anomalies in the present Guidelines. 
In order to invite comments/suggestions/views of the public and others concerned in this regard, proposed amendments have been put on SEBI’s website (www.sebi.gov.in) under the heading "Review of SEBI (SEBI (Employee Stock Option Scheme (ESOP) & Employees Share Purchase Scheme (ESPS). Guidelines 1999) Guidelines – Proposals in the sub-section "Reports for Public Comments" in section "Reports/Documents"on December 26, 2003. Comments on the same may be sent to Ms. Neelam Bhardwaj, DGM / Ms.Usha Rangarajan, Manager, Corporation Finance Department, Division of Issues & Listing, SEBI or emailed to neelamb@sebi.gov.in / ushar@sebi.gov.in or faxed to 91-22-22045633 on or before January 9, 2004.
