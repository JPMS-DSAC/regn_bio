Scheme for the introduction of Exchange Traded Currency Futures


The final Report of the RBI-SEBI Standing Technical Committee on Exchange Traded Currency Futures is placed on the SEBI website (www.sebi.gov.in).  The Report details the scheme for the introduction of Exchange Traded Currency Futures, jointly agreed upon by Reserve Bank of India (RBI) and Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI).
