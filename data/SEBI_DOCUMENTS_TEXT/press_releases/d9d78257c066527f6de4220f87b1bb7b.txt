Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
Khartikatia Tea and Industries Limited 
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated January 31, 2006 granting exemption to the acquirers, Shri Jitendra Kumar Thirani, alongwith persons acting in concert with him from requirement of making a public announcement as required under regulation 10 read with regulations 14,15 and 16 of the Takeover Regulations and submission of the draft letter of offer to SEBI as required under regulation 18 of Takeover Regulations, in respect of the proposed acquisition of 8,27,300 (74.85%) shares of the Khartikatia Tea & Industries Ltd. from Vanguard Eng. Ind. Pvt. Ltd. Shri Ashok Kedia, Smt. Suman Kedia, Ballia Textiles Pvt. Ltd. and Rohit Kedia subject to the conditions stipulated therein. 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirer from making an open offer. 
There are only 113 shareholders in public category and their shareholding in the target company is only 0.62% of the equity share capital of the target company. The acquirers shall make individual offers by registered post acknowledgment due to each of the public shareholders of the target company by directly addressing offer letters offering to buy the shares held by such shareholders at the price to be determined in accordance with the provisions of regulation 20(4) and regulation 20(5) of the Takeover Regulations or the negotiated price of  Rs. 27/- per share, whichever is higher. 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
