
 
Apr 03, 2013

PR No.:  
33/2013


Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated March 25, 2013 cancelling the certificate of registration of M/s. Shri Parshwa Finance, Proprietor, Shri Piyush Jhaveri a sub-broker affiliated to the stock broker, Jyotish Bhogilal Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd., a member of the Ahmedabad Stock Exchange Limited.
The Order shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the Order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
