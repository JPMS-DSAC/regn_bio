PRESS RELEASE
PR No.91/2005

Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
Bhoruka Financial Services Ltd. 
An Order dated June 29, 2005 was issued by Whole Time Member, SEBI granting exemption to the acquirer viz. DLF Commercial Developers Ltd. (acquirer) from making a public announcement in the newspapers, filing of draft letter of offer with SEBI as required under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in respect of open offer to be made under Regulations 10 and 12 of these Regulations pursuant to the proposed acquisition of shares and control of Bhoruka Financial Services Ltd. (BFSL). The exemption is subject to certain conditions including, passing of a special resolution by the shareholders of the target company approving the change in control.
The acquirer proposes to acquire 1,98,850 (98.73%) shares of the target company from the promoters and their relatives @ Rs. 2400/- per equity share.  By virtue of the said acquisition the acquirer would acquire the majority shares/voting rights and the control over the target company.  The acquirer proposes to make an offer to remaining 26 public shareholders of target company, by writing individual letters to each public shareholder and had sought exemption from obligations of making a public announcement, submission of draft letter of offer to SEBI, appointment of merchant banker etc.  as specified under the Regulations.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirer from making an open offer subject to certain conditions.
The full text of the Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
