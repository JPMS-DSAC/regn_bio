PRESS RELEASE
PR No.108/2008


Order against  M/s. Shri Parshwa Finance
in the matter of M/s. Ojas Technochem Products Limited


Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  May 16, 2008 against M/s. Shri Parshwa Finance in the matter of M/s. Ojas Technochem Products Limited warning them to be careful and cautious in future while dealing with the clients. They have also been directed to note that any instances of violation or non compliance of the SEBI Act and Rules and Regulations in future shall be dealt with stringently.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
