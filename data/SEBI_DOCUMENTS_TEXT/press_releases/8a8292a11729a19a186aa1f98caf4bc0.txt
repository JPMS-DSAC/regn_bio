PR No.24/2007 

 
Order against M/s. Salasar Stock Broking Limited in the matter of 
M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Limited
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated January 19, 2007 against M/s. Salasar Stock Broking Limited, broker of Calcutta Stock Exchange Association Ltd. (CSE), National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. (NSE) and the Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Limited, disposing off the show cause notice issued against the broker without imposing any penalty.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
