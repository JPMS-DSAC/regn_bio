PRESS RELEASE
July 22, 2004 PR No.166/2004
 
Amendments to SEBI (Employee Stock Option Scheme and 
Employee Stock Purchase Scheme) Guidelines, 1999
 
SEBI, vide its circular no. SEBI/CFD/DIL/ESOP/3/2004/22/7 dated July 22, 2004, issued to all registered Merchant Bankers and all recognised Stock Exchanges, has amended SEBI (Employee Stock Option Scheme and Employee Stock Purchase Scheme) Guidelines, 1999.
The aforesaid circular is available on the SEBI website at www.sebi.gov.in in the ‘Press Releases’ section under the section ‘News/ Publications’. The said Guidelines as amended vide the aforesaid circular, are also available on the SEBI website in the ‘Guidelines’ section under the section ‘‘Legal Frame Work’.
All listed companies, merchant bankers and stock exchanges are directed to ensure compliance with the said Guidelines. 
Mumbai
July 22, 2004
