
 
Aug 13, 2012

PR No.:  
75/2012


Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated August 10, 2012, in the matter of M/s. International Hometex Ltd. with the following directions that M/s International Hometex Ltd. and its directors Mr. Vineet Kumar Agarwal, Mr. Vinod Kumar Agarwal  and Mr. Vishwinder Nath Gupta :
i.  are restrained from accessing the securities market; and
 
ii. are also prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities market, directly or indirectly, in whatsoever manner,
till all the investor grievances pending against the Company are resolved by them.
The aforesaid directions shall be without prejudice to any other action that may be taken by SEBI against the aforesaid entities in accordance with law.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
