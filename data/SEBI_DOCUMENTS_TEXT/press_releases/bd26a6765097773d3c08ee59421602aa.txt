PR No.271/2007


Order against M/s. Nokia Finance International Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Nokia Finance International
 
 
Shri V.K Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 04, 2007 against M/s. Nokia Finance International Pvt. Ltd., member, Ahmedabad Stock Exchange (ASE) in the matter of M/s. Nokia Finance International, disposing off the Enquiry proceedings against the broker without imposing any penalty or direction.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
