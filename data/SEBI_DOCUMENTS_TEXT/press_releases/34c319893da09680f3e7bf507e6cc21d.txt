PR No.159/2010
Order against Ahilya Commercial Pvt.  Ltd., member, CSE in the matter of 
M/s. Bakra Pratisthan Ltd.
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated July 08, 2010 imposing a penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd., member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd., for a period of fifteen days in respect of its dealings in the shares of M/s. Bakra Pratisthan Ltd.
 
As the certificate of registration of the broker is suspended vide order dated July 08, 2010, for a period of three weeks in respect of its dealings in the shares of Offshore Finvest Ltd. during the period January 2004 – February 2005, the penalty ordered herein would run concurrently with the said penalty.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
