PR No.209/2007


Order against M/s. Pawankumar Parmeshwarlal, Member of BSE, 
 in the matter of M/s. Prabhu Steel Industries Ltd.
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated June 27, 2007 suspending the Certificate of Registration of M/s. Pawankumar Parmeshwarlal, Member of  Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), for a period of two months in the matter of M/s. Prabhu Steel Industries Ltd.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
  
Mumbai
