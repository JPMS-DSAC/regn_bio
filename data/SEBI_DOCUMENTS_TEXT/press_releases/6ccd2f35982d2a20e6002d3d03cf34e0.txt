PR No.297/2009
Order against Shri Vasant H. Bissa in the matter of Somplast Leather Industries Ltd.
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated September 15, 2009, against Shri Vasant H. Bissa in the matter of Somplast Leather Industries Ltd. Shri Vasant H. Bissa has been restrained from accessing the securities market and also prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities in any manner and in any capacity, for a period of six months.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
