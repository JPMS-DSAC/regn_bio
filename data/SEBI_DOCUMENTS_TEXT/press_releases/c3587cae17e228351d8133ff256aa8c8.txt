
 
Feb 21, 2012

PR No.:  
21/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 17, 2012 in the matter of Cupid Ltd., Garnet Constructions Ltd. and Ez-Comm Trade Technologies Ltd. restraining Dr. Mukesh Vora, Shri Yogesh Sakharam Ambekar, M/s. Creative World Telefilms Ltd., M/s. Glider Holdings Ltd. and Shri Ramesh D Agrawal from buying, selling or dealing in securities market whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of one year. 
 
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
