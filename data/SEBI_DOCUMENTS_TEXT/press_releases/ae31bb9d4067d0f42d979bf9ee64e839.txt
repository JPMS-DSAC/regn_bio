PR No.283/2007

 
Order in the matter of Design Auto Systems Ltd.
 
Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 22, 2007 against Coverage & Consultants Ltd. and its directors, viz. Shri Sharad Gujrathi, Shri B L Joshi and Shri Narendra Chouhan in the matter of Design Auto Systems Ltd.
 
These four entities have been found to have violated the provisions of Regulations 3 and 4 of the PFUTP Regulations. The entities have, thus, been restrained from accessing the securities market and also prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities either directly or indirectly for a period of seven years.
 
The full text of the order is available on website: www.sebi.gov.in
