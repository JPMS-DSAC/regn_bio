Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 21, 2014, in compliance with the directions of the Hon'ble Securities Appellate Tribunal made vide its order dated September 03, 2013 read with Order dated June 8, 2012, disposing off the representation dated January 16, 2012 filed by Mr. Amit Bhagvatprasad Barot in the matter of Global Offshore Services Limited. 
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
