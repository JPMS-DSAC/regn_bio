 PRESS RELEASE




January 22, 2004 PR No.17/2004

SEBI WARNS

 
 
 
During the last few days the stock market has witnessed high volatility. It is gathered that baseless rumors were being floated in the market. It is hereby warned that any persons/entities found to be spreading such rumors will be severely dealt with. The investors are advised not to be misled by such rumors.
