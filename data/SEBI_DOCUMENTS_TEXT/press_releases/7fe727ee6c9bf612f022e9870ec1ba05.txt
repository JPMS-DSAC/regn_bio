PRESS RELEASE
PR No.172/2007

Order against M/s. D. K. Khandelwal & Company in the matter of
M/s. Prime Capital Markets Limited
 
 
An order has been passed by Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI on May 17, 2007 against M/s. D. K. Khandelwal & Company,  member of Calcutta Stock Exchange Association Limited (CSE) in respect of the irregular transactions in the shares of M/s. Prime Capital Markets Limited imposing a major penalty of suspension of certificate of registration for a period of four months.
 
This order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of 21 days from the date of this order.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
