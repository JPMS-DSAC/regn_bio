PRESS RELEASE
PR-179/2005
 
Warning issued to M/s. Kanodia Stock Broking (Pvt.) Ltd., Member of CSE
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated December 27, 2005, warning M/s. Kanodia Stock Broking (Pvt.) Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No.INB030878338), Member of Calcutta Stock Exchange.
 
The order has been passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI, against M/s. Kanodia Stock Broking (Pvt.) Ltd. for not maintaining client registration forms containing details of the bank accounts, PAN number, introducer etc., failed to make payments to the clients within the statutory period of 48 hours etc.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
December 30, 2005
