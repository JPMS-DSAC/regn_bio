PR No.52/2011
Order in respect of Adolf Pinto in the matter of G G Automotive Gears Limited
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  March 31, 2011, in the matter of G G Automotive Gears Ltd., suspending the certificate of registration of the stock broker Adolf Pinto for a period of one month. 
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
Mumbai
April 05, 2011
