PR No.30/2010
Interim Order in the matter of S J Corporation Ltd. 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  February 05, 2010, directing 16 entities including Mr. Savjibhai D. Patel and Ms. Usha S Patel, the promoters of S J Corporation Ltd., restraining them from buying, selling or dealing in the shares of S J Corporation Limited, either directly or indirectly, in any manner, till further directions in this regard.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
