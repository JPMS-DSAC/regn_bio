PRESS RELEASE
PR No.274/2008


Order against Shri Ashish Kaushikbhai Shah in the matter of 
Somani Cement Company Limited


Shri M.S.Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 24, 2008 in the matter of Somani Cement Company Limited  restraining  Shri Ashish Kaushikbhai Shah (PAN No. ASCPS1113J) from buying, selling and dealing in securities in whatsoever manner or accessing the securities market directly or indirectly  in any manner for a period of five years.
 
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
