PRESS RELEASE
PR No.45/2008


Order against E-star Infotech Ltd., Shri Mahesh Thanvi, Shri Aga Rasool Mirrkhushal, Shri B. R. Sharma, Shri Vijay B. Sheth, Shri M.M. Thitte and Arvind Thanvi 


Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 13, 2008 against E-star Infotech Ltd., Shri Mahesh Thanvi, Shri Aga Rasool Mirrkhushal, Shri B. R. Sharma, Shri Vijay B. Sheth, Shri M.M. Thitte and Shri Arvind Thanvi, in the matter of E.star Infotech Ltd., directing them to restrain from buying, selling and dealing or accessing the securities market in any manner for a period of two years.
 
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
