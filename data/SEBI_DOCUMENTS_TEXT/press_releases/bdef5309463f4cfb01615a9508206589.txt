Order in the matter of M/s. Times Securities Limited
 
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 08, 2006 in the matter of M/s. Times Securities Ltd. vacating the prohibitions imposed on it vide orders October 29, 2002 and January 16, 2004.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
