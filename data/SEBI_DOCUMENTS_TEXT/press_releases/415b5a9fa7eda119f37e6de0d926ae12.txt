PR No.168/2006

Order in the matter of Design Auto Systems Ltd.
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order deciding preliminary objections to its jurisdiction as raised by Design Auto Systems Ltd. (DASL). DASL has been directed to comply with the show cause notice dated June 30, 2005 within 15 days from the date of the Order.
DASL challenged SEBI’s jurisdiction with regards to: issuing directions u/s 11B and 11(4)(b) to an unregistered entity; issuing directions to the directors of an entity; issuing show cause notice; authority of an individual Member of SEBI; etc. before the Hon’ble High Court of Madhya Pradesh, Indore Bench. The Hon’ble Court disposed of the writ petition with the direction that before proceeding further, SEBI shall take into consideration the preliminary objection regarding jurisdiction as raised by DASL and shall pass a reasoned Order after giving an opportunity of hearing to DASL.   
 The order has been passed by Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, on June 22, 2006.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
