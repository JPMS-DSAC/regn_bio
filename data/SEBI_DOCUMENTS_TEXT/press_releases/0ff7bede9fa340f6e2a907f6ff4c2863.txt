
 
Jun 16, 2014

PR No.:  
60/2014


Shri. Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated June 12, 2014, in compliance with the Order dated May 07, 2012 [made in Interlocutory Application  (I.A.) No. 2 in Petition for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No. 14740 of 2011 - Bhushan Energy Limited vs. Orissa Sponge Iron and Steel Limited and others] of the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India, concluding that the conversion of 35,00,000 warrants into equity shares in favour of Bhushan Energy Limited could not result in the transfer of management in favour of Bhushan Energy Limited, in the target company, i.e., Orissa Sponge Iron and Steel Limited.   
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
