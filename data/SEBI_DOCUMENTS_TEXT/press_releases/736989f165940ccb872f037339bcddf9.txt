PR No.139/2010
Interim Order in the matter of front-running of HDFC AMC 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed directions under Sections 11(1), 11(4) and 11B of SEBI Act, 1992 vide interim ex-parte order dated June 17, 2010, prohibiting Mr. Nilesh Kapadia of HDFC AMC, and Rajiv Ramniklal Sanghvi, Rajiv Ramniklal Sanghvi (HUF), Mr. Chandrakant P. Mehta and Mrs. Dipti Paras Mehta from buying, selling or dealing in securities market, directly or indirectly, till further directions. Further, specific directions have been issued to HDFC AMC.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
