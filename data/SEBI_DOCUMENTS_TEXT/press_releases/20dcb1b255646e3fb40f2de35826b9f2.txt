PRESS RELEASE
PR No.220/2006

Order against M/s. Indavara Securities in the matter of 
M/s. Home Trade Ltd. 
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) vide order dated  September 25, 2006 has imposed a minor penalty of censure on the certificate of registration of M/s. Indavara Securities, SEBI Reg. No INB 080800718, member of Bangalore Stock Exchange (BgSE) in the matter of M/s. Home Trade Ltd.   The penalty is imposed on the broker for not complying with the norms and guidelines relating to Know Your Client norms. 
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
