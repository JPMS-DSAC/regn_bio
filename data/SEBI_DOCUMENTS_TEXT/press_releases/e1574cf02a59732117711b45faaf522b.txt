PR No.270/2009
Order in the matter of Austral Coke and Projects Ltd.
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an ad interim ex-parte order dated September 01, 2009, prohibiting Austral Coke and Projects Ltd. from raising any further capital in any manner, directly or indirectly, whatsoever till further orders.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
