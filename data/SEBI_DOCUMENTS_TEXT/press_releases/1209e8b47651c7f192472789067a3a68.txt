
 
Nov 22, 2012

PR No.:  
99/2012


Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 8, 2012 against M/s Knack Corporate Service Private Limited (Registrar to an Issue and Share Transfer Agent) in the matter of Right Issue of Ram Kaashyap Investment Limited, suspending the certificate of registration granted to M/s Knack Corporate Service Private Limited as a Registrar to an Issue and Share Transfer Agent for a period of three months. 
 
The order shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order. 
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
