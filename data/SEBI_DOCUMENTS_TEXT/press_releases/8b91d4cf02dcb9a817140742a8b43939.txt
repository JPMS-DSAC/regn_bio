PR No.143/2010
Order against M/s. Rakesh Kapoor & Co., in the matter of 
Karishma Floriculture Ltd.
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  June 22, 2010, in the matter of Karishma Floriculture Ltd., cautioning M/s. Rakesh Kapoor & Co., Member, Delhi Stock Exchange Ltd., for the violations committed and advising the stock broker to be careful while dealing in the securities market.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
