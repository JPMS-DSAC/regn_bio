PR No.181 /2007
SEBI constitutes High Powered Advisory Committee on consent orders and compounding of offences
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has constituted a high powered advisory committee on consent orders and compounding of offences.
Justice Hosbet Suresh, former Judge, High Court of Bombay has consented to be the Chairman of the Committee. The other members of the Committee are: Shri M. Balachandran, Ex- Chairman and Managing Director, Bank of India and Shri Ketan Dalal, Executive Director, Pricewaterhouse Coopers.
The Committee will have its first meeting in the second week of June 2007.
It may be recalled that SEBI had decided to introduce consent orders and compounding of offences for all matters which are pending regulatory action or pending before Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT) / Courts. The FAQs on “Consent orders and Compounding of offences” and the Circular dated 20th April, 2007 are available on the website:www.sebi.gov.in
