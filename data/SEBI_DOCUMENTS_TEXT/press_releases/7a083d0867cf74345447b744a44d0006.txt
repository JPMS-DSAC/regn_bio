PR No.:  
16/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed two orders dated January 25, 2012 in the matter of Cyberspace Limited restraining Chandresh Mehta and Harshad M Shah from buying, selling or dealing in securities and from associating in securities market in any manner whatsoever for a period of one year.
 
These orders have come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
