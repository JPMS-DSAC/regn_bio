PR No.212/2007

 
Order against M/s. G.R.Pandya Share Broking Ltd. in the matter of
M/s. Prabhu Steel Industries Ltd.
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  July 02, 2007 suspending the Certificate of Registration of M/s. G.R.Pandya Share Broking Ltd., Member of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), for a period of two months, in the matter of M/s. Prabhu Steel Industries Ltd.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in 
Mumbai
