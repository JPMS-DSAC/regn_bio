Shri Gyan Bhushan took charge as Executive Director, Securities and Exchange Board of India in Mumbai.  Prior to this assignment, he was Chief General Manager, SEBI.   
Shri Bhushan  is a member of the Indian Economic Service and holds an MBA degree with distinction from Maastricht School of Management, The Netherlands and  a post graduate degree in economics from the Delhi School of  Economics, Delhi.
He has worked in various capacities in Ministry of Finance and Planning Commission, Government of India.  
He earlier worked in the Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority (PFRDA) and was in the core team which was instrumental in setting up the defined contribution based pension system in India for civil servants and unorganized sector workers.
