 OFFER DOCUMENTS RECEIVED / WITHDRAWN AND OBSERVATIONS ISSUED BY SEBI DURING LAST WEEK

  
During the period 18th October, 2004 to 24th October, 2004, SEBI had not received any offer documents and had not issued any observations on offer documents filed with it. During the same period, no offer document was withdrawn.
