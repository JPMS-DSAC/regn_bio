PR No.:

SEBI proposes to have a standardized Term Sheet for Securitization transactions and also  rationalize, clarify and enhance various aspects such as eligibility criteria, roles and responsibilities etc. associated to Securitization Trustees.
In view of that a concept paper is placed on SEBI website for Public Comments.
The full text of the Paper is available on the SEBI website at: www.sebi.gov.in
Comments are invited on the Concept Paper latest by August 28, 2014.
