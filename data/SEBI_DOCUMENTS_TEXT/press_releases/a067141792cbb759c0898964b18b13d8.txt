PR No.264/2009
Consent Order on the application submitted by Shri Gourav Banthia 
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri M.S. Sahoo and  Dr. K.M. Abraham, has passed a consent order dated August 21, 2009 on the consent application submitted by Shri Gourav Banthia for violating the provisions of Regulation 4(2)(a),4(2)(b) and 4(2)(e) of SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices Relating to Securities Market) Regulations, 2003. For settling the matter, the applicant, without admitting or denying guilt, has remitted a sum of  Rs. Rs.50,000/- (Rupees Fifty Thousand only)  as well as undertaken voluntary debarment from buying, selling or dealing in the securities market in any manner whatsoever, whether directly or indirectly, for a period of one month from   August 31, 2009. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
