PR No.231/2009
Consent Order in the matter of M/s. Joy Reality Limited
A panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri M.S.Sahoo and  Dr. K.M. Abraham, has passed a consent order dated July 28, 2009 on the consent application submitted by M/s. Joy Reality Limited for non-compliance with Regulation 6(2), 6(4), 7(3) and 8(3) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997. 
The applicant without admitting or denying guilt has remitted a sum of Rs.3,25,000/-(Rupees Three Lakh Twenty Five Thousand only) towards settlement charges, in the matter. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
