PRESS RELEASE
September 10, 2004 PR No.221/2004
 
Registration of Indus Venture Capital Fund – 1, 
Orissa Venture Capital Fund and 
TVS Venture Fund Trust, cancelled
 
The following Venture Capital Funds had informed SEBI that they are Closing / Winding up their Funds.

Indus Venture Capital Fund – 1, Mumbai 
Orissa Venture Capital Fund, New-Delhi 
TVS Venture Fund Trust, Chennai 

Accordingly, SEBI has cancelled their certificates of registration. These entities, therefore, cease to exist as SEBI registered Venture Capital Funds.
 
Mumbai
September 10, 2004
