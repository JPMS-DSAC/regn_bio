PRESS RELEASE
December 03, 2003 PR No.325/2003
 
Suspension of certificate of registration of M/s Rama Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member of CSE
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has suspended the certificate of registration of M/s Rama Securities Pvt. Ltd., SEBI Reg. No. INB 031017730, member of CSE for a period of six months. The penalty is imposed for their indulgence in off-market transactions and fund based activities. 
 
M/s Rama Securities Pvt. Ltd., member of CSE will not be entitled to carry on the business as a Stock Broker with effect from January 6, 2004 till July 5, 2004. The full text of the Order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
