Order against (1) Shri Mahesh Parish, (2) Shri Shalin A.Shah, (3) Shri Ashok C.Shah and (4) Ms. Shivani Ashok Shah, Directors of M/s. Lesha Steels Ltd.

Lesha Steels Ltd.
