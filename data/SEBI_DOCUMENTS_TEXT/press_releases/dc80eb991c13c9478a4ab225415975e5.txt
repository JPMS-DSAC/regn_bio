  PR No.35/2011
Orders in respect of M/s. Pals Overseas Pvt. Ltd. and M/s. Amgis Holdings Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Sunearth Ceramics Limited
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed two orders dated February 24, 2011, in the matter of M/s. Sunearth Ceramics Limited, suspending the certificate of registration of stock brokers M/s. Pals Overseas Pvt. Ltd., member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. and M/s.  Amgis Holdings Pvt. Ltd., member, National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. for a period of two months.
The orders shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the orders.
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
March 01, 2011
