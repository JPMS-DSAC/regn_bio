
 
Mar 11, 2014

PR No.:  
23/2014


Shri Prashan Saran, Whole Time Member (WTM), SEBI vide three separate orders dated March 11, 2014 has disposed of three Show Cause Notices dated June 14, 2007 issued to Bajaj Capital Limited, Marwahs Fine Art Dealers and RR Investors Capital Services Private Limited.
 
In the Show Cause Notices issued to these entities it was alleged that they had violated provision of Section 12 read with Sections 11 and 11AA of SEBI Act and provisions of SEBI (Collective Investment Scheme) Regulations, 1999. 
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
