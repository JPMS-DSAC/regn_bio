PRESS RELEASE
PR No.17/2008 

 
M/s. Premier Shares Ltd., Member, NSE, censured

Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated January 17, 2008, imposing a minor penalty of ‘censure’ on the stock broker viz.  M/s. Premier Shares Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INB230885730), Member, National Stock Exchange of India Ltd.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
