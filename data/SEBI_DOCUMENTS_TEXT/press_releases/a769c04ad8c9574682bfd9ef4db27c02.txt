Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 27, 2009, cancelling the certificate of registration of M/s. Sure Capital Private Limited, Member, Pune Stock Exchange Limited. The order shall come into effect on expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
