PR No.10/2009
Order against KB Capital Markets Private Limited, Portfolio Manager
Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 31, 2008, against KB Capital Markets Private Limited, Portfolio Manager (Registration No. INP 000001041) warning the entity to be more cautious in the conduct of its business and to adhere to and comply with all statutory provisions / instructions received in the discharge of its function as a Portfolio Manager without any fail.
The full text of the order is available on website: www.sebi.gov.in
