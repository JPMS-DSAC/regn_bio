
 
Jan 20, 2012

PR No.:  
6/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated January 13, 2012 suspending the certificate of registration of the stock broker, Khandwala Securities Limited for a period of one month.
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
