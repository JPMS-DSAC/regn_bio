Order against M/s. Sanjay Khemani in the matter of 
M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
Â 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 29, 2007, suspending the certificate of Registration of M/s. Sanjay Khemani, member of Calcutta Stock Exchange, in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd., for a period of fifteen days.
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
