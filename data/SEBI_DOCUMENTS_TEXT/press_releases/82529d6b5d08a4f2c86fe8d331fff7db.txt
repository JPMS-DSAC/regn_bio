SEBI issues warning to five stock brokers 

 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed orders   dated December 30, 2005, warning Bipin Ratilal Vora (SEBI Regn. No. INB010004416), member of the Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd., Mumbai, M/s. Tower Capital and Securities Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Regn. No. INB230599736), M/s. Salasar Stock Broking Ltd. (SEBI Regn. No. INB230752635), M/s. Twenty First Century Shares & Securities Ltd. (SEBI Regn. No. INB230690931) and M/s. Lohia Securities Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INB230777836), all four members of National Stock Exchange.  
 
These orders have been passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI, against Bipin Ratilal Vora, M/s. Tower Capital and Securities Pvt. Ltd., M/s. Salasar Stock Broking Ltd., M/s. Twenty First Century Shares & Securities Ltd. and M/s. Lohia Securities Pvt. Ltd. for non segregation of client funds from own funds, delay in payment of funds and delivery of securities, certain irregularities in the issuance of contract notes, some procedural deficiencies in client registration forms, few instances of trade transfers etc.
 
The full texts of these orders are available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
