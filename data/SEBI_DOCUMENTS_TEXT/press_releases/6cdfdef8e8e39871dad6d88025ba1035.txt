PRESS RELEASE
PR No.219/2007

Certificate of registration of M/s. Abhilashaa Securities Pvt. Ltd., suspended for one month
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated July 17, 2007, against  M/s. Abhilashaa Securities Pvt. Ltd., sub-broker of                    M/s. Angel Broking Ltd. (earlier sub broker of M/s. Kantilal Mangaldas Pvt. Ltd.), in the matter of M/s. MOH Ltd. imposing a minor penalty of suspension of certificate of registration (SEBI Registration no. INS011209934)  for a period of one month.
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.      
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 Mumbai
