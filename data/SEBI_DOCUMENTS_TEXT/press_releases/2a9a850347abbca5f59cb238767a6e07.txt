
 
Oct 20, 2011

PR No.:  
139/2011


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 18, 2011 granting exemption to the acquirers namely Mrs. Jayshree Mohta, M/s. Jay Shree Tea & Industries Limited, M/s. Essel Mining & Industries Limited, UCO Bank A/c-Birla Education Trust, Mr. Kumar Mangalam Birla, Mr. Prakash Kumar Mohta, Mr. Sakate Khaitan, M/s. Jayashree Finvest Private Limited and             M/s. Bhiragacha Finance Company Private Limited from complying with the provisions of Regulation 11(1) of Securities and Exchange Board of India (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 [Takeover Regulations] in respect of their proposed increase in voting rights from 46.93% to 52.13% in the target company consequent upon the proposed buy-back up to 8,75,000 equity shares by the target company.
The application seeking exemption from the provisions of the Takeover Regulations was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Takeover Panel recommended the grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
