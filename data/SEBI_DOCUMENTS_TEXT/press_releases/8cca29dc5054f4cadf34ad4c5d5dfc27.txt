PRESS RELEASE
December 19, 2003 PR No.316/2003

Suspension of certificate of registration of M/s Sanjeev Phumbhra, Member of CSE

 The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has suspended the certificate of registration of M/s Sanjeev Phumbhra, SEBI Reg. No. INB 031066310, member of CSE for a period of six months. The penalty is imposed for their indulgence in off-market transactions and fund based activities.
 
M/s Sanjeev Phumbhra, member of CSE will not be entitled to carry on the business as a Stock Broker with effect from January 5, 2004 till July 4, 2004.
