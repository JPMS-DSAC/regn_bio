Registration of six stock brokers of UPSE, cancelled
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed order dated            January 14, 2005 cancelling the certificate of registration of the following six stock brokers with immediate effect:

The aforesaid six stock brokers were declared defaulters by Uttar Pradesh Stock Exchange (UPSE) and ceased to be member of the Exchange, thereby failed to fulfill the pre-requisite condition of registration as stock broker with SEBI. The registration has been cancelled in terms of Sub-Regulation (5) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002.
