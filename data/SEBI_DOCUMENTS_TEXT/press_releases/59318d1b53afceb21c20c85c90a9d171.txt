
 
Apr 11, 2012

PR No.:  
44/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated April 04, 2012 prohibiting M/s. Promact plastics Limited from buying selling or dealing in securities in any manner, or accessing the securities market directly or indirectly in any manner whatsoever for a period of one year from the date of the order.
 
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
