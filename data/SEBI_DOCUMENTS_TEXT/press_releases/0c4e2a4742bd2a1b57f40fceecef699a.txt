 PRESS RELEASE




January 27, 2004 PR No.21/2004
DIVISION OF CORPORATE RESTRUCTURING 

In the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of Greaves Limited, English Indian Clays Limited, Shree Sitaram Sugar Company Limited, Deoria Sugar Mills Limited And New Savan Sugar & Gur Refining Company Limited

 
An application was made by Shri Brijmohan Thapar, to the Securities and Exchange Board of India (hereinafter referred to as "SEBI") on behalf of DBH International Pvt. Ltd, Karun Carpets Private Ltd, Solaris Chem Tech Ltd, Solaris Holdings Ltd (hereinafter referred to as "the acquirers") seeking exemption from complying with the provisions of regulations 10, 11 and 12 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 (hereinafter referred to as "the said Regulations") in respect of proposed acquisition of shares and voting rights of Greaves Ltd, English Indian Clays Ltd, Deoria Sugar Mills Ltd, Shree Sitaram Sugar Company Ltd and New Savan Sugar & Gur Refining Company Ltd (hereinafter referred to as "the target companies").
 
This application was referred to the Takeover Panel. The Takeover Panel recommended grant of exemption as sought.
 
SEBI in terms of Regulation 4(6) of the Regulations has passed an order dated January 22, 2004, granting exemption, to the Acquirer from complying with the provisions Chapter III of the Regulations with regard to the proposed acquisition of equity shares and voting rights of the target companies.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the SEBI website at www.sebi.gov.in.
