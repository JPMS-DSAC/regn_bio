PR No.211/2009
Consent Order in the matter of M/s. English India Clays Limited
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri M.S. Sahoo and Dr. K.M. Abraham has passed a consent order dated  June 26, 2009 on the consent application submitted by Mr. Karan Thapar,  for delay in submitting the  information required under Regulation 3(3) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers), Regulations, 1997. The applicant without admitting or denying guilt has remitted a sum of Rs.35,000/- (Rupees thirty five thousands only) towards settlement charges in the matter.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
