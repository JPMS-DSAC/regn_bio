PR No.63/2010
Orders under Regulation 33A & 33B of the SEBI (Intermediaries)
Regulations, 2008
 
Shri M.S.Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed two orders dated  March 08, 2010 suspending the certificates of registration of 17 and 56 Stock Brokers respectively of various Stock Exchanges till the time outstanding registration fees are fully paid.
 
Consequently, the certificates of registration of these stock brokers operating as sub-brokers of subsidiaries of Stock Exchanges in terms of Para 2 of the Circular No.SMD-ll/POLICY/CIR-37/99 dated November 26,  1999 shall also remain suspended accordingly.
 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
