PRESS RELEASE
PR No.109/2006

Order in the matter of acquisition of shares of  M/s. Jayaswals Neco Ltd. 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated February 07, 2006, in the matter of vesting of voting rights with M/s. Corporate Ispat Alloys Ltd. (acquirer) and the persons acting in concert (PACs) in M/s. Jayaswals Neco Ltd. The voting rights were acquired on 11.08.2000.
 
Taking into account all the mitigating facts and circumstances, it was held that the acquirer and PACs would not be required to make the public announcement in accordance with the provisions of Chapter III of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
