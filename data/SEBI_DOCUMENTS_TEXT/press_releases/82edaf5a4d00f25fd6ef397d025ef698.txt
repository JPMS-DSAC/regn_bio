Union Finance Minister addresses Board
members and senior officials of SEBI Shri Arun
Jaitley, Hon'ble Union Finance Minister interacted with the members of the SEBI
Board and senior officials of SEBI at New Delhi today.  Shri Shiv Pratap Shukla, Minister of State
for Finance, Shri Subhash Chandra Garg, Secretary, Department of Economic
Affairs, Shri Ajay Bhushan Pandey, Revenue Secretary, Shri Atanu Chakraborty, Secretary,
Department of Investment and Public Asset Management, Dr. Krishnamurthy Subramanian,
Chief Economic Adviser, Shri Anand Mohan Bajaj, Jt. Secretary, Department of
Economic Affairs and Shri K.V.R. Murty, Jt. Secretary, Ministry of Corporate
Affairs also attended the meeting. Shri Ajay Tyagi, Chairman, SEBI welcomed
the Hon'ble Union Finance Minister and apprised him of the recent developments
in the Indian securities market.  Hon'ble
Finance Minister appreciated the initiatives taken by SEBI.
Besides Shri
Ajay Tyagi, Chairman, SEBI, the meeting was attended by Whole Time Members of
SEBI Board namely Shri G Mahalingam, Ms. Madhabi Puri Buch, Shri S K Mohanty
and Shri Ananta Barua.  Executive
Directors and other senior officials of SEBI were also present.
