PRESS RELEASE
PR No.29/2007

M/s. M & M Capital and Investments Pvt. Ltd.,  censured
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  January 22, 2007, imposing a penalty of censure upon M/s. M & M Capital and Investments Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INS231199030), sub-broker affiliated to Cochin Stock Brokers Ltd.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
