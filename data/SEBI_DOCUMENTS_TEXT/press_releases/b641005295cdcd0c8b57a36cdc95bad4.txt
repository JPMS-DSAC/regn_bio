Counter Proposals Received by the Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee to the Proposal Submitted by PACL Ltd. In pursuance of the invitation for counter proposals in response to the proposal submitted by PACL Ltd. and notified by the Justice (Retd.) R.M.
