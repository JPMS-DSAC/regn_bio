PR No.137/2010
Order against M/s. Interface Brokerage and Research Limited, Member, ASE  
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  June 14, 2010 warning the stock broker M/s. Interface Brokerage and Research Limited, Member, Ahmedabad Stock Exchange Limited (ASE) for the deviations observed during the inspection. The stock broker has been further directed to ensure compliance with all rules, regulations and other laws that govern its dealings in the securities market.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
