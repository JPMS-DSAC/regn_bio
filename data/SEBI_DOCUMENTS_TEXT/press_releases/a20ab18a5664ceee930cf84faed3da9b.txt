PR No.335/2009
Order against 227 stock brokers in the matter of summary proceedings
Shri M.S.Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 29, 2009 suspending the certificate of registration of 227 Stock-Brokers of various Stock Exchanges for a period of six months or till such time the outstanding registration fees is fully paid, whichever is later.
Consequently, the certificates of registration of these stock brokers as sub-brokers of subsidiaries of Stock Exchanges also shall remain suspended accordingly.
This order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
