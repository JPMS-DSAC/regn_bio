Consent Order against M/s. Loko Securities Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of 
M/s. Prabhu Steel Industries Ltd.


A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, Dr. T.C.Nair and Shri M.S. Sahoo, has passed a consent order dated October 20, 2008, on an application submitted by M/s. Loko Securities Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Prabhu Steel Industries Ltd. in accordance with SEBI Circular on consent orders dated April 20, 2007. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.6,00,000/- (Rupees six lakh only) towards the terms of consent in the matter.
