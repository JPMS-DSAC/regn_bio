PR No.224/2010
Order in respect of HB Securities Limited in the matter of 
Alps Industries Limited
 
Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  September 28, 2010, in the matter of Alps Industries Ltd., disposing of enquiry proceedings against HB Securities Limited without any penalty.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
October 08, 2010
