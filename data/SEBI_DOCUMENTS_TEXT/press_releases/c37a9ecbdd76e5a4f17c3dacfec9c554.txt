                                                                                                                        PR. No. 87/2007

  
Design Auto Systems Ltd. and its Directors barred from capital market for seven years      
 
 
Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI,  has passed an order dated March 6, 2007 against Design Auto Systems Ltd. and its Directors namely, Shri Sarvesh Garg, Smt. Rita Garg, Shri Prasant Rajagarhia and Shri Rajendra Tare, directing them not to access the capital market, or buy, sell or deal in securities in any manner directly or indirectly for a period of seven years from date of this order.
 
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
