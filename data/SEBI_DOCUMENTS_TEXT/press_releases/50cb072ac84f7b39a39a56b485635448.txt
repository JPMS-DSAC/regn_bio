PRESS RELEASE
PR No.208/2006

Shri Dilip Shah, Member of UPSE, censured
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated September 06, 2006, imposing a penalty of censure upon Shri Dilip Shah (SEBI Reg. No. INB101088715) member of the Uttar Pradesh Stock Exchange Association Ltd. (UPSE).
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
September 14, 2006
