PRESS RELEASE
PR No.206/2006
M/s. Kanudia Capital & Management Services Pvt. Ltd.,
Member of UPSE, censured
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI  has passed an order dated    August 30, 2006, imposing a penalty of censure upon M/s. Kanudia Capital and Management Services Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INB100933132) member of the Uttar Pradesh Stock Exchange Association Ltd. (UPSE).
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
September 14, 2006
