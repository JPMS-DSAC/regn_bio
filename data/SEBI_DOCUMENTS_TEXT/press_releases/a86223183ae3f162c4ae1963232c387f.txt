PRESS RELEASE
PR No.250/2007


Order against M/s. Ventura Securities Ltd. in the matter of
M/s. SMR Universal Softech Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated      August 29, 2007, against M/s. Ventura Securities Ltd. Member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), in the matter of M/s. SMR Universal Softech Ltd. imposing a minor penalty of ‘censure’ on the broker (SEBI Registration  No.INB 010760438).
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
