Shri P.K Mishra takes charge as Executive Director, SEBI
Shri Pradipta Kumar Mishra has assumed charge as Executive Director, Securities and Exchange Board of India, Mumbai on March 15, 2004.
Prior to this assignment, Shri Mishra was Chief General Manager, Department of Banking Supervision in Reserve Bank of India.
A post graduate in Political Science, Shri Mishra has handled various important assignments in Reserve Bank of India, during his career of more than 27 years. He has been Chief General Manager in the Department of Banking Supervision, Regional Director for North Eastern States and Chief General Manager, Guwahati, General Manager in the Exchange Control Department and various positions in the Department of Banking Operations and Development, Issue department etc. Shri Mishra has attended various senior executive level training programmes in India and abroad.
