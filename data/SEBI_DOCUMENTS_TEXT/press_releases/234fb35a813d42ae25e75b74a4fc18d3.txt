PR No.91/2006

 
Order against Consortex Karl Doelitzsch (I) Ltd. and Shri V.P. Rao 
Consortex Karl Doelitzsch (I) Ltd. has been restrained from accessing the securities market and Shri V P Rao has been restrained from buying, selling or dealing in securities in any manner for a period of one year for violations of the provisions of SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade practices Relating to Securities Market), Regulations, 1995 and SEBI (Disclosure & Investor Protection) Guidelines, 1992. The order has been on February 07, 2006 and shall come into force with immediate effect. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
