PRESS RELEASE
PR No.249/2007


Order against M/s. Harvic Management Services India Limited 
in the matter of M/s. Numero Uno Projects Limited
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated August 23, 2007, against M/s. Harvic Management Services India Limited, sub-broker of M/s. B. M. Gandhi Securities Ltd., Member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), in the matter of M/s. Numero Uno Projects Limited imposing penalty of suspension of certificate of registration (SEBI Registration no. INS 010496938) for a period of two months.
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.      
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
August  29,  2007
