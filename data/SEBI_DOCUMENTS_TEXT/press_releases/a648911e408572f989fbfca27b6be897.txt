Registration of M/s. Vinod Kumar & Co., Member of UPSE, suspended
 
Shri V. K. Chopra,  Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 6, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Vinod Kumar & Co. (SEBI Reg. No. INB100694710), member of the Uttar Pradesh Stock Exchange Association Ltd. (UPSE) for a period of fifteen days with effect from  September 27, 2006. 
 
M/s. Vinod Kumar & Co.  will not be entitled to carry on the business as a broker with effect from September 27, 2006 to October 11, 2006.
 
 The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
