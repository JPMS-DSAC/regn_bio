July 27, 2004 PR No.174/2004
 
Offer documents received / withdrawn and observations issued by SEBI from July 19, 2004 to 25 July, 2004  

 

During the period July 19, 2004 to July 24, 2004, one fresh offer document was received by SEBI. SEBI had issued observations on one offer document filed with it. List I gives details of offer document filed during the period and List II gives details of observations issued during the period. No offer documents were closed / withdrawn during the period.  
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in) under "Primary Market".
