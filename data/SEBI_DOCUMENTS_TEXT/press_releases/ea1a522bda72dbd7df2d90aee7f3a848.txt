PR No.59/2008
Consent Order on the application submitted by M/s. Phulchand Sons Investments Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Granules India Ltd.
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, Shri G. Anantharaman and                 Dr. T. C. Nair has passed consent order dated February 15, 2008, on an application submitted by M/s. Phulchand Sons Investments Pvt. Ltd., in the matter of Granules India Ltd., in accordance with SEBI Guidelines dated               April 20, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.50,000/- (Rupees fifty thousand only) and has also consented not buy, sell or deal directly or indirectly in securities for a period of six months from the date of the order towards the terms of consent in the matter.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website : www.sebi.gov.in
