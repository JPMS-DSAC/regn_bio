PRESS RELEASE
PR No.7/2005

Directions to M/s. Classic Credit Limited and M/s. Panther Fincap and Management Services Ltd., to make public announcement to acquire shares of DSQ Industries Ltd.
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) vide order dated January 3, 2005, in the matter of DSQ Industries Ltd., has directed M/s. Classic Credit Ltd. and M/s. Panther Fincap & Management Services Ltd. to make public announcement as required under Chapter III of the Takeover Regulations to acquire shares of DSQ Industries (now known as “Jardine Overseas Ltd.”)..
The full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
