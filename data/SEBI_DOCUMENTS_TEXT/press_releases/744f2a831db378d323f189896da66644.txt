Order against M/s. Networth Stock Broking Ltd., Member, BSE in the matter of M/s. Spanco Telesystems and Solutions Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated     February 22, 2007 directing that a penalty of “Censure” be imposed on        M/s. Networth Stock Broking Ltd. (SEBI Regn. No.INB 010638634), Member of the Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) in the matter of M/s. Spanco Telesystems and Solutions Ltd.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
