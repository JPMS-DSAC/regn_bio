PRESS RELEASE
PR No.209/2006

M/s. HSE Securities Ltd., Member of NSE, censured
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated September 12, 2006, imposing a penalty of censure upon M/s. HSE Securities Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INB231103038), member of National Stock Exchange (NSE).
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in

September 15, 2006
