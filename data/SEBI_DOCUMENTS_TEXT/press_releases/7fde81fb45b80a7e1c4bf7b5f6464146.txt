PRESS RELEASE
PR No.228/2007

Certificate of registration of M/s. Amgis Holdings Pvt. Ltd., suspended
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide Order dated July 31, 2007 in the case of M/s. Roofit Industries Ltd. has directed that Certificate of registration of M/s. Amgis Holdings Pvt. Ltd., Member of National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. (NSE), be suspended for a period of two months for their irregular dealings in the scrip of M/s. Roofit Industries Ltd.
The order shall come into force after the expiry of three weeks from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
