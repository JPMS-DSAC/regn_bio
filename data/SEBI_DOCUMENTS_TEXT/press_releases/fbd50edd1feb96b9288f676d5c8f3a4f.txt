PRESS RELEASE
PR No.42/2007

Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of M/s. Victory Paper and Boards (India) Ltd., (target company)
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI  has passed an Order dated February 02, 2007 granting exemption from applicability of Chapter III of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of the proposed acquisition of 40,00,000 equity Shares of the target company by M/s. Victory Press Pvt. Ltd. (acquirer). The acquirer is part of the promoter group of the target company. Pursuant to the proposed acquisition through preferential allotment, the shareholding of acquirer would increase from 8.29% to 36.37% and the shareholding of the promoter group would increase from 54.29% to 68.28%. Pursuant to the said acquisition, there would not be any change in control of the target company.

 

 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.


The full text of the Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
