PR No.136/2007

 
Grant of exemption to Shri Vikram B.Sanghani and others pursuant to Buy Back of Shares by M/s. Ace Software Exports Limtied (Target Company)
 
 
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an Order dated April 05, 2007, granting exemption to Shri Vikram B.Sanghani and others from making an open offer in terms of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, with regard to increase in their voting rights from 52.40% to 57.35% consequent to the proposed buy back of shares by M/s. Ace Software Exports Limtied.
 
Pursuant to the said increase in voting rights, there would not be any change in control of the target company.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the above Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
