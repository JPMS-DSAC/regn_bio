PR  No. 62/ 2007
 
Prohibition on opening of fresh demat accounts by Centurion Bank of Punjab Ltd.,  lifted
 
 
Dr. T C Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has vide his post hearing order passed on February 21, 2007 in the matter of Initial Public Offering (IPO) Investigations directed that, pending enquiry, the prohibition on opening fresh demat accounts by the Centurion Bank of Punjab Ltd., a Depository Participant, be lifted. The order further states that all issues and contentions are left open to be decided upon completion of  enquiry proceedings being  conducted by the Enquiry Officer.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the above Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
