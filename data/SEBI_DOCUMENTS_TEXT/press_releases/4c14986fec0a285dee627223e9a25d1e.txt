PR No.315/2009
Order in the matter of M/s. Jashnani Leasing and Finance Limited, Sub-Broker
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated October 12, 2009 canceling the certificate of registration of the sub-broker M/s. Jashnani Leasing and Finance Limited, affiliated to Networth Stock Broking Limited, Member of Bombay Stock Exchange Limited.
The order shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
October 14, 2009
