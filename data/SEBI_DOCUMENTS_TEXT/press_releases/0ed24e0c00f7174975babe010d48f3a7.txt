PR No.118/2010
Order in the matter of Karuna Cables Limited
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated May 10, 2010, in the matter of Karuna Cables Limited, restraining Mr. Kenneth Pinto from buying, selling or dealing in securities market whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of one year from the date of the order.
 
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
