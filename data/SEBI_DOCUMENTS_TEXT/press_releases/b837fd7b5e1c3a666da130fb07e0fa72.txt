PR No.226/2007

Order in the matter of M/s. Oasis Media Matrix Limited
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order dated July 31, 2007 in the matter of M/s. Oasis Media Matrix Limited restraining eighteen (18) entities viz. M/s. Oasis Media Matrix Limited (company),  Shri Vasant C Shah, Shri Chanderkant Mehta and Shri Kamlesh Shah (Directors), Shri Jatin Shah, Ms. Varshaben Shah, Shri Manubhai Shah, Shri Asif Chippa, Shri Bankesh Bhavsar, Shri Nirav Pandya, Shri Rupesh Hasmukhlal Shah, Shri Navinbhai Shah, Ms. Yogini Patel, M/s. Shree Gayatri Shares & Services Private Limited, Shri Amit Bhavsar, Shri Rajesh Kantilal Shah, Shri Ramesh K. Jain and Shri Deepak D. Vyas from buying, selling and dealing or accessing the securities market in any manner for a period of two years.
The order comes into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
