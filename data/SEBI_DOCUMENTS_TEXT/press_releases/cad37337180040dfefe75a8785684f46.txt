PR No.18/2010
Grant of exemption for the proposed acquisition of equity shares of
M/s. Nitin Spinners Limited 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated    January 25, 2010, granting exemption to M/s. Redial Trading & Investments Private Limited and others (acquirers) from complying with the provisions of Regulation 11(1) and (2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, with regard to their proposed acquisition of 50,00,000 shares of M/s. Nitin Spinners Limited (Target Company) through preferential allotment, in terms of the CDR Scheme. The proposed preferential allotment would increase the shareholding of the acquirers from 54.99% to 59.90% of the paid up capital of the Target Company.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
