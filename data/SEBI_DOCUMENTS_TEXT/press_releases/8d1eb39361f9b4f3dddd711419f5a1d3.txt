PR No.78/2007

Order against M/s. Vishal J. Shah & Co., Member, CSE
in the matter of M/s. Western Projects Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI vide Order dated February 23, 2007 imposed a penalty of “Censure” on M/s. Vishal J Shah & Co., Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange (SEBI Regn. No. INB 031122618) in the matter of M/s. Western Projects Ltd.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
