PRESS RELEASE
PR No.126/2007

Order against M/s. Lexus Shares & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd., 
Member of CSE
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 26, 2007, against M/s. Lexus Shares & Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd., Member of Calcutta Stock Exchange Association Ltd. (CSE) in respect of the irregular transactions in the shares of M/s. Patriot Trading Company Ltd. imposing a major penalty of suspension of certificate of registration for a period of six months.
The order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
