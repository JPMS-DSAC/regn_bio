
 
Jan 27, 2012

PR No.:  
13/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 18, 2012, in the matter of T. Spiritual World Limited prohibiting Adolf Pinto, member, Bombay Stock Exchange from taking up new clients for a period of one month.
The order shall come into force on expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
