
 
Oct 10, 2012

PR No.:  
91/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 5, 2012, granting exemption to M/s. Grey Cast Foundry Works (Acquirer) from the applicability of regulation 3(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011, for the proposed acquisition of 15% shares of the Target Company resulting in an increase in shareholding of the promoter group (after excluding the transferor) from 42.04% to 57.04% in the Target Company.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel also recommended granting of exemption.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
