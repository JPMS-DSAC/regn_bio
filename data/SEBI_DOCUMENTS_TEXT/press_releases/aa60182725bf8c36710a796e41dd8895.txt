PR No.312 /2003December 17, 2003
SUSPENSION OF CERTIFICATE OF REGISTRATION OF M/S. NAM SECURITIES LTD., MEMBER OF DELHI STOCK EXCHANGE
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has suspended the certificate of registration of M/s. NAM Securities Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INB050771030) member of the Delhi Stock Exchange for a period of fifteen (15) days with effect from December 26, 2003. The penalty is imposed for Irregularities in issuance of Contract Notes, dealing with unregistered sub-broker etc.M/s. NAM Securities Ltd. member of the Delhi Stock Exchange will not be entitled to carry on the business as a Stock Broker with effect from December 26, 2003 till January 09, 2004. A full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
