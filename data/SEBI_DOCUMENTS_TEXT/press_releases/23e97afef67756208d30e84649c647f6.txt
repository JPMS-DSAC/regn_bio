PRESS RELEASE
PR No.241/2006

Order against M/s. Binani Cements Limited
 
Dr.T.C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI vide his order dated November 2, 2006 passed in the matter of M/s. Munga Holdings Ltd. has directed M/s. Binani Cements Limited to be careful in ensuring timely compliance of the listing requirements and other ancillary filing requirements. This order comes into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
