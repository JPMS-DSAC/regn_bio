
 
May 02, 2016

PR No.:  
85/2016


Pursuant to the transfer of schemes of Deutsche Mutual Fund to DHFL Pramerica Mutual Fund and at the request of Deutsche Mutual Fund, Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), vide its letter dated May 2, 2016, has cancelled the certificate of registration of Deutsche Mutual Fund and has withdrawn the approval granted to Deutsche Asset Management (I) Private Limited, to act as the Asset Management Company to the Mutual Fund.
Consequently, with immediate effect, the Deutsche Mutual Fund, Deutsche Trustee Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. and Deutsche Asset Management (I) Private Limited cannot carry out any activity as a Mutual Fund, Trustee Company and Asset Management Company respectively.
