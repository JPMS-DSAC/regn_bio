PR No.256/2010
Order against Century Fiscal Services Limited, member, LSE 
 
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated November 26, 2010 disposing of the enquiry proceeding initiated against the stock broker, Century Fiscal Services Limited (member, Ludhiana Stock Exchange Limited) in the matter of S G Capital Services Limited, with a warning directing it to exercise adequate caution in functioning as a stock broker.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
Mumbai
November 29, 2010
