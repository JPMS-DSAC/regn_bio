                                                                                                    PRESS RELEASE 
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
 PR-288/2004
               ‘Cease and Desist’ Order passed against M/s. Janata Sahakari Bank Limited, Depository Participant (DP), NSDL.

 

Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed a cease and desist order on October 15, 2004 against M/s. JANATA SAHAKARI BANK LIMITED, (Depository Participant), National Securities Depository Ltd. (NSDL), having registration number IN-DP-NSDL-26-97.

 

The order has been passed against M/s. JANATA SAHAKARI BANK LIMITED for violations, deficiencies and irregularities observed in its operations as a Depository Participant. The order shall come into force with immediate effect.

 

A full text of the order is available on our website www.sebi.gov.in. 
 

 

Mumbai
