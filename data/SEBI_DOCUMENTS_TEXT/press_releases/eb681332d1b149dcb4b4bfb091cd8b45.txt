
 
Jun 04, 2009

PR No.:  
181/2009










Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated June 03, 2009 with respect to non compliance of SEBI Circular No. SEBI/IMD/CIR No.12/118340/08 dated February 26, 2008 on ‘Standard warning in Advertisements by Mutual Funds’ by Reliance Mutual Fund and Reliance Capital Asset Management Limited in connection with their New Fund Offer of ‘Reliance Infrastructure Fund’.
Reliance Mutual Fund and Reliance Capital Asset Management Limited have been directed to forthwith withdraw the advertisement in its current form and re-issue the same, if it is so desired, strictly in compliance with the provisions of the said SEBI circular.
Further, Reliance Mutual Fund and Reliance Capital Asset Management Limited have been asked to show cause as to why they should not be restrained from launching any new scheme for an appropriate period.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
