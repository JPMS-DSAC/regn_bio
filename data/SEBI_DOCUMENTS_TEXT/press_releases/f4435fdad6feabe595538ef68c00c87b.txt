PR No.90/2007

Order against the broker M/s. S.P. Rakhecha & Co. (Shanti Prasad Rakhecha) in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  March 06, 2007 against M/s. S.P. Rakhecha & Co. (Shanti Prasad Rakhecha), Member of Calcutta Stock Exchange in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. imposing a penalty of Censure on the broker.
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
