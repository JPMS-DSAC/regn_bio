PRESS RELEASE
PR No.84/2006

M/s. Vinay Capital Pvt. Ltd. debarred from securities market for two months
 
M/s.Vinay Capital Pvt. Ltd. has been debarred from accessing the securities market for a period of two months for violations of the provisions of SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade practices Relating to Securities Market), Regulations, 1995. 
The order has been passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI on February 01, 2006 and shall come into force with immediate effect. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
February 06, 2006
