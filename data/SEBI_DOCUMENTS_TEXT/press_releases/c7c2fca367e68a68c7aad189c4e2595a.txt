Direction issued against M/s. Viniyog and/or Viniyog Portfolio Management Services, Kolkata and its proprietor Shri Bhaskar Kumar Mukherjee
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) vide its order dated  February 01, 2006 has directed that M/s. Viniyog and/or Viniyog Portfolio Management Services, Kolkata and its proprietor Shri Bhaskar Kumar Mukherjee shall cease and desist from acting as an unregistered portfolio manager and shall not buy, sell or deal in securities market in any manner, directly or indirectly, with immediate effect. 
Further, Viniyog and or Viniyog Portfolio Management Services and its proprietor Shri Bhaskar Kumar Mukherjee is also restrained from conducting any programme, including the “Share Bazar” programme, and issuing advertisements in any media, including TV, which are alluring and may be detrimental to the interest of the investors.   
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
