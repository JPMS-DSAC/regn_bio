                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Finance Minister addresses the Board of SEBI    Shri Arun Jaitley, Hon'ble Union Finance Minister, interacted with the members of the SEBI Board and senior officials of SEBI at New Delhi today. Shri P. Radhakrishnan, Minister of State for Finance, Dr. Hasmukh Adhia, Finance Secretary, Ministry of Finance, Shri Injeti Srinivas, Secretary, Ministry of Corporate Affairs and Shri Arvind Subramanian, Chief Economic Advisor also attended the meeting.Shri Ajay Tyagi, Chairman, SEBI welcomed the Hon’ble Union Finance Minister and apprised him of the market trends and the recent initiatives taken by SEBI. Implementation of the Budget announcements related to SEBI was also discussed.Shri Jaitley appreciated the initiatives taken by SEBI.Besides, Shri Ajay Tyagi, Chairman SEBI, the meeting was attended by members of SEBI Board namely, Shri Arun P. Sathe, Shri G. Mahalingam and Ms. Madhabi Puri Buch. Executive Directors and other senior officials of SEBI were also present.New Delhi
