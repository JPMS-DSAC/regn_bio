                PR No.308/2009
Grant of exemption for the proposed acquisition of shares in
M/s. Poddar Pigments Limited (Target Company)
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 30, 2009, granting exemption to Mr. S. S. Poddar, Mrs. Kusum Poddar, Ms. Rochna Poddar, Ms. Mahima Poddar, S. S. Poddar (HUF), Pluto Tradelinks Limited, G K S Holdings Limited and Trustline Capital Finance Limited (acquirers) from complying with the provisions of Regulations 11(1) and 11(2) of Securities and Exchange Board of India (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 [Takeover Regulations] with respect to their proposed increase in percentage shareholding and voting rights from 48.81% to 61.01%, consequent upon the proposed buy-back of equity shares by the target company.
The application seeking exemption from the provisions of the Takeover Regulations was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Takeover Panel recommended the grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
