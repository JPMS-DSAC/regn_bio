Suspension of certificate of registration of M/s S. P. Rakhecha & Co., Member of CSE

 

The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has suspended the certificate of registration of M/s S. P. Rakhecha & Co., SEBI Reg. No. INB 031032718, member of CSE for a period of six months. The penalty is imposed for their indulgence in off-market transactions and fund based activities. 
M/s S. P. Rakhecha & Co., member of CSE will not be entitled to carry on the business as a Stock Broker with effect from January 7, 2004 till July 6, 2004. The full text of the Order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
