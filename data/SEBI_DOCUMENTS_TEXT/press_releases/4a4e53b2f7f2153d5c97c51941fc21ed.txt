PR No.16/2010
Order against M/s. Nova Petrochemicals Limited in the matter of Nova Petrochemicals Limited 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 12, 2010 in the matter of Nova Petrochemicals Limited restraining M/s. Nova Petrochemicals Limited from buying, selling and dealing in the securities market or accessing the securities market directly or indirectly in any manner whatsoever for a period of two years.
The order comes into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
