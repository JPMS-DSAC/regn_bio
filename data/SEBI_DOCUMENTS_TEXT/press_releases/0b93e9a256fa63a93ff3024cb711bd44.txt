PR No.71/2009
Order against M/s. Eupharma Laboratories Ltd. and its directors in the matter of Eupharma Laboratories Ltd.
 
Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 13, 2009, in the matter of M/s. Eupharma Laboratories Ltd.:
 

debarring M/s. Eupharma Laboratories Ltd. and its directors namely, Shri Ramanlal V Shah, Shri Shrenik Shah, Shri Sudhir K. Agarwal and Shri Ashok Saxena from accessing the securities market, or buying, selling or dealing in securities in any manner whatsoever, directly or indirectly for a period of one year. 
debarring M/s. Mahavir Capital Services Pvt. Ltd. and M/s. Mahavir Securities Pvt. Ltd. and its director, Shri Rajesh Shah from accessing the securities market, or buying, selling or dealing in securities in any manner whatsoever, directly or indirectly for a period of one year. 
disposing of the proceedings without any directions against Shri Jayantilal M. Patel, director of Eupharma Laboratories Ltd. 

 
The above order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
