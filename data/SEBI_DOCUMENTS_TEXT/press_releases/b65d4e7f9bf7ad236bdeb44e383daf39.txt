
 
Dec 10, 2012

PR No.:  
104/2012


 
Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has vide order dated December 04, 2012 issued the following directions:-
 Restraining Innovision e-Commerce Ltd. and Mr. Akshay Pravin Sanghavi from accessing the securities market and also prohibiting them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing or associating with the securities market in any manner and in any capacity, for a period of two years from the date of the order.
  
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
