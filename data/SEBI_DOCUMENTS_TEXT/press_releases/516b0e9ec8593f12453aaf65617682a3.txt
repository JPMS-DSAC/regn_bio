PR No.258/2010
Consent Order on the application submitted by 
M/s. Vadilal Enterprises Limited 
 
A panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. K. M Abraham and Shri Prashant Saran has passed a consent order dated November 29, 2010 on the application submitted by M/s. Vadilal Enterprises Limited for non compliance of Regulation 6(2) and 6(4) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 for the year 1997 and Regulation 8(3) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 for the years 1996-97 to 2001-02. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.6,25,000/- (Rupees Six Lakh and twenty five thousand only) towards settlement charges, in the matter. 
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
Mumbai
December 01, 2010
