PRESS RELEASE
PR No.174/2005

Order against M/s. SSKI Securities Pvt. Ltd. 
M/s. SSKI Securities Pvt. Ltd. Broker, BSE Ltd. (SEBI Registration    No. INB 01622230) has been warned and advised to be more careful in future in its dealings in the securities market and diligently adhere to the provisions of the SEBI Act, 1992 and the regulations made thereunder.
The order has been passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI on December 27, 2005 for dealings by the broker in illiquid scrip of M/s. Malvica Engineering Ltd.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
December 30, 2005
