PR No.323/2008
Order against M/s. VRM Share Broking Pvt. Ltd., Member, NSE,  
in the matter of Soundcraft Industries Ltd.
Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 12, 2008, in the matter of Soundcraft Industries Ltd. suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. VRM Share Broking Pvt. Ltd., Member, National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. having SEBI Registration No. INB0 231109837 for a period of one month.
The order shall come into effect on expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
