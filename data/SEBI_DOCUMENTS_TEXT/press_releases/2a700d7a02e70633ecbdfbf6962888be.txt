PRESS RELEASE
PR No.170/2006

Registration of Shri Suresh Kumar Almal, Member, CSE, suspended
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated June 29, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of Shri Suresh Kumar Almal (SEBI Reg. No. INB030071816), member of the Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE) for a period of one (1) month with effect from July 20, 2006. 
 
Shri Suresh Kumar Almal (SEBI Reg. No. INB030071816), member of the Calcutta Stock Exchange will not be entitled to carry on the business as a broker with effect from July 20, 2006 to August 19, 2006.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
