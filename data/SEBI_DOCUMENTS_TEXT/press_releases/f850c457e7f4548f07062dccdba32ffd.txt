
 
Feb 28, 2014

PR No.:  
19/2014


Shri. Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 25, 2014, granting exemption under regulation 4(6) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 (since repealed) read with regulations 11(5) and 35 of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 to Square Investments & Financial Services Private Limited and its PACs from the applicability of regulation 3(2) and (3) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011, with respect to their proposed conversion of 40,00,000 fully paid-up 1% Cumulative Redeemable Preference Shares of ₹100/- each into equity shares with June 23, 2006 being the 'Relevant Date' for computation of price.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
