PR No.133/2010
Order in the matter of Atreyee Chakraborty
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated June 04, 2010, in the matter of Atreyee Chakraborty in compliance with the directions of the Hon’ble High Court at Calcutta dated March 12, 2010 made in Writ Petition no. 733 of 2007 in the matter of Atreyee Chakraborty vs. Securities and Exchange Board of India and others.
 
M/s G. M. Bosu & Co. Private Limited, the depository participant and Central Depository Services (India) Limited have been directed to show cause as to why SEBI should not direct them jointly and severally to compensate Mrs. Atreyee Chakraborty, the petitioner, in respect of 1500 shares of ITC Limited that would have been accrued to her in respect of the 100 shares of ITC Limited from the date of transfer till date.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in. 
Mumbai
