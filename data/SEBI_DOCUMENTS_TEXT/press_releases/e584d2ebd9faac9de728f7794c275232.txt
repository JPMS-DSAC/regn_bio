Orders against Murari Lal Goenka, P. K. Agarwal & Co. and Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd., in the matter of M/s. Coronet Industries Ltd.
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed three orders dated    April 28, 2009, disposing of the enquiry proceedings against the brokers M/s. Murari Lal Goenka, M/s. P. K. Agarwal & Co. and M/s. Ahilya Commercial Pvt. Ltd.  As the said brokers have already been prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities from November 30, 2005 to August 11, 2008, in accordance with interim order passed by SEBI, no further penalty has been imposed.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
