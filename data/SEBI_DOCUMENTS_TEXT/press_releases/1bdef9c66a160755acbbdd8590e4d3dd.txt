PRESS RELEASE

 
PR No.265/2008 
Re-allocation of debt investment limit
 


The enhanced debt limits in corporate debts category were allocated to entities on October 22, 2008.
After assessment of the utilization, the unutilized debt limits are being re-allocated to entities lower down in the list.
The list of entities and the allocated limits is placed in Annexure.
In terms of SEBI circular dated November 06, 2008, in case the allocated limits are not utilized by the entities in 11 working days, i.e. by December 04, 2008, the same shall be withdrawn and allocated to the entities lower down in the list.
