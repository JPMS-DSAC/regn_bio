SEBI FOUNDATION DAY LECTURE

To commemorate its establishment, the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has decided to organize the Foundation Day lecture series and the inaugural lecture on "The Changing Face of Financial Regulation" will be delivered by The Director, London School of Economics and former Chairman, Financial Services Authority (Unified Regulator), UK, Sir Howard Davies at Mumbai on Tuesday, March 23, 2004. The Governor of Maharashtra, H. E. Shri Mohammed Fazal, has kindly consented to be the Chief Guest.
 
The Securities and Exchange Board of India was established as a statutory body in April 1992 to regulate, supervise and develop the Indian Securities Market. SEBI has been assiduously making efforts to make the Indian Securities Market vibrant, transparent and investor friendly.
