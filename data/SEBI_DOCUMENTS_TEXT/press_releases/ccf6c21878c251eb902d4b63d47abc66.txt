Shri Radhakrishnan Nair takes charge as Executive Director, SEBI
 
Shri Radhakrishnan Nair has taken charge as Executive Director in Securities and Exchange Board of India on July 18, 2005. Shri Radhakrishnan Nair will be in charge of General Services Department, Research Department, Office of the Investor Assistance and Education, Integrated Surveillance Department and Vigilance.  Prior to joining SEBI he was General Manager, Corporation Bank at their Corporate Office in Mangalore, in charge of Recovery Management Division, Legal Service Division and Credit Risk Management Division. He was also a Director on the Board of Corp Bank Securities Ltd., Director of the Debt Market  Committee of The Stock Exchange, Mumbai (BSE) and Director of the Primary Dealers’ Association of India (PDAI).
 
He is a post graduate in Science and Law and holds a Post Graduate Diploma in Management. Shri R.K. Nair joined Corporation Bank as a Probationary Officer in August 1976. He has held various assignments in the Bank including Regional Manager, Kerala, Assistant General Manager in Mumbai and in the International Banking Division and Deputy General Manager, Investment and International Banking Division.
 
Shri R.K. Nair has also held the post of Managing Director of Corp Bank Securities Limited, Mumbai- a subsidiary of Corporation Bank. Shri R.K. Nair has attended training in different areas, both in India and abroad. Shri R.K. Nair is also a visiting faculty at various institutions like National Institute of Bank Management, Pune, National Insurance Academy, Pune, Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai, BSE Training Centre etc.
 
Widely travelled, Shri Nair has visited many countries including United States of America, Thailand, Singapore, Muscat, Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Sharjah. 
 
Mumbai,
July 19, 2005
