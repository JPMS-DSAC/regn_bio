Reforms of Mutual Funds Industry – Public comments invited

Asian Development Bank, on behalf of the Ministry of Finance, Government of India, had appointed a consultant to review the various aspects of the mutual fund industry in India. The consultants have submitted their report which is now available on our website at www.sebi.gov.in for public comments/views.
It may be noted that the Securities and Exchange Board of India neither approves nor disapproves the contents, accuracy or adequacy of the report.
The members of public are invited to offer their views on the report, particularly on the recommendations made therein. All correspondences / e-mails in this regard may carry in their subject line "Views on the report on the Mutual Funds Industry in India" and be directed to:
