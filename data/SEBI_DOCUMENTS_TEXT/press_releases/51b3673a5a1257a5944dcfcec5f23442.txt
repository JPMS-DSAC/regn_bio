PR No.70/2009
Order against M/s. Shri Parshwa Finance  in the matter of M/s. Sawaca Communications Ltd.
Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 13, 2009, in the matter of M/s. Sawaca Communications Ltd. directing that M/s. Shri Parshwa Finance be prohibited from associating with any corporate body from buying, selling or dealing in securities, directly or indirectly for a period of one year.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
