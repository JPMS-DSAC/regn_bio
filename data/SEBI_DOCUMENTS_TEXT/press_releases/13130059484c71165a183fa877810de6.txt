PRESS RELEASE



January 29, 2004 PR No.25/2004
Sub: Issue of Press Release: Order under section 11B of the SEBI Act, 1992 read with the SEBI (Collective Investment Schemes) Regulations, 1999 passed by Chairman, SEBI against
 

M/s. Avani Plantations Ltd., Thane (APL)
M/s. Ion Exchange Enviro Farms Ltd., Mumbai (IEFL)
M/s. La Mark Farms Ltd., Mumbai (LMFL) 

  


The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) vide its Orders dated January 22, 2004 has prohibited APL, IEFL and LMFL and their concerned officials from dealing in securities market and also from accessing the securities market for a period of five years from the date of the Orders.
APL, IEFL and LMFL have failed to comply with the earlier Orders of SEBI dated November 18, 2003 (passed against LMFL) and November 27, 2003 (passed against APL and IEFL) directing them to refund the money collected under the scheme(s) with returns due to the investors as per the terms of the offer within a period of one month from the date of the said Orders.
The text of the Orders is available on the SEBI website www.sebi.gov.in
