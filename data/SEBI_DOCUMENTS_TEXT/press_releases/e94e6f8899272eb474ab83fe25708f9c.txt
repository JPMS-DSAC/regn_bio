December 23, 2003PR No.321/2003
DIVISION OF CORPORATE RESTRUCTURING
In the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of Greaves Morganite Crucibles Limited
Greaves Leasing Finance Limited (hereinafter referred to as the "acquirer") is a wholly owned subsidiary of Greaves Limited (Greaves). Greaves Morganite Crucibles Limited (hereinafter referred to as the "target company") is promoted by Greaves. As a part of its ongoing financial restructuring exercise Greaves proposes to transfer its entire share holding of 7,14,000 shares representing 25.50% in the target company to the acquirer. The acquirer does not hold any shares in the target company at present.
The Acquirer made an application to SEBI under Regulation 4(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 (hereinafter referred to as "Regulations") seeking exemption in terms of Regulation 3(1)(l) from the applicability of Regulation 10 and 12 of the Regulations with regard to aforesaid proposed acquisition.
This application was referred to the Takeover Panel. The Takeover Panel recommended grant of exemption as sought.
Taking into consideration the above and the recommendation of the Takeover Panel, SEBI in terms of Regulation 4(6) of the Regulations has passed an order dated 18.12.2003, granted exemption, to the Acquirer from complying with the provisions Chapter III of the Regulations with regard to acquisition of 7,14,000 shares representing 25.50% share capital of the target company from its parent company Greaves.
