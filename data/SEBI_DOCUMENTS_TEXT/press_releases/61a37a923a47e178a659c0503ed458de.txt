PRESS RELEASE
PR No.240/2006

Order in the matter of M/s. Baroda Agro Industries Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated    November 2, 2006 in the matter of M/s. Baroda Agro Industries Ltd.  debarring M/s World Link Finance Ltd. from dealing in securities or associating with any of the activities in the capital market for a period of three years. The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
