 PRESS RELEASE




February 05, 2004 PR No.31/2004
 

SUBJECT: OFFER DOCUMENTS RECEIVED & OBSERVATIONS ISSUED BY SEBI DURING THE PERIOD 12/01/2004 TO 30/01/2004
 

ISSUED BY: CORPORATION FINANCE DEPARTMENT –
DIVISION OF ISSUES AND LISTING
TEXT
 
During the period 12/01/2004 to 30/01/2004, seventeen draft offer documents were received by SEBI. During the same period, SEBI had issued one observation on offer documents filed with it.
 
List I gives details of offer documents received and List II gives details of offer documents on which observations were issued.
 
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in ) under "Primary Markets".
