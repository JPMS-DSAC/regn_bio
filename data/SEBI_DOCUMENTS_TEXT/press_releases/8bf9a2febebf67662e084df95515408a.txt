Order against M/s. Good Fortune Advisory Services Pvt. Ltd. [Fortune Equity Brokers (India) Ltd.] in the matter of Nedungadi Bank Ltd. 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated April 30, 2007, has disposed of the enquiry proceedings against M/s. Good Fortune Advisory Services Pvt. Ltd. [name changed to Fortune Equity Brokers (India) Ltd.] Member, National Stock Exchange (NSE) in the matter of Nedungadi Bank Ltd., imposing no penalty.
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
