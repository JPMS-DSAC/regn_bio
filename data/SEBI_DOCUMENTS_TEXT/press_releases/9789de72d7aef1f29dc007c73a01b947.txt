Press Releases 













PR No.34/2011



Orders in respect of Tropical Securities & Investments Pvt. Ltd. and 
Ms. Aditi Asim Dalal in the matter of Ravalgaon Sugar Farms Ltd. and 
Shrenuj & Company Ltd.
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed orders dated February 24, 2011, suspending the certificate of registration of Tropical Securities & Investments Pvt. Ltd., member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. for a period of two months and warned Ms. Aditi Asim Dalal, member, Ahmedabad Stock Exchange Ltd. to be careful and cautious in the conduct of her business.
 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
February 28, 2011
