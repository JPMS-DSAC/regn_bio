PR No.151/2007

Address of SEBI for lodging investor grievances
Investors may send their grievances falling in the purview of SEBI to the new post office box address as given below:
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India
Post Box No.  – 8140
Bandra East Post Office
Mumbai – 400 051
Investors may also send their grievances to oiae-assistance@sebi.gov.in or lodge their grievances online on www.sebi.gov.in or http://investor.sebi.gov.in. The helpline numbers for investors are 022-26449188/ 26449199.  
 
Mumbai
April 27, 2007
