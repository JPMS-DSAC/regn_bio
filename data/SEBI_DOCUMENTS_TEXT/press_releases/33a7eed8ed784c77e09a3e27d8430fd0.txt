PR No.44/2011
Order in respect of Triumph International Finance India Ltd. 
in the matter of Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI  has passed an order dated March 17, 2011 disposing of the enquiry proceedings in respect of Triumph International Finance India Ltd. in the matter of Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
