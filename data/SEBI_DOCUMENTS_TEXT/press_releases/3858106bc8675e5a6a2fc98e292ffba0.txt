PR No.110/2011
Order in the matter of dealings in the shares of Spectacle Infotek Limited, Goldstone Technologies Limited, Gemstone Investments Limited, LGS Global Limited and Well Pack Papers & Containers Limited
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  July 08, 2011 in the matter of dealings in the shares of Spectacle Infotek Limited, Goldstone Technologies Limited, Gemstone Investments Limited, LGS Global Limited and Well Pack Papers & Containers Limited confirming the interim ex-­parte directions issued vide the Order dated February 02, 2011 against Gemstone Investments Limited, Mr. Narendra Prabodh Ganatra, Mr. Avinash Bothra,  Mrs. Dipika Dinesh Kankaria and Mr. Vishal Pare.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
July 13,  2011
