Order in the matter of Order Book Manipulation
 
 
Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order on  September 03, 2007 in respect of (1) Shri Latesh Chheda  (2) Shri Viren Kenia (3) Shri Bhavin Chheda (4) Shri Chetan Rathod and (5) M/s. Neptune Fincot Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of order book manipulation on the day of listing of securities on stock exchanges, confirming the directions given vide ex-parte interim order dated April 26, 2007.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
