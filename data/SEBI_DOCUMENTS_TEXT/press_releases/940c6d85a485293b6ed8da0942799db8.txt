PRESS RELEASE
PR No.317/2004

Offer documents received / withdrawn and observations issued by SEBI from November 15, 2004 to November 21, 2004
During the period November 15, 2004 to November 21, 2004, SEBI had received two offer documents and had issued observations on one offer document filed with it.  During the same period, no offer document was withdrawn.  
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in) under “Issues and Listing “.
