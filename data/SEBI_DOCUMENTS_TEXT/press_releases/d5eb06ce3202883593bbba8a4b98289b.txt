PRESS RELEASE
PR No. 12/2005
Application for exemption from issue of public announcement rejected  
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India has rejected the application of Shri Jagdish Prasad Agarwalla and persons acting in concert with him seeking exemption from making open offer as and when the shares/voting rights of M/s Abha Property Project Ltd, being target company, are acquired by them.
 
On behalf of the acquirer, Karvy Investors Services Ltd. filed an application under regulation 4(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 seeking exemption from issue of public announcement, opening of Escrow Account, printing and dispatch of letters of offer to the shareholders etc. in respect of the proposed acquisition of 9, 50,000 (50%) equity shares from the 82 public shareholders of the target company. Pursuant to the proposed acquisition from public shareholders the holding of acquirer would increase to 87.55% from 37.55%.  The acquirer proposed to send individual letters to the shareholders for buying shares directly from them.
 
The application was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel did not recommend grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the Order is available on the SEBI website at www.sebi.gov.in
