PRESS RELEASE
PR No.71/2008

M/s. Khandwala Shah & Associates, sub broker, censured

Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated February 21, 2008, imposing a minor penalty of ‘censure’ on M/s. Khandwala Shah & Associates (SEBI Registration No. INS010228224), sub-broker affiliated through M/s. Prakash K Shah Shares and Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd.
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
