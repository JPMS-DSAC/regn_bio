SEBI debars 14 entities from accessing securities market
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has debarred 14 entities from accessing the securities market in the matter of M/s. Sword and Shield Pharma Limited. Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated December 04, 2006 against the following fourteen entities.
 
Shri Mahesh Kumar Patel, M/s. Aarushi Consultancy and Shri Gautam Patel have been debarred from accessing the securities market and prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities, either directly or indirectly, for a period of two years; Smt. Jyotiben M.Patel, Shri Rajni M.Patel, Smt Heena R.Patel,  Shri Nilesh Patel, M/s. Radhe Investment, Shri Pravin Rayani, Shri Hiren Rathod, Shri Parsottam P.Kundanani (partner of erstwhile M/s. Mega Securities), Shri Rajesh Modi, Shri Praful H.Patel and M/s. Kishore Corporation have been debarred from accessing the securities market and prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities, either directly or indirectly, for a period of one year.
 
The full text of the order is available on website: www.sebi.gov.in
