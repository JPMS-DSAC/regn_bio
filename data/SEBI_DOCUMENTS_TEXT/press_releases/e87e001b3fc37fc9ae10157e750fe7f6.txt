PRESS RELEASE 
PR No.178/2007
Registrations of two stock brokers, suspended
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  May 09, 2007 suspending the certificates of registration of the following two  stock brokers for a period of six months or till the time the brokers pay the fees, whichever is later:
 





Sr. No.


Name of Broker


SEBI Registration No.


 
Name of Stock Exchange




1


Anice Kurian M 


INB090281715


Cochin Stock Exchange




2


Aleyamma Thomas


INB090294115


Cochin Stock Exchange





 
The aforesaid stock brokers have failed to pay the required fees and have thereby contravened the provisions of Regulation 10 read with Schedule III of the SEBI (Stock Broker and Sub-broker) Regulations, 1992. These registrations have been suspended in terms of Sub-Regulations (5) and (6) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002 for a period of six months or till the time the brokers pay the fees, whichever is later. 
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
