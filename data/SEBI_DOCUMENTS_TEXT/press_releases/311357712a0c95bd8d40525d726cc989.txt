Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), is organizing its International Research Conference - II on March 20, 2015 in Mumbai. The theme of the Conference is “Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs)".  SEBI has invited experts from academia, corporates, market practitioners, policy makers etc., having experience in the field, from countries such as Australia, Hong Kong, India, Malaysia, Singapore, Switzerland, UK and USA.
During the conference, the participants would discuss issues related to Structure, Performance and Impact, Taxation, Valuation of REITs and Managing the REITs, etc. The detailed programme schedule of International Research Conference- II is available on SEBI website.
