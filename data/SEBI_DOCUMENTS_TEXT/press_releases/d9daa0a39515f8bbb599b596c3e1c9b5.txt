
 
Jul 09, 2013

PR No.:  
62/2013


Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has vide orders dated July 5, 2013 passed the following directions in the matter of trading by connected clients in the scrips of Temptation Foods Limited, Bang Overseas Limited, Confidence Petroleum India Limited, Cals Refineries Limited and Shree Precoated Steels Limited (now known as Ajmera Realty & Infra India Ltd.):-
(a)  suspended the certification of registration of Krishvi Securities P. Ltd. member of BSE for a period of 15 days.
(b)  suspended the certification of registration of Mr. Deepak C. Dalal (Proprietor of M/s Chandravadan J. Dalal) member of BSE for a period of 15 days.
 
The orders shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
