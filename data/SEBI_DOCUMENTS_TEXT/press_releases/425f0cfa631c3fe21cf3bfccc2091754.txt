PR No.81/2011




Order in respect of Macy Securities Private Limited in the matter of
Balaji Industrial Corporation Ltd.
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated June 02, 2011 disposing of the enquiry proceedings in respect of Macy Securities Private Ltd., in the matter of Balaji Industrial Corporation Ltd., without any penalty.   
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
June 06, 2011
