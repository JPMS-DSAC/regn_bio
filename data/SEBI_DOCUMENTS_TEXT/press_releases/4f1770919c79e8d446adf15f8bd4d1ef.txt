PR No.116/2009
Grant of exemption to Shri Shekhar G. Patel, M/s. Shri Ganesh Construction Private Ltd. and others (promoters/acquirers) for the proposed acquisition of voting rights in M/s. Ganesh Housing Corporation Limited (Target Company)
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated   March 24, 2009, granting exemption to Shri Shekhar G. Patel, M/s. Shri Ganesh Construction Private Ltd. and others (promoters/acquirers) from the applicability of Regulation 11(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of their proposed increase in voting rights from 57.49% to 65.52% pursuant to the target company’s proposed buy-back of equity shares.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
