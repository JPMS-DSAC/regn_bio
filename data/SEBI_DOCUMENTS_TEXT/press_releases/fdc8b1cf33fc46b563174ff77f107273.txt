Order against M/s. Shyam Sundar Dalmia in the matter of 
M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated     January 19, 2007 against M/s. Shyam Sundar Dalmia, broker of Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE) in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd., imposing a penalty of “Censure” against the broker.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
