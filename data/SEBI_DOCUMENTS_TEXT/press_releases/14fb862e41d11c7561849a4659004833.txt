PR No.343/2009
Order in the matter of M/s Alka Securities Limited
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated October 30, 2009, in the matter of M/s Alka Securities Limited,  Member, NSE, suspending the certificate of registration of the stock broker for a period of two months. The order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
November 5, 2009
