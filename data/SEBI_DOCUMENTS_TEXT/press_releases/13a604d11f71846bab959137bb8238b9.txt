PRESS RELEASE
PR No.73/2007

 
Order against M/s. Narendra S.Tanna, sub-broker, in the matter of 
M/s. Spanco Telesystems and Solutions Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  February 22, 2007 directing that a penalty of “Censure” be imposed on       M/s. Narendra S.Tanna (SEBI Regn. No.INS 010828510 and INS 01058128), a sub-broker affiliated to M/s. SKSE Securities Ltd., Member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) and  National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. (NSE) and sub-broker to M/s. Hem Securities Ltd., Member, BSE, in the matter of M/s. Spanco Telesystems and Solutions Ltd.
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
February 26, 2007
