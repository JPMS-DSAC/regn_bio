
 
Dec 04, 2012

PR No.:

Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated Nov 29, 2012 vacating  the ad interim directions issued vide Order dated August 30, 2011 and the directions confirmed vide order dated April 04, 2012 in respect of Hemaliben Bimalkumar Mehta, Charuben Jitendrarai Mehta and Bhavana Gautambhai Vaghasiya in the matter of Bodal Chemicals Ltd.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
