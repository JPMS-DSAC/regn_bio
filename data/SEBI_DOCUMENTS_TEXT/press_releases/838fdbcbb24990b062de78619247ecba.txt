
 
Aug 31, 2012

PR No.:  
81/2012


Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed two orders dated  August 29, 2012 in the matter of Soundcraft Industries Limited, suspending the certificate of registration of  Rajkumar C. Basantani, Member, NSE and Kolar Sharex Pvt. Ltd.  Member, BSE  for a period of one year and three months respectively.
 These orders shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the orders.
 The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
