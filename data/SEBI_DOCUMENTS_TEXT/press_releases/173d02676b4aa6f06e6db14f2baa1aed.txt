PRESS RELEASE
PR No.235/2008


Certificate of registration of M/s. Rosy Blue Securities Pvt. Ltd., suspended


Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  October 21, 2008 against  M/s. Rosy Blue Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member, National Stock Exchange of India Ltd., imposing a minor penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of the broker, for a period of 15 days, in the matter of  M/s. DCM Shriram Consolidated Ltd.
 
The order shall come into effect on expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
