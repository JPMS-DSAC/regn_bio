PR No.62/2006

Order in the matter of Gujarat Mineral Development Corporation
 
Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order in the matter of Gujarat Mineral Development Corporation, under Regulation 13(4) of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002 directing that no further action be taken against M/s. Blue Chip Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd., Member, NSE, SEBI Regn. No. INB 230776338. 
In the matter of Gujarat Mineral Development Corporation, SEBI appointed an Enquiry Officer to enquire into the alleged violations of the provisions of SEBI Act, 1992 and SEBI (Stock Brokers and Sub-brokers) Regulations, 1992 by Blue Chip Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. Enquiry officer submitted his report and observed that M/s. Blue Chip Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. has violated  Clause V of Para B of the code of conduct for brokers prescribed in Schedule II read with Regulation 7 of SEBI (Stock Brokers and Sub-brokers) Regulations, 1992 and Rule 4(b) of SEBI (Stock Brokers and Sub-brokers) Rules, 1992. Consequently, SEBI passed the order after considering the submissions made by M/s. Blue Chip Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. 
The order has been passed on January 25, 2006. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
