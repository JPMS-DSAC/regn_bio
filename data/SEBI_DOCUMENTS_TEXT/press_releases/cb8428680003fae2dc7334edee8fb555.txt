PR No.306/2009 
Order in the matter of Yashodham Commercial Enterprises Ltd.
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated September 30, 2009, against M/s. Mangal Bhawan Holdings Pvt. Ltd. and its directors, viz., Mr. Prakash Gaggar, Mr. J.K.Mimani and Mr. Dinesh Rungta in the matter of Yashodham Commercial Enterprises Ltd., prohibiting them from buying, selling or dealing in securities in any manner, or accessing the securities market directly or indirectly in any manner whatsoever for a period of six months from the date of the order.
 
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
