PR No.211/2010
Order in respect of M/s. DPS Shares and Securities Pvt. Ltd., in the matter of M/s. Mega Corporation Ltd.
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 21, 2010, in the matter of M/s. Mega Corporation Ltd., suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. DPS Shares and Securities Pvt. Ltd., member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. for a period of three months.
 
The order shall come into effect on expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
September 29, 2010
