PR No.02/2008


Shri D.S.Pendse, M/s. Nalini Properties Pvt. Ltd. and M/s. Khuda Gawah Investments Pvt. Ltd.  restrained from accessing the securities market 


Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated December 28, 2007 restraining M/s. Nalini Properties Pvt. Ltd, M/s. Khuda Gawah Investments Pvt. Ltd. and its Director Shri D.S. Pendse from accessing the securities market and prohibiting them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing or associating with the securities market, for a period of two years from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
January 03, 2008
