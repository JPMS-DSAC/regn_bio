PRESS RELEASE
PR No.64/2005

Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
Shricon Industries Ltd. 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)  has passed an order dated March 21, 2005 granting exemption to Arani Agro Oil Industries Ltd. (acquirer) from complying with requirement of Regulation 15 of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, with regard to making public announcement in the case of the proposed acquisition of 2,48,000 equity shares of Shricon Industries Ltd. (SIL) from the public shareholders under regulation 10 and 12 subject to certain conditions.
Acquirer is not holding any shares in SIL as on the date of the application. The acquirer proposes to acquire 6,82,000 shares (55%) of the target company held by the existing promoters thus attracting provisions of regulation 10 and 12. Accordingly, the acquirer is required to make a public offer to acquire 20% of the voting capital of SIL. The proposed acquisition from existing promoters is for the purpose of acquiring control and management over SIL. 
The acquirer has sought exemption from making a public announcement, submission of Letter of Offer and provisions of Escrow Account in terms of Takeover Regulations.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from issue of public announcement only subject to certain conditions including the acquirer making individual offers to each of the public shareholders under Registered Post with AD.
The full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
