PR No.104/2011
Order in the matter of M/s Alka Securities Ltd.
Dr. K.M Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated July 1, 2011 in the matter of Alka Securities Ltd disposing of the show cause notice dated July 9, 2010 issued to Alka Securities Ltd.
 
The Order further states that:”The disposal of the show cause notice dated July 9, 2010 is without prejudice to the rights of the Securities and Exchange Board of India to initiate enquiry proceedings in accordance with the provisions of the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Intermediaries) Regulations, 2008 for the alleged violations, as mentioned in the aforesaid notice. A separate Order would be issued for appointing a Designated Authority in this regard and the Designated Authority shall expeditiously complete the enquiry as early as possible but not later than six months from the date of such appointment.”
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
