Order in the matter of Sun Infoways Ltd.

Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated    March 03, 2008 in the matter of Sun Infoways Ltd. disposing of the show cause notice against Shri Prakash Pangarkar and Shri Sanjay P Muthe without any directions and restraining Sun Infoways Ltd., Shri Anil Pujari, Shri Tanvir Zaki, Shri Pravin Sonalkar, Shri Arif Mithani, Shri Umesh Gowda, Shri Hozefa Vohra, Shri Kuldeep Handoo, Shri Suman Pandit, Shri Ajay Bohra, Shri S. Sunil Saxena, Shri Iqbal Rupani, Shri Ashok Kumar Chaudhary, Shri Rajesh Kumar Chaudhary, Great Eastern Mercantile Pvt. Ltd., Act React Holdings Pvt. Ltd., Adinath Propcon Pvt. Ltd., Apoorva Fincap Pvt. Ltd., Concept Institute Pvt. Ltd., Laxmi Securities, Neha Fincap Pvt. Ltd., Sai Investments, Telefinlease Pvt. Ltd., Saksham Financial Services Ltd., Shri Sandip Bohra and Shri Rajender Rai from accessing the securities market and also prohibiting them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing or associating with the securities market in any manner whatsoever for a period of two years.
 
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
