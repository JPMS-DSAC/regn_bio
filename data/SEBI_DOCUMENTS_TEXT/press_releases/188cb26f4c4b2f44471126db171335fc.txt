      PRESS RELEASE
PR No.74/2008

Order in the matter of Blue Information Technology Limited

Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated    March 03, 2008 in the matter of Blue Information Technology Limited prohibiting Shri. Dhaval Jhaveri, M/s. Blue Information Technology, Ms. Seema D. Jhaveri, Shri R.S. Jhaveri, Shri V.K. Gupta, Shri K.C. Gupta, Shri Milan Trivedi, Shri Dilip Kumar Puri, M/s. Beta Agro Ltd., M/s. Omega Housing Ltd., M/s. Ranger Investments and Leasing Ltd., M/s. Kingfisher Overseas Ltd. and M/s. Aadit Medicine Ltd. from accessing the securities market and also prohibiting them from buying, selling or otherwise dealing or associating with the securities market in any manner whatsoever for a period of two years.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
