PR No.36/2008

 
Order against M/s. Harikishan Hiralal (now known as Shreehari Hira Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd.)
 

Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 05, 2008 against M/s. Harikishan Hiralal (now known as Shreehari Hira Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd.), Member of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), in the matter of M/s. SMR Universal Softech Ltd., suspending the certificate of registration granted to them for a period of one month.
 
The order shall come into force immediately after the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
