
 
Jan 10, 2012

PR No.:  
4/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 06, 2012 restraining Empower Industries India Ltd. from accessing the securities market and prohibiting it from buying, selling or otherwise dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, for a period of 6 months
The order has come into force with effect immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
