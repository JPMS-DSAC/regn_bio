Committee to study the future of Regional Stock Exchanges –  post demutualization
A Committee has been constituted by SEBI Chairman, to go into the future of Regional Stock Exchanges – post demutualization.  The Committee will be Chaired by Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI.
The other members of the Committee are:

Prof. Moushmi Ghosh, Professor, Finance & Control,  IIM- Calcutta
Shri J. Jayaraman, Chartered Accountant, Ahmedabad.
Shri M.R. Mayya, Former Executive Director- Bombay Stock Exchange and Former Chairman - The Inter Connected Stock Exchange Ltd.
Shri Sandeep Parekh, Advocate, Supreme Court, New Delhi 
Shri Ramu Sharma, Director - Bangalore Stock Exchange Ltd.  and Chairman, Federation of Indian Stock Exchanges (FISE)
Shri Pratip Kar, Executive Director, SEBI, Mumbai –  Member Secretary.

The terms of reference of the Committee is as follows:
To review and examine the future role of the Regional Stock Exchanges and their subsidiaries – post demutualization, keeping in view the legal requirements of the Securities Contracts  (Regulation) Act  1956. 
The Committee is expected to submit the report within two months from the date of the first meeting.
