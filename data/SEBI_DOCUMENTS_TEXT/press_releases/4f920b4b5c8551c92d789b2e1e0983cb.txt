PRESS RELEASE
PR No.196/2007

Order against M/s. HB Securities Ltd., Member of NSE
 
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  June 19, 2007 imposing no penalty against M/s. HB Securities Ltd.,  Member of National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. (NSE), for their dealing in the scrip of  M/s. Jaiprakash Industries Ltd. 
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
