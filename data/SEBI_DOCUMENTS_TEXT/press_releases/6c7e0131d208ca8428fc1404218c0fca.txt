PRESS RELEASE
December 04, 2003 PR No.292/2003
 
SUBJECT: OFFER DOCUMENTS RECEIVED & OBSERVATIONS ISSUED BY SEBI DURING THE PERIOD 24/11/2003 TO 28/11/2003
ISSUED BY: CORPORATION FINANCE DEPARTMENT – 
DIVISION OF ISSUES AND LISTING 
  
During the period 24/11/2003 to 28/11/2003, one draft offer document was received by SEBI. During the same period, SEBI had issued observations on two offer documents filed with it. List I gives details of offer documents received and List II gives details of offer documents on which observations were issued. Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in ) under "Primary Market".
 
Corrigendum 
In the press release no.287 issued on November 25, 2003, under "List II – Observations issued by SEBI from 17-11-2003 to 21-11-2003" the issue size of QPRO Infotech Ltd. was erroneously mentioned as 87.5 crores instead of 0.87 crores. The error is regretted.
