P. K. Nagpal  promoted as Executive Director
 
Shri P.K. Nagpal has been promoted as Executive Director in Securities and Exchange Board of India. Shri Nagpal will look after the Investigation Department. Prior to his promotion, Shri Nagpal was Chief General Manager in the Investigation Department.
 
A post graduate in Business Administration (MBA), Shri Nagpal has been with SEBI since inception and has worked in almost all operational departments. He has been nominated as member of various internal and external committees and on the boards of stock exchanges and professional bodies.
