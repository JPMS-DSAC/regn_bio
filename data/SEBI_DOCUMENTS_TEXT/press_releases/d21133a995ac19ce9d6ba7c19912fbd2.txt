PR No.49/2007

Exemption application of Shri Shyam Sharma for proposed acquisition of shares pursuant to preferential allotment of   equity shares of M/s. Ganesh Polytex Limited, rejected
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 05, 2007, rejecting the application of Shri Shyam Sharma and others (acquirers) seeking exemption from applicability of Regulation 11(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of the proposed acquisition of 34 lakh equity shares of M/s. Ganesh Polytex Limited by way of preferential allotment.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The panel did not grant exemption for the said acquisition.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
