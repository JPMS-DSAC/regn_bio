PRESS RELEASE
PR No.83/2008

Order in the matter of Shalibhadra Infosec Ltd.

Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated             March 03, 2008 in the matter of Shalibhadra Infosec Ltd. restraining  Ashit Vora, Mahesh Shah,  Pankaj Patel, Vinod Desai, Tushar Jhaveri and Maulik Patwa from accessing the securities market, buying, selling or dealing in securities or associating with the securities market in any manner whatsoever for a period of one year and restraining Santosh Gayakwad and Mukesh Bachubhai Vadecha from accessing the securities market, buying, selling or dealing in securities or associating with the securities market in any manner whatsoever for a period of two years.
 
This order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
