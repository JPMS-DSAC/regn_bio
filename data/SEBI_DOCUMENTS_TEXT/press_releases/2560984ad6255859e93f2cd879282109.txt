 PRESS RELEASE



August 07, 2004 PR No.180/2004
 

CLARIFICATION 

 
In light of media queries and market rumours, it is hereby clarified that today (August 7, 2004), the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) conducted a search at the residence and his office chamber only, of one officer Shri. Nagender Parakh, Chief General Manager. 
This is in connection with a case dating back several years allegedly involving disproportionate assets. This has nothing, whatsoever, to do with the functioning of SEBI. 
 
Mumbai
August 7, 2004
