PRESS RELEASE
PR No.229/2006

Order against The Delhi Stock Exchange Association Limited in the matter of M/s. Shonkh Technologies International Limited
  
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) vide order dated October 09, 2006 has disposed off the show cause notice dated December 07, 2005 issued to The Delhi Stock Exchange Association Limited (DSE) in the matter of   M/s. Shonkh Technologies International Ltd. (STIL).
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
October 10, 2006
