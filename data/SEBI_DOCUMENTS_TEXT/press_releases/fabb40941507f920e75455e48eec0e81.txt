PR No.21/2011
Order in respect of M/s. Ami Impex (proprietor Shri Deven Mehta) in the matter of M/s. Sunearth Ceramics Limited
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 20, 2011 in the matter of M/s. Sunearth Ceramics Limited restraining  M/s. Ami Impex (proprietor Shri Deven Mehta) from buying, selling or dealing in securities market whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of six months.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
January 27, 2011
