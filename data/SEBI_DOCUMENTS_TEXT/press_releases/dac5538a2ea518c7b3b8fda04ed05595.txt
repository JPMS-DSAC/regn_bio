PR No.109/2009
Grant of exemption to M/s Godrej Agrovet Ltd. and others (acquirers) for the proposed acquisition of voting rights in M/s Godrej Industries Limited (target company)
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 23, 2009, granting exemption to M/s Godrej Agrovet Ltd and others (acquirers) from the applicability of Regulation 11(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of their proposed increase in voting rights from 78.57% to 80% pursuant to the target company’s proposed buy-back of equity shares.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
