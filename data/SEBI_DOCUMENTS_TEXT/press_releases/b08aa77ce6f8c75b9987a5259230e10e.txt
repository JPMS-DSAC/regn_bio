Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed two orders dated  April 09, 2012 in the matter of Cyberspace Limited viz., warning M/s. Claridges Investment and Finance Pvt. Ltd. and M/s. Shreepati Holdings and Finance Ltd. to be cautious in their future dealings and exercise due care and diligence in the conduct of their affairs in the securities market.
 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
