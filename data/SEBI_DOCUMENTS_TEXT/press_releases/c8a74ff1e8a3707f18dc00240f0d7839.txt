PRESS RELEASE
PR No.222/2007


Order against M/s. Pioneer TCP Stock Brokers Ltd. in the matter of 
M/s. Sun Infoways Ltd.
 
 
Shri V.K Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  July 19, 2007 against M/s. Pioneer TCP Stock Brokers Ltd., broker, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) in the matter of M/s. Sun Infoways Ltd., directing the broker to be careful and cautious in future while dealing in securities market.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in 
 
 
Mumbai
