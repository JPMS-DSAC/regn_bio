PR No.416/2009




Order in respect of M/s. Integrated Master Securities Pvt. Ltd., in the matter of M/s. Jindal Steel and Power Ltd.
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 21, 2009 warning M/s. Integrated Master Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member, BSE in respect of its dealings in the shares of M/s. Jindal Steel and Power Ltd. The said broker has been further directed to be careful and cautious in the conduct of its business and to adhere and to comply with all the statutory provisions while carrying out its activities in the securities market.
 The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
