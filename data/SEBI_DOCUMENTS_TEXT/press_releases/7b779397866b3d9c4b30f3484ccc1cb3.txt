                                   Smt. Usha Narayanan takes charge as Executive Director, SEBI

Smt. Usha Narayanan has taken charge as Executive Director in the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI). Smt. Narayanan will be in charge of Corporation Finance Department and Investment Management Department - Division of Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) and Custodians. Prior to this promotion, she was Chief General Manager, SEBI.
 
A postgraduate in Economics and Law, Smt. Usha Narayanan joined SEBI in the year 1991 and has held various senior positions in SEBI with all around experience of working in the Divisions of Primary Market, Takeover, FIIs, Investigation and Market Intermediaries Department.As a representative of SEBI, Smt. Usha Narayanan has been a member of various committees such as the International Organisation of Securities Commissions (IOSCO) working group of Risk Based Inspection Methodologies, RBI Committee on Conflict of Interest, High Powered Expert Committee of the Government of India on making Mumbai a Regional Financial Centre etc.
 
Widely travelled, Smt. Narayanan has visited United States of America, Australia, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, China and Pakistan.
