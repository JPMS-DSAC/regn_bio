PR No.266/2010
Grant of exemption to Shri Praful N.Satra and Smt. Minaxi P. Satra for the proposed acquisition of shares with voting rights in M/s. Satra Properties (lndia) Limited
 
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 02, 2010 granting exemption to Shri Praful N. Satra and Smt. Minaxi P. Satra (herein after referred to as acquirers) from the applicability of Regulation 11(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of their proposed acquisition of invoked but unsold 1,63,74,568 shares pledged with Sicom Investments & Finance Ltd., which would increase the shareholding / voting rights of promoter group from 62.80% to 73.34% of total voting capital of M/s. Satra Properties (lndia) Limited. 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
December 07, 2010
