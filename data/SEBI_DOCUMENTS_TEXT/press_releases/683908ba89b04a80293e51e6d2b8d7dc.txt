PRESS RELEASE
PR No.222/2008



Order in the matter of acquisition of shares of Aurobindo Pharma Ltd.



Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated    October 13, 2008, disposing the show cause notice of SEBI dated  December 17, 2007 issued to  Mr. Ramaprasad Reddy for the then proposed acquisition of shares of Aurobindo Pharma Ltd. pursuant to conversion of 25,00,000 convertible warrants and thereby contravening Regulation 11 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
