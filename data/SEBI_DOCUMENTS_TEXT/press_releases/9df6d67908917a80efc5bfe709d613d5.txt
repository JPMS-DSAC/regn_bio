PRESS RELEASE
PR No.233/2008


Order in the matter of Atmaram Kejriwal & Company


Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated October 21, 2008 in the matter of Atmaram Kejriwal & Company (SEBI Registration No. INB100362712), Member, Uttar Pradesh Stock Exchange Association Limited. 
 
Atmaram Kejriwal & Company has been advised to “comply with all statutory provisions/instructions received in the course of discharge of its duty in future, without such delay”.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
