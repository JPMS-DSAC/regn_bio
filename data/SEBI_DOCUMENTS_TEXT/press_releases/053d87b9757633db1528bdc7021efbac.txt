PR No.19/2009
Order against M/s. Sharan Fincap Ltd., Member, LSE in the matter of Karishma Floriculture Ltd. 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 02, 2009, in the matter of Karishma Floriculture Ltd., suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Sharan Fincap Ltd., Member, Ludhiana Stock Exchange Ltd. for a period of fifteen working days.
The order shall come into force from three weeks from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
