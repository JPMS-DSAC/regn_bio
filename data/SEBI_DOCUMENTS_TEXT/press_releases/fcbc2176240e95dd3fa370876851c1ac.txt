PR No.157/2009
Consent Order in the matter of M/s. Arch Pharmalabs Limited
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri M.S. Sahoo and   Dr. K.M. Abraham, has passed a consent order dated May 11, 2009 on the consent application submitted by M/s. Arch Pharmalabs Limited,  for non compliance  of Regulations 6(2) and 6(4) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers), Regulations, 1997 {“Takeover Regulations”} for the year 1997 and under Regulation 8(3) of Takeover Regulations for the year 1998 to 2002.  The applicant without admitting or denying guilt has remitted a sum of Rs.2,00,000/-(Rupees two lakh only ) towards settlement charges in the matter.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
May 14, 2009
