PRESS RELEASE
PR No.120/2007

   
Grant of exemption to Shri Vijay Kumar Gupta, Smt. Sulochana V Gupta, Mr. Manish V Gupta, Mr. Mohit V Gupta, Ms. Shilpa Manish Gupta pursuant to Buy Back of Shares by M/s. Gujarat Ambuja Exports Ltd. (Target Company)
 
An Order dated March 29, 2007 was passed by Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, granting exemption to Shri Vijay Kumar Gupta, Smt. Sulochana V Gupta, Mr. Manish V Gupta, Mr. Mohit V Gupta, Ms. Shilpa Manish Gupta from making an open offer in terms of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, with regard to increase in their voting rights from 63.11% to 66.40% consequent to the proposed  buy back of shares  by M/s. Gujarat Ambuja Exports Ltd.
 
Pursuant to the said increase in voting rights, there would not be any change in control of the target company.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
