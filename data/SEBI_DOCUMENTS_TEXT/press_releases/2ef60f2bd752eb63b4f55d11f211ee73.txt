ClarificationThis is with reference to news reports appearing in a section of media, subsequent to SEBI’s press meet on September 18, 2018, regarding quasi-judicial proceedings pending in SEBI in the matter of ICICI Bank. It is hereby clarified that ICICI Bank and Ms. Chanda Kochhar have filed replies to Show Cause Notices issued by SEBI.  It is further clarified that, SEBI has not received any settlement application from ICICI Bank or from any other entity related to the matter.
