
 
Nov 08, 2012

PR No.:  
95/2012


Shri R K Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated November 05, 2012 cancelling the certificate of registration of Amit Financial Services Private Limited, stock broker of NSE.
 This Order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
