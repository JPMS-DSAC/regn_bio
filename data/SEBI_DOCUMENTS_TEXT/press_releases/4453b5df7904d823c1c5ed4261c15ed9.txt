PRESS RELEASE 
PR No.247/2007
Order against ten entities in the matter of M/s. Gujarat Texspin Ltd.
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated August 27, 2007 against ten entities in the matter of M/s. Gujarat Texspin Ltd.  
The following nine entities have been found guilty of violating the provisions of Regulations 3 and 6(c) of the SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices relating to Securities Market) Regulations, 1995:

M/s. Gujarat Texspin Ltd., and its Directors Shri Taufik Haji Gaffar, Shri Tarik Haji Gaffar, Shri Jivrajbhai M.Patel and Shri Mukund Krishnaswami Rao  
M/s. Ferrari Textiles Pvt. Ltd. and its Director Shri Tohid Hazi Gaffar 
Shri Atul Ratilal Shah and 
Shri Neerav Bhagat, Proprietor of M/s. D.K.Consultancy.

The nine entities have, thus, been restrained from dealing in securities market in any manner for a period of five (5) years.
The proceedings against Shri Krishnakant D.Parekh, erstwhile Director of M/s. Gujarat Texspin Ltd. have been dropped.
The order shall into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
