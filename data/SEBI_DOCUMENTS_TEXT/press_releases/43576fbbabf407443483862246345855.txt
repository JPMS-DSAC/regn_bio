Order in the matter of Ranbaxy Laboratories Limited 
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated August 3,2010 disposing of the enquiry proceedings in respect of N. H. Securities Ltd., K.N.P. Securities Ltd., V.N. Parekh Securities Ltd. and Triumph Securities Ltd. in the matter of Ranbaxy  Laboratories Limited.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
