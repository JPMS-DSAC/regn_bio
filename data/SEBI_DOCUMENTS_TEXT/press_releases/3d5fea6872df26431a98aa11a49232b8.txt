PR No.29/2011



Draft eXtensible Business Reporting Language Taxonomy for Mutual Funds 
In a move to adopt emerging global standards in the area of financial reporting, SEBI is in the process of adopting eXtensible Business Reporting Language (XBRL) for financial reporting.
As a pilot project, SEBI is in the process of adopting XBRL Taxonomy for various data reporting by mutual funds.
The draft XBRL Taxonomy for mutual funds is placed on SEBI website (www.sebi.gov.in) for public comments and is available under the heading: “Reports for public comments”.
Comments / suggestions if any, may be sent by email to mfxbrl@sebi.gov.in or in physical form to the following address on or before March 10, 2011.
