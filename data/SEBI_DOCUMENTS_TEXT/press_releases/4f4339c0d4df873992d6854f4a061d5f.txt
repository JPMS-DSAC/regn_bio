PRESS RELEASE
PR No.277/2006

 
M/s. Nilesh Share Brokers Ltd.  censured 

 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated December 06, 2006, imposing a penalty of censure upon M/s. Nilesh Share Brokers Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No.INS231141636), a sub-broker affiliated to M/s. HSE Securities Ltd., member of National Stock Exchange.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
December 08, 2006
