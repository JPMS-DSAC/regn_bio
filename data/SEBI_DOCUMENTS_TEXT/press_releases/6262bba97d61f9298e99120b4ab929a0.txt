PR No.80/2009
Order against M/s. Portfolio Equity Services in the matter of  Sun Infoways Ltd.
Dr. K M Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated   January 27, 2009, in the matter of Sun Infoways Ltd., censuring M/s. Portfolio Equity Services and directing it to be careful and cautious while dealing in securities market.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
