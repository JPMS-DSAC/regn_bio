PRESS RELEASE
PR No.90/2005

                                                                                                                  
Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
Satia Paper Mills Ltd. 
An Order dated June 27, 2005 was issued by Whole Time Member, SEBI granting exemption to the acquirer viz. Dr. Ajay Satia (acquirer) from making an open offer under Regulation 11(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of Satia Paper Mills Ltd. (SPML) through preferential allotment subject to the fulfilment of the conditions specified therein.
 
The acquirer is a promoter of the target company and along with his associates, friends and relatives holds 35.18% shares in the target company. In terms of the proposal as per the Corporate Debt Restructuring Scheme, a preferential allotment of 20,00,000 shares to the acquirer and persons acting in concert with him would increase their shareholding, from 35.18% to 51.38%.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirer from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
