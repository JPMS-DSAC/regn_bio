PR No.183/2007
Order against M/s. Victory Financial Services Limited in the matter of
M/s. Information Technologies (India) Limited
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated June 07, 2007 against M/s. Victory Financial Services Limited, Broker of National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. (NSE), in the matter of M/s. Information Technologies (India) Limited, imposing a minor penalty of suspension of certificate of registration (SEBI Regn. No. INB 230903037) for a period of two months.
The order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
