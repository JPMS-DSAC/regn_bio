PR No.66/2010
Order against Mackertich Consultancy Services Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of 
DSQ Biotech Ltd.
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated        March 03, 2010 in the matter of DSQ Biotech Ltd. restraining Mackertich Consultancy Services Pvt. Ltd., from buying, selling or dealing in securities market whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of one year from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
