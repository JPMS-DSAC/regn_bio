PR No.261/2007


Order against M/s. A. Jain & Company Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Shonkh Technologies International Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated September 18, 2007, has disposed off the enquiry proceedings against M/s. A. Jain & Company Pvt. Ltd., Member, Delhi Stock Exchange Association Ltd. (SEBI Registration  No.INB 050986932) in the matter of M/s. Shonkh Technologies International Ltd., without imposing any penalty.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
September 20, 2007.
