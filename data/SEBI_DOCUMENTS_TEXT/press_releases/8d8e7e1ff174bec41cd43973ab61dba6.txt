PRESS RELEASE
PR No.218/2006

Registration of M/s. RBS Srivastava Share & Stock Broker,
Member of UPSE, suspended
 
Shri V. K. Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated September 19, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. RBS Srivastava Share & Stock Broker (SEBI Registration No. INB100830714), member of the Uttar Pradesh Stock Exchange (UPSE) for a period of one month with effect from October 10, 2006. 
 
M/s. RBS Srivastava Share & Stock Broker will not be entitled to carry on the business as a broker with effect from October 10, 2006 to  November 9, 2006.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
