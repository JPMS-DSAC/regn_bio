PR No.115/2006

Certificate of registration of M/s. Bipin R. Vora, Member, BSE, 
suspended for four months
The certificate of registration of M/s. Bipin R. Vora, Member – Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), Mumbai (SEBI Registration No. INB 01004416) has been suspended for a period of four months. The broker has been found guilty of violation of code of conduct as specified in SEBI (Stock Brokers and Sub-brokers) Regulations, 1992, while dealing in the shares of M/s. Malvica Engineering Ltd.
The order has been passed by Dr. T C Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI on March 22, 2006. The order shall come into effect after the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
