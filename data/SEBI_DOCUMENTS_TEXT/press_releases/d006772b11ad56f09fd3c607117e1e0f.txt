PR No.59/2011



Order against M/s. Satyshiv Shares & Securities Limited
Shri M.S. Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated   April 19, 2011, rejecting the application dated October 30, 2009, made by    M/s. Satyshiv Shares & Securities Limited for grant of certificate of registration as a Stock Broker of Delhi Stock Exchange Limited. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
Mumbai
April 20, 2011
