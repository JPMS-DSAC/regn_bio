PRESS RELEASE
PR No.150/2007

Order against M/s. Pursarth Trading Company Private Ltd. in the matter of Prudential Pharmaceuticals Ltd. 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated April 26, 2007 restraining M/s. Pursarth Trading Company Private Ltd. from buying, selling and dealing or accessing the securities market in any manner for a period of one month, in the matter of Prudential Pharmaceuticals Ltd.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
