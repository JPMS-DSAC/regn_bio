PR No.142/2007


 
Order against M/s. S.B. Bhutra & Co., Member of CSE 
 
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated April 13, 2007 against M/s. S.B. Bhutra & Co., Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Association Limited (CSE) in respect of the irregular transactions in the shares of M/s. Globe Stocks & Securities Limited imposing a major penalty of suspension of certificate of registration for a period of six months.
The order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order. 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
