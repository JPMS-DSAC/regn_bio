PR No.331/2009
Order against M/s Basant Periwal & Co. in the matter of
M/s. Nageshwar Investment Ltd.
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated October 26, 2009, disposing of the enquiry proceeding against the broker M/s Basant Periwal & Co.  As the said broker has already been prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities from November 30, 2005 to August 11, 2008, in accordance with interim order passed by SEBI, no further penalty would be required to be undergone by the broker.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
