PRESS RELEASE
PR No.200/2007

Certificate of Registration of M/s. JCM Capital Services Pvt. Ltd., 
Member of DSE, suspended
 
 
Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  June 15, 2007, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. JCM Capital Services Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INB050963230), Member of Delhi Stock Exchange for a period of seven days with effect from July 06, 2007. 
 
M/s. JCM Capital Services Pvt. Ltd.  will not be entitled to carry on the business as a broker with effect from July 06, 2007 to July 12, 2007.
  
 The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
