Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 26, 2014, granting exemption under regulation 11 of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 2011 (Takeover Regulations) to the Government of India ("Gol" or "Acquirer") from the obligation to make an open offer under regulation 3(2) of Takeover Regulations with respect to its proposed increase of shares/ voting rights from 69.26% to 77.20% in UCO Bank.
