PRESS RELEASE
PR No.246/2007

 
Order against M/s. Sree Ganesh Enterprise Sub-broker in the matter of
M/s. Datanet Systems Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated August 22, 2007 ‘censured’ M/s. Sree Ganesh Enterprise, Sub-broker [SEBI registration no. INS 010623721], affiliated to M/s. Subhkam Securities Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Datanet Systems Ltd.  It was observed that the sub-broker had contravened the provisions of Section 12 of SEBI Act, 1992.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
August 27, 2007.
