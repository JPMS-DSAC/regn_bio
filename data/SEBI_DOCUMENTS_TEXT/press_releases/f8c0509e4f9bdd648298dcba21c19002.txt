PR No.420/2009



Order against V B Investments and Ketan Investment in the matter of
 T. Spiritual World Limited
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 23, 2009, in the matter of T. Spiritual World Limited restraining M/s. V. B. Investments and M/s. Ketan Investment from buying, selling or dealing in the securities market in any manner whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of three years from the date of this order.
 The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
