 

 

PR No. 58/2017 
 

Order in the matter of Stapes Capital Research Private Limited 

 

Shri G Mahalingam, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an Interim order dated September 
04, 2017 in the matter of Stapes Capital Research Private Limited against the company and its 
directors  under  Sections  11(1),  11(4),  11B  and  11D  of  the  Securities  and  Exchange  Board  of 
India  Act,  1992  read  with  Securities  and  Exchange  Board  of  India  (Investment  Advisers) 
Regulations, 2013. 

Stapes Capital Research Private Limited and its directors â€“ Mr. Ram Singh, and Mr. Prashant 
Gangwar are directed: 

a.  to cease and desist from acting as an investment adviser and cease to solicit or undertake 
such activity or any other activities in the securities market, including buy, sell or otherwise 
deal in securities market, either directly or in directly with immediate effect;  
 

b.  not  to  divert  any  funds  raised  from  investors,  kept  in  bank  account(s)  and/or  in  their 

custody until further orders, except for the purpose of refunds to the clients; and  
 

c.  to  withdraw  and    remove    all  advertisements,    representations,    literatures,    brochures,  
materials,  publications,  documents,  websites,  etc.  in  relation  to  their  investment  advisory 
activity or any other unregistered activity in the securities market with immediate effect. 
 
 

The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
