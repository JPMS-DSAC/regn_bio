PR No.22/2008


M/s. Merbanc BSNV Securities Ltd., Sub broker, censured


Shri G. Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated January 23, 2008, imposing a minor penalty of ‘censure’ on M/s. Merbanc BSNV Securities Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INS230909231), Sub broker affiliated through M/s. HSE Securities Ltd., Member, National Stock Exchange of India Ltd.
 
The  full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
