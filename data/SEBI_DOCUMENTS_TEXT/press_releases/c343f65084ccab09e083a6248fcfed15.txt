PRESS RELEASE
PR No.143/2007


Order against M/s. Harvestdeal Securities Ltd. in the matter of 
Nedungadi Bank Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  April 17, 2007 suspending the Certificate of Registration of M/s. Harvestdeal Securities Ltd., Member of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE, for a period of three months in the matter of Nedungadi Bank Ltd.
The order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order. 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
  
Mumbai
