PRESS RELEASE
PR No.68/2007

Order against M/s. Neha Equisearch Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of 
M/s. Spanco Telesystems and Solutions Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated February 22, 2007 restraining M/s. Neha Equisearch Pvt. Ltd. from dealing in securities in any manner for a period of two months in the matter of M/s. Spanco Telesystems and Solutions Ltd.  The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
