PR No.210/2009
Order against M/s. Rashmi Gem Stocks and Shares Pvt. Ltd., Member, BgSE
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed order dated June 25, 2009 against M/s. Rashmi Gem Stocks and Shares Pvt Ltd., Member of Bangalore Stock Exchange Ltd.   By this order, the enquiry proceeding against M/s. Rashmi Gem Stocks and Shares Pvt. Ltd. has been disposed of advising the entity to strictly adhere to the Regulations and Circulars that regulate its function in the securities market as a registered intermediary of the Securities and Exchange Board of India.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
