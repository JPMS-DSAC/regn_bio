Suspension of certificate of registration of M/s. Munjal Online Stock Access,

Member of the OTC Exchange of India Ltd

 

 



 
 
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated June 09, 2004 suspending the certificate of registration granted to M/s. Munjal Online Stock Access (SEBI Registration No. INB200870116), member of the OTC Exchange of India Ltd., for a period of six months with effect from June 30, 2004. 

M/s. Munjal Online Stock Access, member of the OTC Exchange of India Ltd., will not be entitled to carry on the business as a Stock Broker with effect from June 30, 2004 till December 30, 2004. The penalty is imposed for irregularities in issuance of Contract Notes/Confirmation memos, Non-redressal of investor grievances, Dealing as unregistered sub-broker etc.
A full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
