(HSE - Hyderabad Stock Exchange, OTCEI - OTC Exchange of India )
The aforesaid 17 stock brokers were declared defaulters by the respective Stock Exchanges and ceased to be members of the Exchange and thereby failed to fulfill the pre-requisite condition of registration as stock broker with SEBI.
The registration has been cancelled in terms of Sub-Regulation (5) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002.
A full text of the order is available on our website www.sebi.gov.in
