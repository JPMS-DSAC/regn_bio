PR No.275/2009
Grant of exemption for the proposed acquisition through preferential allotment of equity shares of M/s. Ucal Fuel Systems Limited
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 03, 2009, granting exemption to Dr. V.Krishnamurthy, M/s. Carburettors Limited, Mr. Jayakar Krishnamurthy, M/s. Southern Ceramics Private Limited,  M/s. Sujo Land & Properties Private Limited and M/s. Minica Real Estates Private Limited (Acquirers) from the applicability of regulations 10 and 11 (1) and (2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, in respect of their proposed acquisition of 82,17,625 equity shares of M/s. Ucal Fuel Systems Limited @ of Rs. 36.35 per share, through preferential allotment.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended grant of exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
