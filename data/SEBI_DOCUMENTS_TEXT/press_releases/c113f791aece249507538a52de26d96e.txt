M/s. Akshaya & Co., censured
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), has passed an order dated January 18, 2007, imposing a minor penalty of censure on M/s. Akshaya & Co. (SEBI Reg. No. INS010799513), a sub-broker affiliated to M/s. MSE Financial Services Ltd., member of the Stock Exchange, Mumbai.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
