PRESS RELEASE
PR No.14/2007


Order against M/s. Kanodia Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order dated  January 10, 2007 against M/s. Kanodia Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd., broker of Calcutta Stock Exchange (CSE) in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. imposing a penalty of Censure against the broker.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
