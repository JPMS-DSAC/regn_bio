PR No.283/2010
Order in respect of  Basant Kumar Chirimar in the matter of 
M/s. Twenty First Century (India) Limited
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 15, 2010 in the matter of M/s. Twenty First Century (India) Limited prohibiting  Basant Kumar Chirimar to take up any new assignment for a period of three weeks.
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
December 20, 2010
