PR No.70/2007

 
Order against M/s. Unique Stockbro Pvt. Ltd., Member, BSE in the matter of 
M/s. Spanco Telesystems and Solutions Ltd.
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated   February 22, 2007 directing that a penalty of “Censure” be imposed on M/s. Unique Stockbro Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Regn. No.INB 011072130), Member of the Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE) in the matter of M/s. Spanco Telesystems and Solutions Ltd.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
