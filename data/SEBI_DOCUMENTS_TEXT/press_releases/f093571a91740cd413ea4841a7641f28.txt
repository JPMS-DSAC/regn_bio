PRESS RELEASE
PR No.96/2007

Order against M/s. Shankarlal Chokhany (Kashi Prasad Chokhany) in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  March 15, 2007, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Shankarlal Chokhany (Kashi Prasad Chokhany), member of Calcutta Stock Exchange bearing SEBI Registration No. INB030036811, in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd., for a period of seven days.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in 
 
 
Mumbai
March 16, 2007
