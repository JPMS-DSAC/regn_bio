PRESS RELEASE
PR No.279/2006

 
 Registration of M/s. Prashant J. Patel, broker, NSE, suspended 

Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  December 07, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Prashant J.Patel, broker, National Stock Exchange (SEBI Registration No.INB230649816), in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd., for a period of seven days.
 
This order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of twenty one days from the date of this order.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
