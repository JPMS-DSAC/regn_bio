PR No.140/2010
Allocation of limits to FIIs/sub-accounts for investment in corporate debt through bidding process
 
1.      As per SEBI circular No. CIR/IMD/FII/ 3 /2010 dated June 11, 2010, unutilised investment limits for corporate debt was available for allocation to the FIIs / sub-accounts in the open bidding platform. The bidding for these limits took place today on the BSE offered platform.
 
2.      Pursuant to the bidding, 22 successful bidders got allocation of the investment limit as per Annexure.
3.      These limits shall be utilized by the allocated entities within 45 days of the allocation.
 
4.      As per the aforesaid circular the first come first served window shall open at  08:30 AM IST, June 21, 2010 for receiving request.
