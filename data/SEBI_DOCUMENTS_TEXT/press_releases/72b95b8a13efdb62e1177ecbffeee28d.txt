PRESS RELEASE
PR No.146/2007

Order in the matter of  M/s VBC Ferro Alloys Ltd.
 
 
Dr. T.C. Nair, Whole Time Member (WTM), SEBI, has passed an order dated April 25, 2007 vacating the ad interim ex-parte order dated February 10, 2006 against M/s BLB Ltd. and Mr. Vikram Rathi in the matter of M/s VBC Ferro Alloys Ltd.  WTM has noted that the investigation has been completed and Adjudication Proceedings have been initiated against BLB Ltd. for committing violations of various regulations.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
