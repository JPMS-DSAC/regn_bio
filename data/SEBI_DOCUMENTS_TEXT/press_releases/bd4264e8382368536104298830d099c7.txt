
 
Sep 03, 2012

PR No.:  
82/2012


Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated August 29, 2012 in the matter of Aastha Broadcasting Network Ltd. with the direction that:
"the preferentially allotted shares of Aastha Broadcasting Network Ltd. lying in the demat accounts of M/s. Chneena Impex Pvt ltd., M/s. CEEAN Impex Pvt Ltd., M/s. Smitasha Impex Pvt Ltd. M/s. Prarekha Exim Pvt Ltd and M/s. Bicharshil Traders Ltd. shall remain frozen permanently"
The aforesaid directions shall be without prejudice to any other action that may be taken by SEBI against the aforesaid entities.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
