PR No.189/2010
Consent Order on the application submitted by Kankeshwari Securities Private Limited in the matter of Malvica Engineering Limited
 
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. K. M. Abraham and Shri Prashant Saran has passed a consent order dated August 03, 2010, on an application submitted by Kankeshwari Securities Private Limited in the matter of Malvica Engineering Limited, in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted a sum of Rs.2,00,000/- (Rupees two lakh only) towards the terms of consent in the matter. 
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
August 11, 2010
