PR No.309/2009
Order in the matter of SMIFS Capital Markets Limited
Shri M.S.Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated             October 01, 2009, rejecting the application by SMIFS Capital Markets Limited for renewal of certificate of registration as a Merchant Banker, and directing it to stop carrying on the activities as a Merchant Banker with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
