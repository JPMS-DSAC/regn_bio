PRESS RELEASE
PR No.225/2006

Registration of M/s. Kishore Securities Pvt. Ltd., 
Member of UPSE, cancelled
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated September 28, 2006, cancelling the certificate of registration of M/s. Kishore Securities Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INB101023837), member of the Uttar Pradesh Stock Exchange (UPSE) with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
October 09, 2006
