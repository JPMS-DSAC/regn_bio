Order of Member, SEBI against M/s Prabhudas Lilladher Private Limited Pursuant to investigations in the matter of Sawaca Business Machine Ltd.

 
SEBI vide order dated April 27, 2004 warned M/s Prabhudas Lilladher Private Limited to be careful in future in conducting their business as a stock broker for their dealings in the scrip of Sawaca Business Machines Limited.
The full contents of the order can be viewed at SEBI website: www.sebi.gov.in
