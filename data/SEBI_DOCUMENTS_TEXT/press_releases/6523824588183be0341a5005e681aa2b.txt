PR No.63/2009
Order in the matter of Samtex Fashions Limited
Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order on January 15, 2009 in the matter of Samtex Fashions Limited disposing of directions passed against its promoter related entities viz. Anju Mittal, Rama Mittal, Suresh Goel, Rachana Goel and Sincerely Finance & Investment Pvt. Ltd. in  the interim order dated    March 21, 2006.
The order shall come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
January 22, 2009
