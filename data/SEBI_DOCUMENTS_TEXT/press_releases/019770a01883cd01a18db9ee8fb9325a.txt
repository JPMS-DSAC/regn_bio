PR No.227/2010
Order in respect of HB Securities Limited in the matter of 
DCM Shriram Consolidated Limited
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  October 08, 2010, in the matter of DCM Shriram Consolidated Limited, disposing of the enquiry proceedings initiated against the stock broker, HB Securities Limited (Member, National Stock Exchange of India Limited), in respect of its transactions in the shares of DCM Shriram Consolidated Limited, without imposing any penalty.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
October 12, 2010
