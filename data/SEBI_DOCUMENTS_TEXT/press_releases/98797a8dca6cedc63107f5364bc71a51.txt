PRESS RELEASE
PR No.75/2008

M/s. J.G.A. Shah Share Brokers Pvt. Ltd., Member of BSE, censured
 

Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 26, 2008, imposing a penalty of ‘censure’ upon M/s. J. G. A. Shah Share Brokers Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INB010995237) member of the Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd.
 
The  full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
Mumbai
March 03, 2008
