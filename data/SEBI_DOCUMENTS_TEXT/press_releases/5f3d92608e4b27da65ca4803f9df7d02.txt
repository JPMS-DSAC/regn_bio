PRESS RELEASE
July 02, 2004 PR No.141/2004
Certificate of registration of a stock broker of OTC Exchange of India cancelled
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated June 22, 2004 cancelling the certificate of registration of M/s Piton Securities Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INB200832631) erstwhile member of OTC Exchange of India with immediate effect.
The aforesaid stock broker was expelled from the Stock Exchange and ceased to be a member of the Exchange, thereby failed to fulfill the pre-requisite condition of registration as stock broker with SEBI.
The registration has been cancelled in terms of Sub-Regulation (5) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002.
A full text of the order is available on our website www.sebi.gov.in
