PR No.39/2007

Order against M/s. Kamal Kumar Dugar & Co. in the matter of
M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order datedFebruary 02, 2007 against M/s. Kamal Kumar Dugar & Co., member of Calcutta Stock Exchange, in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. disposing of the show cause notice issued against the broker without imposing any penalty.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
