PRESS RELEASE
September 03, 2004 PR No.209/2004

Warning issued to M/s. Mangal Keshav Share & Stock Brokers Ltd,, Member of The Stock Exchange, Mumbai in the matter of 
M/s. Natural Expo Agro Industries Ltd. 
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order warning M/s. Mangal Keshav Share & Stock Brokers Ltd., Member of The Stock Exchange, Mumbai. 
The Order has been passed by Shri A.K.Batra, Whole Time Member, on August 31, 2004 against M/s. Mangal Keshav Share & Stock Brokers Ltd. for not exercising due care and diligence in the conduct of their business.
 
The  full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
September 3, 2004
