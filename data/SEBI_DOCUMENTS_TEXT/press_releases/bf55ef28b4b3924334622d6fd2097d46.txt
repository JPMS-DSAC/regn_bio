PRESS RELEASE
PR No.245/2007

Order against M/s. OHM Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd., Member of BSE
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  August 23, 2007 against M/s. OHM Stock Brokers Pvt. Ltd., Member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE).   The order has been passed to the effect that no penalty be levied against the broker in the matter of M/s. Color Chips India Ltd.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
