SUBJECT : OBSERVATIONS ISSUED BY SEBI DURING THE PERIOD 12/04/2004 TO 18/04/2004
ISSUED BY : CORPORATION FINANCE DEPARTMENT – DIVISION OF ISSUES AND LISTING
 
TEXT
During the period 12/04/2004 to 18/04/2004, SEBI had issued one observation on offer documents filed with it.
 
List II gives details of offer documents on which observations were issued.
 
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in ) under "Primary Market".
