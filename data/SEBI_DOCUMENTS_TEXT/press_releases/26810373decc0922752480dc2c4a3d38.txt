PR No.271/2006

 
Order against M/s. Jupiter Securities and Properties Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Millennium Cybertech Ltd.
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated December 05, 2006 vacated the ad interim exparte order against M/s. Jupiter Securities and Properties Pvt. Ltd. (JSPPL).  The ad interim exparte order was issued on January 24, 2006 against M/s. JSPPL along with other entities.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
