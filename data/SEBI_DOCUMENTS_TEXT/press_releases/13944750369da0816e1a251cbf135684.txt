PRESS RELEASE
PR No.44/2007

Order against M/s. Somani Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order dated  February 06, 2007 against M/s. Somani Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd., member of Calcutta Stock Exchange and OTCEI, in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. disposing of the show cause notice issued against the broker without imposing any penalty.
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
