PR No.376/2009





Order against M/s. Salasar Stock Broking Ltd., Member, CSE
 
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 23, 2009 warning M/s. Salasar Stock Broking Ltd., Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd. for its dealings in the shares of Global Capital Market Ltd. and directed the broker to strictly abide by laws, rules and regulations which govern its dealings in the securities market.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
