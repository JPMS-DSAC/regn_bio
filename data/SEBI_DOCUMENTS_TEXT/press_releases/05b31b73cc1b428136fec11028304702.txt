
 
Oct 20, 2016

PR No.:  
148/2016



SEBI has introduced an online system for Investment Advisers and Research Analysts to promote ease of operations in terms of e-registration, compliance reporting, etc.
 Accordingly, applicants seeking grant of registration as Investment Advisers and Research Analysts are now required  to submit only online application to SEBI at https://siportal.sebi.gov.in. The user manual available in the portal has instructions on how to submit the applications. 
Emails have already been sent to all the registered Investment Advisers and Research Analysts to activate their online account.
Portal helpline as specified in the user manual may be contacted for any assistance in this regard.
