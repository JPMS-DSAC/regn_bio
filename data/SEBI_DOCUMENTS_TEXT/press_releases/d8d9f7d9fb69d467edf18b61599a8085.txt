PR No.205/2009
Order against M/s. Prakash Nahata & Co., Member, CSE
Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  June 22, 2009 imposing a penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of M/s. Prakash Nahata & Co., Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange Ltd., for a period of one month in respect of its dealings in the shares of M/s. Toplight Commercial Ltd.
The order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
