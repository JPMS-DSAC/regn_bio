PR No.145/2006

  
Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
Balasore Alloys Ltd. 
 
An Order dated May 17, 2006 was passed by Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI granting exemption from applicability of Regulation 11(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, subject to certain conditions as mentioned therein, in respect of the proposed acquisition of 2 crores equity shares of the face value of Rs. 5 each of Balasore Alloys Ltd. by Goldline Tracom Pvt. Ltd., Ushaditya Investments Pvt. Ltd., Ispat Holdings Pvt. Ltd. and Mita Holdings Pvt. Ltd. (acquirers). Pursuant to the proposed acquisition, the shareholding of acquirers would increase from 10.91% to 38.62% and the shareholding of promoter group (including the acquirers) would increase from 27.47% to 50.03% in the total paid up equity capital of Balasore Alloys Ltd.
The proposed acquisition is through preferential allotment to the promoter group as part of their contributions in terms of the Restructuring and the Corporate Debt Restructuring (CDR) proposal sanctioned by the CDR Cell of the Reserve Bank of India and pursuant to the said acquisition, there would not be any change in control of the target company.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
