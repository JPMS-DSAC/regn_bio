PR No.190/2010
Order against Shri Rajendra J Shah in the matter of Top Telemedia Limited 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated July 30, 2010 in the matter of Top Telemedia Limited restraining Shri Rajendra J Shah from buying, selling or dealing in securities market whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of one year. 
 
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
