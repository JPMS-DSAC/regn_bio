PR No.261/2010
Consent order on the application submitted by M/s. Howard Hotels Limited
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Dr. K. M. Abraham and  Shri Prashant Saran, has passed a consent order dated November 29, 2010, on the application submitted voluntarily by M/s. Howard Hotels Limited for non-compliance of provisions of regulations 6 (4), 7 (3) and 8 (3) of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers), Regulations, 1997, in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders.
The applicant, without admitting or denying guilt, has remitted a sum of   Rs.12,25,000/- (Rupees twelve lakh twenty five thousand only) towards settlement charges in the matter.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
December 02, 2010
