
 
Sep 11, 2013

PR No.:  
81/2013


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 6, 2013, granting exemption to Salil Chaturvedi Family Trust (Acquirer) from the applicability of regulation 3(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011, for the proposed acquisition of 6.75% shares of the Target Company.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
