OFFER DOCUMENTS RECEIVED / WITHDRAWN AND OBSERVATIONS
ISSUED BY SEBI FROM JUNE 28, 2004 TO JULY 4, 2004
During the period 28/06/2004 to 4/07/2004, three offer documents were received by SEBI. SEBI gave observations on three offer documents. List I gives details of offer documents received and List II give details of observations issued.
Draft offer documents are available to the public from dealing offices of SEBI (on payment of Rs. 100/- per document by DD in favour of SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA), Lead Managers & Stock Exchanges. The draft offer documents are also posted on the website (www.sebi.gov.in) under "Primary Market".
