PR No.57/2007

 
Order against M/s. Lalit Co. (Kishan Lal Pugalia) in the matter of 
M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, passed an order dated February 13, 2007 against M/s. Lalit Co. (Kishan Lal Pugalia), member of Calcutta Stock Exchange in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. imposing a penalty of “Censure” on the broker.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
 
Mumbai
February 15, 2007
