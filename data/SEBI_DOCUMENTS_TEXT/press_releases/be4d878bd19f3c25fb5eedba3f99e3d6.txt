PR No.34/2009
Orders against M/s. A. V. Shares and Stock Broking Private Limitedand M/s. Shivam Stock Broking Private Limited , Members, Calcutta Stock Exchange in the matter of M/s. Global Capital Markets Limited
Dr.  T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed orders dated January 09, 2009 disposing of the enquiry proceedings against the brokers  M/s. A. V. Shares and Stock Broking Private Limited and M/s. Shivam Stock Broking Private Limited.  As the said brokers have already been prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities from September 29, 2005 to August 10, 2008, in accordance with interim order passed by SEBI, no further penalty has been imposed.
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
