 PRESS RELEASE




 
February 05, 2004 PR No.32/2004
The Secondary Market Advisory Committee (SMAC) has been set up by SEBI as a standing committee to advise on matters relating to secondary market. The committee has deliberated on the various disclosure requirements relating to the shareholding pattern of companies and has arrived at a revised format for the disclosure of shareholding pattern under clause 35 of the listing agreement. This revised format, prepared on the basis of the recommendations of the committee is placed on the SEBI website www.sebi.gov.in for public comments. The comments on this report may please be forwarded to Ms. Aparna Thyagarajan, Manager, SEBI or may be emailed to aparnat@sebi.gov.in on or before February 27, 2004.
