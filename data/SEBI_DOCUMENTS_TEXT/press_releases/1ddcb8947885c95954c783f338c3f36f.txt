Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of
Amit Spinning Industries Ltd.
An Order dated July 9, 2004 was passed by Shri A. K. Batra, Whole Time Member, SEBI granting exemption from making an open offer under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 to Shri Bharat P Shah, Smt. Smita Bharat Shah, Shri Amit Bharat Shah and Kum. Aparna Bharat Shah (acquirers) in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of Amit Spinning Industries Ltd. (ASIL) subject to the fulfillment of the conditions specified therein.
The promoter group (including the acquirers) are holding 37.40% shares in ASIL and are already in control of the target company. The acquirers propose to acquire 1,00,00,000 equity shares of Rs. 5 each in cash by way of preferential allotment in terms of the financial package for restructuring the debt of ASIL under the Corporate Debt Restructuring mechanism. Pursuant to the proposed acquisition, acquirers along with other promoters would hold 59.68%.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the Order is available on our website at www.sebi.gov.in
