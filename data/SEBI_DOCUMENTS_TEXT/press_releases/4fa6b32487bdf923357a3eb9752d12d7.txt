PRESS RELEASE
PR No.279/2008


Order against M/s. ICAP India Pvt. Ltd., Member, NSE


Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 21, 2008 against M/s. ICAP India Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Registration No. INB231140337), Member, National Stock Exchange of India Limited. By this order M/s. ICAP India Private Limited is warned to be more cautious in the conduct of its business and to adhere to and comply with all statutory provisions/instructions received in the discharge of its function as a stock broker. 
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in.
