PR No.98/2006

 
Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of  
Rainbow Denim Ltd. 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated January 31, 2006 granting exemption to the acquirer, Indo US Investments Inc. from complying with the provisions of Regulation 11(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997 with regard to the proposed preferential allotment of 32,93,589 equity shares of Rainbow Denim Ltd. The acquirer is belonging to the promoter group and pursuant to the proposed acquisition its holding would increase from 20.11% to 34.56% and that of the promoter group would increase from 32.30% to 44.54% of the enhanced equity voting capital of the target company. 
The proposed preferential allotment is pursuant to the stipulation imposed by the Corporate Debt Restructuring mechanism for restructuring the debts of the target company.  There would not be any change in control in the target company pursuant to the proposed allotment. The target company had already taken effective steps and passed necessary resolution in the Annual General Meeting giving the facility of postal ballot to the shareholders in accordance with rule 2A and rule 5 of The Companies (Passing of the Resolution by Postal Ballot) Rules, 2001, wherein the acquirer had abstained from voting. 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirer from making an open offer. 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
