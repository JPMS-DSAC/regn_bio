PR No.:

Shri Rajeev Kumar Agarwal, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an Order dated April  18,  2012 in the matter of SVPCL Limited, directing payment of interest in a time bound manner on the application money and interest thereon for the period of delay, to the applicants in the public issue of SVPCL Limited.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
