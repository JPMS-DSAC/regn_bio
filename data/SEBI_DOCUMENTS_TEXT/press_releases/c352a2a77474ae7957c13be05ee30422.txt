                                                                                      SEBI Cause ListSEBI initiates enforcement action against entities for alleged violation of securities laws. Hearings are regularly held as part of the quasi-judicial proceedings.In order to make entities aware of the details of quasi-judicial hearings, SEBI has decided to publish a daily "Cause-List" on the SEBI website.Details of the quasi-judicial hearings conducted under the securities laws by Adjudicating Officers, Designated Authorities and Whole-Time Members of the Board, will be uploaded on the website to enable all stakeholders to be aware of the different stages of pending proceedings. The Cause list may be accessed at https://www.sebi.gov.in/sebiweb/home/cause-list.jsp.
