PRESS RELEASE
PR No.60/2006

Certificate of registration of M/s. Kasat Securities (P) Ltd., suspended 
for four months
 
Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order against M/s. Kasat Securities (P) Ltd. (KSPL), Member of National Stock Exchange and Pune Stock Exchange under Regulation 13(4) of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002 suspending certificate of registration of KSPL for four months. 
In the matter of Snowcem India Ltd., SEBI appointed an Enquiry Officer to enquire into the alleged violations of SEBI (Stock Broker) Regulations 1992, SEBI (FUTP) Regulations 1995, and circular SMDRP/Policy/Cir-35/98 by KSPL. Enquiry officer submitted his report and observed that KSPL has violated A(2), A(3) and A(4) of the code of conduct as laid down under Schedule II read with regulation 7 of Broker Regulations, provisions of Regulation 4(a) and (b) of SEBI (Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices relating to Securities Market) Regulations 1995 and SEBI Circulars No.SEBI/SMD/Cir/93/23321 dated 18.11.93 and SEBI/Policy/Cir-35/98 dated 04.12.1998. Consequently, SEBI passed the order after considering the submissions made by KSPL. 
The order has been passed on January 25, 2006. The order shall come into force after the expiry of three weeks from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website : www.sebi.gov.in
