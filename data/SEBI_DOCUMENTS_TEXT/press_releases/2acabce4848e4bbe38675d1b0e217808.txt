
 
Sep 19, 2013

PR No.:  
91/2013


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated September 13, 2013 granting exemption to the acquirer, namely Visa Group Family Foundation from the applicability of regulation 3(1) read with regulation 5(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 [Takeover Regulations] in respect of its proposed indirect acquisition of 8,25,00,000 equity shares (constituting 75%) of the Target Company.
The applicant has filed the application dated April 26, 2013 seeking the said exemption.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
