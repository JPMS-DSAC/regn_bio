
 
Oct 12, 2011

PR No.:  
136/2011


A Panel consisting of Chairman, SEBI, Shri U. K. Sinha and  Whole Time Member, SEBI, Shri Prashant Saran has passed consent order dated October 4, 2011, on an application submitted by  Vikas Vatsal Garg (HUF) in the matter of Alka Securities Ltd., in accordance with SEBI Circular dated April 20, 2007 for consent orders. The applicant has remitted a sum of 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakh only) towards the terms of consent in the matter. The applicant has also undertaken to undergo voluntary debarment for a period of two years.
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
