PR No.210/2007


 
Order against M/s. Bonanza Portfolio Limited and M/s. Bonanza Stock Brokers Limited in the matter of M/s. Information Technologies 
(India) Limited
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  June 28, 2007 against M/s. Bonanza Portfolio Limited, Member of NSE and OTCEI and M/s. Bonanza Stock Brokers Limited, Member of BSE in the matter of M/s. Information Technologies (India) Limited. In case of the broker,  M/s. Bonanza Stock Brokers Limited proceedings have been dropped and in case of M/s. Bonanza Portfolio Limited the broker has been directed to be careful and cautious in its dealing in securities market.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
 
Mumbai
June 28, 2007
