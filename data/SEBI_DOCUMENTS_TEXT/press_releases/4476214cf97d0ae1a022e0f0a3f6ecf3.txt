Order against M/s. Khandwala Integrated Financial Services Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated March 16, 2007, has disposed of the enquiry proceedings against M/s. Khandwala Integrated Financial Services Pvt. Ltd., Member, National Stock Exchange, in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. imposing no penalty.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
