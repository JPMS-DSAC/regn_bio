PR No.220/2009
Order against M/s. M. Bhiwaniwala & Co., Member, CSE
Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated July 07, 2009, disposing of the enquiry proceedings against the broker M/s. M. Bhiwaniwala & Co., Member, Calcutta Stock Exchange, in the matter of Amluckie Investment Co. Ltd.  As the broker has already been prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities from September 29, 2005 to August 11, 2008, in accordance with interim order passed by SEBI, no further penalty has been imposed.
The full text of the order is available on thewebsite: www.sebi.gov.in
