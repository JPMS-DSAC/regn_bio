The orders have been passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI, against the above mentioned brokers for non segregation of client funds from own funds, delay in payment of funds and delivery of securities, certain irregularities in the issuance of contract notes, some procedural deficiencies in client registration forms, not having written code of internal procedure for prevention of insider trading, acceptance of cash from clients, failed to collect margin from clients etc.
 
The full text of the orders are available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
