PRESS RELEASE
PR No.205/2007


Certificate of Registration of M/s. A. Nitin Capital Services Ltd., 
Member of DSE, suspended
Dr. T.C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated   June 19, 2007, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. A. Nitin Capital  Services Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INB050720634.), Member of the Delhi Stock Exchange Association Ltd. (DSE) for a period of one month.
The order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order. 
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in

June 22, 2007
