Order against M/s. Herald Equities Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of
M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.

Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated December 15, 2006 against M/s. Herald Equities Pvt. Ltd., broker, Calcutta Stock Exchange, in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. disposing of the show cause notice issued against the broker.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
