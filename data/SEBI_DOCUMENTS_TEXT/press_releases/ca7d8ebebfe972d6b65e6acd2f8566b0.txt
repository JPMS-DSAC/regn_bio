PRESS RELEASE
PR No.96/2006

Order against M/s. H T Nanavati Securities Pvt. Ltd. and 
M/s. Hiralal Thakordas Nanavati   
 
M/s. H T Nanavati Securities Pvt. Ltd. Member – BSE (Regn. No. INB 010990330) and M/s. Hiralal Thakordas Nanavati, Member – NSE (Regn. No. INB 230439326) have been censured and directed to be more diligent in complying with the provisions of SEBI Act, 1992 and the Rules and Regulations framed thereunder for their trading in the scrip of Jayant Agro Organics Ltd. 
The order has been passed on February 01, 2006. 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
