PRESS RELEASE
PR No.65/2005

AMENDMENTS TO SEBI (DISCLOSURE AND INVESTOR PROTECTION) GUIDELINES, 2000
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has amended SEBI (DIP) guidelines 2000  vide its circular no. SEBI/CFD/DIL/DIP/15/2005/29/3  dated March 29, 2005 issued to all the registered Merchant Bankers and stock exchanges.  The amendments pertain mainly to book building.
The amendments inter-alia include enhancing the allocation of shares to retail individual investors from existing 25% to 35%, redefining the retail individual investor, as one who applies or bids for securities of or for a value not exceeding Rs.1 lac (as against the existing limit of Rs.50,000), reducing the bidding period, granting an  option to listed issuers to disclose price band / floor price one day before bid opening and ensuing relevant and uniform bid - data display  on websites of stock exchanges.
Details of the aforesaid circular is available on our website at www.sebi.gov.in.
Full text of SEBI (DIP) guidelines 2000 including the amendments issued vide this circular is also available on our website under the head “ Issues and listing “.
