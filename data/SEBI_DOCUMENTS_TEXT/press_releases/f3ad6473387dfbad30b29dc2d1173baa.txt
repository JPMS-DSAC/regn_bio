
 
Dec 29, 2011

PR No.:  
149/2011


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 27, 2011, in the matter of G G Automotive Gears Ltd., suspending the certificate of registration of the stock broker Networth Stock Broking Limited for a period of one month.
 
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
