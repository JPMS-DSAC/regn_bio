PRESS RELEASE
PR No.60/2005
 

SEBI censures M/s. Punjab National Bank
 
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated February 18, 2005 censuring M/s. Punjab National Bank for violation of code of conduct by the erstwhile M/s. PNB Capital Services Ltd. (as a merchant banker) which has since amalgamated with M/s. Punjab National Bank pursuant to the Scheme of Amalgamation approved by the Hon’ble High Court u/s 391 – 394 of the Companies Act, 1956. 
 
The full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
March 24, 2005
