Framework for recognition and supervision of stock exchanges/ platforms of stock exchanges for small and medium enterprises


The ‘Framework for recognition and supervision of stock exchanges/ platforms of stock exchanges for small and medium enterprises’ is placed on the website (www.sebi.gov.in).  The Framework, inter-alia, details the eligibility criteria for setting up of new stock exchange/platform of an existing stock exchange for the SME sector.
