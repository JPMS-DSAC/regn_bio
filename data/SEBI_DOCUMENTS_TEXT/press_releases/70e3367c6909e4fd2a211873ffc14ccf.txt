
 
Jul 21, 2011

PR No.:  
115/2011


Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  July 19, 2011 rejecting the application filed by Mr. Gaurav Dalmia on behalf of  Mr. Raghu Hari Dalmia and others for exemption from the applicability of Regulation 11(1) of Takeover Regulations in respect of their proposed increase in shareholding/voting rights from 62.56% to 75%  in OCL India Ltd.  consequent to buyback of equity shares by the company in year 2003.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
