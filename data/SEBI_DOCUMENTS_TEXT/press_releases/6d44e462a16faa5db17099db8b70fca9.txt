Order against Ramrakh R. Bohra in the matter of Granules India Ltd.
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 28, 2010 in the matter of Granules India Ltd. warning Ramrakh R. Bohra to be careful and cautious in the conduct of his business and to adhere to and comply with all the statutory provisions while carrying out his activities in the securities market.
