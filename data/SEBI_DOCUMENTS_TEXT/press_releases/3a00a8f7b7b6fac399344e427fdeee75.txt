
 
Apr 16, 2013

PR No.:  
38/2013


Shri S. Raman, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated April 10, 2013 cancelling the certificate of registration of M/s Mudra Bullions Private Limited, a member of the MCX Stock Exchange Limited.
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the Order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
