PRESS RELEASE
PR No.226/2006

Registration of M/s. Shree Yadugiri Investments,
Member of BgSE, suspended
 
Dr. T.C.Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated    September 27, 2006, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Shree Yadugiri Investments (SEBI Registration No. INB080169014), member of the Bangalore Stock Exchange (BgSE) for a period of fifteen days with effect from October 18, 2006. 
 
M/s. Shree Yadugiri Investments will not be entitled to carry on the business as a broker with effect from October 18, 2006 to November 1, 2006.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
