PRESS RELEASE
PR No.236/2007

Order against Shailesh Shah Group entities
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  August 16, 2007 against Shailesh Shah Group entities viz.  M/s. Shailesh Shah Securities Pvt. Ltd. (broker of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd.), M/s. Dolat Capital Markets Pvt. Ltd. (brokers of National Stock Exchange of India Ltd., Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd., Delhi Stock Exchange, Ahmedabad Stock Exchange and OTC Exchange of India) and M/s. Nirpan Securities Pvt. Ltd. (broker of National Stock Exchange of India Ltd. - in both capital market and Derivative Segment), imposing a minor penalty of Censure against the brokers.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
