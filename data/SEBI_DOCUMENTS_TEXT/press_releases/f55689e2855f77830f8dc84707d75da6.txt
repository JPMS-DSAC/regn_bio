PR No.64/2010
Certificate of registration of Jai Sharefin Ltd., suspended
 
Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an Order dated  March 08, 2010 suspending the certificate of registration of Jai Sharefin Ltd. for a period of three weeks.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
