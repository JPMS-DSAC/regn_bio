
 
Mar 12, 2012

PR No.:  
30/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated March 02, 2012 suspending the certificate of registration of the stock broker Subhkam Securities Pvt. Ltd. for a period of two weeks.
 
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
