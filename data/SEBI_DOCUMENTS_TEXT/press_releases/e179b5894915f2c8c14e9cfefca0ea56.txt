PRESS RELEASE
June 29, 2004 PR No.136/2004
M/s. Zoom Industrial Services Ltd. prohibited from accessing
the securities market and dealing in securities
M/s. Zoom Industrial Services Limited has been prohibited from accessing the securities market and dealing in securities, directly or indirectly, for a period of eighteen months. The order was passed by Shri A K Batra, Whole Time Member, SEBI on June 25, 2004 under Section 11B of the SEBI Act, 1992, for irregularities in the rights issue of Subhash Projects & Marketing Ltd (SPML) and price manipulation in the scrip. The order has come into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the above order is available in our website www.sebi.gov.in.
