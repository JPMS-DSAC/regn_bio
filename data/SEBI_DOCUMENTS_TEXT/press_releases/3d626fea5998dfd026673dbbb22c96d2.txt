PR No.242/2009
SEBI passes Consent Order on the application submitted by Mr. Bhavani Soni
A Panel consisting of Whole Time Members, SEBI, Shri M.S. Sahoo and     Dr. K.M. Abraham has passed a consent order dated July 29, 2009 on the consent application submitted by Mr. Bhavin Soni in the matter of M/s. Joy Reality Limited for non-compliance with Regulation 10 and 12 of the SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997.
The applicant without admitting or denying guilt has remitted a sum of Rs.50,000/-(Rupees Fifty Thousand only) towards settlement charges, in the matter.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
