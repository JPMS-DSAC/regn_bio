Order in the matter of M/s. Oasis Media Matrix Ltd.


Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 14, 2008 in the matter of M/s. Oasis Media Matrix Ltd. (OMML) restraining M/s. Oasis Media Matrix Ltd. and its directors Shri Vasant C Shah, Shri Chanderkant Mehta and Shri Kamlesh Shah and Shri Jatin Shah (Authorized Signatory of OMML) from accessing the securities market and to buy, sell or deal in securities for a period of two years.
 
The order also restrains twenty six (26) allottees viz. Shri Amit Bhavsar, Shri Anil Modi, Ms. Anitaben Shah, Shri Asif Chhipa, Shri Ashwin C Shah, Shri Bankesh Bhavsar, Shri Falgun Shah, Shri Harshad C Shah, Shri Hemant J Upadhyay, Shri Himanshu J Shah, Shri Hitesh P Shah, Ms. Jyotsenaben M Shah, Ms. Malti B Shah, Ms. Mona Falgun Shah, Shri Nageshbhai R Shah, Ms. Parulben N Shah, Shri Rohitbahi R Shah, Shri Saurabh S Pandya, Ms. Sonali P Shah, Ms. Varshaben J Shah, Shri Vijay H Shah, Shri Vipin Bhardwaj, Shri Vipul J Bhavsar, Shri Vrajesh R Patel, Ms. Yogini V Patel And Ms. Zaheda A Chhipa from accessing the securities market and to buy, sell or deal in securities for a period of one year.
 
The order comes into force with immediate effect.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
