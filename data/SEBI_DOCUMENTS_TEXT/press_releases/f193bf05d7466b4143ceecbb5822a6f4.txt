PR No.45/2009
Order against M/s. P.P.Choudhary - HUF, M/s. P.L. Choudhary & Co, 
M/s. Pawan Kumar Choudhary - HUF, Mrs. Sabitaridevi Choudhary, 
Smt. Sarita Choudhary  in the matter of 
M/s. Jagsonpal Pharmaceuticals Ltd.
Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated     January 09, 2009, in the matter of M/s. Jagsonpal Pharmaceuticals Ltd. restraining M/s. P. L. Choudhary & Co, M/s. Pawankumar Choudhary – HUF, M/s. P. P. Choudhary – HUF, Smt. Sabitaridevi Choudhary and Smt. Sarita Choudhary from accessing the securities market and from buying, selling or dealing in securities for a period of three months.
The order shall come into effect immediately.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
