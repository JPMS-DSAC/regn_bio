PRESS RELEASE
PR No.221/2006

Order in the matter of M/s. Millennium Cybertech Limited. 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI vide order dated  September 26, 2006 confirmed the ad-interim ex-parte order against the company M/s. Millennium Cybertech Limited (MCL), in the interest of investors safety and integrity of the securities market.  The ad-interim ex-parte order was issued  on January 24, 2006 against the company MCL and other entities. 
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
