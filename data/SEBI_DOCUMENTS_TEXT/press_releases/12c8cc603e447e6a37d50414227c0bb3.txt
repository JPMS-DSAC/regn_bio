
 
Mar 12, 2012

PR No.:  
29/2012


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 01, 2012 in the matter of Softrak Technology Export Ltd. restraining Rajiv Acharya from buying, selling or dealing in securities and from associating with the securities market in any manner whatsoever for a period of six months.
 
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
