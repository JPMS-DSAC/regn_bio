PRESS RELEASE
PR No.201/2007

 
Certificate of Registration of sub broker M/s. Aditya & Co., suspended
Dr. T.C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated  June 18, 2007, suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Aditya and Co. (SEBI Reg. No. INS230758711), a sub-broker affiliated to M/s. BgSE Financials Ltd., Member of the National Stock Exchange for a period of fifteen days. This order will come into force on the expiry of 21 days from the date of this order. 
 The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
