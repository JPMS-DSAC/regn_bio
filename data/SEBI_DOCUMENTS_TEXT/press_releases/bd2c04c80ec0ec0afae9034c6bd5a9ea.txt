PRESS RELEASE
PR No.169/2005

Order against M/s. Chandravadan J. Dalal 
M/s. Chandravadan J. Dalal, Member, BSE Ltd. (SEBI Registration Â No. INB 010005111) has been awarded a penalty of censure and advised to be more careful in future in its dealings in the securities market and diligently adhere to the provisions of the SEBI Act, 1992 and the regulations made thereunder. The broker has been found guilty of violation of code of conduct as specified in SEBI (Stock Brokers and Sub-broker) Regulations, 1992, while dealing in the shares of Sri Adhikari Brothers Television Network Ltd.
The order has been passed by Shri Madhukar, Whole Time Member, SEBI on December 22, 2005.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
