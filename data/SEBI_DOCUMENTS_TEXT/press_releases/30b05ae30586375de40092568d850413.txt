PR No.379/2009
Orders against Surendra Kumar Dugar & Co, Arihant Stocks Ltd.  and Shraddha Stock Broking Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Cyberscape Multimedia Ltd.
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed three orders dated December 02, 2009, in the matter of M/s. Cyberscape Multimedia Ltd. against the entities Surendra Kumar Dugar & Co., Arihant Stocks Ltd. and Shraddha Stock Broking Ltd. warning them to be careful and cautious in the conduct of their business and directing them to adhere to and comply with all the statutory provisions while carrying out their activities in the securities market.
 
The full text of the orders is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
December 04, 2009
