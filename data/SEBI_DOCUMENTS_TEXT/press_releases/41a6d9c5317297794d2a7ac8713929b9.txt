PR No.259/2010
Order in the matter of M/s. Rishabh Shares & Securities
 
Shri M. S. Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated November 23, 2010, cancelling the certificate of registration of M/s. Rishabh  Shares & Securities, a sub-broker of National Stock Exchange of India Ltd.
 
The order shall come into force on the expiry of 21 days of the date of the Order. 
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in 
 
Mumbai
December 01, 2010
