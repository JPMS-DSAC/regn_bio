
 
Feb 25, 2013

PR No.:  
23/2013


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated February 22, 2013 granting exemption to the acquirer namely AMI Holdings Private Limited from the obligation to make an open offer under regulation 3(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 with respect to the proposed acquisition of equity shares of the Target company pursuant to the conversion of 3,72,76,700 warrants.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
