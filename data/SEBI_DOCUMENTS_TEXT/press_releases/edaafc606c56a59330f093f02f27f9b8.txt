
 
Sep 29, 2014

PR No.:  
120/2014


SEBI has granted recognition to  National Securities Clearing Corporation Ltd. (NSCCL) for a period of one year commencing on the 3rd day of October, 2014 and ending on the 2nd day of October, 2015  subject to compliance with conditions which include:
1- NSCCL shall appoint compliance officer exclusively for the Clearing Corporation.
2- NSCCL shall take immediate steps to rectify the deficiencies pointed out in the systems audit.
 
The grant of recognition has been notified in the Gazette of India.
