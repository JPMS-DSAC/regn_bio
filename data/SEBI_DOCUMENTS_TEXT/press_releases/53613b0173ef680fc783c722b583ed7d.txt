PR No.227/2009
Order in the matter of M/s. Jhaveri Securities, Sub-Broker
Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated July 23, 2009 canceling the certificate of registration of the sub-broker M/s. Jhaveri Securities (Proprietor Shri Tushar Jhaveri), affiliated to Rajesh Jhaveri Stock Brokers Private Limited, Member of Ahmedabad Stock Exchange Limited.
 The order shall come into force immediately on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
 The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
July 28, 2009
