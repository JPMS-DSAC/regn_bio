PRESS RELEASE
PR No.63/2005

Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of ITI Limited
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)  has passed an order dated March 23, 2005 granting exemption to the acquirer viz. Union of India from making an open offer under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of ITI Limited (ITI).
The acquirer holds 76.67% of the total paid up share capital of ITI and after the proposed acquisition the shareholding will increase to 92.87%. Exemption is sought in respect of proposed acquisition of shares of 20,00,00,000 by the Union of India by way of preferential allotment pursuant to a Revival Package sanctioned by the acquirer to the target company.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the order is available on our website: www.sebi.gov.in
