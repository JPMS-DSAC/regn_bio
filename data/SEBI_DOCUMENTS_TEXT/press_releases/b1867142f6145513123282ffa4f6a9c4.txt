
 
May 07, 2013

PR No.:  
47/2013


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated May 7, 2013 granting exemption to the acquirer namely HD Trust from the obligation to make an open offer under regulation 3(1) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 with respect to the proposed acquisition of 1,43,323 equity shares of the Gujarat Organics Limited. 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in.
