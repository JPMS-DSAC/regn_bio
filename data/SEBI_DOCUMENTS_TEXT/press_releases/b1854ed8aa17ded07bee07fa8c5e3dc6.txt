PRESS RELEASE
PR No.248/2008
Orders against Mr. Sunil Kedia and M/s. Ashish Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd., Members, Calcutta Stock Exchange in the matter of M/s. Goenka Business and Finance Ltd. and M/s. Globe Stocks and Securities Ltd.
 
Dr. K.M.Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed orders dated  October 29, 2008 and October 31, 2008, disposing of the enquiry proceedings against the brokers Mr. Sunil Kedia and M/s. Ashish Stock Broking Pvt. Ltd.  As the said brokers have already been prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities from November 30, 2005 to August 11, 2008, in accordance with interim order passed by SEBI, no further penalty has been imposed.
 
The full text of the above orders is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
