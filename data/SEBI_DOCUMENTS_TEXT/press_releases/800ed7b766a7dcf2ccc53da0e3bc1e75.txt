PRESS RELEASE
PR No.10/2007

Grant of exemption to acquirers pursuant to Buy Back of Shares by 
M/s. Natco Pharma Ltd. (Target Company)
 
An Order dated January 05, 2007 was passed by Shri G.Anantharaman,  Whole Time Member, SEBI, granting exemption to the acquirers viz.     Mr. V.C.Nannapaneni, Smt. Durga Devi Nannapaneni, Mr. Rajeev Nannapaneni,  Ms. Neelima Sita, M/s. Timecap Pharma Labs Pvt. Ltd., M/s. Natsoft Information Systems Pvt. Ltd.  from making an open offer in terms of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 1997, with regard to increase in their voting rights from 62.28% to 63.37% consequent to the proposed  buy back of shares  by M/s. Natco Pharma Ltd.
 
Pursuant to the said increase in voting rights, there would not be any change in control of the target company.
 
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
 
The full text of the above Order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
