Order of SEBI against entities pursuant to investigations in the matter of M/s. Magic Trading and Agencies Ltd.
 
SEBI vide orders dated May 6, 2004 restrained the following entities from associating with any corporate body in accessing the securities market and prohibited them from buying, selling or dealing in securities, directly or indirectly for a period of one year:-
Mr. Dinesh Shah
Mrs. Usha Shah
Ms. Monica Patel
Mr. Sushil J. Motishaw
The full contents of the orders can be viewed at SEBI website: www.sebi.gov.in
