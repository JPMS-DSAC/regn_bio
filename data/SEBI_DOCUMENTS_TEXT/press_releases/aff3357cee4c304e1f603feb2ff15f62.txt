PR No.38/2009
Order against M/s. Khandwala Finstock Pvt. Ltd., Member, ASE
Shri M. S. Sahoo, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 14, 2009, in the matter of GCCL Infrastructure & Projects Ltd. suspending the certificate of registration of M/s. Khandwala Finstock Pvt. Ltd., Member, Ahmedabad Stock Exchange Ltd., having SEBI Registration No. INB 020723638, for seven days.
The order shall come into effect on expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
