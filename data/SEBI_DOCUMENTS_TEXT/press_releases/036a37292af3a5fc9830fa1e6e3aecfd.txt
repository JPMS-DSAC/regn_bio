Order against M/s. M.P. Vora Shares and Securities Pvt. Ltd. in the matter of M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd.
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide order dated March 15, 2007, has disposed of the enquiry proceedings against M/s. M.P.Vora Shares and Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. in the matter of   M/s. Ranbaxy Laboratories Ltd. imposing no penalty.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in 
 
 
Mumbai
March 20, 2007
