PR No.168/2007


M/s. Jayantilal Khandwala and Sons Private Limited, Member, BSE suspended for fifteen days
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide Order dated May 23, 2007, has imposed a minor penalty of suspension of certificate of registration of M/s. Jayantilal Khandwala and Sons Private Limited, a registered broker of Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), for a period of fifteen days for its dealings in the scrip of M/s. Roofit Industries Ltd.   This Order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the Order.
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
