PRESS RELEASE
PR No.247/2004
  
Registration of two stock brokers, cancelled
 
The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed two orders dated September 18, 2004 cancelling the certificate of registration of the following two stock brokers with immediate effect:
 




Sr. No.


Name of the broker


SEBI Regn. No.


Exchange

 



1


Amita Dua (Trade name: M/s. Naresh Dua & Co.)


INB120308213


LSE

 



2


M/s. Kuber Securities Ltd.


INB050942936


DSE

 



 
The aforesaid stock brokers were declared defaulters by Ludhiana Stock Exchange and Delhi Stock Exchange respectively and ceased to be members of the Exchanges, and thereby failed to fulfill the pre-requisite condition of registration as stock broker with SEBI. The registrations have been cancelled in terms of Sub-Regulation (5) of Regulation 16 of SEBI (Procedure for Holding Enquiry by Enquiry Officer and Imposing Penalty) Regulations, 2002.
