PR No.10/2011
Order in respect of M/s. Krishna Engineering Works Limited and 
Mr. Gurbachan Juneja in the matter of M/s. Krishna Engineering Works Limited
 
 
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated January 14, 2011, in the matter of M/s. Krishna Engineering Works Limited restraining M/s. Krishna Engineering Works Limited and Mr. Gurbachan Juneja from buying, selling, or dealing in securities market whatsoever or accessing the securities market, directly or indirectly, for a period of one year.
 
The order has come into force with immediate effect.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
 
Mumbai
January 18, 2011
