Order against Zee Telefilms Ltd. and its promoter entities in the matter of Zee Telefilms Ltd.
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order warning Zee Telefilms Ltd. (since changed to Zee Entertainment Enterprises Ltd. ) and its promoters viz., Churu Trading Co. Pvt. Ltd., Briggs Trading Co. Pvt. Ltd., Prajatma Trading Co. Pvt. Ltd., Ganjam Trading Co. Pvt. Ltd., Premier Finance and Trading Co. Pvt. Ltd. and Digital Super Highway Pvt. Ltd. Zee Telefilms and its promoters are also directed to note that any similar activity and/or instances of violations or non-compliance of the provisions of Securities and Exchange Board of India Act and the Rules and Regulations framed there under, in future, shall be dealt with stringently.
The order has been passed by Dr. T.C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI, on March 19, 2008 in the matter of Zee Telefilms Ltd.
The full text of the order is available on the website www.sebi.gov.in
