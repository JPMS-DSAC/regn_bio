PR No.124/2007

 
Order against M/s. N.M.Lohia & Co. in the matter of 
M/s. Global Stocks & Securities Ltd.
Shri G.Anantharaman, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated March 26, 2007, against M/s. N.M.Lohia & Co., Member of Calcutta Stock Exchange Association Ltd. (CSE) in respect of the irregular transactions in the shares of M/s. Global Stocks & Securities Ltd. imposing a major penalty of suspension of certificate of registration for a period of six months.
 
The order shall come into force immediately on the expiry of 21 days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
