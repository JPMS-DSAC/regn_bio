PR No.170/2009
Order in the matter of Anugrah Stock & Broking Pvt. Ltd., Member, BSE and NSE
Vide Order dated May 22, 2009, Anugrah Stock & Broking Pvt. Ltd. Member, BSE and NSE, has been prohibited from buying, selling or dealing in securities, in any manner whatsoever, with immediate effect until further orders.
Subsequently, on May 23, 2009, NSE, inter alia, represented that the clients of the stock broker Anugrah Stock & Broking Pvt. Ltd. need to be given an opportunity to close their positions that are open. NSE therefore requested that the stock broker Anugrah Stock & Broking Pvt. Ltd. may be put in square off mode, wherein he would be in a position to only bring down the existing open positions by closing the position. However, he may not be allowed to take fresh positions or increase the open position.
In order to ensure that the interest of the investors who are trading through the stock broker Anugrah Stock & Broking Pvt. Ltd. are not prejudicially affected, Dr. K.M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI, vide Order dated May 23, 2009 modified the operative portion of the order dated April 22, 2009 to permit the concerned stock exchanges to square off existing open client positions in F&O segment of the stock broker, but, no fresh position would be permitted. The order further clarifies that that the permission to square off the existing client open positions in F&O segment does not apply to the open client positions of restrained persons/ entities and the stock broker’s open proprietary positions.  The remaining part of the order dated May 22, 2009 remains unchanged.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
