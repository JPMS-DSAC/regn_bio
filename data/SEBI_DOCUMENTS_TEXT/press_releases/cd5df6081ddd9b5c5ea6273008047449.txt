
 
Dec 04, 2013

PR No.:  
116/2013


Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated December 3, 2013, granting exemption to the Government of India (“GoI” or “Acquirer”) from the applicability of Regulation 3(2) of SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeover) Regulations, 2011, in respect of the proposed increase in shareholding from 82.232% to 87.998%, pursuant to allotment of 18,00,41,152 equity shares of United Bank of India (Applicant) by way of preferential allotment.
The applicant has filed the application dated November 6, 2013 seeking the said exemption.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
