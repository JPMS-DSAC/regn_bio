PRESS RELEASE
August 31, 2004 PR No.202/2004

Grant of exemption for proposed acquisition of shares of 
M/s. Larsen & Toubro Limited 
An order dated August 25, 2004 was passed by Shri A. K. Batra, Whole Time Member, granting exemption from making an open offer under SEBI (Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 1997 to the acquirer viz. M/s.Larsen & Toubro Employees Welfare Foundation in the matter of proposed acquisition of shares of M/s. Larsen & Toubro Limited (L&T).
The acquirer holds 14.95% of the total paid up share capital of the target company and after the proposed acquisition the shareholding will increase to 15.72%. The acquirer is established for promoting the welfare activities for the benefit of the employees of the target company. The acquirer does not intend to trade in the shares of the target company in the open market or for any commercial purpose and further there is no intention on the part of the acquirer to acquire the control on the management of the target company.  The present acquisition by the acquirer is mainly for protecting the welfare of the employees of the target company and at the same time to preserve the professional management model of the target company.
The application seeking exemption was forwarded by SEBI to the Takeover Panel. The Panel recommended for exemption to the acquirers from making an open offer.
The full text of the order is available on our website www.sebi.gov.in
