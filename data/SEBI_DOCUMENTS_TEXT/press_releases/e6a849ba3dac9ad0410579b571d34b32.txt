PRESS RELEASE
December 08, 2003PR No.295/2003
Investigations Department
Order u/s 11 B against M/s Kosha Investments Ltd
Pursuant to investigations in the matter of Snowcem India Ltd.
SEBI vide order dated December 03, 2003 restrained M/s Kosha Investments Ltd, from buying, selling or dealing in securities in any manner, directly or indirectly for a period of two years for their role in the price manipulation of the scrip Snowcem India Ltd.
The full contents of the order can be viewed at www.sebi.gov.in
