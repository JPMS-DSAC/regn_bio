Order in the matter of Genus Commutrade Ltd.
 Dr. K. M. Abraham, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order dated March 31, 2010, issuing warning to Mr. Rajeshkumar V. Patel, Mrs. Kirtiben R. Patel, Mr. Paresh R. Patel, Mrs. Hema Paresh Patel and Mr. Dhaval V. Shah and also directing them to strictly comply with the securities laws and other requirements which govern their conduct in the securities market.
