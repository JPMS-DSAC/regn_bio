PRESS RELEASE
PR No.93/2007

Order against M/s. Rolex Finvest Pvt. Ltd.
 
 
Shri V.K.Chopra, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated  March 12, 2007 against the sub-broker M/s. Rolex Finvest Pvt. Ltd. in respect of its dealings in the scrip of M/s. Warner Multimedia Ltd., imposing a minor penalty of ‘censure’ on the sub-broker.
 
The full text of the above order is available on the website:  www.sebi.gov.in
