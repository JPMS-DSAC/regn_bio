PR No.64/2009
Order in the matter of M/s. Tropical Securities & Investments Private Limited in the scrip of M/s. DCM Shriram Consolidated Limited
Dr. T. C. Nair, Whole Time Member, SEBI has passed an order on January 14, 2009 in the matter of investigation into the price manipulation in the scrip of M/s. DCM Shriram Consolidated Limited imposing a penalty of suspension of the certificate of registration of M/s. Tropical Securities & Investments Private Limited for a period of six months.
This order shall come into effect on expiry of 21 days from the date of this order.
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
