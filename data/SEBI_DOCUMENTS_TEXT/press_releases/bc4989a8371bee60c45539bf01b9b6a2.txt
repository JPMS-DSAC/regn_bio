PRESS RELEASE
PR No.130/2006

M/s. Rama Securities Pvt. Ltd., Member, CSE, censured
Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has passed an order dated  April 28, 2006, imposing a penalty of censure upon Rama Securities Pvt. Ltd. (SEBI Reg. No. INB031017730) member of the Calcutta Stock Exchange.
 
The order has been passed by the Whole Time Member, SEBI against Rama Securities Pvt. Ltd. for Non-segregation of client’s and own funds.
The full text of the above order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
Mumbai
May 04, 2006
