PR No.48/2011
Order in respect of Sanchay Fincom Limited  in the matter of 
Doctor’s Biotech Limited
Shri Prashant Saran, Whole Time Member, SEBI, has passed an order dated   March 17, 2011 in the matter of Doctor’s Biotech Limited, suspending the certificate of registration of Sanchay Fincom Limited., member, Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. (BSE), for a period of one month.
The order shall come into force on expiry of twenty one days from the date of the order.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
 
Mumbai
March 25, 2011
