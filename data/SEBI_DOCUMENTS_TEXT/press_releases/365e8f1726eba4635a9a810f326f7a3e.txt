
 
Nov 07, 2012

PR No.:  
94/2012


A Panel  consisting of  Whole Time Members,  SEBI,  Shri  Rajeev Kumar Agarwal and Shri Prashant Saran has passed a consent  order dated November 01, 2012   on the application submitted  by M/s  "Small is beautiful" Fund for violation of Regulation 12(c) and 12(d) of the SEBI (Venture Capital funds) Regulations, 1996.The applicant  has remitted  a sum of Rs. 37,50,000/-( Rupees Thirty Seven Lakh and Fifty Thousand only) towards settlement charges.
 
The full text of the order is available on the website: www.sebi.gov.in
