 

 

 

CAUTION NOTICE 

by  
 

 

 
 

 

 

  

SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE BOARD OF INDIA 

about the illegal activities carried out by  

Shri Rajeev Kapoor,  

M/s Imax Equity Research  

1.  The general public are hereby informed that SEBI has launched prosecution proceedings u/s 24 
of the SEBI Act 1992 against Shri Rajeev Kapoor (whose photograph is given below) the 
proprietor of Imax Equity Research, 501-502, Ansal Pragati Deep Tower, District 
Centre,  Laxmi  Nagar,  Delhi-  110092  for  the  violation  of  the  provisions  of  SEBI 
(Prohibition  of  Fraudulent  and  Unfair  Trade  Practices  Relating  to  Securities  Market), 
Regulations, 2003 and SEBI (Portfolio Managers) Regulations, 1993 in the court of the Learned 
Chief Metropolitan Magistrate, Tis Hazari Courts, Delhi on 11.07.2008. 

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
                               
2.  His modus operandi includes activities like soliciting investments from public through grossly 
misleading advertisements ,mass messaging, emails and telephone calls. Advertisements issued 
by  him  contain  deceptive  claims  like  ‘Loss  100%borne  by  us…’,  ‘Get  4%  monthly  fixed 
return..’. Recently on 04.07.2008 an advertisement was released on page 11 of The Economic 
Times  by  M/s  Imax  Equity  Research  containing  deceptive  claims  like  ‘Loss  100%  borne  by 
us…’.  The  said  offence  is  punishable  with  imprisonment  for  a  term  which  may  extend  to  10 
years or with fine which may extend to 25 Crores rupees or with both. 

Sh. Rajeev Kapoor is an habitual offender and has been fraudulently operating in the securities 
market in violation of the statutory provisions. Restraint orders dated 02.01.2004 and 16.08.2004 
and adjudication order dated 03.11.2004 have been passed by SEBI against him. Despite these 
orders he continues to carry on operations. SEBI has therefore filed prosecution cases at Kolkatta 
(No.  C/11328/2006,  Court  of  CMM),  Mumbai  (No.  10/M/2006,  Court  of  ACMM)  and  also 
before the Learned CMM, Delhi.  

 
4.  Besides  the  cases  filed  by  SEBI,  it  has  also  been  learnt  that  numerous  police  cases  are  also 
pending  against  him  by  different  State  Police  departments  and  he  has  been  in  judicial/  police 
custody on several occasions in the past. 

 
5.  Therefore the public are hereby cautioned against dealing with him or any of his concerns like 
M/s Imax Equity Research. The public in general are also advised not to be attracted or lured by 
similar advertisements which solicit investments and promise unrealistic returns.
