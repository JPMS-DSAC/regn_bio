                 

    
 

July 31, 2020 

Public Notice 

 
Extension of last date to view status of/ deficiency in claim applications and making good those 
deficiencies 
 
The Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee (in the matter of PACL Ltd.) (the “Committee”) vide press 
release  dated  January  15,  2020  had  intimated    provision  of    a  facility  to  view  the  status 
of/deficiency in claim applications having claim amount upto Rs. 5,000/- and making good those 
deficiencies on or before July 31, 2020.  
 
The Committee has now extended the last date for checking the status of such claim applications 
and/or  rectification  of  deficiencies  to  October  31,  2020.  Investors/applicants  whose  claim 
applications were  found deficient, are  requested to check  the  status of their claim applications 
online  on www.sebipaclrefund.co.in  and rectify deficiencies, if any, in their claims on or before 
October 31,2020.
