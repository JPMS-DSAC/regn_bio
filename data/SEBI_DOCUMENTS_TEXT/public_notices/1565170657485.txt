 
Sub- In the matter of properties of PGF Ltd 
 

 PUBLIC NOTICE 

It is brought to the notice of the public at large that in compliance of the Orders 
passed by the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in  Civil Appeal No. 6572/2004 
titled as M/s. PGF Ltd. & Ors. V. Union of India & Anr., a Committee has been 
constituted  by  Supreme  Court  of  India  under  the  chairmanship  of  Justice 
Vikramajit Sen, Former Judge of the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India and Justice 
R. V. Easwar, Former Judge of the Hon’ble High Court of Delhi  for disposing of 
the properties of PGF Ltd., so that the sale proceeds can be paid to the investors 
(the “Committee”).  
 
The properties of PGF Ltd. seized by CBI and made available to the Committee 
are listed on the SEBI’s website www.sebi.gov.in. 
 
In this respect, it is reiterated that in terms of the above Order dated 1st April 2015, 
only the Committee is authorised to sell the properties of PGF Ltd. or properties 
wherein  PGF  Ltd.  or  any  of  its  associates/subsidiaries  have  any  interest/right, 
directly  or  indirectly.  Accordingly,  it  is  clarified  that  the  Committee  has  not 
authorised any individual/ entity to sell the properties of PGF Ltd. It is also stated 
that  any  attempt  by  individuals/  entities  to  illegally  and  unauthorizedly  take 
possession of the properties of PGF Ltd. shall invite necessary action under law.  
 
In view of the above, the public at large is once again cautioned against buying 
or dealing with the properties of PGF Ltd. or properties wherein PGF Ltd. or any 
of its associates/subsidiaries have any interest/right, directly or indirectly. For any 
information with respect to the matter of PGF Ltd., only the public notices and 
press  releases  published  by  the  Committee,  available  on  the  website: 
www.sebi.gov.in may be relied upon.
