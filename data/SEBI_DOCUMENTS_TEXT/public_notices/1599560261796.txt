PUBLIC NOTICE 

September 08, 2020 

 

STATUS OF REFUND EFFECTED TO INVESTORS OF PACL LTD. 

 

The  Justice  (Retd.)  R.  M.  Lodha  Committee  (in  the  matter  of  PACL  Ltd.)  has,  as  on  date,  successfully 
effected refund with respect to  12,48,344 eligible applications (with claims upto Rs. 10,000/-) aggregating 
to Rs. 429.13 crore. 

The  Committee  had,  after  effecting  refund  with  respect  to  9,72,481  eligible  applications  having  claim 
amount  upto  Rs.  8,000/-,  initiated  the  process  of  refund  to  investors  with  claim  amount  between  Rs. 
8,001/- and Rs. 10,000/- and has effected payment with respect to  2,75,863 eligible applications.
