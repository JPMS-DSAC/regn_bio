Sub:  Information to investors about fraudulent website created by Trading India 
Portfolios Ltd/ TIPL Ltd and its Chairman & CMD Mr. Ankush Srivastava.  

SEBI is in receipt of complaint against Trading India Portfolios Ltd/ TIPL Ltd (TIPL) and 
its  Chairman  &  CMD  Mr.  Ankush  Srivastava  regarding  unauthorized  portfolio 
management  activity.    From  the  website  (www.tiplwealth.com)  of  TIPL  it  has  been 
observed  that    TIPL  has  placed  false  and  misleading  information  related  to  portfolio 
management  and      stock  broking  services.  TIPL  is  not  registered  with  SEBI  in  any 
capacity and the registration details given on its website are false. 

As per extant regulatory requirements, it is mandatory for entity  to obtain registration 
from  SEBI  prior  to  operating  as  a  portfolio  manager  or  stock  broker.  Therefore, 
aforesaid claims made by the entity are false and misleading. 

In  this  context,  the  general    public  is  hereby  cautioned  against  dealing  with  Trading 
India  Portfolios  Ltd  /TIPL  Ltd  and  its  Chairman  &  CMD  Mr.  Ankush  Srivastava.  The 
public in general is also advised against being misled by similar advertisements which 
solicit investments and/ or promise unrealistic returns.     

This Public Notice is issued in the general interest of investors in the securities market. 

 

Mumbai 

24/07/2012
