 

 

PUBLIC NOTICE 

Date: October 15, 2020 

    Sub: Last date for investors/applicants with claims upto Rs.7,000/- for making good 

the deficiencies in their claims, if any. 
 

January  15,  2020  provided  an  opportunity 

1.  The  Justice  (Retd.)  R.M.  Lodha  Committee  (the  “Committee”)  vide  Press 
Release  dated 
to  all 
investors/applicants with claims upto Rs 5000/-, whose claim applications were 
found deficient, to make good deficiencies as may be applicable to their claims, 
so  as  to  enable  their  claims  to  be  processed.    Accordingly,  web-portal 
(www.sebipaclrefund.co.in)  providing  such  facility  was  made  operational  on 
January 24, 2020. The last date for accepting such rectified application was July 
31,  2020.  Thereafter,  the  Committee  vide  Public  Notice  dated  July  31,  2020 
extended the last date to October 31, 2020. 
 

2.  The Committee has vide Public Notice dated July 21, 2020 also provided similar 
facility to the investors with claim amount between Rs 5001/- and Rs 7000/- 
and accordingly, the web-portal was made operational from August 01, 2020. 
The last date for accepting such rectified application is October 31, 2020. 
 

3.  Investors/applicants with claims upto Rs.7,000/- whose claim applications were 
found deficient, are again requested to expedite and rectify the deficiencies, if 
any, in their applications before October 31, 2020. 
 
 

  Nodal Officer cum Secretary 

Justice (Retd.) R.M. Lodha Committee 
(In the matter of PACL Ltd.)
