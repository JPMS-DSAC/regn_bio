1.  The  Justice  (Retd.)  R.M.  Lodha  Committee  (the  “Committee”)  vide  Press

from  January  24,  2020.  The  last  date  for  checking  the  status  of  claim 
applications and/or rectification of deficiencies is July 31, 2020. 
 

3.  The investors/applicants with claims upto Rs. 5,000/-, whose claim applications 
were  found  deficient,  are  again  requested  to  check  the  status  of  their  claim 
applications  online  on   https://www.sebipaclrefund.co.in and  rectify  the 
deficiencies, if any, before July 31, 2020.
