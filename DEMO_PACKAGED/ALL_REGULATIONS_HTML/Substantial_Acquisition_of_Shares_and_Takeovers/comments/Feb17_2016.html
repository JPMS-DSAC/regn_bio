(vii)  Requirements in relation to establishing, maintaining written internal policies and control procedures governing the dealing and trading by any research analyst have been prescribed in the regulations. (xiii)   The regulations specify restrictions on trading and on compensation of the persons who make comments or recommendations concerning securities or public offer through public media.
