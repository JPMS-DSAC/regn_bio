Securities and Exchange Board of India (Payment of Fees and Mode of Payment) (Amendment) Regulations, 2017 w.e.f. March 06, 2017 (notified on March 29, 2017) Details: The amendment made to various Regulations to enable market participants to make payments to SEBI through digital mode (such as NEFT/RTGS) as well and also provides for revision of fees/charges in various regulations covered therein.
