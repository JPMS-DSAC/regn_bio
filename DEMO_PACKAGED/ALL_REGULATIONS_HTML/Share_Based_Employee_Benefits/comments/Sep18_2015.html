In this regard, RBI has notified the Foreign Exchange Management (Transfer or Issue of Security by a Person Resident Outside India) (Ninth Amendment) Regulations, 2014 on June 30, 2014. (DIR Series) circular No. 3 dated July 14, 2014 ("RBI circular") declaring the said instruments as eligible for the purpose of FDI and FPI subject to compliance with FDI and FPI schemes.
